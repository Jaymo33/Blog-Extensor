<h2>How to Convert 2 Cups of Water to Ounces</h2>  
<p>The conversion from cups to ounces is straightforward but depends on whether you're measuring fluid ounces (volume) or weight ounces (mass). In the UK, 1 cup of water is typically 8.45 fluid ounces, meaning 2 cups equal roughly 16.9 fluid ounces. However, for simplicity, most recipes round this to 16 fluid ounces.</p>  
<p>If you're working with weight, 2 cups of water weigh approximately 16.7 ounces (473 grams). This distinction is crucial for baking or making sauces in your air fryer. For a handy reference, check out our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-conversion-chart>air fryer conversion chart</a> to simplify your cooking process.</p>  

<h3>Fluid Ounces vs. Weight Ounces</h3>  
<p>Fluid ounces measure volume, while weight ounces measure mass. For water, the two are nearly equivalent, but this isn't true for all ingredients. When measuring 2 cups of water for air fryer recipes, fluid ounces are typically used unless specified otherwise. Always double-check your recipe to avoid confusion.</p>
