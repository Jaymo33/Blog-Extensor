<h3>How to Measure 0.375 Cup of Ground Cinnamon</h3>  
<p>Measuring 0.375 cup of ground cinnamon requires either a liquid measuring cup or dry measuring spoons. For accuracy, spoon the cinnamon into the cup and level it off with a straight edge. Avoid packing it down, as this can lead to an incorrect measurement. If you're using a scale, 1.5 ounces is the equivalent weight for 0.375 cup. This method is particularly useful for air fryer recipes where consistency is crucial.</p>
