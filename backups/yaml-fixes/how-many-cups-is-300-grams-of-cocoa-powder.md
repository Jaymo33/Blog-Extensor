<h3>US vs. UK Cup Measurements</h3>  
<p>The difference between US and UK cup measurements might seem minor, but it can affect your recipe. A US cup holds 240ml, while a UK cup is 250ml. <h2>How to Measure Cocoa Powder Correctly</h2>  
<p>Scooping cocoa powder directly from the bag can lead to inconsistent measurements due to compaction. Instead, spoon the powder lightly into your measuring cup and level it off with a knife. This method ensures you're not packing in too much, which could throw off your recipe. For air fryer baking, precision is key—so take your time measuring.</p>

<h3>Common Mistakes When Measuring Cocoa Powder</h3>  
<p>One of the biggest mistakes is using a liquid measuring cup for dry ingredients like cocoa powder. Dry measuring cups are designed to be filled and levelled, ensuring accuracy.
