<h3>US vs UK Cup Measurements</h3>
<p>The difference between US and UK cup measurements might seem small, but it can add up, especially in larger quantities like 985 ml of half and half. In the US, a cup is 240 ml, so 985 ml equals roughly 4.16 cups. In the UK, a cup is 284 ml, making 985 ml approximately 3.46 cups.</p>
