<h2>How to Convert 2.25 Cups of Honey to ml</h2>  
<p>The standard conversion for honey is 1 cup = 236.588 ml. Multiplying this by 2.25 gives you 532.32 ml. However, it’s worth noting that honey’s density can vary slightly depending on temperature and type (e.g., raw, processed). For most cooking purposes, 532 ml is a safe and accurate measurement.</p>
