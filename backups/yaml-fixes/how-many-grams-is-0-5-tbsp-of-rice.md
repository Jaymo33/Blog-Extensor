<h2>Types of Rice and Their Weights</h2>  
<p>Not all rice weighs the same per tablespoon. Long-grain white rice, for instance, is lighter than short-grain or brown rice. Here’s a quick breakdown of common varieties:</p>  
<ul>  
<li>White rice (long-grain): ~6g per 0.5 tbsp</li>  
<li>Brown rice: ~7g per 0.5 tbsp</li>  
<li>Basmati or jasmine: ~5.5g per 0.5 tbsp</li>  
</ul>
