<h3>How to Measure 0.125 Cup of Ground Almonds</h3>  
<p>Use a dry measuring cup for the most accurate results. Spoon the ground almonds into the cup lightly without packing them down, then level off the top with a straight edge. For smaller quantities like 0.125 cup (which is 2 tablespoons), a kitchen scale is even more reliable.</p>
