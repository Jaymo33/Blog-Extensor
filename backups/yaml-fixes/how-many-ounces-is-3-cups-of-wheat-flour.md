<h3>How to Measure 3 Cups of Wheat Flour Correctly</h3>  
<p>To measure 3 cups of wheat flour accurately, fluff the flour with a spoon before lightly scooping it into your measuring cup. Avoid packing it down, as this can lead to over-measuring. Level off the top with a knife for consistency. For best results, consider investing in a kitchen scale to weigh your flour directly in ounces or grams.</p>
