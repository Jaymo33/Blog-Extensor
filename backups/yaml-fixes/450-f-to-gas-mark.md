<h2>Understanding Oven Temperature Conversions</h2>
<p>When converting 450 Fahrenheit to Gas Mark, it's helpful to understand how these scales relate. <p>The Gas Mark system runs from 1 to 9, with each number representing about 25-30°C (45-54°F) increments. Gas Mark 8 at 450°F falls at the higher end of the scale, ideal for quick cooking methods. <h2>Using 450 F in Your Air Fryer</h2>
<p>Modern air fryers often display temperatures in Fahrenheit, making the 450 F to Gas Mark conversion useful for UK cooks. <p>When cooking at 450°F (Gas Mark 8), always preheat your air fryer for 3-5 minutes.
