<h2>How to measure 250g cocoa powder without a scale</h2>  
<p>If you don’t have a digital scale, you can still measure 250g of cocoa powder using measuring cups. One cup of cocoa powder typically weighs around 100g, so 250g would be roughly 2.5 cups. However, this method isn’t as precise as weighing, as scooping techniques can vary. For best results, fluff the cocoa powder with a spoon before lightly spooning it into the cup. Avoid packing it down, as this can lead to over-measuring.</p>
