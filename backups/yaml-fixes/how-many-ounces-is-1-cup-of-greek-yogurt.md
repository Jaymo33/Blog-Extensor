<h2>Why accurate measurements matter in air fryer cooking</h2>
<p>Air fryers rely on precise ingredient ratios for even cooking and perfect texture. The dense consistency of greek yogurt means volume-to-weight conversions differ from regular yogurt.</p>

<h2>How to measure greek yogurt correctly</h2>
<p>For the most accurate 1 cup greek yogurt to ounces conversion, use these professional tips. Always spoon the yogurt into the measuring cup rather than scooping to avoid air pockets. Level off the top with a straight edge for precise measurement.</p>

<h3>Using kitchen scales for perfect results</h3>
<p>Digital scales give the most reliable 1 cup of greek yogurt in ounces measurement. Simply tare your bowl, then add yogurt until you reach 8.64oz. <h2>Storage and handling tips for greek yogurt</h2>
