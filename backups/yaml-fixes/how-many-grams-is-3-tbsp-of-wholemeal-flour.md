<h2>How to measure 3 tbsp of wholemeal flour correctly</h2>  
<p>Using a kitchen scale is the most accurate way to measure 3 tbsp of wholemeal flour in grams. Simply spoon the flour into the tablespoon without packing it down, then level it off with a knife. This method prevents over-measuring, which can happen if you scoop directly from the bag.</p>  
<p>For those without scales, fluffing the flour with a fork before measuring helps maintain accuracy. Wholemeal flour tends to settle, so aerating it ensures a lighter, more consistent tablespoon. If you're baking frequently, investing in a digital scale will save time and improve results.</p>  

<h3>Common mistakes when converting tbsp to grams</h3>
