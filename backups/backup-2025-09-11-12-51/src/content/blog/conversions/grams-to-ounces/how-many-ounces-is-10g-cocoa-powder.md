<h2>How to Measure 10g of Cocoa Powder Without a Scale</h2>
<p>If you don’t have a scale, you can still measure 10g of cocoa powder using spoons. One tablespoon of cocoa powder typically weighs around 7g, so 10g is roughly 1.5 tablespoons. Keep in mind that scooping methods can vary, so it’s best to fluff the cocoa powder first and level it off for accuracy.</p>
