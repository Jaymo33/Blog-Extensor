<h3>How to Measure 125g of Breadcrumbs Accurately</h3>  
<p>For the best results, use a digital kitchen scale to measure 125g of breadcrumbs. If you don’t have one, a standard measuring cup can approximate the weight—125g is roughly 1.5 cups of fine breadcrumbs. However, scales are more reliable, especially for air fryer recipes where precision matters. Fluff the breadcrumbs lightly before measuring to avoid compacting them, which can lead to inaccuracies.</p>
