<h2>How to Measure 1000g of Oats Accurately</h2>  
<p>Using a digital kitchen scale is the easiest way to measure 1000g of oats. Simply place your bowl on the scale, tare it to zero, and add the oats until you hit 1000g. If your scale displays ounces, you'll see it settle around 35.27 oz. This method eliminates guesswork and ensures your air fryer recipes turn out perfectly every time.</p>  

<h3>Using Measuring Cups for Oats</h3>
