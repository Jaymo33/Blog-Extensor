<h2>How to measure 15g of soft cheese without scales</h2>  
<p>Not everyone has kitchen scales handy, but there are easy ways to estimate 15g of soft cheese. A level tablespoon of most soft cheeses weighs roughly 15g, which converts to 0.53 ounces. For firmer varieties like goat cheese, a slightly heaped tablespoon should do the trick.</p>  

<h3>Using common household items</h3>
