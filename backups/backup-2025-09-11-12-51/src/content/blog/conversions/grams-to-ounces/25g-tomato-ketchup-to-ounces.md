<h2>How to Measure 25g of Tomato Ketchup Without Scales</h2>
<p>No scales? No problem. Roughly 2 level tablespoons of tomato ketchup weigh close to 25g (0.88 oz). For sticky ingredients like ketchup, lightly grease the spoon first for easier release. This trick works well for air fryer sauces and marinades.</p>
<p>If you’re adapting a recipe from <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>our collection</a>, note that 25g of ketchup is about 1.5 UK tablespoons. For thicker sauces, always opt for weight over volume to avoid over-pouring.</p>
