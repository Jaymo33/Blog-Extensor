<h3>How to measure 25g of baking soda without a scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a scale, 25g of baking soda is roughly 5 teaspoons. However, spooning methods can be inconsistent due to settling or packing. For best results, fluff the baking soda first, then lightly spoon it into a measuring spoon. Level it off with a knife for accuracy. Remember, this method is less precise than weighing, so invest in a scale for reliable results.</p>
