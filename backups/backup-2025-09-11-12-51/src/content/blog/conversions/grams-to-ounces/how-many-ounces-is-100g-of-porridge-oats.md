<h3>How to Convert 100g of Porridge Oats to Ounces</h3>  
<p>To convert 100g of porridge oats to ounces, multiply the grams by 0.035274. This gives you 3.53 ounces, which is the standard conversion for most cooking purposes. If you don’t have a scale, you can also use measuring cups, though weighing is more accurate.
