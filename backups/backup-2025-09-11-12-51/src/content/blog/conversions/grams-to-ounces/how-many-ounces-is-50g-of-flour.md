<h2>How to measure 50g of flour accurately</h2>
<p>Using a digital kitchen scale is the most accurate way to measure 50g of flour. Simply place your bowl on the scale, tare it to zero, and add flour until it hits 50g. If you don’t have a scale, spoon the flour into a measuring cup and level it off. Remember, 50g of flour in ounces is roughly 1.76, but slight variations can occur based on how packed the flour is. For air fryer recipes, consistency is key, so we recommend sticking to weight measurements.</p>
