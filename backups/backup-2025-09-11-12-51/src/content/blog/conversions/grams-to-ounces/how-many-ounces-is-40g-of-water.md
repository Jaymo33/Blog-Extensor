<h3>How to Measure 40g of Water Accurately</h3>  
<p>Using a digital kitchen scale is the easiest way to measure 40g of water. Set the scale to grams, place a container on it, and tare the weight. Then, pour water until it reaches 40g. If your scale only displays ounces, aim for 1.41 oz. For smaller quantities, a measuring spoon can also work—20ml is roughly equivalent to 20g, but this method is less precise.</p>
