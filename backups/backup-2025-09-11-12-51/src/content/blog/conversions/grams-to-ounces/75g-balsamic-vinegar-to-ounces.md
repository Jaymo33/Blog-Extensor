<h2>How to measure 75g of balsamic vinegar</h2>
<p>There are several ways to measure out 75g of balsamic vinegar (2.65 ounces) for your air fryer recipes. The most accurate method is using digital kitchen scales set to grams. Simply place your measuring container on the scales, tare to zero, then pour the vinegar until you reach 75g.</p>
<p>If you don't have scales, you can use volume measurements. Since balsamic vinegar has a similar density to water, 75g equals about 75ml or 5 tablespoons. However, this method is slightly less precise as different brands of balsamic vinegar can vary in viscosity. For best results in your air fryer cooking, we recommend investing in a good set of digital scales.</p>
