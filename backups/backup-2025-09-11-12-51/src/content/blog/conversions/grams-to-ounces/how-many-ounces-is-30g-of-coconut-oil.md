<h3>How to measure 30g of coconut oil without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can still measure 30g of coconut oil using tablespoons. One tablespoon of coconut oil weighs roughly 14g, so 30g is just over two tablespoons.
