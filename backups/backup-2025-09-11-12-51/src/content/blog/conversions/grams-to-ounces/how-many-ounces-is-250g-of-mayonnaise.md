<p>250g of mayonnaise equals approximately 8.82 ounces. This conversion is essential when following air fryer recipes that require precise measurements for perfect results. Whether you're making crispy coatings or creamy dips, knowing your conversions ensures your dish turns out just right.</p>

<h2>Why accurate mayonnaise measurements matter</h2>
<p>Getting your mayonnaise quantities spot-on makes all the difference in air fryer cooking. Too much can make coatings soggy, while too little might leave your dish dry. Many recipes on <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/>airfryerrecipe.co.uk</a> specify weights rather than volumes because it's more precise.</p>
<p>When converting 250g mayonnaise to ounces, remember that UK and US fluid ounces differ slightly. For solid ingredients like mayonnaise, we use the avoirdupois ounce (28.35g). This distinction matters when preparing international recipes or using imported ingredients.</p>

<h2>How to measure 250g of mayonnaise</h2>
<p>While kitchen scales are ideal, you can estimate 250g of mayonnaise using common household items. A standard UK tablespoon holds about 15g, meaning 250g equals roughly 17 tablespoons. However, we recommend using scales for accuracy, especially when making <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>air fryer recipes</a> where precision counts.</p>

<h3>Conversion methods for 250g mayonnaise</h3>
<p>The simplest way to convert 250g mayonnaise to ounces is dividing by 28.35 (grams per ounce). This gives you 8.82 ounces. Some cooks round this to 9 ounces for convenience, but for baking or delicate coatings, stick to the precise figure.</p>

<h3>Storing leftover mayonnaise</h3>
<p>If your recipe only uses part of your 250g mayonnaise, store the remainder properly. Transfer it to an airtight container and refrigerate immediately. Properly stored, opened mayonnaise lasts about 2 months in the fridge.</p>
