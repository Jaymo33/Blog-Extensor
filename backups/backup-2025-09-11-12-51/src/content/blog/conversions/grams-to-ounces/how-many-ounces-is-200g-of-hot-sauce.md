<h3>How to measure 200g of hot sauce without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can still measure 200g of hot sauce using volume. One tablespoon of hot sauce weighs roughly 15g, so 200g is about 13 tablespoons. This method isn’t as precise as weighing, but it’s handy in a pinch. For best results, invest in a digital scale for accurate measurements.</p>
