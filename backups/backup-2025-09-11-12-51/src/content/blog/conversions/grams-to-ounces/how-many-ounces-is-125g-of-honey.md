<h2>How to measure 125g of honey without scales</h2>  
<p>If you don’t have digital scales, you can still measure 125g of honey using household utensils. A standard UK tablespoon holds about 21g of honey, so 6 tablespoons will give you close to 125g. For smaller adjustments, a teaspoon holds roughly 7g.
