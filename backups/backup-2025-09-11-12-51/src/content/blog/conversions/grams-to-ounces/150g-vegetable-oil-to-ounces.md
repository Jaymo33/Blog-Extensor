<h2>How to Measure 150g of Vegetable Oil Without Scales</h2>  
<p>Don't have a kitchen scale? No problem. You can still measure 150g of vegetable oil (5.29 oz) using common household items. One tablespoon of vegetable oil weighs roughly 14g, so 150g is about 10.7 tablespoons. A standard UK measuring cup holds around 240ml, so 150g of oil is roughly 2/3 of a cup.</p>  
<p>For air fryer cooking, precision matters. Too much oil can cause smoke, while too little may leave food dry. If you're unsure, start with slightly less oil and adjust as needed.
