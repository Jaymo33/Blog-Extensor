<h2>How to Measure 10g of Icing Sugar Without a Scale</h2>
<p>If you don’t have a scale, you can use volume measurements to approximate 10g of icing sugar. One tablespoon of icing sugar typically weighs around 8g, so 1.25 tablespoons will give you roughly 10g. However, this method isn’t as precise as weighing, so for best results, invest in a kitchen scale.</p>
