<h3>How to measure 100g of Nutella without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can estimate 100g of Nutella using tablespoons. One tablespoon of Nutella weighs roughly 19g, so 100g is about 5.25 tablespoons. This method isn’t as precise as weighing, but it works in a pinch for recipes like air fryer brownies or stuffed French toast.</p>
