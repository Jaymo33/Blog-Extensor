<h2>How to measure 250g of sesame oil without scales</h2>
<p>If you don't have digital scales handy, you can still measure out 250g (8.82oz) of sesame oil using volume measurements. Approximately 250g of sesame oil equals 275ml or about 1 cup plus 2 tablespoons.
