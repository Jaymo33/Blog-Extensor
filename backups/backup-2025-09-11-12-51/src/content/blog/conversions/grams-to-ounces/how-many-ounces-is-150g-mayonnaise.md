<h3>How to measure 150g of mayonnaise without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can use measuring spoons or cups to approximate 150g of mayonnaise. One tablespoon of mayonnaise weighs roughly 15g, so 10 tablespoons would equal 150g. For ounces, this translates to just over 5 ounces, making it easy to measure for recipes like <a href="https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/chicken">air fryer chicken dishes</a>.</p>  

<h2>Common uses for 150g of mayonnaise in air fryer recipes</h2>  
<p>Mayonnaise is a versatile ingredient in air fryer cooking. From crispy coatings to creamy dips, 150g (or 5.29 ounces) is a common quantity for many recipes. <h3>Converting other mayonnaise quantities</h3>  
<p>If your recipe calls for a different amount of mayonnaise, the same conversion principle applies. For example, 100g of mayonnaise is roughly 3.53 ounces, while 200g equals about 7.05 ounces. Keeping these conversions handy simplifies cooking, especially when experimenting with new dishes in your air fryer.</p>  

<h2>Tips for storing and using mayonnaise in air fryer recipes</h2>  
<p>Mayonnaise can spoil if not stored correctly, so always check the expiry date before use. Once opened, keep it refrigerated and use it within a month. For air fryer recipes, room-temperature mayonnaise blends more easily with other ingredients, ensuring even distribution.</p>
