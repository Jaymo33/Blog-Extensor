<h2>Understanding 196 Fan Oven to Conventional Oven Conversion</h2>
<p>196°C in a fan oven equals approximately 180°C in a conventional oven. This 10-20°C reduction is crucial because fan ovens circulate hot air more efficiently, cooking food faster at lower temperatures. When converting 196 fan oven to conventional oven settings, you'll need to adjust both temperature and cooking time for best results. <p>The science behind this conversion lies in heat distribution - fan ovens eliminate cold spots by constantly moving air, while conventional ovens have more temperature variation. Remember that 196 fan oven to celsius conversion is straightforward (it's already in celsius), but the conventional oven adjustment is what matters most. Professional bakers recommend keeping an oven thermometer handy to verify your oven's accuracy after making these conversions.</p>
