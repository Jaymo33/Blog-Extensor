<p>The Gas Mark scale is commonly used in British recipes and oven settings. When your air fryer recipe calls for 473 F, setting your oven to Gas Mark 9 will give you the equivalent temperature. Many air fryer users find this conversion helpful when adapting traditional oven recipes for their appliance.</p>

<h2>When to Use 473 F (Gas Mark 9) in Your Air Fryer</h2>
<p>This high temperature setting is ideal for several air fryer cooking techniques. Use 473 F to Gas Mark 9 when you want to achieve maximum crispiness on the outside while keeping food moist inside. It's perfect for roasting vegetables, cooking frozen foods, or giving meats that restaurant-quality sear.</p>
<p>Many of our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>air fryer recipes</a> recommend starting at this temperature before reducing heat for even cooking.
