<h2>Understanding 253 F to Gas Mark Conversion</h2>
<p>The Gas Mark system is uniquely British, with temperatures ranging from Gas Mark ¼ to Gas Mark 9. Converting 253 Fahrenheit to Gas Mark gives you Gas Mark ½, which sits between the very low Gas Mark ¼ (225°F) and Gas Mark 1 (275°F). This temperature is ideal for delicate baking tasks like drying herbs or making meringues.
