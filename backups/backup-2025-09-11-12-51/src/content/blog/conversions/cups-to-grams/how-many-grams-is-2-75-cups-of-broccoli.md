<h2>Converting Cups to Grams for Different Broccoli Cuts</h2>  
<p>The way you chop broccoli affects its cup-to-gram ratio. Florets weigh slightly less than stems when measured by volume, so 2.75 cups of chopped florets may be closer to 200 grams. If you're using a mix of stems and florets, aim for the 220-gram benchmark. For recipes like air-fried broccoli cheese bites, consistency in size ensures even cooking.</p>  

<p>If you're working with frozen broccoli, note that 2.75 cups will weigh more due to ice content—around 250 grams. Thaw and drain it well before air frying to avoid excess moisture.
