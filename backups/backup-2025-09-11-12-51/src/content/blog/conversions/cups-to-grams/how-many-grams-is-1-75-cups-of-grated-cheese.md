<h2>Why Accurate Cheese Measurements Matter</h2>  
<p>Using the right amount of grated cheese ensures your dish isn’t overly dry or soggy. In air fryer recipes, especially, cheese melts quickly, and too much can lead to uneven cooking. Too little, and you’ll miss that gooey, golden finish. <p>Different cheeses have varying densities—cheddar, mozzarella, and parmesan all weigh differently per cup. Always check your recipe’s requirements or use a kitchen scale for precision.</p>  

<h3>How to Measure Grated Cheese Correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate conversion of 1.75 cups of grated cheese to grams, lightly pack the cheese into the measuring cup without pressing down. Overpacking can add up to 20% extra weight, throwing off your recipe.
