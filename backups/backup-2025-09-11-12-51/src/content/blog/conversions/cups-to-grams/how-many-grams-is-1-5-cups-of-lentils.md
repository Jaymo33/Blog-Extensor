<h2>Converting 1.5 Cups of Lentils to Grams</h2>
<p>Converting cups to grams is straightforward once you know the standard weight. One cup of uncooked lentils typically weighs around 200 grams, so 1.5 cups equal 300 grams. This conversion is essential for recipes like air-fried lentil koftas or spiced lentil snacks, where precision affects texture.</p>
<p>If you're using split lentils, the weight might differ slightly due to density. For example, 1.5 cups of split red lentils could weigh closer to 285 grams. Always refer to your recipe or our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>lentil recipe collection</a> for tailored guidance.</p>

<h3>Types of Lentils and Their Weights</h3>
<p>Different lentil varieties have slight weight variations per cup. Brown and green lentils are denser, so 1.5 cups will reliably weigh 300 grams. Split lentils, like red or yellow, may weigh less due to their smaller size. For air fryer cooking, stick to the 300-gram benchmark unless your recipe specifies otherwise.</p>
