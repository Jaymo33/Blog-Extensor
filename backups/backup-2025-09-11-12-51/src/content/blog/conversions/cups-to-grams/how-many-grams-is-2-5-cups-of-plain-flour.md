<h3>How to Measure 2.5 Cups of Plain Flour Correctly</h3>  
<p>The most accurate way to measure 2.5 cups of plain flour is to spoon it into the measuring cup and level it off with a knife. Scooping directly from the bag compacts the flour, potentially adding 10-15% extra weight. For the 313-gram equivalent of 2.5 cups, this technique matters. If your recipe calls for sifted flour, measure after sifting for the correct volume-to-weight ratio. These small adjustments ensure your air fryer bakes turn out perfectly every time.</p>
