<h2>Why Accurate Lentil Measurements Matter</h2>  
<p>Accurate measurements are crucial when cooking lentils in an air fryer. Too many lentils can overcrowd the basket, leading to uneven cooking, while too few may dry out. Converting 0.75 cups of lentils to grams (150g) helps maintain the right texture and flavour. This is especially important for recipes like air-fried lentil patties or roasted lentils, where precision ensures crispiness without burning.
