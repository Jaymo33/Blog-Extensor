<h3>Adjusting Recipes for Frozen Blueberries</h3>  
<p>Frozen blueberries often weigh more per cup due to moisture content. If substituting frozen for fresh, reduce the amount slightly or pat them dry to prevent excess liquid in your air fryer recipe. This small adjustment ensures your dish stays crisp and well-balanced.</p>
