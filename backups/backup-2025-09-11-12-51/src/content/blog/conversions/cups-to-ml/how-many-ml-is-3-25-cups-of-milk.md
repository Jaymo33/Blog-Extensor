<h2>Converting Cups to Millilitres: The Basics</h2>  
<p>One US cup of milk is equivalent to 236.59 ml, while a UK cup is slightly larger at 284.13 ml. For this conversion, we’ve used the US standard, as it’s the most commonly referenced in recipes. To convert 3.25 cups of milk to ml, multiply 236.59 by 3.25, giving you 768.91 ml. This measurement is handy for both cooking and baking in your air fryer.</p>
