<h2>Why Convert 349 Fan Oven to Fahrenheit?</h2>
<p>Fan ovens circulate hot air, reducing cooking time and often requiring lower temperatures. A 349 fan oven setting is roughly equivalent to 325°F in a conventional oven. This conversion ensures your recipes turn out perfectly, whether you're baking cakes or roasting vegetables. Many modern recipes, especially those on <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>airfryerrecipe.co.uk</a>, provide both fan and conventional oven settings. Understanding these conversions helps you adapt recipes for any kitchen appliance.</p>

<h2>How to Adjust Cooking Times</h2>
<p>When converting 349 fan oven to conventional oven, you'll also need to adjust cooking times. Conventional ovens take longer to heat up and cook food evenly. Start checking your dish 5-10 minutes earlier than the recipe suggests. This is especially important for delicate items like pastries or <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/cookies>cookies</a>, which can burn quickly.</p>
