<h2>Why Convert 336 Fan Oven to Conventional Oven?</h2>
<p>Fan ovens circulate hot air, cooking food faster and more evenly than conventional ovens. This means you often need to lower the temperature when converting recipes. A 336 fan oven setting roughly translates to 300°F in a conventional oven. This adjustment prevents overcooking and ensures your dishes retain moisture. For example, a cake baked at 336 fan might burn if cooked at the same temperature in a conventional oven.</p>
