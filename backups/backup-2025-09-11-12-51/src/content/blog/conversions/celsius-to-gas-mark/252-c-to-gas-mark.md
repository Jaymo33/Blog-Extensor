<h2>Why 252°C (Gas Mark 9) Matters</h2>
<p>Gas Mark 9 is the go-to setting for dishes that need a strong initial blast of heat, like Yorkshire puddings or puff pastry. In an air fryer, this temperature mimics a professional oven’s performance, giving you restaurant-quality results. Many air fryer models cap at 200°C, but some high-end units reach 250°C or higher. Always check your appliance’s manual before attempting Gas Mark 9 recipes. <h3>Baking at 252°C (Gas Mark 9)</h3>
<p>Baking at this high temperature requires quick reactions, as items like bread or pastries can brown rapidly.
