<h2>UK vs. US Cup Measurements</h2>  
<p>In the UK, 1 cup equals 284 ml, while in the US, it's 240 ml. This means 510 ml of vegetable oil is roughly 2.15 UK cups or 2.16 US cups. The slight difference might not seem significant, but for baking, precision matters. Always check which measurement system your recipe uses.</p>
