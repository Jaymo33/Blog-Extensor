<h2>Common Mistakes When Measuring Oat Milk</h2>
<p>One of the biggest mistakes people make is using a dry measuring cup for liquids. Dry cups and liquid cups aren’t interchangeable, and using the wrong one can throw off your measurements. Always opt for a liquid measuring cup when working with oat milk or any other liquid ingredient.</p>
