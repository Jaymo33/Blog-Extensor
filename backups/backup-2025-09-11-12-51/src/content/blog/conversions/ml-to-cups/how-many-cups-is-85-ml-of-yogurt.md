<h2>Converting 85 ml of Yogurt to Cups</h2>  
<p>The conversion depends on whether you're using US or UK measurements. In the US, 1 cup equals 240 ml, so 85 ml is roughly 0.36 cups. In the UK, 1 cup is 284 ml, making 85 ml about 0.3 cups. This difference might seem small, but it's crucial for recipes requiring exact proportions.</p>  

<h3>Using a Measuring Cup</h3>  
<p>If you don't have a metric measuring tool, use a standard cup and fill it just over a third for US measurements or slightly less for UK. For air fryer recipes, especially those involving marinades or batters, accuracy ensures even cooking and perfect results every time.</p>
