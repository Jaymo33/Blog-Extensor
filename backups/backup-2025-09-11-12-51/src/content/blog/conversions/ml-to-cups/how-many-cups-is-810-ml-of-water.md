<h3>US vs. UK Cup Measurements</h3>  
<p>The difference between US and UK cup measurements might seem minor, but it can impact recipes. In the US, 1 cup equals 240 ml, while in the UK, it’s 250 ml. For 810 ml of water, this means 3.42 US cups versus 3.56 UK cups. If you’re using an American air fryer recipe, stick to US measurements for best results.</p>  

<h2>How to Measure 810 ml of Water Without a Scale</h2>  
<p>Not everyone has a kitchen scale, but most have measuring cups. To measure 810 ml of water, use a standard cup and fill it to the appropriate line. If precision is critical, consider investing in a liquid measuring jug with millilitre markings for air fryer cooking.</p>
