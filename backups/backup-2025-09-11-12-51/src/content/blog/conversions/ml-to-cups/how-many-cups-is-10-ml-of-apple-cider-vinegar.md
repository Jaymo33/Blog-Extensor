<h3>How to Measure 10 ml of Apple Cider Vinegar</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a metric measuring cup, you can use a standard tablespoon. One tablespoon holds about 15 ml, so 10 ml is roughly two-thirds of a tablespoon. Alternatively, a teaspoon holds 5 ml, meaning two teaspoons equal 10 ml. This method is handy for quick conversions when following recipes like those in our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/chicken>chicken category</a>.</p>
