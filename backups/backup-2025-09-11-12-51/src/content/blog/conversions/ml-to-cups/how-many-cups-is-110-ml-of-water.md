<h3>UK vs. US Cup Measurements</h3>  
<p>In the UK, a standard cup holds 250 ml, while in the US, it’s 240 ml. This means 110 ml of water converts to roughly 0.46 UK cups or 0.47 US cups. Though the difference seems minor, it can add up in larger batches. Always check whether your recipe uses UK or US measurements to avoid errors.</p>
