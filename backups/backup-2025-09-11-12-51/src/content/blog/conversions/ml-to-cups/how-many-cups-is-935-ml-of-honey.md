<h3>How to Measure Honey Without the Mess</h3>  
<p>Measuring honey can be tricky due to its sticky consistency. To get an accurate 935 ml of honey in cups, lightly coat your measuring cup with oil or non-stick spray first. This helps the honey slide out easily, ensuring you get the full amount without waste. Alternatively, use a kitchen scale for even greater precision—honey weighs roughly 340 grams per cup.</p>
