<h3>How to Measure 185 ml of Coconut Milk Without a Measuring Cup</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a measuring cup handy, you can use a standard tablespoon to approximate 185 ml of coconut milk. Since 1 tablespoon equals about 15 ml, you’d need roughly 12 tablespoons (or ¾ cup plus 1 tablespoon) to reach 185 ml. For sticky ingredients like coconut milk, lightly greasing your spoon or cup can help ensure an accurate pour.</p>
