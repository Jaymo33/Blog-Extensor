<h3>How to measure 0.75 tbsp of mayonnaise without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have scales, you can still measure 0.75 tbsp of mayonnaise accurately. Fill a tablespoon three-quarters full, levelling off the excess with a knife. Remember, 0.75 tbsp equals 11 grams, so this visual method gets you close enough for most recipes. For sticky ingredients like mayo, lightly greasing the spoon first helps it slide off cleanly. This trick is handy when making <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/potatoes>air fryer potato wedges</a> with a mayo-based dip.</p>
