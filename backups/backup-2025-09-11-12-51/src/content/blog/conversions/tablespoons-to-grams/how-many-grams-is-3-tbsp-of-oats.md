<h3>Measuring oats without scales</h3>
<p>If you don't have digital scales, you can still measure 24 grams of oats using household items. For best results, spoon the oats into the tablespoon rather than scooping to ensure consistent measurements.</p>

<h3>Storing oats for air fryer use</h3>
<p>To keep your oats fresh for air fryer recipes, store them in an airtight container in a cool, dark place. Proper storage ensures your 3 tablespoon (24g) measurements remain consistent over time.
