<h3>How to measure 0.5 tbsp of protein powder without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have scales, use the spoon-and-level method for accuracy. Gently scoop the powder into a tablespoon, then level it off with a straight edge. Split this in half for 0.5 tbsp, which should roughly equal 7.5 grams. For best results, avoid packing the powder down, as this can lead to over-measuring. Keep in mind that this method works well for most recipes, but for baking, grams are always more reliable.</p>
