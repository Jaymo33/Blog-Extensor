<h2>Why Convert Tablespoons to Grams?</h2>  
<p>Measuring olive oil by weight (grams) is more accurate than using volume (tablespoons). Oil density can vary slightly, but 1 tbsp typically weighs around 13.6 grams. <h3>How to Measure Olive Oil Accurately</h3>  
<p>Use a digital kitchen scale for the most accurate conversion of 5 tbsp olive oil to grams. Pour the oil into a measuring spoon, then transfer it to the scale to confirm the weight. This method eliminates inconsistencies caused by air bubbles or uneven pours. For sticky ingredients, lightly coat the spoon with oil first to ensure a clean release.</p>
