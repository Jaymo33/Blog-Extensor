<h3>How to measure 2 tbsp of plain flour correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate results, spoon the flour into the tablespoon and level it off with a knife. Scooping directly from the bag can compact the flour, leading to an incorrect measurement. If you're serious about baking, investing in a digital kitchen scale is a game-changer. <h3>Converting other flour measurements</h3>  
<p>If your recipe calls for a different amount of plain flour, here's a quick reference: 1 tbsp equals about 8g, while 4 tbsp (or ¼ cup) is roughly 32g. These conversions are handy when scaling recipes up or down for your air fryer. Remember, air fryer baskets have limited space, so adjusting portion sizes is often necessary.</p>
