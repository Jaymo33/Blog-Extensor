<h2>How to measure 0.25 tbsp of soy sauce</h2>
<p>Measuring small amounts like 0.25 tbsp of soy sauce requires either precise measuring spoons or a digital scale. For those without specialist equipment, here's a practical method: fill a standard tablespoon with soy sauce, then divide it visually into four equal parts. One of these quarters represents 0.25 tbsp, which our conversion shows is about 3.75 grams.</p>
