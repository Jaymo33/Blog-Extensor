<h3>How to measure mayonnaise without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have kitchen scales, use a standard UK tablespoon (15ml) to measure mayonnaise. Fill it level for accuracy, and avoid packing it down. Three level tablespoons will give you roughly 45 grams. For sticky ingredients like mayo, a silicone spatula helps scrape every bit out of the spoon.</p>
