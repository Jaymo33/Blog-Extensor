<h3>How we calculated 0.25 tbsp to grams</h3>
<p>The standard UK conversion for lemon juice is 1 tbsp = 14.79 grams. Therefore, 0.25 tbsp equals about 3.7 grams. This calculation accounts for lemon juice's density and viscosity, which differs from water or oils. Always use fresh lemon juice for most accurate measurements.</p>
