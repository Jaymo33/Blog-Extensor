<h2>How to Measure 425 Grams of Cheddar Cheese</h2>
<p>To measure 425 grams of cheddar cheese in cups, start by grating the cheese if it isn't already shredded. Pack the grated cheese lightly into a measuring cup without pressing too hard, as over-packing can lead to an inaccurate measurement. For best results, use a kitchen scale to weigh the cheese first, then transfer it to cups if needed.</p>

<p>If you don't have a scale, remember that 1 cup of grated cheddar cheese typically weighs around 100-110 grams. This means 425 grams would be just under 4 cups.
