<h3>UK vs. US Cup Measurements</h3>  
<p>UK and US cup measurements differ slightly, which affects how 325 grams of butter converts. A UK cup holds 250ml, while a US cup is 240ml. This means 325g of butter equals 1.42 UK cups or 1.37 US cups. Always check which measurement system your recipe uses to avoid mistakes.
