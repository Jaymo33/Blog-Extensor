<h2>How to Measure 75 Grams of Chicken Breast in Cups</h2>  
<p>The exact cup equivalent depends on how the chicken is prepared. Diced chicken breast fills about 0.4 cups, while shredded chicken may measure closer to 0.3 cups due to air pockets. Always lightly pack shredded chicken for consistent measurements.</p>  

<h3>Using a Kitchen Scale for Precision</h3>  
<p>For absolute accuracy, weigh your chicken breast before dicing or shredding. Place a bowl on your kitchen scale, tare it to zero, then add chicken until it reads 75 grams. This method eliminates guesswork and ensures perfect portions for <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/chicken>chicken recipes</a>.</p>
