<h2>UK vs. US Cup Conversions</h2>  
<p>In the UK, a standard cup holds 250ml, while in the US, it’s 240ml. This means 475 grams of butter equals about 2.1 UK cups or 2 US cups. For air fryer recipes, sticking to one measurement system avoids confusion. If you’re following a US recipe but using UK measurements, adjust slightly to avoid over- or under-buttering your dish.</p>

<h3>Tips for Measuring Butter Without Scales</h3>  
<p>No kitchen scales? No problem. Butter packaging often has markings for tablespoons or grams, making it easy to measure 475 grams. Alternatively, use the displacement method: fill a measuring cup with water, add butter until the water rises to the 2-cup mark (for US measurements), then drain the water. This method works well for air fryer recipes where precision is key.</p>
