<h3>Types of Cheese and Their Cup Measurements</h3>  
<p>Hard cheeses like parmesan or pecorino will yield more cups per 400 grams compared to softer cheeses like mozzarella due to their lower moisture content. For example, 400 grams of finely grated parmesan might fill 4.5 cups, while the same weight of shredded mozzarella could be closer to 3.5 cups. Always adjust based on the cheese you're using.</p>
