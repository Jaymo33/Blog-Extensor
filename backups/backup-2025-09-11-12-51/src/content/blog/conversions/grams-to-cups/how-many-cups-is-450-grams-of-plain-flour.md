<h2>UK vs. US Cup Measurements</h2>
<p>Did you know that a UK cup and a US cup aren’t the same? A UK cup holds 250ml, while a US cup is slightly smaller at 240ml. This means 450 grams of plain flour equals roughly 3.6 cups in the UK and 3.75 cups in the USA. <h3>Tips for Measuring Flour Without a Scale</h3>
<p>No scale? No problem. To measure 450 grams of plain flour using cups, use the spoon-and-level method. Gently stir the flour in its bag or container to loosen it. Then, spoon it into your measuring cup until it’s heaped, and level off the excess with a straight edge. Repeat until you’ve reached the correct cup equivalent. This method minimises compaction and gives you a closer approximation to the weight measurement.</p>
