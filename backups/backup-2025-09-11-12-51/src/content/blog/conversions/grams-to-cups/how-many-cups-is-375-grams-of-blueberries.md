<h3>UK vs. US Cup Measurements</h3>  
<p>It's worth noting that cup sizes differ slightly between the UK and the US. A UK cup is 250ml, while a US cup is 240ml. For 375 grams of blueberries, this means you'll get approximately 2.5 cups in the UK and about 2.6 cups in the US. The difference is minimal, but if precision is crucial, it's good to be aware of the variation.</p>
