<h3>How to Measure 50 Grams of Chicken Breast</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, using cups is a handy alternative. Start by dicing or shredding the chicken breast finely for the most accurate measurement. A packed 0.25 cups of chopped chicken will roughly equal 50 grams. For larger batches, multiply accordingly—100 grams would be about 0.5 cups. Always fluff the chicken lightly in the cup to avoid overpacking.</p>
