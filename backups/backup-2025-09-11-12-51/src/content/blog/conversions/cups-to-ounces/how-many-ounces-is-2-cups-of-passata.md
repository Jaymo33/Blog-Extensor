<h3>Storing Leftover Passata</h3>  
<p>If you have leftover passata after measuring out 2 cups (16 ounces), store it in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 5 days. Alternatively, freeze it in portion-sized containers for future air fryer meals. Thawed passata may separate slightly, but a quick stir will restore its smooth texture. Label containers with the date and weight to make future recipes easier. Proper storage ensures you always have passata ready for your next air fryer creation.</p>
