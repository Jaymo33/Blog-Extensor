<h2>How to Measure 1.75 Cups of Milk Correctly</h2>
<p>Measuring 1.75 cups of milk is simple with the right tools. Use a liquid measuring cup for accuracy, filling it to the 1.75-cup mark. Alternatively, a kitchen scale can measure 14 ounces directly. <h3>Common Mistakes When Converting Cups to Ounces</h3>
<p>One common mistake is confusing fluid ounces with weight ounces. Fluid ounces measure volume, while weight ounces measure mass. For milk, 1.75 cups (14 fluid ounces) is roughly 14.7 weight ounces due to density. Always use fluid ounces for liquids unless specified otherwise. This distinction is crucial for air fryer recipes, where precision matters.</p>
