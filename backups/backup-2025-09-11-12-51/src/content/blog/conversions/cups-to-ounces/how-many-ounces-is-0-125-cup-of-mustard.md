<h3>How to measure 0.125 cup of mustard</h3>  
<p>Use a liquid measuring cup for accuracy, filling it to the 1/8 cup mark. For thicker mustards, level it off with a spatula. If you don’t have a 1/8 cup measure, two tablespoons equal 0.125 cup. This method works for both yellow and Dijon mustard, though weights may vary slightly.</p>
