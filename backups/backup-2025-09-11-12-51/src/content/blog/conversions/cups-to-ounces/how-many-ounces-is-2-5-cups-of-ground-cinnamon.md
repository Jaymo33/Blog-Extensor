<p>For the most accurate results, use a kitchen scale to weigh your ground cinnamon. Scooping directly from the bag can lead to inconsistencies due to settling or packing. Lightly spoon the cinnamon into your measuring cup, then level it off with a knife for a precise 2.5 cups. This method ensures you get the correct 10-ounce equivalent every time.</p>
