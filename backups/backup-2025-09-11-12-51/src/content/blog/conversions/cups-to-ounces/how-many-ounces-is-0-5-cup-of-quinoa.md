<h2>Tips for Cooking Quinoa in an Air Fryer</h2>  
<p>Cooking quinoa in an air fryer requires the right balance of liquid and heat. For 0.5 cup (3.5 oz) of quinoa, use 1 cup of water or broth. Preheat the air fryer to 180°C (350°F) and cook for 12–15 minutes. Stir halfway to ensure even cooking. For fluffy quinoa, let it rest for 5 minutes after cooking.</p>
