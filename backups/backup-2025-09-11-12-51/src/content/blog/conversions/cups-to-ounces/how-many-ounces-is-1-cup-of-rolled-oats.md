<h2>How to measure 1 cup of rolled oats correctly</h2>
<p>To measure 1 cup of rolled oats accurately, use the spoon-and-level method. Gently spoon the oats into a measuring cup without packing them down. Then, level off the excess with a straight edge for a precise cup. This method prevents over-measuring, which can throw off your recipe. Remember, 1 cup prepared this way equals roughly 3.5 ounces.</p>

<h3>Using a kitchen scale for better accuracy</h3>
<p>For absolute precision, weigh your rolled oats instead of using cups. Place your bowl on the scale, tare it to zero, then add oats until you reach 3.5 ounces. This method eliminates guesswork and ensures perfect results every time.
