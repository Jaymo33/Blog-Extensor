<h3>Storing golden syrup for air fryer recipe prep</h3>
<p>Proper storage maintains golden syrup’s consistency for accurate measuring. Keep it in an airtight container at room temperature, where it will stay pourable for easy 1.33 cup (16 oz) portions. Crystallisation occasionally occurs but can be fixed by gently warming the syrup in a water bath. Never microwave golden syrup directly, as overheating alters its density and measurement reliability. For frequent bakers, buying golden syrup in bulk ensures you’ll always have enough for those 16-ounce recipe requirements.</p>
