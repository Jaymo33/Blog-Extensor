<h3>How to Measure Hoisin Sauce Correctly</h3>
<p>To measure 0.375 cup of hoisin sauce, you can use a standard measuring cup or a kitchen scale. If using a cup, fill it to just below the ½ cup mark—0.375 cup is three-quarters of a ½ cup measure. For greater accuracy, a kitchen scale set to ounces will show you exactly 3 ounces. This method is especially useful if you’re scaling recipes up or down.</p>

<p>Once opened, hoisin sauce should be stored in the fridge to maintain freshness. It typically lasts for several months, but always check for changes in smell or texture.
