<h3>How to measure paprika correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate 3 cups of paprika to ounces conversion, use the spoon-and-level method. Lightly spoon paprika into a measuring cup without packing it down, then level it off with a knife. This prevents compacting, which can lead to over-measuring. If you’re using a kitchen scale, set it to ounces and tare the weight before adding your paprika.
