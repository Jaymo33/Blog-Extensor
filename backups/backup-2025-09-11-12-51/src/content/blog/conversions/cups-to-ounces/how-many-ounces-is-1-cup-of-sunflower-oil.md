<h3>How to measure sunflower oil for air frying</h3>  
<p>To measure 1 cup of sunflower oil accurately, use a liquid measuring cup designed for oils. Fill it to the 1-cup mark, ensuring the oil sits level for an exact 7.7-ounce measurement. Avoid using dry measuring cups, as they can lead to inaccuracies. For smaller quantities, a kitchen scale can help verify the weight in ounces.</p>
