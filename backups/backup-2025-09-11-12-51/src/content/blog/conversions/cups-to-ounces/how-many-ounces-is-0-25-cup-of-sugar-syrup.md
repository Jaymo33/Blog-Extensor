<p>0.25 cup of sugar syrup equals approximately 2 ounces (oz). This conversion is essential for air fryer recipes where precise measurements can make or break your dish. Whether you’re drizzling syrup over air-fried desserts or using it as a glaze, knowing the exact weight ensures consistency.</p>

<h2>How to measure 0.25 cup of sugar syrup accurately</h2>
<p>Using a liquid measuring cup is the easiest way to measure 0.25 cup of sugar syrup. Fill the cup to the ¼ mark, ensuring the syrup settles evenly. For even greater accuracy, a kitchen scale can confirm the weight is 2 oz. This method is especially useful for sticky ingredients like sugar syrup, which can cling to measuring cups.</p>
