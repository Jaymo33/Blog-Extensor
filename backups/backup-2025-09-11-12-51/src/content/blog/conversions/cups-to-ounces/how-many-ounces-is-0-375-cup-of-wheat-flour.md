<p>0.375 cup of wheat flour equals approximately 1.5 ounces. <h2>Why accurate flour measurements matter</h2>
<p>Accurate measurements are crucial in baking because flour can compact or settle, leading to inconsistent results. Using weight (ounces) instead of volume (cups) eliminates this variability. For air fryer recipes, where space and heat distribution are key, precise measurements help achieve even cooking and perfect texture.</p>

<h2>How to measure 0.375 cup of wheat flour</h2>
<p>Measuring 0.375 cup (which is 3/8 cup) can be tricky without the right tools. The easiest way is to use a 1/4 cup measure and a 1/8 cup measure, filling each once and combining them. Alternatively, you can use a digital kitchen scale set to ounces for the most accurate measurement.</p>
