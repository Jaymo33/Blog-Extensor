<p>Air fryers cook food quickly and evenly, but they rely on precise ingredient ratios for the best texture and flavour. <p>Condensed milk is denser than regular milk, so volume-to-weight conversions are crucial. A 0.25 cup of condensed milk to ounces conversion (2 oz) helps standardise recipes, especially when scaling up or down. If you're adapting a traditional oven recipe for your air fryer, this measurement ensures consistency.</p>  

<h3>How to Measure 0.25 Cup of Condensed Milk</h3>  
<p>For accuracy, use a liquid measuring cup for 0.25 cup of condensed milk, then weigh it on a kitchen scale for 2 ounces. If you don’t have a scale, spoon the condensed milk into the measuring cup and level it off. Avoid packing it down, as this can lead to excess.</p>
