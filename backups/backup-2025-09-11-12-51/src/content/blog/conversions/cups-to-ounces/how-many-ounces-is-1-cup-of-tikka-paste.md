<h2>How to measure tikka paste correctly</h2>
<p>For the most accurate 1 cup tikka paste to ounces conversion, follow these simple steps. First, use a liquid measuring cup for sticky ingredients like tikka paste. Lightly grease the cup with oil to help the paste slide out easily. Fill it to exactly the 1 cup mark without packing it down, then level off the top with a straight edge.</p>
