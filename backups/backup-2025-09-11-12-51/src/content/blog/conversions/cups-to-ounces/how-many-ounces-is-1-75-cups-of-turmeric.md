<h3>How to Measure Turmeric for Air Fryer Recipes</h3>  
<p>When converting 1.75 cups of turmeric to ounces, use a kitchen scale for accuracy. Scooping directly from the bag can lead to inconsistencies due to settling or clumping. Fluff the turmeric lightly before measuring to ensure an even distribution. If you don’t have a scale, remember that 1 cup of turmeric weighs roughly 8 ounces, so 1.75 cups would be around 14 ounces.</p>
