<h3>How to measure quinoa for air fryer recipes</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate results, use a kitchen scale to weigh your quinoa. If you don’t have one, fluff the quinoa lightly before measuring to avoid compacting it in the cup. Level off the top with a straight edge for consistency. This method ensures your 2 cups of quinoa to ounces conversion is spot-on.</p>
