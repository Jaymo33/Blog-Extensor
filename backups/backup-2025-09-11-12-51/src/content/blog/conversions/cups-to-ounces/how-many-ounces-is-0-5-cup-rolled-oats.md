<h2>Common Mistakes When Measuring Rolled Oats</h2>  
<p>One of the biggest mistakes is using a liquid measuring cup for dry ingredients like rolled oats. This can lead to inconsistent amounts, as the oats may settle unevenly. Always use dry measuring cups for 0.5 cup of rolled oats to ensure 1.5 ounces accuracy. Another tip: store your oats in an airtight container to prevent moisture absorption, which can affect weight.</p>
