<h2>How Many Ounces Are in 3 Cups of Hot Sauce?</h2>  
<p>3 cups of hot sauce equals approximately 24 fluid ounces. This conversion is essential for air fryer recipes, where precise measurements can make or break a dish. <h2>Converting 3 Cups of Hot Sauce to Ounces: A Step-by-Step Guide</h2>  
<p>To convert 3 cups of hot sauce to ounces, simply multiply the number of cups by 8 (since 1 cup = 8 fluid ounces). This gives you 24 fluid ounces, the standard measurement for most hot sauces. If you’re using a UK measuring cup, note that it holds the same volume as a US cup, so the conversion remains unchanged. For thicker sauces like sriracha or chipotle, the weight might differ slightly, but the fluid ounce measurement stays the same.</p>  

<p>For those who prefer weighing ingredients, 24 fluid ounces of hot sauce roughly translates to 680 grams. This is handy if you’re using a kitchen scale instead of measuring cups.
