<p>3 cups of miso soup equals approximately 24 fluid ounces (fl oz) or about 21 ounces (oz) by weight, depending on the density of your miso soup. This conversion is essential for air fryer recipes where precise measurements can make all the difference. Whether you're meal prepping or adjusting portion sizes, knowing how to convert cups to ounces ensures your dishes turn out perfectly every time.</p>

<p>Miso soup's density can vary based on ingredients like tofu, seaweed, or dashi stock. A standard cup of miso soup weighs around 7 ounces, so 3 cups would be roughly 21 ounces by weight. Fluid ounces, however, measure volume, not weight, so 3 cups equal 24 fl oz. This distinction is crucial for recipes requiring precise liquid measurements.</p>  

<h3>How to Measure Miso Soup for Air Fryer Recipes</h3>  
<p>For best results, use a kitchen scale to weigh your miso soup if your recipe calls for ounces by weight. If you're working with fluid ounces, a liquid measuring cup will do the trick.
