<h3>How to Measure 1.75 Cups of Vegetable Oil</h3>  
<p>For best results, use a liquid measuring cup for fluid ounces or a kitchen scale for weight ounces. Vegetable oil has a density of about 0.92 grams per millilitre, meaning 1.75 cups (414 ml) weighs roughly 13.9 ounces. If your air fryer recipe calls for 1.75 cups of vegetable oil, this conversion ensures accuracy.</p>

<h3>Does the Type of Vegetable Oil Affect the Weight?</h3>  
<p>While most vegetable oils have similar densities, slight variations exist. For example, sunflower oil and canola oil weigh nearly the same, so the 1.75 cups to ounces conversion remains consistent. However, heavier oils like coconut oil may require adjustments—always check your recipe notes.</p>
