<h2>How to measure 3 cups of cream correctly</h2>  
<p>Not all measuring cups are created equal, especially when dealing with thick liquids like cream. For the most accurate 3 cups of cream to ounces conversion, use a liquid measuring cup with clear markings. Fill it to the 3-cup line without packing or shaking the cream down. This ensures you’re getting the true volume needed for your air fryer recipe.</p>
