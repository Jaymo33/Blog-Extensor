<h2>How to Measure Shredded Coconut Correctly</h2>  
<p>Measuring shredded coconut by volume can be tricky due to its light, fluffy texture. For the most accurate results, fluff the coconut with a fork before lightly spooning it into a measuring cup. Avoid packing it down, as this can lead to an overly dense measurement. If your recipe calls for ounces or grams, using a kitchen scale is the best way to ensure precision.</p>
