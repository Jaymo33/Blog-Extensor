<h3>How to measure 0.375 cup of protein powder</h3>  
<p>To measure 0.375 cup of protein powder accurately, use a dry measuring cup and level it off with a straight edge. Alternatively, a kitchen scale set to ounces will give you the exact 3 oz weight. Scooping directly from the bag can lead to compacted powder and incorrect measurements. For best results, fluff the powder with a spoon before measuring. This method works well for all types of protein powder, whether whey, plant-based, or casein.</p>
