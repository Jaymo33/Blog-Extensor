<h3>Types of Cheese and Their Weights</h3>  
<p>Not all cheeses weigh the same when grated. Hard cheeses like cheddar or Parmesan are denser, while softer cheeses like mozzarella may be lighter. For 1.5 cups of grated cheddar, you’ll get roughly 6 ounces, but mozzarella might be closer to 5.5 ounces. Always consider the cheese type when measuring for air fryer recipes to avoid inconsistencies.</p>

<h3>Common Mistakes When Measuring Cheese</h3>  
<p>One mistake people make is using pre-shredded cheese, which often contains anti-caking agents and may weigh differently. For the best results, grate your cheese fresh and measure it lightly. Another error is confusing fluid ounces with weight ounces—1.5 cups of grated cheese is 6 weight ounces, not fluid ounces. Always double-check your measurements for air fryer success.</p>
