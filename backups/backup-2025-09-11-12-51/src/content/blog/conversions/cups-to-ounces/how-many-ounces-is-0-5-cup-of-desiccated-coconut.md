<h3>How to measure desiccated coconut accurately</h3>
<p>When measuring 0.5 cup of desiccated coconut, it’s best to use proper measuring cups rather than estimating. Spoon the coconut into the cup lightly without packing it down, then level it off with a straight edge. This ensures you get the correct amount without compressing the coconut, which could lead to using more than intended. For even greater accuracy, consider investing in a kitchen scale to weigh your ingredients directly in ounces.</p>

<h3>Using desiccated coconut in air fryer recipes</h3>
<p>Desiccated coconut is incredibly versatile in air fryer cooking. It adds texture to baked goods, makes a delicious coating for chicken or fish, and can even be toasted separately for garnishes. When using it in batters or coatings, the 0.5 cup to 1.5 ounce conversion ensures you get just the right amount. Toasted coconut is particularly delicious in the air fryer – just spread it in a single layer and cook at 160°C for 2-3 minutes, shaking occasionally.</p>
