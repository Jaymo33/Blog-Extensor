<p>1.25 cups of cornflour equals approximately 5.3 ounces (oz). This conversion is essential for air fryer recipes where precision ensures crispy, even results. Whether you're coating chicken or thickening sauces, getting the measurement right makes all the difference.</p>

<h2>Why accurate cornflour measurements matter in air frying</h2>
<p>Cornflour is a staple in air fryer cooking, often used for crispy coatings or as a thickening agent. Using 1.25 cups (5.3 oz) instead of eyeballing it guarantees consistent texture. Too little and your coating won't crisp properly; too much and you'll get a chalky taste. <h3>How we calculated 1.25 cups to ounces</h3>
<p>Standard UK cornflour weighs about 4.25 oz per cup. For 1.25 cups, we multiply 4.25 by 1.25 to get 5.3125 oz, which rounds to 5.3 oz. This matches the <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-conversion-chart>air fryer conversion chart</a> we use across all our recipes. Factors like compaction can slightly alter the weight, so we recommend the spoon-and-level method for measuring.</p>

<h3>Tips for measuring cornflour perfectly every time</h3>
<p>Always use dry measuring cups for 1.25 cups of cornflour, not liquid ones. Gently spoon the flour into the cup without packing it down, then level off with a straight edge. For the 5.3 oz measurement, a kitchen scale eliminates guesswork. Store cornflour in airtight containers to maintain consistent density between uses.</p>
