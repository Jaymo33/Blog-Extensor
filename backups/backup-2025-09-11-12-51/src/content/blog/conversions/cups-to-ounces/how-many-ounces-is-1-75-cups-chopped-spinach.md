<h3>How to Measure Chopped Spinach Properly</h3>  
<p>To measure 1.75 cups of chopped spinach accurately, lightly pack the leaves into a measuring cup without crushing them. Avoid pressing down too hard, as this can lead to an incorrect weight. If you're using pre-chopped spinach from a bag, fluff it up before measuring to ensure even distribution.</p>  

<h2>Converting Cups to Ounces for Different Ingredients</h2>  
<p>While 1.75 cups of chopped spinach equals roughly 3.5 ounces, other ingredients have different conversion rates. For example, 1.75 cups of flour or sugar would weigh significantly more. <p>Spinach's light, leafy structure means it takes up more volume for less weight compared to denser foods. <h2>Tips for Storing and Prepping Spinach</h2>  
<p>To keep spinach fresh for air fryer recipes, store it unwashed in an airtight container with a paper towel to absorb moisture. When ready to use, wash and dry thoroughly before chopping. Excess water can lead to steaming instead of crisping in the air fryer.</p>
