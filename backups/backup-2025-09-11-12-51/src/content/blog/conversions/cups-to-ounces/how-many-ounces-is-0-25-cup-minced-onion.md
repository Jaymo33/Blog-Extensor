<h2>How to Measure 0.25 Cup of Minced Onion Correctly</h2>  
<p>Measuring 0.25 cup of minced onion might seem straightforward, but technique matters. First, ensure your onion is finely minced to avoid air pockets that can skew the measurement. Gently spoon the minced onion into a measuring cup without packing it down, then level it off with a straight edge. This method ensures you’re getting an accurate 0.25 cup, which translates to roughly 1 ounce. If you’re working with a kitchen scale, simply weigh out 1 ounce for the same result.</p>  

<h3>Common Mistakes When Measuring Minced Onion</h3>  
<p>One of the most frequent errors is packing the minced onion into the measuring cup, which can nearly double the weight. Another mistake is using unevenly sized pieces, leading to inconsistent measurements. For best results, aim for uniformly small pieces and avoid compressing them.
