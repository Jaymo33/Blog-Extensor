<h3>How to measure ground cumin without a scale</h3>  
<p>If you don't have a scale, use the spoon-and-level method for cups. Fluff the cumin with a fork, spoon it into a measuring cup, and level off the excess. <h3>Buying and storing ground cumin</h3>  
<p>For the freshest flavour, buy whole cumin seeds and grind them as needed. Pre-ground cumin loses potency faster, so store it in a cool, dark place.
