<h2>How to Convert 3 Cups of Water to Ounces</h2>
<p>The conversion from cups to ounces is straightforward. In the UK, 1 cup of water equals 8 fluid ounces. Therefore, 3 cups of water is 24 fluid ounces. This applies to all liquid measurements, whether for cooking or baking.</p>
<p>For dry ingredients, the weight may vary, but water’s density makes it consistent. If you’re using a scale, 24 fluid ounces of water also weighs approximately 680 grams.
