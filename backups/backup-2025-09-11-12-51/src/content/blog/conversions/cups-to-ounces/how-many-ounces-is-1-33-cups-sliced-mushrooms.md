<p>Air fryers rely on hot air circulation, so ingredient quantities affect cooking times and textures. Too many mushrooms can lead to steaming instead of crisping, while too few might burn. Converting 1.33 cups of sliced mushrooms to ounces (3.2 oz) ensures consistency. <h3>How to Measure Sliced Mushrooms Correctly</h3>  
<p>Pack sliced mushrooms lightly into a measuring cup without crushing them. Overpacking can skew the weight—1.33 cups should fill just below the 1.5-cup mark. For recipes requiring exactness, like air-fried stuffed mushrooms, weighing is best.</p>
