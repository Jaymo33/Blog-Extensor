<h2>How to measure 0.375 cup of margarine accurately</h2>
<p>Measuring 0.375 cup of margarine can be tricky without the right tools. Here are some methods to ensure accuracy:</p>

<h3>Using measuring cups</h3>
<p>0.375 cup is equal to 3/8 cup. If your measuring set doesn't have a 3/8 cup marker, you can measure 1/4 cup plus 2 tablespoons. For even more precision, use 6 tablespoons, as each tablespoon equals 0.5 ounces of margarine. This method gives you exactly 3 ounces of margarine for your air fryer recipes.</p>

<h3>Using a kitchen scale</h3>
<p>The most accurate way to measure 0.375 cup of margarine is by weight. Simply set your kitchen scale to ounces, place your container on it, tare to zero, then add margarine until it reads 3 ounces. This method eliminates any guesswork and ensures perfect measurements every time.</p>
