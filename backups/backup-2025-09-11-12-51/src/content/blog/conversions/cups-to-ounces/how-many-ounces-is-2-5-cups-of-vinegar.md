<h2>How to measure 2.5 cups of vinegar without a scale</h2>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, use liquid measuring cups to portion out 2.5 cups of vinegar. Fill the cup to the 8-ounce mark three times, then halve the fourth cup to reach 20 ounces total. This method works perfectly for most air fryer recipes, from crispy <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/chicken>chicken</a> wings to tangy vegetable stir-fries.</p>  

<p>For sticky vinegars like balsamic, lightly coat your measuring cup with oil to ensure all the liquid pours out cleanly. This avoids waste and guarantees you’re using the full 2.5 cups (20 oz) required. Proper measurement techniques are just as important as the conversion itself when cooking with vinegar in your air fryer.</p>
