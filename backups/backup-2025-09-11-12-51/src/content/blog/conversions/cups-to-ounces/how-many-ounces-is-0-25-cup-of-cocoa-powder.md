<h3>How to Measure 0.25 Cup Cocoa Powder</h3>  
<p>Use a dry measuring cup for cocoa powder, levelling it off with a straight edge for accuracy. Spoon the powder into the cup rather than scooping to avoid compacting it. If you have a kitchen scale, weigh 0.75 ounces for the most precise measurement. This method works perfectly for air fryer brownies or mug cakes.</p>
