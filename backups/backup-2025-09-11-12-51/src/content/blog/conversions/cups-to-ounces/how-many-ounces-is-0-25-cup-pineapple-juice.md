<p>Air fryers rely on even cooking, and incorrect liquid measurements can throw off texture and flavour. <h3>How to Measure 0.25 Cup Pineapple Juice Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a measuring cup, use tablespoons: 0.25 cup equals 4 tablespoons. For thicker juices like pineapple, level off the top for accuracy. Alternatively, repurpose a shot glass (typically 1 oz) and fill it twice. This method works well for quick conversions and avoids guesswork.</p>
