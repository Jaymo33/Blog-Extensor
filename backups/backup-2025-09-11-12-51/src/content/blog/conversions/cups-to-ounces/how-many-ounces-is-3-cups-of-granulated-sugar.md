<p>3 cups of granulated sugar equals approximately 21.16 ounces (600 grams) in UK measurements. <h2>Why accurate sugar measurements matter in air fryer baking</h2>
<p>Air fryers cook food faster and more evenly than traditional ovens, which means ingredient ratios are crucial. Too much sugar can cause over-browning or even burning, while too little can affect texture and taste. For example, if you're making <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>air fryer cookies</a>, the right sugar amount ensures perfect spread and caramelization.</p>
<p>Granulated sugar's weight can vary slightly depending on how it's packed, but the standard UK conversion is 1 cup = 7.05 ounces (200g). This measurement is particularly important when adapting recipes from volume (cups) to weight (ounces), which many professional bakers prefer for accuracy. <h3>How to measure 3 cups of granulated sugar properly</h3>
<p>For the most accurate 3 cups to ounces conversion, use the spoon-and-level method: gently spoon sugar into your measuring cup without packing it down, then level off the top with a straight edge. This prevents compacting, which could add extra ounces to your measurement. Digital kitchen scales will give you the most precise 21.16 ounce measurement.</p>
