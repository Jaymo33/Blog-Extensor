<h3>How to measure 1.25 cups of water accurately</h3>
<p>For the most precise measurement, use a liquid measuring cup with clear markings. Fill it to the 1.25-cup line, which is equivalent to 10 ounces. If you don’t have a measuring cup, you can use a kitchen scale set to ounces—just weigh out 10 ounces of water for the same result.</p>

<h2>Converting cups to ounces for different ingredients</h2>
