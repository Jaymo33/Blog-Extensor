<h3>How Fan Ovens Differ From Conventional Ovens</h3>
<p>Fan ovens use a circulating fan to distribute heat evenly throughout the cavity. This means they cook food about 20°C faster than conventional ovens at the same temperature setting. When converting 286°F, you must account for this efficiency difference. The same principle applies to air fryers, which are essentially mini convection ovens.</p>
