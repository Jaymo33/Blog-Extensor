<h3>How to measure 125g of chopped garlic</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can estimate 125g of chopped garlic as roughly 4.41 ounces. A standard clove weighs about 3-7g, so 125g would be around 18-42 cloves, depending on size. For precision, though, a digital scale is best.</p>
