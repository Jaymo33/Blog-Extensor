<h3>How to measure 25g of icing sugar without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don't have scales, you can use a measuring spoon or cup to estimate 25g of icing sugar. One tablespoon of icing sugar is roughly 10g, so 2.5 tablespoons will give you close to 25g. However, for baking, especially in an air fryer, weighing ingredients is always more reliable.</p>
