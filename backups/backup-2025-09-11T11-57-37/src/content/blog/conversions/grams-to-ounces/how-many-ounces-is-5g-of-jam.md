<h2>How to Measure 5g of Jam Accurately</h2>  
<p>Use a digital kitchen scale for the most accurate measurement. Place a small container on the scale, tare it to zero, then add jam until it reads 5g. If you don’t have a scale, a teaspoon holds roughly 5g of jam, but this can vary based on thickness. For sticky ingredients like jam, lightly oil the spoon to help it slide off easily.</p>
