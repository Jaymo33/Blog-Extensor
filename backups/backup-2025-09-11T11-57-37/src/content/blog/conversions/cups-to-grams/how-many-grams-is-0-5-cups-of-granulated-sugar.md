<h2>How to Measure 0.5 Cups of Granulated Sugar Correctly</h2>
<p>Measuring sugar by volume (cups) can be tricky, as packing density varies. For the most accurate results, use a kitchen scale to weigh 100 grams directly. If you don’t have a scale, fluff the sugar with a spoon before lightly scooping it into a measuring cup. Level it off with a straight edge for consistency. This method ensures your <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/cakes>air fryer cakes</a> turn out perfectly every time.</p>

For air fryer recipes, this minor difference won’t drastically affect your results, but it’s worth noting for precision baking.
