<h3>How to Measure 1.25 Cups of Granulated Sugar Correctly</h3>
<p>To measure 1.25 cups of granulated sugar accurately, use the spoon-and-level method. Spoon the sugar into a measuring cup without packing it down, then level it off with a straight edge. This prevents compacting, which can lead to excess sugar. For even better precision, a digital scale set to grams eliminates guesswork. If you’re converting from a US recipe, note that UK cups may vary slightly, so grams are the safest bet.</p>
