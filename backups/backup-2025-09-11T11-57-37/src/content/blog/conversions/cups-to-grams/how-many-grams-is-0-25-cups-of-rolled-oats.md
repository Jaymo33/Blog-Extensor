<h3>How to Measure 0.25 Cups of Rolled Oats Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, use the spoon-and-level method for accuracy. Lightly spoon rolled oats into a measuring cup until slightly heaped, then level off the excess with a knife. This prevents compacting, which can lead to using more oats than intended. For 0.25 cups, fill a 1-cup measure one-quarter full using this technique. While weighing is more precise, this method works in a pinch.</p>
