<h3>How to Measure 0.75 Cups of Coconut Oil Accurately</h3>  
<p>Use a liquid measuring cup for coconut oil in its melted state for the most accurate results. If your recipe calls for solid coconut oil, pack it firmly into the cup before levelling it off. A kitchen scale can also help verify the weight (0.75 cups ≈ 170 grams). For air fryer recipes, melted coconut oil is often preferred for even coating and crispiness.</p>
