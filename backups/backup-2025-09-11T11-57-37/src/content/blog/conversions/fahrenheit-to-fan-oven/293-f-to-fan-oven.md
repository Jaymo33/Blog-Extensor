<h2>Why Convert 293 F to Fan Oven?</h2>
<p>Many air fryer recipes, especially those from the US, use Fahrenheit measurements. Converting 293 F to fan oven settings (145 C) helps British cooks replicate dishes accurately. <h2>How Fan Ovens Differ From Conventional Ovens</h2>
<p>Fan ovens cook about 20°C faster than conventional ovens at the same temperature setting. When converting 293 F, remember that fan ovens distribute heat more efficiently. This means 145 C in a fan oven behaves similarly to 293 F in a traditional American oven or air fryer.</p>
<p>The circulating air in both fan ovens and air fryers reduces hot spots. This is why many <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/chicken>chicken recipes</a> recommend fan settings - they ensure crispy skin without drying out the meat. Always preheat your fan oven or air fryer for best results at 293 F equivalents.</p>
