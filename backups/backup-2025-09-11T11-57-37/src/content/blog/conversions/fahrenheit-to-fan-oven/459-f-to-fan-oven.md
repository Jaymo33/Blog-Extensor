<h2>Understanding Fan Oven Conversions</h2>
<p>Fan ovens circulate hot air using a built-in fan, cooking food more evenly and often faster than conventional ovens. The general rule for converting Fahrenheit to Celsius in fan ovens is to subtract 32, multiply by 5, then divide by 9. For 459 F, this calculation gives us 237 C.
