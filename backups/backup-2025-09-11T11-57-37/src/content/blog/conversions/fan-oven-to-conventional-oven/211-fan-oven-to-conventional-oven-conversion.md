<p>211°C in a fan oven equals approximately 190°C in a conventional oven. This conversion is essential when adapting recipes between different oven types or when using an air fryer. The difference occurs because fan ovens circulate hot air more efficiently, cooking food faster at lower temperatures.</p>

<h2>Understanding Fan Oven Conversions</h2>
<p>Fan ovens, also known as convection ovens, operate differently from conventional ovens. The built-in fan circulates hot air around the food, creating more even heat distribution. This means you can typically reduce the temperature by about 20°C when converting from 211 fan oven to conventional oven settings. Many modern air fryers work on similar principles to fan ovens, making these conversions particularly useful for air fryer users.</p>
