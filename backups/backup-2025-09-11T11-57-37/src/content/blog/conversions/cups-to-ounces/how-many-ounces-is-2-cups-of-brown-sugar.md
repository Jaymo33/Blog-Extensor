<h3>How to measure brown sugar correctly</h3>  
<p>To measure 2 cups of brown sugar accurately, spoon it into a measuring cup and press it down firmly with the back of a spoon. This “packed” method ensures you’re getting the full weight of 16 ounces. Loose brown sugar can lead to under-measuring by up to 25%, throwing off your air fryer recipes. If your recipe calls for ounces but you only have cups, this method guarantees precision.</p>

<h3>Converting other sugar measurements</h3>  
<p>If your recipe uses grams or kilograms, 16 ounces equals roughly 454 grams. This conversion helps when using European recipes or digital scales. Remember that brown sugar’s weight differs from powdered or white sugar, so never substitute volume measurements directly. Always check whether your air fryer recipe specifies packed or loose brown sugar for best results.</p>
