<h2>How to Measure 1.25 Cups of Onion</h2>  
<p>Measuring onions by volume can be tricky due to their irregular shape. For 1.25 cups, start by dicing or slicing your onion uniformly. Pack the onion lightly into a measuring cup without pressing down too hard, as this can compress the pieces and skew the measurement. If you’re using pre-chopped onions, ensure they’re evenly distributed in the cup.</p>
