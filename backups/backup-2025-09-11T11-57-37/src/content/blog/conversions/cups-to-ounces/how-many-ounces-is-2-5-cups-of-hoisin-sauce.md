<h3>How to Measure Hoisin Sauce Correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate 2.5 cups to ounces conversion, use a liquid measuring cup for hoisin sauce. Scoop it gently to avoid air pockets, and level it off with a spatula. If you’re converting 2.5 cups of hoisin sauce to ounces for a UK recipe, note that fluid ounces are the same in both metric and imperial systems for liquids.</p>
