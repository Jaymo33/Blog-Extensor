<h3>Storing golden syrup properly</h3>  
<p>After measuring out your 0.25 cup (2.5 ounces) of golden syrup, it’s important to store the rest correctly. Keep it in an airtight container at room temperature, away from direct sunlight. Proper storage prevents crystallisation and maintains the syrup’s smooth texture for future air fryer adventures. If your syrup does harden, placing the tin in warm water will return it to pouring consistency.</p>
