<h2>How to Measure 0.75 Cup of Wheat Flour</h2>  
<p>Measuring flour by volume (cups) can be tricky due to settling and packing. For the most accurate 0.75 cup wheat flour to ounces conversion, fluff the flour with a spoon before lightly scooping it into a measuring cup. Level it off with a knife for consistency. Alternatively, use a kitchen scale for exact ounces.</p>  

<h3>Using a Kitchen Scale for Precision</h3>  
<p>A digital scale eliminates guesswork, giving you the exact weight of 0.75 cup of wheat flour in ounces. Simply place your bowl on the scale, tare it to zero, and add flour until you reach 3.17 oz. This method is ideal for air fryer recipes where precision matters.</p>
