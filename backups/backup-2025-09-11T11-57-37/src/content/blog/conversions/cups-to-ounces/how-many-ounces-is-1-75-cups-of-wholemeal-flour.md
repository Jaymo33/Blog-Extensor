<p>1.75 cups of wholemeal flour equals approximately 7.7 ounces (or 220 grams) in UK measurements. This conversion is essential for bakers who prefer weighing ingredients for accuracy, especially when using an air fryer for recipes like bread or muffins. Wholemeal flour has a slightly different density compared to plain flour, so measuring by weight ensures consistent results every time.</p>  

<h2>Why Measuring Wholemeal Flour Correctly Matters</h2>  
<p>Baking is a science, and precise measurements can make or break your recipe. Wholemeal flour is denser than white flour, meaning 1.75 cups will weigh more than the same volume of plain flour. If you're using an air fryer for baking, accurate measurements prevent dry or dense results. For example, air fryer recipes like <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>wholemeal muffins</a> rely on the right flour-to-liquid ratio.</p>  

<p>Many UK bakers prefer weighing ingredients in ounces or grams for better consistency. A kitchen scale is a worthwhile investment if you frequently bake with wholemeal flour. If you don’t have one, fluffing the flour before scooping and levelling it off can help approximate the correct amount. For more conversion tips, check our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-conversion-chart>air fryer conversion chart</a>.</p>  

<h3>How to Measure 1.75 Cups of Wholemeal Flour Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a scale, spoon the flour into your measuring cup and level it off with a knife. Avoid packing it down, as this can lead to using too much flour. For 1.75 cups, measure one full cup, then fill another three-quarters of the way. This method ensures you’re close to the 7.7-ounce mark, though weighing is still more precise.</p>

<h3>Adjusting Recipes for Wholemeal Flour</h3>  
<p>Wholemeal flour absorbs more liquid than white flour, so you may need to tweak recipes slightly. If a recipe calls for 1.75 cups of plain flour, you can substitute wholemeal but consider adding a tablespoon or two of extra liquid. This adjustment ensures your air fryer bakes stay moist and tender.</p>
