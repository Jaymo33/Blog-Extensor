<h3>How to measure flaxseed without a scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a scale, use the spoon-and-level method for cups. Gently spoon flaxseed into a measuring cup and level it off with a knife. Avoid packing it down, as this can lead to an inaccurate measurement. For 1.66 cups, you’d need roughly 7.5 ounces, which is about 213 grams.
