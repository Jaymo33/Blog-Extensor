<h2>How to measure 0.5 cup of gluten-free flour</h2>  
<p>Use a dry measuring cup for the most accurate results. Spoon the flour into the cup and level it off with a knife. Avoid packing it down, as this can lead to excess flour. For best results, consider investing in a kitchen scale. This ensures your 0.5 cup of gluten-free flour to ounces conversion is spot-on.</p>
