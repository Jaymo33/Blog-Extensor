<h2>How to measure 0.75 cup of skimmed milk correctly</h2>
<p>To measure 0.75 cup of skimmed milk accurately, use a liquid measuring cup with clear markings. Fill it to the 3/4 line for the correct amount. If you don't have a measuring cup, you can use a kitchen scale to weigh out 6 ounces instead. This method is often more precise, especially for air fryer recipes where consistency is key.</p>
<p>When measuring, ensure the milk is at room temperature for the most accurate reading. Cold liquids can sometimes contract slightly, leading to minor discrepancies. <h3>Converting other milk types</h3>
