<h3>How to Measure 1.66 Cups of Wheat Flour Correctly</h3>  
<p>The best way to measure flour is by using a kitchen scale for accuracy. Scooping flour directly from the bag can compact it, leading to over-measuring. Instead, spoon the flour into your measuring cup and level it off with a knife. This method ensures you’re getting the right amount—1.66 cups of wheat flour should weigh roughly 7.5 ounces.</p>
