<h2>How to measure 0.5 cup of whipping cream correctly</h2>  
<p>For the most accurate conversion of 0.5 cup whipping cream to ounces, use a kitchen scale. Liquid measuring cups can vary slightly, but a scale provides a precise 4-ounce measurement. If you don't have a scale, fill a liquid measuring cup to the 0.5 cup mark, ensuring the cream sits level at eye height for accuracy.</p>  
<p>When preparing air fryer recipes like <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/dessert>whipped cream-topped desserts</a>, consistency matters. Too little cream may result in a runny texture, while too much can make desserts overly dense.</p>
