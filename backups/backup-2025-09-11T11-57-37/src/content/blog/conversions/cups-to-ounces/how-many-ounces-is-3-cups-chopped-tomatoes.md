<h3>How to Measure Chopped Tomatoes Correctly</h3>  
<p>For consistent results, lightly pack chopped tomatoes into a measuring cup without compressing them. A loosely filled 3-cup measure equals roughly 24 ounces. If you’re using a kitchen scale, aim for 680 grams for precision. This method works for fresh or canned tomatoes, though canned varieties may vary slightly in density.</p>
