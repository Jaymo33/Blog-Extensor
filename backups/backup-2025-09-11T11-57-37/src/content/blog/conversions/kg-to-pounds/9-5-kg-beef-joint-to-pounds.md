<h2>How to cook a 9.5 kg beef joint in your air fryer</h2>

<p>The cooking time for a 9.5 kg beef joint will vary depending on how you like your meat cooked and the specific model of your air fryer. As a general rule, for a joint of this size (20.94 pounds), you should allow approximately 15-20 minutes per pound for medium-rare, but always use a meat thermometer for accuracy. Insert the thermometer into the thickest part of the joint, avoiding any bone, and aim for an internal temperature of 135°F for medium-rare, 145°F for medium, and 160°F for well-done. Remember that the meat will continue to cook slightly after removal from the heat, so factor in a resting time of at least 20-30 minutes.
