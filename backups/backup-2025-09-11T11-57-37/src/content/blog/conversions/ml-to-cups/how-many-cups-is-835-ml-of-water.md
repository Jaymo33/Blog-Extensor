<h3>UK vs. US Cup Measurements</h3>  
<p>In the UK, a standard cup holds 250 ml, while in the US, it's 240 ml. This means 835 ml of water converts to 3.52 UK cups or 3.53 US cups. The difference is minimal but worth noting for precision.
