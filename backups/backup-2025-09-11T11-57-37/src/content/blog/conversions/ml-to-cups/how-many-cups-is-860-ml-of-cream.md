<h2>Understanding the Conversion: 860 ml of Cream to Cups</h2>  
<p>Converting millilitres to cups can be tricky, especially when dealing with ingredients like cream, which has a different density than water. For most practical purposes, 860 ml of cream translates to approximately 3.63 cups. This measurement is based on the standard UK metric cup, which holds 250 ml. If you're using a US cup (240 ml), the conversion would be slightly different, coming in at around 3.58 cups.</p>

<h2>How to Measure 860 ml of Cream Without a Measuring Cup</h2>  
<p>Don’t have a measuring cup handy? No problem. You can use a kitchen scale to measure 860 ml of cream by weight. However, this method is less precise and best used as a last resort.</p>
