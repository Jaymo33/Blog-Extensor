<h2>How to measure 5 tbsp of vinegar correctly</h2>  
<p>For the most accurate 5 tbsp vinegar to grams conversion, use a digital kitchen scale. Spoon vinegar into a tablespoon measure without packing or compressing it. Level off the top with a knife for consistency. If you don’t have scales, remember that 1 UK tbsp equals roughly 15g, making 5 tbsp = 75g.</p>
