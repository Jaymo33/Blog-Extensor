<h2>How to measure 0.25 tbsp of chilli sauce without scales</h2>

Remember, chilli sauce’s viscosity can vary, so these methods are best for approximate measurements.</p>

<h3>Common mistakes when converting tablespoons to grams</h3>
<p>One major error is assuming all sauces weigh the same. Chilli sauce is denser than water, so its weight per tablespoon differs.
