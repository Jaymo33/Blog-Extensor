<h3>How to measure coconut oil for air frying</h3>  
<p>Coconut oil’s consistency changes with temperature, making volume measurements tricky. Solid coconut oil packs differently into a tablespoon compared to melted oil. For 2.5 tbsp of coconut oil in grams, we recommend weighing it for accuracy. If you don’t have a scale, lightly pack solid oil into the spoon and level it off. Melted oil should be poured to the brim without overflowing.</p>
