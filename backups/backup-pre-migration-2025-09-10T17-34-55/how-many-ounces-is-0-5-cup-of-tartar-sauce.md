<h3>How to measure tartar sauce without a scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can still measure 0.5 cup of tartar sauce accurately. Use a liquid measuring cup and fill it to the 0.5 cup mark, ensuring the sauce is level with the rim. Avoid packing it down, as this can lead to over-measuring. For thicker sauces like tartar, a spoon can help level off the top. This method will get you close to the 4-ounce mark, though a scale is always more precise.</p>
