<h2>Understanding Coconut Milk Measurements</h2>  
<p>Coconut milk is a staple in many recipes, from curries to desserts. Measuring it correctly ensures your dish turns out as intended. The difference between US and UK cup sizes can affect your results. A US cup holds 240 ml, while a UK cup is 284 ml. This means 935 ml of coconut milk equals 3.95 US cups or 3.29 UK cups. Always check your recipe’s origin to avoid confusion.</p>  

<p>For air fryer recipes, precision is key. Too much or too little liquid can alter cooking times and textures. <h2>Converting 935 ml of Coconut Milk for Recipes</h2>  
<p>If your recipe calls for 935 ml of coconut milk, here’s how to measure it without a scale. Use a liquid measuring cup for the most accurate results. Fill it to the 3.95-cup mark for US measurements or 3.29 cups for UK. Stir the coconut milk well before measuring, as it can separate. This ensures an even distribution of fat and liquid.</p>
