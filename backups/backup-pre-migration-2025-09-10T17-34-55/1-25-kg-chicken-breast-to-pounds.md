<p>The conversion from kilograms to pounds is straightforward once you know the basic formula. Doing the math gives you 2.755775 pounds, which rounds to 2.76 pounds for most practical purposes. This calculation is useful when you come across a recipe that lists ingredients in pounds and your kitchen scale uses kilograms.
