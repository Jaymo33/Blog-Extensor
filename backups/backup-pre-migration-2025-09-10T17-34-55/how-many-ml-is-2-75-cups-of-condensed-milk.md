<h3>How to Measure 2.75 Cups of Condensed Milk Without a Scale</h3>
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, use the ml markings on a measuring jug. Pour slowly to the 650 ml line for accuracy. For sticky condensed milk, lightly grease the cup or jug first. This method works perfectly for air fryer recipes like <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/dessert>desserts</a> or glazes.</p>

<h2>US vs. UK Cup Measurements for Condensed Milk</h2>
<p>In the UK, 1 cup equals 284 ml, while in the US, it’s 240 ml. For 2.75 cups, this means roughly 781 ml (UK) or 660 ml (US). However, most recipes standardise to the US measurement (650 ml). Always check the recipe origin to avoid confusion. For more global conversions, visit our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-conversion-chart>conversion chart</a>.</p>
