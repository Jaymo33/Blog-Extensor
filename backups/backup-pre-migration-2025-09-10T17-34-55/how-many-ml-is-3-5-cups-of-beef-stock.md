<h3>How to Measure 3.5 Cups of Beef Stock Without a Measuring Cup</h3>  
<p>Don’t have a measuring cup? No problem! A standard UK mug holds roughly 284 ml, so 3.5 cups would be just under three mugs. Alternatively, use a kitchen scale—since 1 ml of beef stock weighs approximately 1 gram, 828 ml will weigh around 828 grams. This method is especially useful if you’re scaling recipes up or down for your air fryer.</p>
