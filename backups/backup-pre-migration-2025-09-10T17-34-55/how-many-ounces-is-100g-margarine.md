<h3>How to Measure 100g of Margarine Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can still measure 100g of margarine (3.53 oz) using tablespoons. One tablespoon of margarine weighs roughly 14g, so 100g equals about 7 tablespoons. <h3>Margarine vs. Butter: Does the Conversion Differ?</h3>
