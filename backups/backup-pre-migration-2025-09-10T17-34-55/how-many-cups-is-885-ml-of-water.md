<h2>Understanding the Conversion: 885 ml to Cups</h2>  
<p>Converting 885 ml of water to cups depends on whether you're using UK or US measurements. In the UK, 1 cup is 250 ml, making 885 ml roughly 3.74 cups. In the US, 1 cup is 236.59 ml, so 885 ml equals about 3.75 cups. This slight difference matters in baking or air fryer recipes where precision is key. For example, a <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>cake recipe</a> might require exact liquid measurements for the perfect rise.</p>
