<h2>Common Mistakes When Converting Grams to Cups</h2>  
<p>One frequent error is assuming all potato cuts weigh the same per cup. Whole baby potatoes, for instance, leave more gaps in a measuring cup compared to uniformly diced pieces. Another pitfall is forgetting to account for moisture content; boiled or steamed potatoes will weigh more per cup than raw ones.</p>
