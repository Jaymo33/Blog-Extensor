<h2>Understanding Cocoa Powder Measurements</h2>
<p>Converting 175 grams of cocoa powder to cups requires knowing your cup type – UK cups (250ml) differ slightly from US cups (240ml). For UK bakers using an air fryer, 175g translates to 1.75 cups, while US measurements would be about 1.8 cups. This small variation matters in recipes where cocoa powder acts as the primary dry ingredient. Our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-conversion-chart>air fryer conversion chart</a> helps eliminate guesswork.</p>
<p>Cocoa powder's density affects measurements too. Unlike flour, it doesn't compact as easily, so spooning it into cups gives consistent results. For 175 grams of cocoa powder, lightly spoon it into your measuring cup without packing or tapping. This method prevents over-measuring, which could make your air fryer chocolate desserts overly dense.</p>
