<h3>How to Measure Minced Beef Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, use the spoon-and-level method to measure 175 grams of minced beef. Gently spoon the meat into a measuring cup without pressing down, then level it off with a knife. This method ensures a close approximation to 0.75 cups. For recipes requiring exact proportions, like meatloaf or patties, investing in a scale is worthwhile.
