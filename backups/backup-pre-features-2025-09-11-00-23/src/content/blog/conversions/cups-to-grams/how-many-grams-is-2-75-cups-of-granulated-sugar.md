<h2>How to Measure 2.75 Cups of Granulated Sugar</h2>
<p>Measuring 2.75 cups of granulated sugar accurately starts with using the right tools. A standard measuring cup is fine, but for the most precise results, a kitchen scale is highly recommended. Simply scoop the sugar into your measuring cup, level it off with a straight edge, and then weigh it to confirm you've got the correct amount.</p>
<p>If you don't have a scale, remember that 1 cup of granulated sugar typically weighs around 200 grams. Therefore, 2.75 cups would be roughly 550 grams. This approximation works well for most home baking needs, including air fryer recipes where precision matters but slight variations are acceptable.</p>
