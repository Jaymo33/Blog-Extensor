<h3>How to Adjust Cooking Times</h3>  
<p>When using 413 F in a fan oven, cooking times may be shorter than in a conventional oven. Start checking your food 5-10 minutes earlier to prevent burning. <h3>Common Mistakes to Avoid</h3>  
<p>One mistake is not accounting for the fan oven’s efficiency, leading to overcooked food. Always reduce the temperature or time slightly when converting from conventional ovens.
