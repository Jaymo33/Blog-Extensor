<h3>Converting Cups to ml for Evaporated Milk</h3>  
<p>The standard conversion for liquid measurements is 1 cup = 236.59 ml. Therefore, 0.5 cups of evaporated milk is half of that, which is roughly 118 ml. This applies whether you're in the UK or the US, as metric measurements are universal in cooking.</p>
