<p>162 C in a fan oven is equivalent to approximately 142 C in a conventional oven. This 20-degree reduction is standard for fan-assisted cooking, where circulating air distributes heat more efficiently. When using your air fryer - essentially a compact fan oven - this conversion becomes particularly important for achieving consistent results.</p>

<h2>Understanding Fan Oven Conversions</h2>
<p>The principle behind converting 162 C to fan oven settings lies in understanding convection cooking. Fan ovens and air fryers work by constantly moving hot air around the food, which cooks items faster and more evenly. This means you can typically reduce temperatures by 20°C compared to conventional oven recipes. For example, when following a recipe from <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>airfryerrecipe.co.uk</a> that calls for 162 C, you'd set your fan oven or air fryer to 142 C.</p>
<p>This conversion applies to most baked goods and roasted dishes. However, some delicate items like soufflés or custards might require slight adjustments. <h2>Why 162 C Matters in Air Fryers</h2>
<p>162 C (or 142 C fan) is a sweet spot for many air fryer recipes. It's hot enough to crisp foods without burning, yet gentle enough for even cooking. This temperature works particularly well for items like roasted vegetables from our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/vegetables>vegetable recipes</a> collection, where you want caramelisation without drying out the produce.</p>
<p>When converting 162 C to fan oven settings for your air fryer, remember that cooking times may also need adjusting. The powerful fan in air fryers can cook food 20% faster than conventional ovens, so always check items a few minutes early. For frozen foods like those in our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/frozen-foods>frozen foods section</a>, this temperature conversion ensures thorough cooking without excess browning.</p>

<h3>Special Considerations for Air Fryers</h3>
<p>Air fryers intensify the fan oven effect due to their compact size and powerful fans.
