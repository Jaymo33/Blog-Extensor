<h3>How to measure almonds correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate results, use a kitchen scale to weigh your almonds. If you don’t have one, spoon the almonds into a measuring cup and level them off without packing too tightly. This method prevents excess air gaps or overfilling, which can throw off your recipe.</p>
