<h3>How to Measure Almond Butter Correctly</h3>  
<p>To measure 3 cups of almond butter accurately, use a dry measuring cup and level it off with a straight edge. For sticky ingredients like almond butter, lightly greasing the cup can help it slide out easily. If you have a kitchen scale, weighing it in ounces is even better. This method eliminates guesswork and ensures your air fryer dishes turn out just right.</p>

<h3>Tips for Storing Almond Butter</h3>  
<p>To keep almond butter fresh, store it in an airtight container in a cool, dark place. Stirring it occasionally prevents oil separation. If you’ve measured out 3 cups of almond butter but only need part of it, the rest can be refrigerated for later use. Proper storage ensures it stays ready for your next air fryer creation.</p>
