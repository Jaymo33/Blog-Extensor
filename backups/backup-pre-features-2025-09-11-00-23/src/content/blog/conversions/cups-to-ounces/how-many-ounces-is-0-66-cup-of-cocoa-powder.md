<h3>How to Measure 0.66 Cup of Cocoa Powder Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a scale, use the spoon-and-level method for 0.66 cup of cocoa powder. Lightly spoon the powder into a measuring cup without packing it down, then level it off with a knife.
