<h2>Common Mistakes When Measuring Whipping Cream</h2>  
<p>One frequent error is confusing fluid ounces with weight ounces. In the UK, 1.75 cups of whipping cream is roughly 14 fluid ounces, but weight may vary slightly. Always double-check if your recipe specifies weight or volume.</p>  
<p>Another pitfall is not accounting for whipped cream expansion. If your recipe calls for whipped cream, measure it before whipping. For more air fryer tips, visit <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk>airfryerrecipe.co.uk</a>.</p>
