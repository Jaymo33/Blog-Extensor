<h3>How to measure coconut oil for air frying</h3>  
<p>Coconut oil can be tricky to measure because it changes texture with temperature. Solid at room temperature and liquid when heated, it’s best measured by weight for consistency. If your recipe lists 1.75 cups of coconut oil in ounces, use a kitchen scale for precision. Scooping solid coconut oil into a measuring cup can lead to air pockets, skewing the amount.</p>  

<h2>Converting other coconut oil measurements</h2>  
<p>Beyond 1.75 cups, you might need to convert smaller or larger amounts. Half a cup of coconut oil is about 4 ounces, while 3 cups equate to roughly 24 ounces. These conversions are handy for scaling recipes up or down.
