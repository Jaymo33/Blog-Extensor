<h3>Converting cups to ounces for condensed milk</h3>  
<p>If your recipe lists condensed milk in cups but you prefer ounces, remember that 1 cup equals 10 oz. This conversion applies to most standard condensed milk brands in the UK. <h2>Common mistakes when measuring condensed milk</h2>  
<p>One frequent error is confusing evaporated milk with condensed milk, as their densities differ. Condensed milk is thicker and sweeter, so 1 cup in ounces won’t match evaporated milk. Always check labels to ensure you’re using the right product. Another pitfall is scooping straight from the can, which can lead to uneven measurements. For best results, spoon condensed milk into a measuring cup and level it off.</p>
