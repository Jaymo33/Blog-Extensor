<h3>How to Measure Powdered Milk Correctly</h3>  
<p>To convert 2 cups of powdered milk to ounces accurately, use a kitchen scale for the best results. Scooping powdered milk directly from the bag can lead to uneven measurements due to settling. Instead, spoon it lightly into a measuring cup and level it off with a knife. This method ensures you’re not packing in too much, which could throw off your recipe.</p>
