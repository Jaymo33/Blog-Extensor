<h2>Converting cups to ounces for UK cooks</h2>  
<p>In the UK, fluid ounces and weight ounces can cause confusion, but for apple juice, the difference is minimal. 2 cups (a US measurement) translates to 16 US fluid ounces, which is roughly 473 ml. If your recipe uses UK imperial fluid ounces, 2 cups equal about 16.7 UK fluid ounces. Most modern recipes, including those on <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes</a>, standardise measurements for ease.</p>
