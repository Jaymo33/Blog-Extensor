<h3>How to Measure 0.125 Cup of Cocoa Powder</h3>  
<p>Measuring small quantities like 0.125 cup can be tricky without the right tools. A standard UK measuring cup holds 250ml, so 0.125 cup is roughly 30ml. For the most accurate results, use a digital kitchen scale to weigh out 0.5 ounces (14 grams) of cocoa powder. This method eliminates guesswork and ensures your <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/cakes>air fryer cakes</a> turn out perfectly every time.</p>
