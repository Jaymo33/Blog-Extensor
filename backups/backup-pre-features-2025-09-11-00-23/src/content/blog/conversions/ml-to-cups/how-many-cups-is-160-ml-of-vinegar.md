<h3>UK vs. US Cup Measurements</h3>  
<p>In the UK, 1 cup equals 284 ml, while in the US, it's 240 ml. This means 160 ml of vinegar is roughly 0.68 cups in the UK and 0.67 cups in the US. The slight difference might not seem significant, but for baking, every millilitre counts. Always double-check which measurement system your recipe uses to avoid mishaps.</p>
