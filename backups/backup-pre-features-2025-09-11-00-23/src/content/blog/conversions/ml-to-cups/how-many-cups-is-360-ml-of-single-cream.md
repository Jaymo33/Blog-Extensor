<h2>UK vs US Cup Measurements</h2>
<p>In the UK, 1 cup equals 250 ml, making 360 ml of single cream roughly 1.5 cups. In the US, 1 cup is 240 ml, so 360 ml converts to about 1.52 cups. This slight difference might not seem like much, but it can affect the outcome of delicate recipes.</p>
