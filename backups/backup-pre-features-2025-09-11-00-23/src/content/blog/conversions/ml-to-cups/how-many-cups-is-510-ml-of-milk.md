<h2>How to Convert 510 ml of Milk to Cups</h2>
<p>Converting 510 ml of milk to cups is straightforward. In the UK, 1 cup is 284 ml, so 510 ml is roughly 2.15 cups. In the US, where 1 cup is 240 ml, 510 ml equals about 2.16 cups. This small difference usually doesn’t affect most recipes, but it’s good to know for precision.</p>

<h3>Measuring 510 ml of Milk Without a Scale</h3>
<p>If you don’t have a measuring jug, you can use standard cups to measure 510 ml of milk. A UK cup holds 284 ml, so two cups give you 568 ml—slightly more than 510 ml. For 510 ml, fill two cups and remove about 58 ml. In the US, two cups equal 480 ml, so you’d need to add an extra 30 ml (about two tablespoons) to reach 510 ml.</p>
