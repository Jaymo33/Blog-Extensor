<h3>UK vs. US Cup Measurements for Oat Milk</h3>  
<p>It’s important to note that UK and US cup sizes differ slightly. A UK cup holds 284 ml, while a US cup is 240 ml. This means 610 ml of oat milk converts to 2.57 UK cups or 2.58 US cups.
