<h3>US vs UK Cup Measurements for Corn Syrup</h3>  
<p>In the US, 1 cup equals 240 ml, meaning 160 ml of corn syrup is roughly 0.68 cups. However, UK cups measure 284 ml, so 160 ml converts to about 0.56 cups.
