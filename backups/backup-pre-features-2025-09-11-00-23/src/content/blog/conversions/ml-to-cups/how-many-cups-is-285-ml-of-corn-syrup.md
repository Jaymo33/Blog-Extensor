<h2>How to Measure 285 ml of Corn Syrup Without a Measuring Cup</h2>
<p>Don’t have a measuring cup handy? No problem. You can still measure 285 ml of corn syrup using a kitchen scale. Since corn syrup has a density of about 1.36 g/ml, 285 ml weighs roughly 387 grams. This method is just as accurate and ensures your air fryer recipes turn out perfectly every time.</p>

<h3>Using Tablespoons as an Alternative</h3>
<p>If you’re in a pinch, tablespoons can also work. There are 16 tablespoons in a cup, so 285 ml of corn syrup would be around 19 tablespoons. While not as precise as a measuring cup, this method works well for smaller batches or when you’re adjusting recipes on the fly.</p>
