<h3>How to chop onions for accurate measurements</h3>  
<p>Chopping onions uniformly ensures consistent weight and even cooking. Start by peeling the onion and cutting it in half. Slice vertically, then horizontally, for evenly sized pieces. This method helps when measuring 150g of chopped onion, as irregular cuts can affect the final weight.</p>
