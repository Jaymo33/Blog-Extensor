<h2>How to Convert 40g of Cream to Ounces</h2>  
<p>The conversion factor for grams to ounces is straightforward: 1 gram equals 0.035 ounces. For 40g of cream, multiply 40 by 0.035 to get 1.41 ounces. This small but crucial measurement ensures your air fryer recipes stay consistent.</p>  
<p>Digital scales are the easiest way to measure 40g of cream accurately. If you don’t have one, a tablespoon holds roughly 15g of cream, so about 2.5 tablespoons would be close. However, for best results, especially in baking, weighing is always preferable.</p>

<h2>Tips for Measuring Cream Without a Scale</h2>  
<p>If you’re in a pinch, use volume measurements. Heavy cream weighs about 15g per tablespoon, so 40g is roughly 2.5 tablespoons. For lighter creams, check the density—some may vary slightly. Always stir cream before measuring to ensure consistency.</p>  
<p>For sticky ingredients like cream, a <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-cleaner>non-stick measuring spoon</a> helps.
