<p>If you own a 2 quart air fryer, the right liner size is typically 6 to 7 inches in diameter for round liners or 6x6 inches for square liners. Using the correct liner ensures even cooking, prevents food from sticking, and makes cleanup a breeze. Whether you prefer silicone, parchment paper, or reusable options, finding the perfect fit is key to getting the most out of your air fryer.</p>  

<h2>Why Use Liners in a 2 Quart Air Fryer?</h2>  
<p>Liners are a game-changer for air fryer users, especially for smaller models like the 2 quart size. They prevent food from sticking to the basket, reduce oil splatter, and make cleaning much easier. Whether you’re cooking crispy chicken wings or reheating leftovers, a good liner ensures your air fryer stays in top condition. Plus, they help distribute heat evenly, so your food cooks perfectly every time. For more tips on air fryer maintenance, check out our guide on <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-cleaner>how to clean your air fryer</a>.</p>  

<p>Different liner materials offer unique benefits. Silicone liners are reusable and eco-friendly, while parchment liners are disposable and convenient. Some liners even come pre-cut to fit specific air fryer models, so you don’t have to worry about trimming them. If you’re unsure which type to choose, consider how often you use your air fryer and whether you prefer convenience or sustainability.</p>  

<h2>Types of Liners for 2 Quart Air Fryers</h2>  
<h3>Silicone Liners</h3>  
<p>Silicone liners are a popular choice for 2 quart air fryers because they’re durable and reusable. These liners usually measure around 6 to 7 inches in diameter and can withstand high temperatures. They’re also non-stick, making them ideal for cooking delicate foods like fish or tofu. If you’re looking for a long-term solution, silicone liners are a great investment. <h3>Parchment Paper Liners</h3>  
<p>Parchment paper liners are disposable and perfect for quick, mess-free cooking. These liners often come pre-cut to fit 2 quart air fryers, so you don’t have to worry about sizing. They’re ideal for baking cookies or reheating frozen foods, as they prevent sticking without any extra oil. Just make sure to avoid using parchment liners at extremely high temperatures, as they can burn. <h2>How to Measure Your Air Fryer Basket</h2>  
<p>If you’re unsure about the exact size of your 2 quart air fryer basket, measuring it is simple. Use a tape measure to check the diameter for round baskets or the length and width for square ones. Most 2 quart air fryers have baskets that are 6 to 7 inches in diameter, but it’s always best to double-check. Knowing your basket’s dimensions ensures you buy liners that fit snugly and don’t interfere with the cooking process.</p>  

<p>Some air fryer brands, like Ninja or Philips, may have slightly different basket shapes.
