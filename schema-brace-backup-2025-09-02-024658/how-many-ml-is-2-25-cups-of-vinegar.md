<h2>Converting 2.25 Cups of Vinegar to ml</h2>  
<p>The conversion from cups to millilitres is straightforward once you know the ratio. One US cup equals 236.588 ml, so 2.25 cups of vinegar is 2.25 x 236.588 = 532.32 ml. This measurement is crucial for UK cooks using metric recipes or scaling ingredients. If you’re working with a British recipe, double-check whether it uses UK or US cup measurements, as they differ slightly.
