<h3>UK vs. US Cup Measurements</h3>  
<p>It's worth noting that UK and US cup measurements differ slightly. A UK cup holds 284 ml, while a US cup is 240 ml. This means 610 ml of maple syrup converts to 2.57 cups in the UK and 2.58 cups in the US. For most recipes, this small difference won’t matter, but for precision baking, it’s good to be aware of the distinction.</p>  

<h2>How to Measure 610 ml of Maple Syrup Without a Measuring Cup</h2>  
<p>If you don’t have a measuring cup handy, you can still measure 610 ml of maple syrup using common kitchen tools. A standard tablespoon holds about 15 ml, so 610 ml would be roughly 40 tablespoons. Alternatively, a kitchen scale can help—maple syrup weighs approximately 312 grams per cup, so 610 ml would weigh around 790 grams.</p>
