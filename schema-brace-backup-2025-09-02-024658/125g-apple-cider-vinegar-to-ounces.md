<h3>How to measure 125g of apple cider vinegar accurately</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a scale, you can use a liquid measuring cup to measure 125g (4.41 ounces) of apple cider vinegar. Since vinegar has a similar density to water, 125g will roughly equal 125ml, which is just under half a cup. For best results, we recommend using a digital scale for precision, especially when working with air fryer recipes where consistency is key.</p>

<h3>Tips for storing apple cider vinegar</h3>  
<p>Once opened, apple cider vinegar should be stored in a cool, dark place to maintain its flavour and potency. A 125g (4.41-ounce) portion will last for months if stored properly, making it a pantry staple for air fryer enthusiasts. Always check for freshness before use—if the vinegar smells off or has changed colour, it’s best to replace it.</p>
