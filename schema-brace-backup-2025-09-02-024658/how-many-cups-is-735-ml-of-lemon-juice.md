<h3>How to Measure 735 ml of Lemon Juice Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a measuring jug, use standard cup sizes. A UK metric cup holds 250 ml, so 735 ml is roughly 3 cups plus 1 tablespoon. For US cups (240 ml), it’s closer to 3 cups and 2 tablespoons. Freshly squeezed lemon juice may vary in acidity, so adjust to taste.
