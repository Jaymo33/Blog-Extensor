<h2>How to Measure 185 ml of Tomato Sauce Without a Scale</h2>  
<p>Not everyone has a kitchen scale, but most have measuring cups. To measure 185 ml of tomato sauce, fill a liquid measuring cup to just under 4/5 of a cup. Alternatively, use a standard 250 ml cup and fill it about three-quarters full. For sticky sauces like tomato, lightly grease the cup for easy pouring.</p>  

<h3>Common Mistakes When Converting ml to Cups</h3>  
<p>One frequent error is confusing fluid ounces with millilitres, leading to incorrect measurements. Another is packing tomato sauce into the cup, which adds extra volume.
