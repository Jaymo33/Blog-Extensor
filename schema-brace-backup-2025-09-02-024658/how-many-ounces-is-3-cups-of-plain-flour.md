<p>3 cups of plain flour equals approximately 13.23 ounces (oz). This conversion is essential for precise baking, especially when using an air fryer, where accurate measurements can make or break your recipe. Whether you're whipping up cakes, cookies, or bread, knowing the weight of your flour ensures consistent results every time.</p>  

<h2>Why Convert Cups to Ounces?</h2>  
<p>Measuring flour by weight (ounces) is far more accurate than using cups, as volume measurements can vary based on how the flour is packed. For air fryer recipes, precision is key to achieving the perfect texture and rise. A slight miscalculation can lead to dense cakes or crumbly cookies, which is why many bakers prefer weighing ingredients.</p>  
<p>At <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk>airfryerrecipe.co.uk</a>, we recommend using a kitchen scale for the best results. If you don’t have one, our conversion guide will help you approximate the weight of 3 cups of plain flour in ounces. This is particularly useful for UK bakers, where metric measurements are more common.</p>  

<h3>How to Measure 3 Cups of Plain Flour Correctly</h3>  
<p>To ensure accuracy, spoon the flour into your measuring cup and level it off with a knife. Avoid scooping directly from the bag, as this can compact the flour and lead to an incorrect measurement. For air fryer baking, where even distribution matters, this step is crucial.</p>  

<h2>Common Uses for 3 Cups of Plain Flour</h2>  
<p>Three cups of plain flour is a standard measurement for many recipes, including air fryer cakes, cookies, and bread. For example, our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>air fryer cookie recipes</a> often call for this amount, yielding perfectly crispy edges and soft centres. It’s also a common quantity for homemade pizza dough, which cooks beautifully in the air fryer.</p>

<h2>Tips for Baking with Plain Flour in an Air Fryer</h2>  
<p>Air fryers circulate hot air, which can dry out baked goods if not monitored. To combat this, slightly reduce the cooking time or temperature when using 3 cups of plain flour in recipes like cakes or muffins. Always preheat your air fryer for even baking, and consider using parchment liners for easy removal.</p>

<h3>UK vs. US Measurements</h3>  
<p>In the UK, plain flour is typically measured in grams, but many recipes still use cups. If you're following a UK recipe, 3 cups of plain flour is roughly 375 grams, or 13.23 ounces. This slight variation is important to note for accuracy, especially in air fryer baking where precision matters.</p>
