<h2>How to Convert 1.33 Cups of Vegetable Oil to Ounces</h2>
<p>Converting cups to ounces is straightforward once you know the density of vegetable oil. One cup of vegetable oil typically weighs 8 ounces, so 1.33 cups would be 10.64 ounces. This conversion is handy for air fryer recipes, where small adjustments can affect cooking times and outcomes.</p>

<p>If you’re using a kitchen scale, simply measure out 10.64 ounces of vegetable oil for your recipe. For those without a scale, a liquid measuring cup marked in ounces will do the trick. <h2>Tips for Measuring Vegetable Oil in Air Fryer Recipes</h2>
<p>When measuring 1.33 cups of vegetable oil for your air fryer, always use a clear liquid measuring cup for accuracy. Pour the oil slowly to avoid spills, and check the measurement at eye level. For sticky ingredients, lightly grease the measuring cup first to ensure all the oil slides out easily.</p>
