<h3>How to measure 5g of powdered milk without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a scale, you can use a teaspoon to measure 5g of powdered milk. One level teaspoon holds roughly 5g, which converts to 0.18 ounces. However, for baking, we recommend using digital scales for accuracy. A slight variation can impact the final result, especially in delicate recipes like soufflés or custards.
