<h2>How to measure 0.75 tbsp of chilli sauce without scales</h2>  
<p>Not everyone has a kitchen scale, but you can still measure 0.75 tbsp of chilli sauce accurately. A standard UK tablespoon holds 15ml, so three-quarters of that is roughly 11.25ml. Since chilli sauce has a similar density to water (1g per ml), this translates to about 11.25 grams. For thicker sauces, the weight might vary slightly, but this method works well in most cases.</p>
