<h2>Tips for cooking chicken thighs in the air fryer</h2>

<h3>Storing and reheating cooked chicken thighs</h3>
<p>After converting and cooking 8.75 kg chicken thighs to pounds, you'll likely have leftovers. Store cooked chicken thighs in airtight containers in the refrigerator for up to 3-4 days. To reheat, use the air fryer at 180°C for 3-5 minutes until warmed through, which helps maintain crispiness. Avoid microwaving if possible, as it can make the skin soggy. For longer storage, you can freeze the cooked thighs for up to 3 months. When ready to eat, reheat from frozen in the air fryer, adding a few extra minutes to ensure they're hot all the way through.</p>
