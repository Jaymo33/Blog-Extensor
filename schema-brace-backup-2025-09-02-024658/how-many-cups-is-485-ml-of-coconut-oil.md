<h3>How to Measure 485 ml of Coconut Oil</h3>  
<p>Measuring 485 ml of coconut oil is straightforward with the right tools. If you're using a measuring cup, fill it to just over the 2-cup mark (since 485 ml equals roughly 2.05 cups). For precision, a kitchen scale can also help—485 ml of coconut oil weighs about 446 grams. If your recipe calls for melted coconut oil, measure it in liquid form for accuracy. Always level off the top with a straight edge to avoid excess.</p>
