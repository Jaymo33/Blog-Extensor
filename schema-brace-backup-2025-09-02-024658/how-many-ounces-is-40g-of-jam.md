<p>40g of jam equals approximately 1.41 ounces. This conversion is handy when following air fryer recipes that require precise measurements. Whether you're making a glaze or a filling, knowing the exact weight ensures perfect results every time.</p>

<h2>Why Convert 40g of Jam to Ounces?</h2>
<p>Many UK recipes use grams, while others prefer ounces. Converting 40g of jam to ounces (1.41 oz) helps maintain accuracy, especially in air fryer cooking where precise measurements matter. A slight variation can affect texture and taste, so it's best to get it right.</p>

<h2>How to Measure 40g of Jam Accurately</h2>
<p>Using a digital kitchen scale is the easiest way to measure 40g of jam. Simply place your container on the scale, tare it to zero, then spoon in the jam until you hit 40g. If you don't have a scale, two level tablespoons roughly equal 40g.</p>

<p>40g of jam (1.41 oz) works perfectly for glazing pastries or adding sweetness to air fryer desserts. Try it in our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>jam-filled doughnut recipe</a> for a delicious treat. The precise measurement ensures even distribution and perfect results.</p>

<h2>Common Uses for 40g of Jam</h2>
<p>Beyond air fryer recipes, 40g of jam is ideal for single-serve portions. Use it to top pancakes, mix into yoghurt, or as a filling for thumbprint cookies. Its versatility makes it a kitchen staple for quick and easy meals.</p>

<h3>Storing Leftover Jam</h3>
<p>If you have leftover jam after measuring 40g, store it in an airtight container in the fridge. Proper storage keeps it fresh for weeks, ready for your next air fryer adventure. Always use a clean spoon to prevent contamination.</p>
