<h3>US vs. UK Cup Measurements</h3>  
<p>In the US, a standard cup is 240 ml, while a UK metric cup is 250 ml. This means 635 ml of coconut oil is about 2.68 US cups or 2.54 UK cups. If you're following a recipe from <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk>airfryerrecipe.co.uk</a>, double-check whether it uses UK or US measurements to avoid errors.</p>  

<h2>How to Measure Coconut Oil for Air Frying</h2>  
<p>For best results, use a liquid measuring cup for melted coconut oil. If your oil is solid, spoon it into the cup and level it off. When converting 635 ml of coconut oil to cups, remember that melted and solid oil have the same volume. This tip is handy for recipes like air-fried doughnuts or crispy tofu.</p>
