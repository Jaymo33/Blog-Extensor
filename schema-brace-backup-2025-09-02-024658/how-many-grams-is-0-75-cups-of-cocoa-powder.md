<p>When baking in an air fryer, precision is key. Unlike traditional ovens, air fryers circulate hot air rapidly, which means even slight measurement discrepancies can affect texture and taste. <h3>UK vs. US Cup Measurements</h3>  
<p>It’s worth noting that UK and US cup sizes differ slightly. In the UK, 0.75 cups of cocoa powder is roughly 75 grams, while in the US, it’s closer to 72 grams due to minor volume variations. <h2>How to Measure 0.75 Cups of Cocoa Powder Correctly</h2>  
<p>To measure 0.75 cups of cocoa powder accurately, fluff the powder with a spoon before lightly spooning it into a measuring cup. Avoid tapping or packing the cup, as this compacts the powder and leads to excess. Level off the top with a straight edge for precision. For best results, use a kitchen scale to confirm 75 grams.</p>

<h2>Tips for Storing Cocoa Powder</h2>  
<p>To maintain freshness, store cocoa powder in an airtight container in a cool, dark place. Humidity can cause clumping, which affects measurement accuracy.
