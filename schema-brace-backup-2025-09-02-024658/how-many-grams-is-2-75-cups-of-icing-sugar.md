<h2>Why Accurate Measurements Matter in Air Fryer Baking</h2>
<p>Air fryers cook food quickly and evenly, but inaccurate measurements can lead to dry or undercooked results. <h3>How to Measure Icing Sugar Correctly</h3>
<p>To measure 2.75 cups of icing sugar accurately, fluff the sugar first to avoid compacting it. Spoon it lightly into a measuring cup and level it off with a knife. Alternatively, use a kitchen scale for 330 grams, which is far more reliable. This method is especially useful for air fryer desserts like <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/cakes>cakes</a> or cookies.</p>

<h3>Common Mistakes When Measuring Icing Sugar</h3>
<p>One big mistake is packing icing sugar into the cup, which leads to over-measuring. Another is using a liquid measuring cup instead of a dry one. Both can throw off your recipe, especially in air fryer baking where balance is crucial. Always use the right tools and techniques for the best results.</p>
