<h3>How to Measure 250g of Almond Milk Accurately</h3>  
<p>To measure 250g of almond milk, use a digital kitchen scale for the most precise results. Place your measuring jug on the scale, tare it to zero, then pour the almond milk until it reaches 250g. If you don’t have a scale, 250g is roughly 250ml, as almond milk has a similar density to water. However, for baking or air frying, a scale is always best.
