<h3>Tips for measuring cream cheese accurately</h3>  
<p>For best results, use a kitchen scale to weigh cream cheese directly. If you don’t have one, spoon the cream cheese into a measuring cup and level it off. Avoid packing it down, as this can lead to over-measuring. Softened cream cheese is easier to work with, especially for air fryer recipes like <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/dessert>desserts</a> or stuffed dishes.</p>  

<h3>Common cream cheese measurement mistakes</h3>  
<p>One frequent error is confusing fluid ounces with weight ounces. Cream cheese is measured by weight, not volume. Another pitfall is using whipped cream cheese interchangeably with block cream cheese without adjusting quantities. Always check your recipe notes and use the right type for consistent results in your air fryer creations.</p>
