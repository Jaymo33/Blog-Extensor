<h2>Why accurate measurements matter in air fryer cooking</h2>  
<p>Air fryers rely on even cooking, and incorrect ingredient quantities can throw off your dish. Too much minced onion might overpower the flavour, while too little could leave it bland. Using grams instead of tablespoons ensures precision, especially for dry ingredients like minced onion. <h3>How to measure minced onion without a scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a scale, lightly spoon the minced onion into a tablespoon without packing it down. Level it off with a knife for an approximate measure. Remember, 0.75 tbsp is roughly three-quarters of a standard UK tablespoon. For best results, consider investing in a scale—it’s a game-changer for air fryer cooking.</p>
