<h2>How to measure 25g of porridge oats</h2>
<p>Measuring 25g of porridge oats accurately is simple with the right tools. A digital kitchen scale is the most precise method, but if you don't have one, you can use measuring cups. One tablespoon of porridge oats typically weighs around 5g, so 25g would be roughly 5 tablespoons. However, for air fryer recipes, using a scale is recommended for best results.</p>
<p>If you're converting 25g of porridge oats to ounces for a recipe, remember that 1 ounce is approximately 28.35g. This means 25g is just under 1 ounce.
