<h3>How to measure quinoa correctly</h3>  
<p>To measure 2.5 cups of quinoa in ounces, use a kitchen scale for precision. Scoop the quinoa into a measuring cup and level it off for consistency. Avoid packing the quinoa down, as this can lead to inaccurate measurements. If you don’t have a scale, remember that 1 cup equals roughly 6 ounces. This method ensures your air fryer dishes turn out perfectly every time.</p>

<h3>Tips for cooking quinoa in an air fryer</h3>  
<p>Cooking quinoa in an air fryer requires a bit of technique. Rinse the quinoa thoroughly to remove any bitterness before measuring. Use a light spray of oil to prevent sticking and ensure even cooking. Cook at 180°C for 12-15 minutes, stirring occasionally for best results. This method yields fluffy, perfectly cooked quinoa every time.</p>
