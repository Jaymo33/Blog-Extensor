<h3>How to measure 40g of double cream without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have kitchen scales, you can still measure 40g of double cream (1.41 oz) using tablespoons. One tablespoon of double cream weighs roughly 15g, so 2.5 tablespoons will give you close to 40g. This method isn’t as precise as weighing, but it works in a pinch. For best results, especially in baking, we recommend investing in a digital scale for accuracy.</p>
