<h2>How to Measure 2.25 Cups of Rice Correctly</h2>
<p>To measure 2.25 cups of rice accurately, use a dry measuring cup and level it off with a knife. Scooping rice directly from the bag can compress it, leading to more grams than intended. For air fryer recipes, consistency is vital—whether you’re making fried rice or rice pudding. A kitchen scale is the best tool for verifying 2.25 cups of rice in grams.</p>
<p>If you don’t have a scale, fluff the rice with a fork before measuring to avoid compaction. This ensures your 2.25 cups of rice conversion to grams stays consistent.
