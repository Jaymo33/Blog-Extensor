<p>1.25 cups of apple cider vinegar equals 10 fluid ounces (fl oz). This conversion is essential for air fryer recipes where precise measurements ensure perfect results. Whether you’re marinating chicken or whipping up a tangy glaze, knowing how to convert cups to ounces is a game-changer. At <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/>airfryerrecipe.co.uk</a>, we focus on making cooking simpler with accurate conversions and delicious recipes.</p>  

<h2>Why Accurate Measurements Matter in Air Fryer Cooking</h2>  
<p>Air fryers rely on precise ingredient ratios to achieve crispy, evenly cooked dishes. Too much or too little liquid, like apple cider vinegar, can affect texture and flavour. For example, a marinade with 1.25 cups of apple cider vinegar (10 fl oz) ensures balanced acidity for tender meats. Check out our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>air fryer recipes</a> for dishes that use vinegar perfectly.</p>  

<p>Measuring liquids correctly also prevents spills and uneven cooking. Unlike dry ingredients, liquids expand when heated, so exact amounts matter. A digital scale or liquid measuring cup helps avoid guesswork. If you’re converting 1.25 cups of apple cider vinegar to ounces, remember it’s always 10 fl oz in the UK and US.</p>  

<h3>How to Measure Apple Cider Vinegar for Air Fryer Recipes</h3>  
<p>Use a clear liquid measuring cup for accuracy. Pour the apple cider vinegar to the 1.25-cup mark or weigh 10 fl oz on a scale. For sticky liquids like vinegar, lightly grease the measuring cup to ensure all the liquid pours out. This trick is handy for recipes like our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/chicken>air fryer chicken dishes</a>.</p>  

<h2>Common Uses for Apple Cider Vinegar in Air Fryers</h2>  
<p>Apple cider vinegar adds tangy depth to marinades, sauces, and even baked goods. In an air fryer, it helps tenderise meats and balance rich flavours. For instance, 1.25 cups (10 fl oz) of vinegar works wonders in a pulled pork marinade. Explore our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-conversion-chart>conversion chart</a> for more measurement tips.</p>  

<p>It’s also great for deglazing or adding a punch to dressings. Just 1.25 cups of apple cider vinegar (10 fl oz) can transform a simple salad into a zesty side dish. For cleaning your air fryer, diluted vinegar works too—visit our guide on <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-cleaner>air fryer cleaning</a> for details.</p>  

<h3>Converting Other Vinegar Amounts to Ounces</h3>  
<p>Need to convert different quantities? Here’s a quick reference: 1 cup of apple cider vinegar equals 8 fl oz, so 1.25 cups is 10 fl oz. For smaller amounts, ½ cup is 4 fl oz.
