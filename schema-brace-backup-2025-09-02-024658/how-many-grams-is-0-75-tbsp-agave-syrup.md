<h3>How to measure agave syrup accurately</h3>
<p>To measure 0.75 tbsp of agave syrup, use a digital kitchen scale for the most precise results. Simply tare your scale with the container on it, then pour the syrup until it reaches 15 grams. If you don’t have a scale, a measuring spoon can work, but be aware that agave syrup’s sticky consistency can lead to slight variations. For sticky ingredients like agave, lightly greasing the spoon can help it slide off more easily, ensuring you get the full 0.75 tbsp.</p>
