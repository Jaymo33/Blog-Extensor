<h3>How to Measure 435 ml of Water Without a Measuring Cup</h3>
<p>If you don’t have a measuring cup handy, you can still measure 435 ml of water accurately. A standard UK metric cup holds 250 ml, so 435 ml is roughly 1 and 3/4 cups. Alternatively, use a kitchen scale—1 ml of water weighs 1 gram, so 435 ml equals 435 grams. This method is particularly useful for air fryer recipes where precision is key.</p>

<h3>UK vs. US Cup Measurements</h3>
<p>It’s worth noting that a UK cup (250 ml) differs slightly from a US cup (240 ml). While 435 ml of water is 1.84 UK cups, it’s approximately 1.81 US cups. For most air fryer recipes, this small difference won’t matter, but for baking, it’s best to stick to one system. Always check where the recipe originates to ensure accuracy.</p>
