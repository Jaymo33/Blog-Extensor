<h3>How to measure 0.25 tbsp plain flour without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don't have digital scales, use the spoon-and-level method. Lightly spoon flour into a tablespoon without packing it down, then level off the excess with a knife. Divide this into quarters for 0.25 tbsp of plain flour UK grams measurement. This method works well for small quantities in air fryer recipes.</p>
