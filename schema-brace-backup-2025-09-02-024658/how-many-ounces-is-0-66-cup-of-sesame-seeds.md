<h3>How to Measure Sesame Seeds Correctly</h3>  
<p>Measuring sesame seeds by volume (cups) can be tricky due to their small size and tendency to settle. For the most accurate results, use a kitchen scale to weigh 0.66 cup of sesame seeds in ounces. If you don't have a scale, fluff the seeds with a fork before lightly spooning them into a measuring cup. Avoid packing them down, as this can lead to an inaccurate measurement.
