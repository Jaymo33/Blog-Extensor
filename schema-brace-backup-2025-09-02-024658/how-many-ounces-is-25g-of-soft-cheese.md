<h3>How to measure 25g of soft cheese without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can still measure 25g of soft cheese using tablespoons. One tablespoon of soft cheese weighs roughly 15g, so 1.5 tablespoons will give you close to 25g. For more precise measurements, invest in a digital scale—it’s a game-changer for air fryer cooking.</p>

<h3>Converting other cheese types from grams to ounces</h3>  
<p>While 25g of soft cheese equals 0.88 ounces, harder cheeses like cheddar have a different density. Always check specific conversions for the cheese you’re using. For example, 25g of grated cheddar is roughly 0.9 ounces due to air gaps between shreds.</p>  

<h2>Tips for storing and using soft cheese in air fryer recipes</h2>
