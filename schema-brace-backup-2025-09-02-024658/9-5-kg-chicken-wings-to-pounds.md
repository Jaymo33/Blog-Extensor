<h2>Tips for cooking 9.5 kg chicken wings in your air fryer</h2>
<p>Cooking a large quantity like 9.5 kg chicken wings (20.94 pounds) in an air fryer requires some strategy. First, always pat the wings dry before seasoning to ensure maximum crispiness. Since air fryers have limited capacity, plan to cook in batches to avoid overcrowding. Each batch should take about 20-25 minutes at 200°C, depending on the size of the wings. Shake the basket halfway through for even cooking. For sauced wings, add sauce after cooking to prevent burning and stickiness. These tips will help you achieve restaurant-quality wings at home, with less grease and more flavour.</p>

<p>Meal prepping with 9.5 kg chicken wings converted to pounds is a great way to save time during the week.
