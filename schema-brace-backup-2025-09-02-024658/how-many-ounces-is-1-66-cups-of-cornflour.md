<h3>How to Measure 1.66 Cups of Cornflour Correctly</h3>  
<p>Use a dry measuring cup and level off the excess with a straight edge for accuracy. Scooping directly from the bag can compress the cornflour, leading to an incorrect weight. If you don’t have scales, remember that 1.66 cups of cornflour is roughly 7.5 ounces. This method works for both UK and US measurements.</p>
