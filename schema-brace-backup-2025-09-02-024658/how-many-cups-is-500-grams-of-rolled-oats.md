<h3>How to Measure Rolled Oats Correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate 500 grams of rolled oats in cups, use the spoon-and-level method. Gently spoon oats into a measuring cup without packing them down, then level off the top with a knife. Avoid tapping the cup, as this compresses the oats and skews measurements. This technique ensures your air fryer recipes, like oat-based muffins or granola clusters, turn out perfectly every time.</p>
