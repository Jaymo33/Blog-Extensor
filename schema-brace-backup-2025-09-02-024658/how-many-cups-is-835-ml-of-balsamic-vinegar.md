<h3>How to Measure 835 ml of Balsamic Vinegar Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a scale, use a liquid measuring cup marked in millilitres and cups. Pour the balsamic vinegar slowly to avoid spills and check at eye level. For 835 ml, fill the cup to the 3.5-cup mark, or use three full cups plus half another. This method works perfectly for marinades and sauces in your air fryer.</p>
