<p>Air fryers rely on hot air circulation to cook food evenly, and the right amount of oil is crucial. Too much oil can lead to soggy results, while too little may cause dryness. <h3>How to measure 0.5 cup of sunflower oil accurately</h3>  
<p>Using a liquid measuring cup is the easiest way to measure 0.5 cup of sunflower oil. Pour the oil slowly to the 0.5 cup mark, then check at eye level for precision. If you prefer weighing, 4 ounces (113g) is your target. A kitchen scale ensures accuracy, especially for recipes requiring exact proportions.</p>
