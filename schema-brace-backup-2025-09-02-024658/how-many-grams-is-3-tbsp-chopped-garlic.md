<h3>How to measure chopped garlic correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate 3 tbsp chopped garlic in grams conversion, use a kitchen scale. Spoon the garlic lightly into the tablespoon without packing it down. Level off the top with a knife for an even measurement. If you don't have scales, remember that 1 tbsp of chopped garlic weighs about 15 grams.</p>

<h2>Storing chopped garlic</h2>  
<p>If you've measured out 3 tbsp chopped garlic weight in grams but don't need it all, proper storage maintains freshness.
