<h2>Why Accurate Measurements Matter in Air Fryer Cooking</h2>  
<p>Air fryers rely on even cooking, and incorrect measurements can lead to overly salty or bland dishes. Fish sauce, in particular, is potent, so 0.25 cup (2 ounces) must be measured carefully. Too much can overpower your meal, while too little might leave it lacking depth. For best results, use a kitchen scale to weigh 0.25 cup of fish sauce if precision is crucial. <h3>How to Measure 0.25 Cup of Fish Sauce Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, use a liquid measuring cup for 0.25 cup of fish sauce. Fill it to the quarter-cup mark, ensuring the meniscus (the curve at the liquid’s surface) aligns with the measurement line. Alternatively, 4 tablespoons equal 0.25 cup, so you can use a tablespoon measure for smaller batches. This method is handy when preparing sauces or dressings for air-fried dishes like <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/chicken>air fryer chicken recipes</a>.</p>  

<h2>Common Uses for 0.25 Cup of Fish Sauce in Air Fryer Recipes</h2>  
<p>Fish sauce adds umami depth to marinades, glazes, and dipping sauces. A 0.25 cup (2-ounce) portion is perfect for coating proteins like chicken, shrimp, or tofu before air frying. For example, mix it with garlic, lime, and honey for a quick marinade. You can also dilute it with water or citrus juice for a lighter dipping sauce. Explore our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/shrimp>shrimp recipes</a> for ideas on incorporating fish sauce into your meals.</p>  

<p>Another great use for 0.25 cup of fish sauce is in stir-fry-style air fryer dishes. Toss vegetables or proteins with a small amount for an instant flavour boost. Since air fryers cook quickly, the sauce’s intensity remains balanced. For easy cleanup after cooking, check out our guide on <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-cleaner>air fryer cleaner</a> solutions to keep your appliance in top shape.</p>  

<h3>Storing Leftover Fish Sauce</h3>  
<p>Once opened, fish sauce lasts up to a year in the fridge. If you’ve measured out 0.25 cup (2 ounces) and have leftovers, store them in an airtight container. This ensures the sauce retains its flavour for future air fryer recipes. Glass jars or squeeze bottles work well for easy pouring when you need just a small amount.</p>
