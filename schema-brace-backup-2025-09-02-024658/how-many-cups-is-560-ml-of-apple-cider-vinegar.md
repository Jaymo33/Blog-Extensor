<h3>How to Measure 560 ml of Apple Cider Vinegar Without a Scale</h3>
<p>If you don’t have a measuring jug, use a standard cup (240 ml) to portion out 560 ml of apple cider vinegar. Fill it twice, then add another third of a cup to hit roughly 2.37 cups. For sticky liquids like vinegar, lightly grease the cup first for easier pouring. This method works perfectly for marinades or dressings in your air fryer.</p>
