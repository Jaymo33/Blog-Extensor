<h3>How to measure wheatgerm for air fryer recipes</h3>  
<p>To measure 0.66 cup of wheatgerm accurately, use a dry measuring cup and level it off with a straight edge. If you don’t have a measuring cup, a kitchen scale set to ounces (2.64 oz) works perfectly. This method is especially useful for recipes like air-fried falafel or breaded fish fillets, where consistency is key.</p>
