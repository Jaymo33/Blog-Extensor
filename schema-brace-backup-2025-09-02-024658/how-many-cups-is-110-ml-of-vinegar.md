<h3>How to Measure 110 ml of Vinegar Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a measuring jug, you can still measure 110 ml of vinegar using common kitchen tools. A standard US cup holds 240 ml, so 110 ml is just under half a cup. For UK cooks, a metric cup is 250 ml, making 110 ml roughly 0.44 cups. Check out our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-conversion-chart>air fryer conversion chart</a> for more handy tips.</p>

<h3>Converting 110 ml of Vinegar in Different Cup Systems</h3>  
<p>The exact cup equivalent of 110 ml of vinegar depends on your country’s measuring system. In the US, 110 ml is about 0.46 cups, while in the UK, it’s closer to 0.44 cups. For air fryer recipes, slight variations won’t ruin a dish, but precision helps. If you’re scaling a recipe, always double-check conversions.</p>
