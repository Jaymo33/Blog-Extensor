<h2>How to Measure 0.75 Cup of Cottage Cheese Correctly</h2>  
<p>To measure 0.75 cup of cottage cheese accurately, use a liquid measuring cup for consistency. Spoon the cottage cheese gently into the cup without packing it down, then level it off. This method ensures you get the correct 6-ounce weight. If you're using a kitchen scale, tare it to zero before measuring for precision.</p>  

<p>For UK cooks, 0.75 cup of cottage cheese converts to roughly 170 grams or 6 ounces. This measurement is particularly useful for <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/breakfast>breakfast recipes</a> like air fryer pancakes or savoury muffins. Always double-check your conversions to avoid recipe mishaps.</p>

<h3>Storing Leftover Cottage Cheese</h3>  
<p>If you have leftover cottage cheese after measuring 0.75 cup, store it in an airtight container in the fridge for up to five days. For longer storage, freeze it in portioned bags—though the texture may change slightly. Always use clean utensils to prevent contamination and keep your air fryer ingredients fresh.</p>
