<h3>How to Measure Lime Juice Correctly</h3>
<p>To measure 2.0 cups of lime juice accurately, use a liquid measuring cup with clear markings. Fill it to the 2-cup line, ensuring the juice is level for an exact measurement. If you’re converting to ml, remember that 1 cup equals 236.6 ml, so 2.0 cups is roughly 473 ml. This method works whether you’re using fresh-squeezed or bottled lime juice, though fresh always delivers the best flavour.</p>
