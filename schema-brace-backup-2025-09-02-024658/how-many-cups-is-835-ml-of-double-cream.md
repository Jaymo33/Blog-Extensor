<h2>Understanding Double Cream Measurements</h2>  
<p>Double cream is a rich, thick dairy product commonly used in desserts, sauces, and baking. Its density means that volume conversions, like ml to cups, can sometimes be tricky. For accuracy, it's best to use a measuring jug for liquids or a kitchen scale for weight. If you're working with 835 ml of double cream, you'll need just over 3.5 cups to match the volume.</p>  

<p>Different countries use varying cup sizes, so it's essential to clarify whether you're using a UK or US cup. A UK cup holds 284 ml, while a US cup is slightly smaller at 240 ml. For 835 ml of double cream, that's about 2.94 UK cups or 3.48 US cups.
