<h2>Why measure Nutella in ounces?</h2>  
<p>Measuring Nutella by weight rather than volume ensures consistency in your air fryer recipes. A cup of Nutella can vary slightly in weight due to settling or temperature, but ounces provide an exact measurement. This is particularly useful for recipes like Nutella-filled croissants or chocolate lava cakes, where too much or too little filling affects the outcome. <h3>How to measure Nutella accurately</h3>  
<p>Using a kitchen scale is the best way to measure Nutella in ounces. Simply place your container on the scale, tare it to zero, and scoop out Nutella until you reach 3 ounces for 0.375 cup. If you don’t have a scale, a measuring cup works, but be sure to level it off for the closest approximation. For more tips on perfect measurements, explore our <a href="https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/dessert">dessert category</a>.</p>
