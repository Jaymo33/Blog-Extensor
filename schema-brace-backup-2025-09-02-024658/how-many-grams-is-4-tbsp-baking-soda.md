<h3>How to measure 4 tbsp baking soda accurately</h3>  
<p>For the most precise 4 tbsp baking soda to grams conversion, use digital kitchen scales. Scoop the baking soda lightly into your tablespoon measure without packing it down. Level off the top with a straight edge for consistent results every time. This method ensures your 4 tablespoon baking soda UK grams measurement is spot-on.</p>

<p>Beyond baking, 4 tablespoons (48g) of baking soda has multiple air fryer applications. <h2>Tips for working with baking soda in air fryers</h2>  
<p>When using 4 tablespoons of baking soda in grams for air frying, remember it's a powerful leavener. Always mix it evenly with dry ingredients to prevent uneven rising. For best results in <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/cookies>air fryer cookies</a>, combine it with an acid like cream of tartar for optimal lift.</p>
