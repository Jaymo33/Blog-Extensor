<h3>Common Mistakes When Measuring Cottage Cheese</h3>  
<p>One mistake is draining the liquid before measuring, which can lead to inaccuracies. For 25g of cottage cheese, measure it as-is, including the whey. Another error is packing it into measuring spoons, which adds excess weight. For best results, spoon it lightly and level it off.</p>
