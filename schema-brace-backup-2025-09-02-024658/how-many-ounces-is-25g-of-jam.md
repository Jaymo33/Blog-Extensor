<h2>Why Convert 25g of Jam to Ounces?</h2>  
<p>Many UK recipes, especially those for air fryers, use grams, but some kitchen scales or international recipes might list ingredients in ounces. Converting 25g of jam to ounces ensures accuracy, preventing overly sweet or under-flavoured dishes. <h3>How to Measure 25g of Jam Without Scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a scale, you can still measure 25g of jam using tablespoons. One tablespoon of jam is roughly 20g, so 25g is just over a tablespoon. For sticky ingredients like jam, lightly greasing the spoon helps it slide off easily. This method is great for quick fixes when baking in your air fryer.</p>  

<h2>Common Uses for 25g of Jam in Air Fryer Recipes</h2>  
<p>25g of jam is perfect for small-batch air fryer desserts or glazes. Try it in thumbprint cookies, where each dollop needs to be just right. You could also mix it into a marinade for chicken or brush it over puff pastry for a quick turnover. For more inspiration, browse our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/dessert>dessert recipes</a>.</p>  

<p>Another great use is in air fryer pastries—spreading 25g of jam evenly ensures the filling isn’t too runny or sparse. If you're making a jam swirl in cake batter, this measurement helps maintain balance. Always store leftover jam properly to keep it fresh for future <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk>airfryerrecipe.co.uk</a> creations.</p>  

<h3>Does Jam Weight Vary by Type?</h3>  
<p>Yes, thicker jams like marmalade may weigh slightly more per volume than runny jams. However, 25g of any jam will still be close to 0.88 ounces. For recipes where consistency matters, like air fryer glazes, consider straining runny jams to avoid excess liquid.</p>  

<h2>Tips for Working with Jam in Air Fryers</h2>  
<p>When using jam in air fryer recipes, avoid overfilling to prevent spills and sticky messes. A light coating of oil on baking paper helps release jammy treats easily. For easy cleanup, try our recommended <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-cleaner>air fryer cleaner</a> to tackle any sticky residue.</p>  

<p>If your recipe calls for heating jam, do so in short bursts to prevent burning. Air fryers cook quickly, so monitor dishes like jam-filled pastries closely. Experiment with different jam flavours—25g of raspberry, strawberry, or apricot jam can transform simple recipes into something special.</p>
