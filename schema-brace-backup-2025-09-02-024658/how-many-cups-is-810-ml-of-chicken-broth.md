<h2>Understanding the Conversion</h2>  
<p>Converting millilitres to cups can be tricky, especially when recipes call for specific amounts like 810 ml of chicken broth. In the US, 1 cup is 240 ml, while in the UK, it's 250 ml. This means 810 ml translates to roughly 3.42 US cups or 3.5 UK cups. Knowing this helps when following recipes from different regions or adjusting portion sizes for your air fryer meals.</p>  

<p>If you're using an air fryer, accurate liquid measurements ensure even cooking and prevent spills. Too much broth can make dishes soggy, while too little might dry them out. <p>To measure 810 ml of chicken broth correctly, use a liquid measuring cup with clear markings. A kitchen scale can also help, as 810 ml of chicken broth weighs roughly 810 grams.
