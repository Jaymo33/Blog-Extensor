<p>Using the right amount of sesame oil can elevate your air fryer dishes from good to great. Too little oil, and your food might dry out; too much, and it could become greasy. <h3>How to Measure Sesame Oil for Air Fryer Recipes</h3>  
<p>Measuring liquids like sesame oil can be tricky, but using a kitchen scale ensures accuracy. If you don’t have one, remember that 1 cup of sesame oil weighs about 8 ounces, so 1.66 cups is roughly 13.28 ounces. This precision is key for dishes like air-fried tempura or glazed salmon.</p>
