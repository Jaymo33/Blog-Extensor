<h2>How to measure 125g of vinegar without scales</h2>  
<p>Not everyone has a kitchen scale handy, but don’t worry—there are other ways to measure 125g of vinegar. Since 1 fluid ounce of vinegar weighs roughly 28.35g, 125g translates to about 4.41 fluid ounces. A standard UK measuring cup can help you approximate this amount. For more precision, use a tablespoon: 15ml (or 0.5 oz) of vinegar equals about 14.7g, so 8.5 tablespoons will get you close to 125g.</p>

Whether you’re converting 125g of apple cider vinegar to ounces or another variety, the 4.41 oz measurement holds true. This consistency makes it easy to swap vinegars without recalculating weights.</p>
