<h3>How to Measure 0.5 Cup of Buttermilk Correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate 0.5 cup buttermilk to ounces conversion, use a liquid measuring cup on a flat surface. Pour buttermilk to the 0.5 cup line, checking at eye level. If your recipe lists ounces, 4 oz is the exact equivalent. For thicker batters, like those in our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/cakes>air fryer cakes</a>, precision ensures even cooking.</p>

If a recipe calls for millilitres, 0.5 cup converts to roughly 120 ml.
