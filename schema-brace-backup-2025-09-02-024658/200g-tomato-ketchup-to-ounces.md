<h3>How to measure 200g of tomato ketchup without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can still measure 200g of tomato ketchup. One tablespoon holds about 15g, so 200g is roughly 13 tablespoons. For ounces, this translates to just over 7 ounces. Keep in mind that ketchup’s thickness can vary, so adjust as needed.
