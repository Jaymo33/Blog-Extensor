<h3>How to Measure Yogurt Correctly</h3>
<p>To measure 2.25 cups of yogurt in ml, use a liquid measuring jug for the most accurate results. Scoop the yogurt gently to avoid trapping air, which can skew the measurement. For sticky yogurts like Greek yogurt, a spatula helps level it off. Remember, 1 US cup equals 237 ml, so 2.25 cups is 532.5 ml. UK cups differ slightly (284 ml per cup), so adjust if your recipe specifies.</p>
