<h3>How to Measure Apple Cider Vinegar Correctly</h3>  
<p>Use a liquid measuring cup for accuracy, filling it to the 1.75-cup mark at eye level. For smaller quantities, a kitchen scale set to ounces can also work—14 fl oz of apple cider vinegar weighs roughly 414 grams. Avoid using dry measuring cups, as they won’t give you the precise volume needed for liquids like vinegar. Proper tools prevent recipe mishaps and wasted ingredients.</p>
