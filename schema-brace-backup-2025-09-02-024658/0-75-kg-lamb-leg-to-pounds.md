<h2>How to Convert 0.75 kg Lamb Leg to Pounds and Ounces</h2>
<p>To convert 0.75 kg lamb leg to pounds, you multiply the kilogram value by 2.20462, giving you roughly 1.65 pounds. If you prefer more precision or need the weight in pounds and ounces, you can break it down further. Since there are 16 ounces in a pound, 0.65 of a pound is about 10.4 ounces, so 0.75 kg is approximately 1 pound and 10.4 ounces. This level of detail can be useful for very precise recipes or when using a digital kitchen scale that switches between units. Remember, accuracy matters most for perfect air frying results.</p>

<h2>Tips for Cooking a 0.75 kg Lamb Leg in Your Air Fryer</h2>
<p>Cooking a 0.75 kg lamb leg in your air fryer is a fantastic way to enjoy a roast without heating up the whole oven. First, ensure your lamb is at room temperature for even cooking—take it out of the fridge 30 minutes beforehand. Pat it dry with kitchen paper and season generously with salt, pepper, rosemary, and garlic for a classic flavour. Preheat your air fryer to 180°C to get a good sear on the outside, which locks in the juices. Place the lamb in the basket or on the rack, making sure there's space for air circulation around it.</p>
<p>Cook for roughly 20-25 minutes for medium-rare, turning halfway through for an even colour. Remember, air fryers can vary, so start checking a few minutes early to avoid overcooking. Let the lamb rest for 5-10 minutes after cooking to allow the juices to redistribute, resulting in a more tender eat. This method saves time and energy compared to traditional roasting, and you'll still get a crispy exterior and moist interior.
