<h3>How to Measure 760 ml of Tomato Sauce Without a Measuring Cup</h3>
<p>If you don’t have a measuring cup, you can use a standard mug or glass. A typical UK mug holds about 250 ml, so 760 ml would be roughly three mugs plus a little extra. For US measurements, remember that 1 cup is 240 ml, making 760 ml just over 3 cups. Always level off the sauce for the most accurate results. This method works well for quick adjustments mid-recipe.</p>
