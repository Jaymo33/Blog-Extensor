<h2>How to Measure 60 ml of Beef Stock Without a Measuring Cup</h2>
<p>Don’t have a measuring cup? No problem. You can use a tablespoon to measure 60 ml of beef stock. Since 1 tablespoon equals 15 ml, you’ll need 4 tablespoons to reach 60 ml. This method is handy for quick adjustments while cooking. Alternatively, a standard UK metric cup holds 250 ml, so 60 ml is just under a quarter of that.</p>
