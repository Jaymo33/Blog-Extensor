<h2>How to measure agave syrup accurately</h2>
<p>Measuring sticky ingredients like agave syrup can be tricky, but a few simple tips can help. First, lightly coat your measuring cup with oil to prevent the syrup from sticking. This ensures you get the full 0.33 cup (2.64 ounces) without waste. Alternatively, use a kitchen scale for the most precise measurement, especially for recipes where balance is key.</p>

<h3>Using a kitchen scale for precision</h3>
<p>A digital kitchen scale is the best tool for measuring agave syrup by weight. Simply place your container on the scale, tare it to zero, and pour the syrup until you reach 2.64 ounces. This method eliminates the guesswork and ensures your air fryer recipes turn out perfectly every time.
