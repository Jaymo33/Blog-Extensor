<h3>How to Measure Maple Syrup Correctly</h3>  
<p>To measure 1.0 cups of maple syrup accurately, use a liquid measuring cup with clear ml markings. Pour the syrup slowly to avoid air bubbles, and check the measurement at eye level. If you don’t have a measuring cup, a kitchen scale can help—1.0 cups of maple syrup weighs roughly 312 grams, which converts to 240 ml. This method is especially useful for sticky ingredients like maple syrup.</p>
