<h3>Storing rice flour for consistent measurements</h3>  
<p>Keep rice flour in an airtight container to prevent moisture absorption, which can affect the weight of your 0.125 cup measurement. Humidity can make flour compact, meaning you’d get more than 1 ounce in your 0.125 cup scoop. For best results, store it in a cool, dry place and give it a stir before measuring.</p>
