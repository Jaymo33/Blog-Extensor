<h2>How to measure 0.66 cup of plain flour</h2>
<p>For the most accurate 0.66 cup measurement (2.93 oz), use proper measuring techniques. First, fluff your flour with a fork to aerate it, then gently spoon it into your measuring cup. Level off the excess with a straight edge for perfect 0.66 cup portions every time.</p>
