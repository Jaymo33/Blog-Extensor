<h3>How to measure 1000g of onion without scales</h3>
<p>If you don’t have kitchen scales, you can estimate 1000g of onion by counting. Typically, one medium onion weighs about 150g, so you’d need roughly 6-7 medium onions to reach 1000g. This equals about 35.27 ounces. Remember that onion sizes can vary, so this method isn’t as precise as using scales. For best results in your air fryer, we recommend investing in a good set of digital kitchen scales.</p>
