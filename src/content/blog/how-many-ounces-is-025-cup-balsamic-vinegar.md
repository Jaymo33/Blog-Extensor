<h2>How to measure 0.25 cup of balsamic vinegar accurately</h2>
<p>For the most precise measurement of your 0.25 cup (2 oz) balsamic vinegar, use a liquid measuring cup with clear markings. Place it on a level surface and pour until the meniscus reaches the ¼ cup line. Alternatively, a kitchen scale set to ounces will give you the exact 2 oz weight. This method eliminates any guesswork, especially important for delicate <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/dessert>air fryer desserts</a> where balance is key.</p>
