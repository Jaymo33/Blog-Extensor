<h2>Why Convert 500g of Baking Soda to Ounces?</h2>  
<p>Baking soda is a crucial ingredient in many air fryer recipes, from crispy fried chicken to light, airy cakes. The difference between grams and ounces might seem small, but it can affect your dish’s outcome. For example, too much baking soda can leave a bitter taste, while too little might not give the desired rise. Using 17.64 oz (500g) ensures balanced results every time.</p>
