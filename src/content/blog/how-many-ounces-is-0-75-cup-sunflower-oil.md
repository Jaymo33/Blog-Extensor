<h3>How to measure 0.75 cup of sunflower oil accurately</h3>  
<p>For the most precise measurement, use a liquid measuring cup marked in ounces or millilitres. Fill it to the 0.75 cup line, or weigh it on a kitchen scale set to ounces—it should read close to 6 oz. If you don’t have a scale, remember that 1 cup of sunflower oil is roughly 8 ounces, so 0.75 cup is three-quarters of that.</p>

<h2>Tips for storing and reusing sunflower oil in air frying</h2>  
<p>After measuring out 0.75 cup of sunflower oil for your air fryer, store any leftover oil in a cool, dark place to maintain freshness.
