<h3>How to Measure 500g of BBQ Sauce Without Scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have kitchen scales, you can estimate 500g of BBQ sauce (17.64 ounces) using measuring cups. One cup of BBQ sauce weighs roughly 250g, so two cups will give you close to 500g. For more precision, use fluid ounces: 17.64 oz is just over 2 cups.
