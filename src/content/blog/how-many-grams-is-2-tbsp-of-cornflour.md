<h3>Converting other cornflour quantities</h3>  
<p>Need to scale up? 1 UK tablespoon of cornflour equals 8 grams, while a US tablespoon is slightly less at 7.5 grams. For larger batches, remember that 100 grams of cornflour equals about 12.5 UK tablespoons. Always check recipe origins, as US and UK tablespoon volumes differ.</p>
