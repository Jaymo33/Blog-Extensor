<h2>How to measure 0.25 tbsp of greek yogurt accurately</h2>
<p>Measuring small quantities like 0.25 tbsp can be tricky without the right tools. For the most accurate measurement, use a digital kitchen scale set to grams. If you don't have one, fill a tablespoon with greek yogurt and divide it visually into four equal parts. Remember that greek yogurt's thickness means it won't level as easily as liquids, so take extra care.</p>
<p>For those who frequently cook with greek yogurt, consider pre-measuring common amounts and noting their weights.
