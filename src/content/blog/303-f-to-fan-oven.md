<h2>Temperature Conversion Tips</h2>
<p>Remember that 303 F to fan oven conversion (150 C) is approximate. Ovens may vary, so use an oven thermometer to verify your appliance's accuracy. This is particularly important for air fryers, as their compact size can affect temperature distribution. Always check food doneness with a meat thermometer for safety.</p>
<p>When converting between Fahrenheit and Celsius for fan ovens, the general rule is to reduce the Celsius temperature by 20 degrees from conventional oven settings. So 303 F (150 C conventional) becomes 130 C in a fan oven. However, our testing shows 150 C fan works best for most <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>air fryer recipes</a> at this temperature range.</p>

<h2>Common Mistakes to Avoid</h2>
<p>One frequent error is forgetting that fan ovens cook faster at lower temperatures. If you use 303 F (150 C) in a fan oven without adjusting cooking times, your food may overcook. Start checking for doneness 5-10 minutes earlier than the recipe suggests. This applies doubly to air fryers, where cooking times are typically shorter anyway.</p>
