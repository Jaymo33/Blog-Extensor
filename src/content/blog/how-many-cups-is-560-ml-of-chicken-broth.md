<h3>How to Measure 560 ml of Chicken Broth</h3>
<p>To measure 560 ml of chicken broth, you can use a liquid measuring jug with metric markings. If your jug only has cup measurements, fill it to the 2.37-cup mark. Alternatively, you can use a standard 250 ml cup and measure a little over two cups (500 ml), then add an additional 60 ml (about 1/4 cup) to reach 560 ml. This method ensures you get the exact amount needed for your recipe.</p>
