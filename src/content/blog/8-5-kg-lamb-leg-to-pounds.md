<h2>How to convert 8.5 kg lamb leg to pounds accurately</h2>
<p>Converting 8.5 kg lamb leg to pounds is straightforward once you know the basic conversion factor. One kilogram is approximately equal to 2.20462 pounds, so to find the weight in pounds, you multiply 8.5 by 2.20462. This calculation gives you 18.73927 pounds, which is typically rounded to 18.74 pounds for practical use. While this might seem precise, it’s perfectly adequate for cooking purposes, as air fryer recipes often allow a small margin for error. If you prefer a quicker method, you can use the rounded conversion of 2.2 pounds per kilogram, which gives 18.7 pounds—still very close and useful for most home cooking scenarios.</p>

<h2>Tips for cooking a large lamb leg in the air fryer</h2>
<p>Cooking an 8.5 kg lamb leg—approximately 18.74 pounds—in your air fryer requires some special considerations to ensure success. First, make sure your air fryer is large enough to hold the lamb leg without overcrowding; if necessary, you can bend or tie the leg to fit more compactly. Preheating the air fryer to around 180°C helps sear the outside and lock in juices, while reducing the temperature to 160°C after the initial sear allows for even cooking throughout. Using a meat thermometer is highly recommended, as it takes the guesswork out of determining doneness and prevents overcooking. Let the lamb rest for at least 15 minutes after cooking to allow the juices to redistribute, resulting in a more tender and flavourful roast.</p>
