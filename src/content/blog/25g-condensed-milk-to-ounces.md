<h3>How to Measure 25g of Condensed Milk</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a scale, you can use tablespoons to measure 25g of condensed milk. One tablespoon holds roughly 20g, so 1.25 tablespoons will give you the equivalent of 25g or 0.88 ounces. For sticky ingredients like condensed milk, lightly greasing the spoon helps with accurate pouring.</p>
