<h2>How to measure gelatin powder correctly</h2>  
<p>When converting 2.5 cups of gelatin powder to ounces, it’s important to use the right tools. Scooping directly from the bag can lead to uneven measurements. Instead, spoon the powder lightly into the measuring cup and level it off with a straight edge. This method ensures you’re not packing too much into the cup, which could throw off your recipe.</p>  

<h3>Using a kitchen scale for precision</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate results, weigh your gelatin powder instead of relying on cups. Place your bowl on the scale, tare it to zero, then add the powder until you reach 20 ounces.
