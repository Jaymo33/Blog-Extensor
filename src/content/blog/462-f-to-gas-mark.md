<p>Many professional recipes specify 462°F for good reason - it's the sweet spot for Maillard reactions (that delicious browning). When using your air fryer at Gas Mark 9 equivalent, you'll notice faster cooking times than traditional ovens. This makes it perfect for weeknight dinners when you need <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>quick recipes</a> without sacrificing quality.</p>
<p>For baking enthusiasts, 462 degrees Fahrenheit to Gas Mark conversion becomes crucial when making laminated doughs or vol-au-vents.
