<h2>Practical Tips for Cooking 2.5 kg Turkey Crown in Air Fryer</h2>
<p>Cooking a 2.5 kg turkey crown in an air fryer requires some specific techniques to achieve the best results. First, ensure your turkey crown is thoroughly pat dried with kitchen paper to help the skin crisp up beautifully during cooking. Season generously with your preferred herbs and spices, rubbing some under the skin for maximum flavor penetration. Preheat your air fryer to 180°C (350°F) for about 3 minutes before adding the turkey crown to ensure even cooking from the start. Place the crown in the basket skin-side up, making sure there's enough space around it for air circulation. Cooking time will typically be around 110-120 minutes for a 2.5 kg turkey crown to pounds conversion of 5.51 pounds.</p>

<p>During the cooking process, it's advisable to check the internal temperature with a meat thermometer to ensure doneness. The thickest part of the crown should reach 75°C (165°F) to be safely cooked through. If the skin is browning too quickly, you can tent it loosely with foil to prevent burning while the interior continues cooking. Let the turkey crown rest for at least 20 minutes after cooking to allow the juices to redistribute throughout the meat. This resting period is crucial for tender, moist turkey that slices beautifully.
