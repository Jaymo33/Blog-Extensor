<h3>How to measure 0.125 cup of powdered milk</h3>  
<p>Measuring small quantities like 0.125 cup can be tricky without the right tools. A standard 1/8 cup measure is ideal, but if you don’t have one, use two tablespoons (since 2 tbsp = 0.125 cup). For even greater accuracy, a kitchen scale will confirm that 0.125 cup of powdered milk weighs roughly 1 ounce. This precision is especially useful for air fryer baking, where exact ratios matter.</p>

<h2>Tips for storing and using powdered milk</h2>  
<p>To keep your powdered milk fresh, store it in an airtight container in a cool, dry place. Once opened, it typically lasts 6-12 months. When using it in air fryer recipes, always sift powdered milk to remove lumps for smoother batters and coatings. However, many air fryer recipes use powdered milk directly for its concentrated flavour and thickening properties.</p>
