<h3>How to Measure 2.75 Cups of Apple Cider Vinegar in ml</h3>  
<p>To measure 651 ml of apple cider vinegar, use a liquid measuring jug with clear millilitre markings. If your jug only shows cups, remember that 1 cup equals roughly 237 ml, so 2.75 cups is 651 ml. For smaller quantities, a tablespoon (15 ml) or teaspoon (5 ml) can help fine-tune the measurement. Precision matters, especially when working with strong flavours like vinegar.</p>
