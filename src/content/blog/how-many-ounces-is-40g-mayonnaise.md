<h3>How to measure 40g of mayonnaise without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can use tablespoons to measure 40g of mayonnaise. One tablespoon holds about 14g, so roughly 2.8 tablespoons equal 40g (1.41 oz).
