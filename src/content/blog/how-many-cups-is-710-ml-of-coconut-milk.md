<h3>How to Measure 710 ml of Coconut Milk Without a Measuring Cup</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a measuring cup handy, you can still measure 710 ml of coconut milk using everyday kitchen tools. A standard UK mug holds about 284 ml, so 2.5 mugs will give you roughly 710 ml. Alternatively, use a tablespoon—15 ml per tablespoon means you’d need around 47 tablespoons. It’s not the most precise method, but it works in a pinch!</p>
