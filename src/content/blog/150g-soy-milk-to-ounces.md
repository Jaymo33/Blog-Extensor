<h2>How to Measure 150g of Soy Milk Accurately</h2>  
<p>Using a digital kitchen scale is the easiest way to measure 150g of soy milk. Set your scale to grams, pour the soy milk until it hits 150g, and you’re done. If your scale only displays ounces, aim for 5.29 oz. For those without scales, a measuring jug with both metric and imperial markings can work, though scales are more precise.</p>
