<h2>How to Measure 3.0 Cups of Chicken Broth in ml</h2>
<p>Measuring 3.0 cups of chicken broth in ml is straightforward with the right tools. A liquid measuring cup with ml markings is ideal, but if you only have a standard cup measure, remember that 1 cup equals about 237 ml. Therefore, 3.0 cups of chicken broth in ml is roughly 710 ml.</p>

<h3>Using a Kitchen Scale for Precision</h3>
<p>For even more accuracy, you can weigh your chicken broth. Since 1 ml of water weighs 1 gram, and chicken broth has a similar density, 710 ml of chicken broth will weigh approximately 710 grams. This method is especially useful for baking or making sauces in your air fryer.</p>
