<h3>How to Measure 275 Grams of Lentils Without Scales</h3>
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can still measure 275 grams of lentils using a standard measuring cup. For more precision, lightly spoon the lentils into the cup and level off the top without packing them down. This method ensures you’re not overestimating the quantity.
