<h3>How to measure mayonnaise accurately</h3>  
<p>Measuring mayonnaise can be tricky due to its thick consistency. For 2.5 cups, spoon the mayonnaise into a measuring cup and level it off with a knife. Alternatively, weigh it on a kitchen scale for precision—20 ounces is your target. If you’re scaling a recipe up or down, remember that 1 cup of mayonnaise equals 8 ounces.</p>
