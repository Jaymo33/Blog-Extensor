<h3>How to measure 50g of olive oil without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have digital scales, you can use volume measurements. One tablespoon of olive oil weighs roughly 14g, so 3.5 tablespoons equal 50g (or 1.76 oz). A standard UK measuring jug will also show fluid ounces, helping you approximate the weight. For best results, invest in a kitchen scale—especially if you frequently use your air fryer.</p>
