<h2>How to measure 0.25 tbsp of quinoa</h2>
<p>Measuring small amounts like 0.25 tbsp of quinoa can be tricky without the right tools. The most accurate method is using digital kitchen scales that measure in grams. Simply scoop your quinoa into the spoon, level it off, and weigh it to confirm you've got about 2.5 grams.</p>
<p>If you don't have scales, visual estimation can work in a pinch. 0.25 tbsp is roughly one quarter of a standard UK tablespoon (about 10ml). For reference, that's slightly less than a teaspoon (5ml) of quinoa. When making <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/vegetables>vegetable quinoa bowls</a> in your air fryer, these small measurements add up to big flavour.</p>

<p>Different quinoa varieties can vary slightly in weight. White quinoa tends to be slightly denser than red or black varieties. While 0.25 tbsp of white quinoa weighs about 2.5 grams, coloured quinoa might be closer to 2.3 grams for the same volume.</p>
