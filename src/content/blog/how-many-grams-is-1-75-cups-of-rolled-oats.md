<h3>How to Measure Rolled Oats Correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate conversion, use a kitchen scale to weigh your oats. Scooping directly from the bag can lead to uneven measurements, as rolled oats vary in density. Fluff the oats lightly before measuring to avoid compacting them. If you don’t have a scale, spoon the oats into your measuring cup and level it off. This method ensures you’re as close to 140 grams as possible for 1.75 cups.</p>
