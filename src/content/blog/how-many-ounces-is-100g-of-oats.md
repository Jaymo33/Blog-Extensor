<h3>How to measure 100g of oats without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have scales, you can use a measuring cup to approximate 100g of oats. One cup of rolled oats typically weighs around 90-100g, so it’s a close match. For steel-cut oats, the weight may vary slightly, so adjust accordingly. Remember, 100g oats to ounces is 3.53 oz, so you can also use a liquid measuring jug for rough estimates.</p>
