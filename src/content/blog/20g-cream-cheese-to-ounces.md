<h3>How to measure 20g of cream cheese without a scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a scale, use tablespoons for a rough estimate. One tablespoon of cream cheese weighs about 15g, so 20g is roughly 1.5 tablespoons. For softer cream cheese, pack it lightly into a measuring spoon. Remember, this method isn’t as precise as weighing, but it works in a pinch for recipes like air fryer bagels or frosting.</p>
