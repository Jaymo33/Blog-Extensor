<h2>Understanding the 8.75 kg Whole Chicken to Pounds Conversion</h2>
<p>Converting 8.75 kg to pounds is straightforward once you know the conversion factor. One kilogram equals approximately 2.20462 pounds, so multiplying 8.75 by this figure gives you the weight in pounds. This calculation is vital for air fryer users because most manufacturer guidelines and recipes use imperial measurements. Getting the weight right means you can set the correct temperature and timing, avoiding undercooked or dried-out chicken. It’s a simple step that makes a huge difference to your cooking success.</p>
