<h2>Understanding the 5.5 kg whole chicken to pounds conversion</h2>
<p>Converting 5.5 kg to pounds is straightforward once you know the basic formula. One kilogram equals approximately 2.20462 pounds, so multiplying 5.5 by this figure gives you the weight in pounds. This means a 5.5 kg whole chicken weighs about 12.125 pounds, which is useful for following American or older British recipes. Many modern air fryer recipes provide weights in both metric and imperial, but it's always good to double-check. <h2>How to convert 5.5 kg whole chicken to pounds and ounces</h2>
<p>If you need more precise measurements, converting 5.5 kg whole chicken to pounds and ounces is simple. <h2>Tips for cooking a 5.5 kg whole chicken in your air fryer</h2>
<p>Cooking a large whole chicken in an air fryer requires some preparation to ensure even cooking. Truss the chicken neatly to help it cook uniformly and prevent limbs from overcooking. Using a drip tray or liner can make cleanup easier and prevent smoke from dripping fat.</p>
