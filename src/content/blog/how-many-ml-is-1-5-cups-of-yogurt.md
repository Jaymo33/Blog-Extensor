<h2>How to Measure 1.5 Cups of Yogurt Correctly</h2>
<p>Measuring yogurt can be tricky due to its thick consistency. To get an accurate 355 ml (1.5 cups), use a liquid measuring jug with clear ml markings. Spoon the yogurt into the jug without packing it down, and level it off with a spatula. For sticky yogurts, lightly grease the measuring cup first to ensure it slides out easily. This method guarantees you’re using the right amount for your air fryer recipes.</p>
