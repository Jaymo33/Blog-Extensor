<h2>How to measure 200g of coconut oil in ounces</h2>
<p>Converting 200g of coconut oil to ounces is straightforward. One gram equals 0.035 ounces, so 200g x 0.035 = 7.05 ounces. If you don’t have a scale, you can use measuring spoons or cups as a rough guide. For example, 7.05 ounces is roughly 14 tablespoons of coconut oil. This method works well for most <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/dessert>dessert recipes</a> or savory dishes.</p>

<h3>Tools for accurate measurement</h3>
<p>A digital kitchen scale is the best tool for measuring 200g of coconut oil in ounces. Scales provide precise readings, eliminating guesswork. If you’re using liquid coconut oil, a measuring jug with ounce markings can also work. For sticky solid coconut oil, lightly grease your measuring spoon or cup to ensure easy release.
