<h2>US vs UK Cup Measurements</h2>  
<p>In the US, 1 cup equals 240 ml, so 460 ml of coconut oil is roughly 1.94 cups. In the UK, where 1 cup is 284 ml, it's about 1.62 cups. This difference matters when following international recipes. If you're using an air fryer, always double-check which measurement system your recipe uses.</p>  

<h3>How to Measure Coconut Oil Properly</h3>  
<p>Coconut oil can be tricky to measure because it changes texture with temperature. For solid oil, pack it into a measuring cup and level it off. For liquid oil, pour it into a liquid measuring cup. Either way, 460 ml of coconut oil will give you consistent results in your air fryer dishes.</p>
