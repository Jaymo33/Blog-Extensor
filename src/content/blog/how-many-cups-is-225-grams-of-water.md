<h2>How to Measure 225 Grams of Water Without Scales</h2>
<p>Not everyone has a kitchen scale, but you can still measure 225 grams of water accurately. One cup of water equals 236.59 grams, so 225 grams is just shy of a full cup. Use a liquid measuring cup and fill it to just below the 1-cup mark. For even more precision, a tablespoon holds about 14.79 grams of water, so 15 tablespoons will get you close to 225 grams. This method works well for air fryer recipes where small adjustments matter.</p>

<h2>Converting 225 Grams of Water for UK and US Measurements</h2>
<p>In the UK, a standard cup measures 250 ml, while in the US, it’s 236.59 ml. This means 225 grams of water is roughly 0.9 UK cups or 0.95 US cups. For air fryer cooking, this small difference usually isn’t critical, but it’s good to know for precision.
