<h2>How to Measure 810 ml of Maple Syrup</h2>
<p>Measuring liquids like maple syrup can be tricky, especially if you don’t have a metric measuring cup. A standard US cup holds 240 ml, while a UK cup holds 284 ml. For 810 ml of maple syrup, you’d need roughly 3.42 US cups or 2.85 UK cups. Always use a liquid measuring cup for accuracy, and pour slowly to avoid spills.</p>
