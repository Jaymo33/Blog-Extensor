<p>Different countries use varying cup sizes, which can lead to confusion. In the UK, a standard metric cup holds 250 ml, while in the US, it's 240 ml. For this conversion, we’ve used the US cup measurement (1.75 cups = 414 ml) since many recipes originate from American sources. If you’re using a UK metric cup, 1.75 cups would be 437.5 ml—so always double-check your recipe’s origin!</p>  

<h3>How to Measure Beef Stock Correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate results, use a liquid measuring jug with clear millilitre markings. Pour the beef stock slowly to avoid spills and read the measurement at eye level. If you don’t have a jug, a kitchen scale can help—1.75 cups of beef stock weighs roughly 414 grams, as 1 ml of stock is about 1 gram.
