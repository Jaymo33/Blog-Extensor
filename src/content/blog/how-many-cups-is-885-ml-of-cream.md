<h2>UK vs. US Cup Measurements</h2>
<p>It's worth noting that cup measurements differ between the UK and the US. In the UK, 1 cup is 250 ml, while in the US, it's 240 ml. This means 885 ml of cream converts to 3.74 cups in the UK and 3.5 cups in the US. Always check which measurement system your recipe uses to avoid mistakes.</p>
