<h3>How to measure 125g of coconut oil without a scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a scale, you can use volume measurements as a rough guide. One tablespoon of coconut oil weighs about 14g, so 125g is roughly 9 tablespoons. However, this method isn’t as precise as weighing, especially for baking.
