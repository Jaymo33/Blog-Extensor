<h3>How to Measure 560 ml of Coconut Milk Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a measuring jug, you can use standard cups to measure 560 ml of coconut milk. One cup equals roughly 236.6 ml, so 560 ml is just over 2 and 1/3 cups. For best results, use a liquid measuring cup with clear markings. This method works for both canned and homemade coconut milk, ensuring your air fryer dishes turn out perfectly.</p>
