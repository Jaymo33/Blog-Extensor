<p>Honey’s thick, sticky consistency makes it tricky to measure, especially when converting between cups and millilitres. <h3>How to Measure Honey Without the Mess</h3>  
<p>Lightly coating your measuring cup or spoon with oil prevents honey from sticking, making it easier to pour. Alternatively, warm the honey slightly to loosen its viscosity, but avoid overheating, as this can alter its flavour. For sticky ingredients like honey, a digital kitchen scale can also be a game-changer—just weigh out 710 grams for 3.0 cups (since honey’s density is close to 1.42 g/ml).</p>
