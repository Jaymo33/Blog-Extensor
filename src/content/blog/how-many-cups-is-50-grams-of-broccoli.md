<h3>How to Measure 50 Grams of Broccoli Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can estimate 50 grams of broccoli using a measuring cup. Lightly packed chopped broccoli fills about half a standard US cup (120ml). For UK cooks, this is roughly 0.42 imperial cups. Remember, florets take up more space than finely chopped pieces, so adjust accordingly.</p>

<h3>Does Fresh vs. Frozen Broccoli Affect the Measurement?</h3>  
<p>Fresh and frozen broccoli have similar cup measurements for 50 grams, but frozen broccoli may release more moisture during cooking. If using frozen, pat it dry before air frying to ensure crispiness. For best results, stick to fresh broccoli when precision is crucial, like in <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/meal-prep>meal prep recipes</a>.</p>
