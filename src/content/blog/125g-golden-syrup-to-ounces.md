<h3>How to Measure 125g of Golden Syrup Accurately</h3>  
<p>Using a digital kitchen scale is the easiest way to measure 125g of golden syrup. If you don’t have one, you can use a measuring spoon, but keep in mind that golden syrup is sticky and dense. One tablespoon of golden syrup weighs roughly 25g, so 125g would be about 5 tablespoons.
