<h2>How to Measure 1000g of Rolled Oats</h2>
<p>Measuring 1000g of rolled oats is straightforward with a digital kitchen scale. Simply place your bowl or container on the scale, tare it to zero, and add the oats until you reach 1000g. If your scale only shows ounces, you'll need to stop at 35.27 ounces.</p>
<p>For those without a scale, 1000g of rolled oats is roughly 10 UK cups. However, we always recommend using a scale for accuracy, especially when preparing <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/breakfast>breakfast recipes</a> in your air fryer. The density of rolled oats can vary, so weight measurements are more reliable than volume.</p>
