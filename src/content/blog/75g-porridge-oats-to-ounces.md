<h2>Common mistakes when measuring 75g porridge oats</h2>
<p>One frequent error is confusing weight (grams/ounces) with volume (cups). While 75g equals 2.65 oz by weight, the volume measurement can vary based on how the oats are packed. Always use weight measurements for the most accurate 75g porridge oats UK oz conversion in your air fryer recipes.</p>
<p>Another pitfall is using different types of oats interchangeably. Rolled oats, quick oats, and steel-cut oats all have slightly different weights per volume. When a recipe specifies 75g of porridge oats to ounces, it typically means traditional rolled oats unless stated otherwise.</p>
