<h3>How to measure 0.25 cup of jam without a scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, use a liquid measuring cup for accuracy. Fill it to the 0.25 cup mark, levelling off the top with a spatula. Alternatively, two tablespoons equal 0.25 cup, making it easy to measure smaller quantities. This method works perfectly for sticky ingredients like jam, ensuring you get the right amount every time.</p>
