<h3>How to Measure 360 ml of Olive Oil Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a measuring jug, you can use standard cups to measure 360 ml of olive oil. One UK metric cup holds 250 ml, so 360 ml is roughly 1 and a half cups plus 1 tablespoon. For US cups, which are 240 ml, it’s about 1.5 cups plus 2 teaspoons. This small difference won’t drastically affect most recipes, but precision matters in baking.
