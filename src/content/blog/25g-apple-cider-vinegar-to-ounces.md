<h3>How to measure 25g of apple cider vinegar without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can still measure 25g of apple cider vinegar using volume. One tablespoon of apple cider vinegar weighs roughly 15g, so 25g is just under two tablespoons. For more precise results, use a measuring jug with fluid ounce markings, as 0.88 ounces is close to 1 fluid ounce.</p>
