<h3>How to measure sugar syrup correctly</h3>  
<p>To measure 0.5 cup of sugar syrup accurately, use a liquid measuring cup with clear markings. Pour the syrup slowly to avoid air bubbles, and level it off at the 0.5 cup line. For sticky syrups, lightly greasing the cup can help it slide out easily. This method ensures you get the correct 4.2-ounce measurement every time.</p>
