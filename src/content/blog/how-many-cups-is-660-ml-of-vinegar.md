<h2>Understanding the Conversion</h2>
<p>Converting millilitres to cups can be tricky because the size of a cup varies between the US and UK. In the US, one cup equals 240 ml, while in the UK, it's 284 ml. This means 660 ml of vinegar translates to roughly 2.79 US cups or 2.32 UK cups. For air fryer recipes, where precision matters, using the right measurement system is key to achieving the desired flavour and texture.</p>

<h3>Measuring Without a Scale</h3>
<p>If you don't have measuring cups or a kitchen scale, you can still measure 660 ml of vinegar using common household items. A standard UK mug holds about 284 ml (roughly one UK cup), so 660 ml would be just over two mugfuls. In the US, where a cup is smaller, you'd need nearly three mugfuls to reach 660 ml. However, for precise baking or pickling, we always recommend using proper measuring tools.</p>
