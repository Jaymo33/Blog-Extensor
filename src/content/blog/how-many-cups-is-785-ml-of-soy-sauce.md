<h3>How to Measure 785 ml of Soy Sauce Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can still measure 785 ml of soy sauce accurately. A standard metric measuring jug is your best friend here. Fill it to the 785 ml mark, then pour the soy sauce into a cup measure to see the equivalent (3.32 cups). For smaller quantities, use tablespoons—1 cup equals 16 tablespoons, so 3.32 cups would be roughly 53 tablespoons.
