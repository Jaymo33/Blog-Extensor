<h2>How to Convert 20g Wholemeal Flour to Ounces</h2>
<p>The conversion from grams to ounces is straightforward once you know the ratio. One gram equals 0.035 ounces, so 20g of wholemeal flour is 20 x 0.035 = 0.705 ounces. This conversion works for all types of flour, but wholemeal flour’s density means it’s best weighed rather than measured by volume.
