<h2>How to Measure 1.0 Cups of Granulated Sugar</h2>
<p>Measuring granulated sugar accurately starts with the right tools. Use a dry measuring cup for cups and a kitchen scale for grams. Scoop the sugar into the cup and level it off with a straight edge for consistency. If you’re using a scale, 1.0 cups of granulated sugar will weigh 200 grams.</p>

<h3>Tips for Measuring Sugar in Air Fryer Recipes</h3>
<p>When measuring 1.0 cups of granulated sugar for air fryer recipes, always use room-temperature sugar for consistency.
