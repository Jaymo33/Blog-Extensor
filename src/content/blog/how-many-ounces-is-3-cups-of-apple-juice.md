<h3>How to measure apple juice for air fryer recipes</h3>  
<p>Use a liquid measuring cup for accuracy when converting 3 cups of apple juice to ounces. Pour the juice to the brim without spilling, and check at eye level. If you're scaling recipes up or down, remember that 1 cup equals 8 fl oz. For sticky ingredients like honey or syrup, lightly grease the cup first—find more tricks in our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-cleaner>cleaning guide</a>.</p>
