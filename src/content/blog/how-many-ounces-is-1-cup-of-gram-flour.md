<h3>How to measure gram flour correctly</h3>
<p>To get the most accurate measurement, spoon the gram flour into your measuring cup and level it off with a knife. Avoid packing it down, as this can lead to over-measuring. For best results, consider using a kitchen scale to weigh your gram flour in ounces for absolute precision.</p>
