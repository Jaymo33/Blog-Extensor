<h2>How to Measure 610 ml of Heavy Cream</h2>
<p>Measuring 610 ml of heavy cream doesn’t have to be complicated. A liquid measuring cup is your best tool for this task. Simply pour the cream into the cup until it reaches the 610 ml mark. If your cup only shows cups, remember that 610 ml is roughly 2.57 cups. For more air fryer-friendly recipes, explore our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>recipes section</a>.</p>

<h3>Using a Kitchen Scale</h3>
<p>If you prefer using a kitchen scale, 610 ml of heavy cream weighs about 630 grams. This method is even more precise, especially for baking. Simply place your container on the scale, tare it, and pour the cream until you hit 630 grams. This ensures your air fryer dishes, like creamy pastas or desserts, turn out perfectly every time.</p>
