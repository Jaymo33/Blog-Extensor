<h2>Why Convert 311 Fan Oven to Conventional Oven?</h2>
<p>Fan ovens circulate hot air for even cooking, while conventional ovens rely on radiant heat. This means a 311°F fan oven cooks faster and more evenly than a conventional oven at the same temperature. If your recipe specifies 311 fan oven and you’re using a conventional oven, you might need to adjust cooking times slightly. For example, a cake that takes 25 minutes in a fan oven could take 30-35 minutes in a conventional oven at 311°F.</p>
