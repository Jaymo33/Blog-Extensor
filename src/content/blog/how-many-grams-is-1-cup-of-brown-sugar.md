<h3>How to Measure Brown Sugar Correctly</h3>  
<p>Packing brown sugar into a measuring cup can vary the weight slightly. For the most accurate 220 grams, lightly spoon the sugar into the cup and level it off. Avoid pressing it down, as this can add extra grams. If you're using a kitchen scale, simply weigh out 220 grams directly. This method is foolproof and ensures consistency across all your air fryer recipes.</p>
