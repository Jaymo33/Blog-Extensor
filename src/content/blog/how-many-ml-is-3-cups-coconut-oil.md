<h3>How to Measure Coconut Oil for Air Fryer Recipes</h3>  
<p>Measuring coconut oil can be tricky due to its solid-to-liquid transition at room temperature. To convert 3.0 cups of coconut oil to ml accurately, melt it first for a liquid measurement. Alternatively, pack solid coconut oil tightly into a measuring cup, then level it off for consistency. This method ensures you’re using the correct 709.76 ml equivalent, whether you’re baking or frying.</p>
