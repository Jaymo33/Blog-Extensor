<h2>How to Measure 0.75 Cups of Corn Syrup Correctly</h2>  
<p>Measuring sticky ingredients like corn syrup can be tricky. To get exactly 0.75 cups of corn syrup in ml (177 ml), use a liquid measuring cup with clear markings. Lightly grease the cup with oil to help the syrup slide out easily. Pour slowly to the 177 ml line, then scrape out any remaining syrup with a spatula.</p>  

<h3>Using a Kitchen Scale for Precision</h3>  
<p>If you prefer weighing ingredients, 0.75 cups of corn syrup weighs roughly 255 grams. This method is especially useful for recipes requiring ultra-precision. A digital kitchen scale eliminates guesswork and ensures your air fryer dishes turn out perfectly every time.</p>
