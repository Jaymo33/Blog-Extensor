<h2>Why converting grams to ounces matters</h2>  
<p>Many UK air fryer recipes use grams, but some cooks prefer ounces for measuring ingredients like cream. Converting 125g of cream to ounces (4.41 oz) helps avoid guesswork. Accurate measurements are especially crucial for baking, where ratios affect texture and rise. <p>Cream’s density means 125g takes up slightly less volume than water. This is why weight measurements (grams/ounces) beat volume (cups) for precision. Whether you’re making custards or sauces, 125g of cream equals roughly 4.41 ounces every time. <h3>How to measure 125g of cream without scales</h3>  
<p>No kitchen scales? Use tablespoons – 125g of cream is about 8 UK tablespoons. Remember, whipped cream will measure differently due to air incorporation. For sticky ingredients like cream, lightly grease your measuring spoon first.
