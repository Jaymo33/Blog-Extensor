<h3>How to Measure Coconut Milk Correctly</h3>
<p>To measure 2.0 cups of coconut milk accurately, use a liquid measuring cup with clear millilitre markings. Pour the coconut milk slowly to avoid spills and check at eye level for precision. If your recipe requires 473 ml, this method ensures you won’t over or under-measure. Shake the coconut milk can well before measuring, as separation can affect the volume. For sticky ingredients like coconut milk, a silicone spatula helps scrape out every last bit.</p>

<h3>Common Mistakes When Measuring Coconut Milk</h3>
<p>One frequent error is using a dry measuring cup for liquids, which can lead to inaccuracies. Another is not accounting for the cream layer in canned coconut milk, which skews the measurement. Always stir or shake the can thoroughly before measuring 2.0 cups (473 ml) for consistency. If using homemade coconut milk, ensure it’s well-blended to avoid separation. For sticky residues, rinse your measuring cup with warm water or use a light cooking spray.</p>
