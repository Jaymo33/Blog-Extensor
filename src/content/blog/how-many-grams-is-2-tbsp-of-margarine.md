<p>2 tablespoons (tbsp) of margarine weigh approximately 28 grams in the UK. This conversion is essential for air fryer recipes where precise measurements can make all the difference. Whether you're baking, frying, or roasting, knowing the exact weight ensures consistent results every time.</p>  

<h2>Why accurate measurements matter in air fryer cooking</h2>  
<p>Air fryers rely on even heat distribution, and incorrect ingredient quantities can throw off cooking times and textures. Using 28 grams of margarine instead of guessing ensures your dishes turn out perfectly. For more air fryer tips, check out <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-conversion-chart>our conversion chart</a>.</p>  

<p>Margarine is a common ingredient in many air fryer recipes, from crispy potatoes to golden pastries. Measuring 2 tbsp of margarine in grams helps avoid greasy or dry results. If you're new to air frying, explore our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>recipe collection</a> for inspiration.</p>  

<h3>How to measure margarine without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have kitchen scales, use a standard UK tablespoon to measure margarine. Pack it firmly into the spoon and level it off for accuracy. Remember, 2 tbsp of margarine equals roughly 28 grams.</p>  

<h2>Common uses for 2 tbsp of margarine in air fryer recipes</h2>  
<p>From roasting vegetables to making air-fried cakes, 28 grams of margarine adds richness and crispiness. Try it in <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/potatoes>potato recipes</a> for extra flavour or in <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/cakes>air fryer cakes</a> for a moist texture.</p>  

<p>Margarine also works well for greasing air fryer baskets to prevent sticking. Just melt 2 tbsp (28g) and brush it lightly onto the surface. For cleaning tips, visit <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-cleaner>our guide</a>.</p>  

<h3>Converting other margarine measurements</h3>  
<p>Need to adjust your recipe? 1 tbsp of margarine is about 14 grams, while 4 tbsp equals 56 grams. Keep these conversions handy for hassle-free cooking.</p>
