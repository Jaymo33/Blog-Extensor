<h2>How to measure 30g of mayonnaise without scales</h2>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can use tablespoons to measure 30g of mayonnaise. One tablespoon of mayonnaise weighs roughly 15g, so two tablespoons will give you close to 30g. This method is handy for quick conversions when you’re in a pinch.</p>
