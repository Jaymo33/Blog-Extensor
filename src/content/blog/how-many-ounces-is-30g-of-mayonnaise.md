<h2>How to measure 30g of mayonnaise without scales</h2>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can use tablespoons to measure 30g of mayonnaise. One tablespoon of mayonnaise weighs roughly 15g, so two tablespoons will give you close to 30g. This method is handy for quick conversions when you’re in a pinch.</p>  

<p>For sticky ingredients like mayonnaise, lightly greasing your measuring spoon can help it slide off easily. This trick ensures you get the full 30g without leaving half stuck to the spoon. <h3>Converting other mayonnaise quantities</h3>  
<p>If your recipe requires more or less than 30g of mayonnaise, here’s a quick reference: 15g is about 0.53 ounces, and 60g is roughly 2.12 ounces. Keeping these conversions handy saves time and ensures accuracy in your cooking.</p>  

<h2>Tips for using mayonnaise in air fryer recipes</h2>  
<p>Mayonnaise works wonders in air fryers, but a little goes a long way. For 30g of mayonnaise, spread it evenly to avoid clumping. Too much can cause smoke, so stick to measured amounts for best results.</p>
