<h3>How to Measure 2.75 Cups of Corn Syrup in ml</h3>  
<p>To measure 2.75 cups of corn syrup in millilitres, you’ll need a liquid measuring cup or a kitchen scale. One US cup is roughly 236.59 ml, so multiplying this by 2.75 gives you approximately 650 ml. If you’re using a UK cup, note that it’s slightly larger at 284 ml, so adjust accordingly.</p>

<h3>Tips for Working with Corn Syrup</h3>  
<p>Corn syrup can be messy, so lightly greasing your measuring cup helps it slide out easily. Warming it slightly (not too hot) also makes it easier to pour. Store any leftover syrup in an airtight container to prevent crystallisation.</p>  

<h2>Converting Other Common Measurements</h2>  
<p>If your recipe uses different amounts, here’s a quick reference: 1 cup of corn syrup is about 236.59 ml, and half a cup is roughly 118 ml. For larger batches, 3 cups would be around 710 ml. Always double-check conversions to ensure accuracy.</p>
