<p>Measuring by weight (grams) is more accurate than measuring by volume (cups) because cheese can vary in density. Shredded mozzarella packs differently than cubed or sliced, which affects the cup measurement. Always aim for consistency, especially when following air fryer recipes where cooking times are precise.</p>

<h2>How to Measure Mozzarella Cheese for Air Fryer Recipes</h2>
<p>To measure 425 grams of mozzarella cheese accurately, use a kitchen scale for the best results. If you don’t have one, lightly pack the shredded cheese into a measuring cup without pressing too hard. This method ensures you’re close to the 4.25-cup mark. For dishes like air fryer pizza rolls or mozzarella sticks, precise measurements ensure even melting and crispiness.</p>

<h3>Shredded vs. Cubed Mozzarella</h3>
<p>Shredded mozzarella cheese will yield about 4.25 cups per 425 grams, while cubed mozzarella may measure slightly less due to air gaps.
