<h3>Tips for Measuring Ground Cinnamon</h3>  
<p>To get the most accurate measurement, lightly spoon the cinnamon into your measuring cup and level it off with a knife. Avoid packing it down, as this can lead to an overly dense measurement. For air fryer recipes, consistency is crucial, so take your time with measurements.</p>
