If you don’t have one, a tablespoon holds about 15 ml, so you can estimate slightly less than a full spoon.</p>

<h3>Converting 10 ml of Cream in Different Cup Sizes</h3>  
<p>Depending on where you live, cup measurements can vary. In the UK, a standard metric cup is 250 ml, while in the US, it’s 240 ml. This means 10 ml of cream converts to 0.04 UK cups or 0.042 US cups. While the difference is minor, it’s worth noting if you’re following an international recipe.</p>
