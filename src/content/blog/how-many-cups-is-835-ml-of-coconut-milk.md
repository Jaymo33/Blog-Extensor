<h3>How to Measure 835 ml of Coconut Milk Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a measuring jug, you can use standard cups to measure 835 ml of coconut milk. A UK metric cup holds 250 ml, so 3.5 cups will give you the right amount. Stir the coconut milk well before measuring, as it can separate in the tin.</p>
