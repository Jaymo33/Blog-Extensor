<h3>How to Convert 4 kg Prawns to Pounds and Ounces</h3>

First, convert kilograms to pounds by multiplying by 2.20462, giving you 8.81848 pounds. The whole number part is 8 pounds. To find the ounces, take the decimal part (0.81848) and multiply it by 16, since there are 16 ounces in a pound. This results in approximately 13.1 ounces. Therefore, 4 kg of prawns is equal to 8 pounds and 13.1 ounces. <h2>Practical Tips for Cooking 4 kg of Prawns in Your Air Fryer</h2>
<p>Cooking a large batch of prawns, such as 4 kg, requires some strategic planning to ensure everything turns out perfectly. Since most home air fryers cannot accommodate 8.82 pounds at once, you will need to cook in batches. Preheat your air fryer to around 200°C for a few minutes before adding the first batch to promote even cooking. Lightly oil and season the prawns, then arrange them in a single layer without overlapping for the best crispness. Each batch should take about 6-8 minutes, depending on the size of the prawns, and remember to shake the basket halfway through. This method guarantees that every prawn is juicy inside and lightly crispy outside.</p>
