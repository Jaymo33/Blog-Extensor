<h3>How to convert 0.375 cup of jam to ounces</h3>  
<p>Converting 0.375 cup of jam to ounces is straightforward. One cup of jam typically weighs 8 ounces, so 0.375 cup equals 3 ounces. This conversion works for most jams, including strawberry, raspberry, or apricot. If you're working with a denser jam, like marmalade, it’s best to weigh it for precision. <h3>Tips for measuring jam without a scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, use measuring cups to portion out 0.375 cup of jam. Fill a 1/4 cup measure and then add half of another 1/4 cup to reach 0.375 cup. For sticky jams, lightly grease the measuring cup for easy release. This method ensures you get the correct 3-ounce amount for your air fryer recipes.</p>
