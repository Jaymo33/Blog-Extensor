<h3>How to measure 200g of cooked rice</h3>
<p>The easiest way to measure 200g of cooked rice is with a digital kitchen scale. Simply place your bowl on the scale, tare it to zero, and add rice until you hit 200g. If you don't have a scale, 200g of cooked rice is roughly one packed cup. Remember, rice expands when cooked, so 200g of cooked rice starts as about 70g of uncooked rice.</p>
