<h3>Types of oats and their weights</h3>
<p>Not all oats weigh the same per cup, which is why knowing the exact conversion for 3 cups to ounces helps. Rolled oats typically weigh around 3 ounces per cup, while quick oats might be slightly lighter. Steel-cut oats are denser, so 3 cups would weigh closer to 10 ounces.</p>

<h2>How to measure 3 cups of oats accurately</h2>
<p>For the most precise measurement, use a kitchen scale to weigh out your 9 ounces of oats. If you don’t have one, fluff the oats in the container first, then spoon them into your measuring cup. Level off the top with a straight edge for an accurate 3-cup measure.</p>
