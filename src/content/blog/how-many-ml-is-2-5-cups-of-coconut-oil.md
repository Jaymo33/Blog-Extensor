<h2>How to Measure Coconut Oil for Air Fryer Recipes</h2>
<p>Measuring coconut oil can be tricky due to its solid-to-liquid state change. Here’s a foolproof method: if your recipe lists 2.5 cups of coconut oil in ml, gently melt the oil first for an accurate liquid measurement. Use a kitchen scale for solid coconut oil—1 ml roughly equals 0.92 grams. This technique is especially useful for baking, where precision is non-negotiable.</p>
