<h3>UK vs. US Cup Measurements</h3>
<p>It’s worth noting that UK and US cup measurements differ slightly. In the UK, 1 cup is 284 ml, while in the US, it’s 240 ml. So, 385 ml of water is about 1.36 UK cups or 1.63 US cups. Always check which standard your recipe uses to avoid confusion.</p>
