<h3>How to Measure Buttermilk Correctly</h3>  
<p>To measure 235 ml of buttermilk accurately, use a liquid measuring cup with clear markings. Pour the buttermilk up to the 235 ml line, or just under the 1-cup mark if you’re using a standard US cup. Avoid using dry measuring cups, as they won’t give you the same precision. For best results, always measure liquids at eye level to avoid over or under-pouring.</p>

<h3>Common Mistakes When Measuring Buttermilk</h3>  
<p>One common mistake is confusing fluid ounces with millilitres, which can lead to incorrect measurements. Another is using a dry measuring cup for liquids, which doesn’t account for the meniscus (the slight curve at the top of the liquid). Always use a liquid measuring cup for buttermilk and other wet ingredients to ensure accuracy.
