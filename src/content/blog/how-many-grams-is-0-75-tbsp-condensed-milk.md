<h3>How to measure 0.75 tbsp of condensed milk</h3>  
<p>Measuring sticky ingredients like condensed milk can be tricky. Use a levelled tablespoon and fill it three-quarters full for 0.75 tbsp. Alternatively, weigh 11.25 grams on a kitchen scale for precision. This method works well for air fryer recipes requiring exact quantities.</p>
