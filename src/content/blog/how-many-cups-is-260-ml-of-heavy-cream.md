<h3>US vs UK Cup Measurements</h3>
<p>The difference between US and UK cup measurements might seem small, but it can add up in larger recipes. For 260 ml of heavy cream, the US measurement is 1.1 cups, while the UK measurement is 1.04 cups. This discrepancy is due to the different standards used in each country.</p>
