<h3>How to Measure 100g of Chilli Sauce Without Scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can estimate 100g of chilli sauce using tablespoons. Roughly, 100g equals about 6-7 tablespoons, depending on the sauce’s thickness. For thicker sauces, like sriracha, it might be closer to 5 tablespoons. Always stir the sauce well before measuring to ensure consistency.
