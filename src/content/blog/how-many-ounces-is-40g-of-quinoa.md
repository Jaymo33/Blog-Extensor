<h2>Why Convert 40g of Quinoa to Ounces?</h2>  
<p>Many UK air fryer recipes use grams, while others rely on ounces. Converting 40g of quinoa to ounces (1.41 oz) helps you follow instructions precisely. This is especially useful for air fryer cooking, where portion sizes affect cooking times and results.</p>  

<p>Quinoa is a fantastic ingredient for air fryer recipes, offering a protein-packed alternative to rice or couscous. <h3>How to Measure 40g of Quinoa Accurately</h3>  
<p>Using a digital kitchen scale is the easiest way to measure 40g of quinoa. If your scale displays ounces, it should show around 1.41 oz for 40g. For those without scales, 40g of quinoa is roughly 3 tablespoons, but weighing is always more precise.</p>  

<h2>Cooking Quinoa in Your Air Fryer</h2>  
<p>Once you’ve converted 40g of quinoa to ounces, it’s time to cook it perfectly in your air fryer. Rinse the quinoa first to remove its natural coating, which can taste bitter. Then, combine it with water or broth in a heatproof dish that fits your air fryer basket.</p>  

<p>For fluffy quinoa, cook at 180°C (350°F) for about 15–20 minutes.
