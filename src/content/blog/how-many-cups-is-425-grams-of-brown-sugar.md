<h3>UK vs. US Cup Measurements</h3>  
<p>In the UK, a standard cup holds 250ml, while in the US, it's 240ml. This small difference means 425 grams of brown sugar may fill slightly more than 2 cups in the UK. For air fryer recipes, stick to one measurement system to avoid inconsistencies. If your recipe is American, use US cups for the 425g brown sugar to cups conversion.</p>  

<h2>Tips for Measuring Brown Sugar Perfectly</h2>  
<p>To measure 425 grams of brown sugar accurately, fluff it with a fork to break up clumps before spooning it into a measuring cup. Avoid packing it down unless the recipe specifies "firmly packed." Lightly packed brown sugar ensures the 425 grams to cups ratio stays consistent. For sticky recipes like air fryer brownies, this step is essential.</p>
