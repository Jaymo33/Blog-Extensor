<p>585 ml of maple syrup equals approximately 2.47 cups. This conversion is essential for baking, cooking, or preparing air fryer recipes where precise measurements matter. <h3>How to Measure 585 ml of Maple Syrup</h3>  
<p>To measure 585 ml of maple syrup accurately, use a liquid measuring cup with clear markings. Pour slowly to avoid bubbles and check at eye level. If you don’t have a measuring cup, 585 ml is roughly 2.47 standard US cups or 2.34 UK cups. For sticky ingredients like syrup, lightly greasing the cup can help it slide out easily.</p>

<h3>Adjusting Recipes for Different Cup Sizes</h3>  
<p>Recipes from the US and UK may use slightly different cup measurements. A US cup is 240 ml, while a UK cup is 250 ml. For 585 ml of maple syrup, this means 2.47 US cups or 2.34 UK cups. Always check the recipe’s origin to ensure accuracy, especially when baking.</p>  

<h2>Tips for Working with Maple Syrup</h2>  
<p>Maple syrup can crystallise or thicken over time. Gently warming it before measuring ensures consistency. Avoid overheating, as it can alter the flavour. For even distribution in batters or marinades, mix the syrup with other wet ingredients first.
