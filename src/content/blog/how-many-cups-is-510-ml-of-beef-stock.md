<h3>UK vs. US Cup Measurements</h3>  
<p>In the UK, 1 cup equals 284 ml, while in the US, it's 240 ml. This slight difference means 510 ml of beef stock converts to roughly 2.15 cups in the UK and 2.16 cups in the US. For air fryer cooking, sticking to one measurement system avoids confusion.</p>
