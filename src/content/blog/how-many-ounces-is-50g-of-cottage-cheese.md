<h3>How to Measure 50g of Cottage Cheese</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate results, use a digital kitchen scale set to grams. Scoop the cottage cheese until the scale reads 50g, which translates to roughly 1.76 ounces. If you don’t have a scale, 50g is about 3 tablespoons, but weighing is more reliable for air fryer recipes where consistency matters.</p>
