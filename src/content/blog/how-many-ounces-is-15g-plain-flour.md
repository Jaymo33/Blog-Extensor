<h2>How to measure 15g of plain flour</h2>  
<p>Measuring 15g of plain flour accurately is simple with the right tools. A digital kitchen scale is the most precise method. If you don’t have one, use a measuring spoon—15g is roughly two level tablespoons. For best results, fluff the flour before scooping to avoid compacting it. This ensures your air fryer dishes, like <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/cakes>air fryer cakes</a>, turn out light and fluffy.</p>
