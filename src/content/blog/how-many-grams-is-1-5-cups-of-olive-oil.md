<h2>Converting Cups to Grams for Olive Oil</h2>
<p>Converting 1.5 cups of olive oil to grams is straightforward once you know the density. Olive oil weighs about 227 grams per cup, so 1.5 cups equal roughly 340 grams. This conversion is useful for both UK and US measurements, though cup sizes can vary slightly. Always use a kitchen scale for the most accurate results, especially in baking. Need more conversions? <h3>Measuring Olive Oil Without a Scale</h3>
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can still measure 1.5 cups of olive oil accurately. Use a liquid measuring cup and fill it to the 1.5-cup mark, ensuring it’s level for precision. Remember, 1.5 cups of olive oil in grams is 340g, so this method works in a pinch. For best results in air fryer recipes, though, a scale is recommended.
