Accurate measurements also help when scaling recipes up or down, making meal prep a breeze.</p>

<h3>How to measure 0.25 cup of greek yogurt without a measuring cup</h3>  
<p>No measuring cup? No problem. A standard tablespoon holds about 0.5 ounces, so four tablespoons will give you roughly 2 ounces (0.25 cup) of greek yogurt. For thicker yogurts, leveling off the spoon ensures accuracy. If you’re using a kitchen scale, simply weigh out 2 ounces for precision. This method is especially useful for <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/breakfast>breakfast recipes</a> like yogurt parfaits or smoothie bowls.</p>  

<h2>Common uses for 0.25 cup of greek yogurt in air fryer recipes</h2>  
<p>Greek yogurt is a staple in many air fryer dishes, from marinades to baked goods. A 0.25 cup (2 ounces) portion works well in recipes like tandoori chicken or as a base for creamy dips. It’s also great for binding ingredients in <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/dessert>desserts</a> like air-fried cheesecake bites. <p>For healthier alternatives, greek yogurt can replace mayo or sour cream in dressings and sauces. Measuring 0.25 cup ensures you get the right consistency without overpowering other flavors.
