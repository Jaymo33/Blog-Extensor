Ground coriander adds warmth and citrusy notes, perfect for dishes like <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/chicken>air fryer chicken</a> or roasted vegetables. Always measure spices carefully to avoid disappointing results.</p>

<h3>How to Measure Ground Coriander Correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate conversion of 0.66 cup ground coriander to ounces, use a kitchen scale. Scoop the spice lightly into a measuring cup without packing it down. If you don’t have a scale, remember that 1 cup of ground coriander weighs roughly 4 ounces, so 0.66 cup is about 2.64 ounces. This method works for most powdered spices in air fryer cooking.</p>
