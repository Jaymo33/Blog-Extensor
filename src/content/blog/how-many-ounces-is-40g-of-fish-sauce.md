<h2>How to measure fish sauce for air fryer recipes</h2>
<p>Fish sauce is a potent ingredient, so measuring it correctly is crucial. Use a digital kitchen scale for grams or a liquid measuring cup for ounces. For 40g of fish sauce, you’ll need about 1.41 ounces, which is roughly 2 tablespoons and 2 teaspoons. This precision ensures your air fryer dishes aren’t too salty or lacking in umami.</p>
