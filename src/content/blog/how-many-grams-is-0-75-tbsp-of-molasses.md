<h3>How to measure molasses without a scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a scale, use a tablespoon measure and fill it three-quarters full for 0.75 tbsp. Molasses is thick, so lightly greasing the spoon helps it slide off easily. For sticky ingredients like this, a silicone spatula can scrape every last gram into your mix. This method works well for air fryer batters and glazes.</p>
