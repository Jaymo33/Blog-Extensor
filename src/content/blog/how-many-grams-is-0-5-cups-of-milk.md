<h3>Common Mistakes When Measuring Milk</h3>  
<p>One frequent error is using a dry measuring cup for liquids like milk. Dry cups can’t account for the meniscus (the slight curve at the top of the liquid), leading to inaccuracies. Another mistake is eyeballing measurements instead of using proper tools. For best results, always measure 0.5 cups of milk carefully or weigh it at 122 grams.</p>
