<h3>How to Measure 150g of Plain Flour Accurately</h3>  
<p>To measure 150g of plain flour correctly, use a digital kitchen scale for precision. Scooping flour directly from the bag can compact it, leading to over-measuring. Instead, spoon the flour into your measuring cup and level it off. For 5.29 ounces, this method ensures consistency. If you’re adapting a recipe from <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/cakes>air fryer cakes</a>, this step is crucial for light, fluffy results.</p>
