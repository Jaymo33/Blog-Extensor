<h3>UK vs US Cup Measurements for Coconut Milk</h3>
<p>In the UK, a standard cup holds 250 ml, so 785 ml of coconut milk is roughly 3.3 cups. In the US, where a cup is 240 ml, it's about 3.1 cups. This small difference can affect recipes, especially in baking. Always check whether your recipe uses UK or US measurements to avoid mishaps.</p>

<h2>How to Measure 785 ml of Coconut Milk Without a Scale</h2>
<p>If you don't have a measuring jug, you can use a standard cup to measure 785 ml of coconut milk. Fill the cup to the brim three times, then add a small amount to reach the full 785 ml. For more precise measurements, consider using a liquid measuring cup with millilitre markings.</p>
