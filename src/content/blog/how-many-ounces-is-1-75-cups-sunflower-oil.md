<p>1.75 cups of sunflower oil equals approximately 14 ounces (or 14.2 oz to be precise). This conversion is essential for air fryer cooking, where precise measurements can make or break your dish. Whether you’re making crispy chips or a succulent roast, getting the oil quantity right ensures even cooking and perfect texture.</p>  

<h2>Why accurate oil measurements matter in air fryers</h2>  
<p>Air fryers rely on hot air circulation to cook food, and using the right amount of oil ensures crispiness without excess grease. <h3>How to measure sunflower oil without a scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, use standard measuring cups for liquids. One cup of sunflower oil weighs about 8 ounces, so 1.75 cups would be 14 ounces. For UK cooks, note that fluid ounces and weight ounces are slightly different, but in this case, the difference is negligible. Always level off the oil in your measuring cup for accuracy.</p>
