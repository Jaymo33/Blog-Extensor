<h3>How to measure 0.25 tbsp of sesame oil accurately</h3>
<p>Measuring small quantities like 0.25 tbsp of sesame oil can be tricky without the right tools. Alternatively, a digital kitchen scale set to grams will give you the most accurate result – just tare the scale with your container and pour until it reads 3.7 grams.</p>
