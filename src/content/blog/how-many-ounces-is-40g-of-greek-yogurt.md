<h3>How to measure 40g of greek yogurt without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can estimate 40g of greek yogurt using tablespoons. One tablespoon of greek yogurt weighs roughly 15g, so 40g is just over 2.5 tablespoons. This method isn’t as precise as weighing but works in a pinch for recipes like air fryer dips or marinades.</p>
