<p>Marmalade’s sticky consistency makes volume measurements tricky. A slight variation in how you fill the tablespoon can alter the weight. That’s why weighing 0.75 tbsp of marmalade in grams (11.25g) is more reliable. This method is especially useful for recipes like air-fried citrus cakes or sticky wings.</p>

<h3>How to measure 0.75 tbsp marmalade without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have digital scales, use the displacement method. Fill a measuring jug with 50ml water, then add marmalade until the water rises to 56.25ml (since 1 tbsp ≈ 15ml). This roughly equals 0.75 tbsp or 11.25 grams. For more clever hacks, browse our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>recipe collection</a>.</p>
