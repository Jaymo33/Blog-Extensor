<h2>How to Measure 15g of Greek Yogurt</h2>
<p>Measuring 15g of Greek yogurt doesn’t require a scale if you know the conversion. One tablespoon of Greek yogurt typically weighs around 15g, which is roughly 0.53 ounces. This makes it easy to measure without specialised equipment. For more tips on measuring ingredients, visit our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>recipes section</a>.</p>

<h3>Using a Kitchen Scale</h3>
<p>For the most accurate measurement, a kitchen scale is your best bet. Simply place your container on the scale, tare it to zero, and scoop out 15g of Greek yogurt. This method eliminates any guesswork and ensures precision, especially for baking or delicate air fryer dishes.</p>

<h2>Tips for Storing Greek Yogurt</h2>
<p>To keep your Greek yogurt fresh, always store it in an airtight container in the fridge. If you’ve measured out 15g (0.53 ounces) and have leftovers, ensure the container is sealed properly to maintain its texture and flavour. For more storage tips, visit our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-cleaner>cleaning and storage guide</a>.</p>
