<p>Fan ovens circulate hot air for even cooking, requiring slightly lower temperatures than conventional ovens. When converting 340 F to fan oven settings, you'll typically reduce the temperature by about 20 degrees Celsius. This means 340 F (171 C conventional) becomes 170 C in a fan oven. Many modern air fryers operate similarly to fan ovens, making this conversion doubly useful.
