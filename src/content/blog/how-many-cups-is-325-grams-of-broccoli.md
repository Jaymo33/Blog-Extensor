<h3>How to Measure Broccoli for Air Fryer Recipes</h3>  
<p>To measure 325 grams of broccoli in cups, start by chopping the florets into even-sized pieces. Pack them lightly into a measuring cup without pressing down too hard, as this can compress the broccoli and skew the measurement. If you're using a kitchen scale, weigh the broccoli first, then transfer it to cups for visual confirmation. This method ensures you get the right amount for your air fryer basket.</p>
