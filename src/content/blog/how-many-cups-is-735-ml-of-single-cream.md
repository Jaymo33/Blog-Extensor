<h3>How to Measure 735 ml of Single Cream Without a Scale</h3>
<p>If you don’t have a measuring jug with millilitre markings, you can use standard cup measurements. A UK metric cup holds 250 ml, so 735 ml of single cream is just over 3 cups (750 ml). For a more precise measurement, use 3 cups plus 1 tablespoon. This method ensures your air fryer recipes turn out perfectly every time.
