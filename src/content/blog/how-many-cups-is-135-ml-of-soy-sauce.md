<h3>How to Measure 135 ml of Soy Sauce Without a Measuring Cup</h3>  
<p>Don’t have a measuring cup? No problem. A standard UK tablespoon holds about 15 ml, so 135 ml of soy sauce is roughly 9 tablespoons. Alternatively, use a kitchen scale—135 ml weighs around 135 grams for soy sauce.
