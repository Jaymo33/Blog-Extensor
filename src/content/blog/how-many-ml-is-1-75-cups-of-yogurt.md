<h3>Converting Cups to ml for Different Yogurt Types</h3>  
<p>Not all yogurts weigh the same due to variations in thickness. Greek yogurt, for instance, is denser than regular yogurt, but the ml measurement remains consistent for 1.75 cups. If you're using a kitchen scale, 414 ml of yogurt roughly translates to 420-430 grams, depending on the brand. For best results, always measure liquids in ml when following air fryer recipes.</p>
