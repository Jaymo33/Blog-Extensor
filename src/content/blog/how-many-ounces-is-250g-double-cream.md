<h2>Why Accurate Measurements Matter in Air Fryer Cooking</h2>  
<p>Air fryers rely on even cooking, and incorrect measurements can throw off texture and consistency. Double cream, with its high fat content, behaves differently when measured in grams versus ounces. <h3>How to Convert 250g Double Cream to Ounces</h3>  
<p>The conversion from grams to ounces is straightforward: 1 gram equals 0.035 ounces. For 250g of double cream, multiply 250 by 0.035 to get 8.82 ounces. This is particularly useful for UK cooks following American recipes, which often use ounces. If you’re making a creamy pasta sauce or a dessert in your air fryer, this conversion ensures you’re spot-on.</p>
