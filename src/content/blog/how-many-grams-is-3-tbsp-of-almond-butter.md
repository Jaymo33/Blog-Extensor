<h2>Why accurate measurements matter</h2>  
<p>Accurate measurements are crucial in cooking, especially when using an air fryer. Too much or too little almond butter can affect texture, moisture, and cooking times. For example, adding extra almond butter to a cookie dough might make it too dense. On the other hand, skimping could leave your dish dry. That's why converting 3 tbsp of almond butter to grams (48g) ensures reliability.</p>

<h3>How to measure almond butter correctly</h3>  
<p>Measuring sticky ingredients like almond butter can be tricky. For the most accurate 3 tbsp to grams conversion, use a spoon to scoop and level off the excess. Alternatively, weigh your jar before and after scooping to calculate the exact grams used. This method is especially useful for sticky or dense ingredients.</p>
