<h2>How to Measure 735 ml of Olive Oil Without a Measuring Cup</h2>
<p>Don’t have a measuring cup handy? No problem. You can use a standard metric measuring jug to measure 735 ml of olive oil. Alternatively, if you’re working with a kitchen scale, 735 ml of olive oil weighs roughly 680 grams, as olive oil has a density of about 0.92 g/ml.</p>
<p>For those who prefer visual cues, 735 ml is just under three-quarters of a litre. This conversion is particularly useful when scaling up recipes or preparing meals in bulk. <h3>US vs UK Cup Measurements</h3>
<p>It’s worth noting that US and UK cup measurements differ slightly. In the US, 1 cup is 240 ml, while in the UK, it’s 250 ml. For 735 ml of olive oil, this means approximately 3.06 US cups or 2.94 UK cups. For most practical purposes, rounding to 3.1 cups works perfectly.</p>
