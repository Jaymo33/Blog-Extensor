<h2>Understanding Almond Milk Measurements</h2>  
<p>Almond milk is a popular dairy-free alternative used in many recipes, from smoothies to baked goods. When converting 510 ml of almond milk to cups, it's important to note that US and UK cup measurements differ slightly. A US cup holds 240 ml, while a UK cup holds 250 ml. This means 510 ml of almond milk is roughly 2.15 US cups or 2.13 UK cups.</p>
