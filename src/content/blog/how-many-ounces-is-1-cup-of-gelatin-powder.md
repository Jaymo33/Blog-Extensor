<h3>How to Measure Gelatin Powder Correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate 1 cup gelatin powder to ounces measurement, use a kitchen scale. Scooping directly from the bag can lead to inconsistencies due to settling. Lightly spoon the powder into a measuring cup, then level it off for uniformity. This method ensures your air fryer recipes turn out flawless every time.</p>
