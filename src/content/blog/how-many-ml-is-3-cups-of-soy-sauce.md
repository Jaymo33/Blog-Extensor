<h2>How to Measure 3.0 Cups of Soy Sauce in ml</h2>  
<p>To convert 3.0 cups of soy sauce to ml, you’ll need a reliable measuring jug or kitchen scale. One US cup equals 236.59 ml, so multiplying this by 3 gives you 709.77 ml, which rounds up to 710 ml for practicality. If you’re using a UK cup, note that it’s slightly larger at 284.13 ml, making 3.0 cups approximately 852 ml. Always check which cup measurement your recipe uses to avoid discrepancies.</p>
