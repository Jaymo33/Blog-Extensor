<h3>UK vs. US Cup Measurements</h3>  
<p>In the UK, 0.75 cups of self-raising flour weighs about 94 grams, while in the US, it's closer to 90 grams. This difference stems from slight variations in cup sizes and flour density. If your recipe originates from another country, always double-check which standard it uses. <h2>How to Measure 0.75 Cups of Self-Raising Flour</h2>  
<p>For the most accurate results, use a kitchen scale to weigh 94 grams (UK) or 90 grams (US) of self-raising flour. If you don’t have a scale, spoon the flour into a measuring cup and level it off with a knife. Avoid packing the flour down, as this can lead to over-measuring.
