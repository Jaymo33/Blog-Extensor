<h3>How to measure 500g of vinegar without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can still measure 500g of vinegar using volume. Since vinegar has a similar density to water, 500g is roughly 500ml, which converts to about 17 fluid ounces. However, for absolute accuracy, especially in recipes like <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/dessert>air fryer desserts</a>, a digital scale is recommended. <h3>Converting other vinegar quantities</h3>  
<p>Need to convert a different amount of vinegar? The same principle applies: 1 gram of vinegar is approximately 0.035 ounces. So, 250g would be 8.82 oz, and 1kg would be 35.27 oz. This flexibility is handy when adjusting recipes or working with larger batches. Keep this conversion in mind for all your air fryer adventures, from crispy veggies to succulent meats.</p>
