Whether you’re whipping up crispy roast potatoes or a moist cake, knowing the exact grams in 1 tbsp of margarine ensures consistency. <h2>Why measuring margarine accurately matters</h2>  
<p>Using the correct amount of margarine in your air fryer recipes ensures even cooking and the right texture. Too little margarine can leave your food dry, while too much can make it greasy. For example, our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>air fryer cake recipes</a> rely on precise measurements for the perfect rise. Margarine’s weight can vary slightly by brand, but 14 grams per tbsp is a reliable standard. Always level off your tablespoon for the most accurate measurement.</p>  

<p>If you’re converting from volume to weight, a digital kitchen scale is your best friend. Many air fryer enthusiasts swear by weighing ingredients for consistent results. Whether you’re making savoury or sweet dishes, knowing how many grams are in 1 tbsp of margarine takes the guesswork out of cooking.</p>  

<h2>How to measure margarine without scales</h2>  
<p>Not everyone has a kitchen scale handy, but you can still measure margarine accurately. A standard UK tablespoon holds about 14 grams of margarine when levelled off. If your recipe calls for melted margarine, measure it solid first for accuracy. For more tips on measuring tricky ingredients, explore our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/dessert>dessert recipes</a>, where precision is crucial.</p>  

<h3>Margarine vs butter in air fryer cooking</h3>  
<p>Margarine and butter have similar weights, but their water content differs. This can affect how your air fryer recipes turn out. Margarine typically contains more water, which may require slight adjustments in cooking times. If you’re adapting a butter-based recipe, keep this in mind. Our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-cleaner>air fryer cleaner guide</a> also helps manage any extra grease from margarine-based dishes.</p>  

<h2>Common air fryer recipes using margarine</h2>  
<p>Margarine is a staple in many air fryer recipes, from crispy vegetables to golden pastries. Our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/potatoes>potato recipes</a> often use a tablespoon of margarine for that perfect crunch. Remember, 1 tbsp equals about 14 grams, whether you’re brushing it on or mixing it in. For best results, use margarine at room temperature unless the recipe specifies otherwise.</p>
