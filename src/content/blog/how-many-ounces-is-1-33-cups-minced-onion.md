<h2>How to measure minced onion for air fryer recipes</h2>  
<p>Measuring minced onion correctly ensures your dishes have the right balance of flavour. Lightly pack the minced onion into a measuring cup without pressing too hard, as this can compact it and skew the measurement. For 1.33 cups, you’ll need roughly 4.25 ounces of minced onion. This is ideal for recipes like air-fried onion rings or savoury toppings.</p>
