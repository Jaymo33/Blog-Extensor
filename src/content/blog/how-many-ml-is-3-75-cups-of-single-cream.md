<h2>Understanding Cup to ml Conversions</h2>
<p>Converting cups to millilitres can be tricky, especially when dealing with liquids like single cream. In the UK, 1 cup is typically 250 ml, so 3.75 cups would be 3.75 x 250 = 937.5 ml. However, single cream has a slightly different density than water, which is why the actual conversion is closer to 887.5 ml. This difference matters when precision is key, such as in air fryer recipes where exact measurements affect texture and consistency.</p>
