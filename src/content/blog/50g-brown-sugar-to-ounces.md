<h2>How to measure 50g of brown sugar without scales</h2>  
<p>If you don't have a kitchen scale, you can still measure 50g of brown sugar in ounces using household items. A standard UK tablespoon holds roughly 12.5g of brown sugar, so four level tablespoons will give you 50g (1.76 oz). Pack the sugar lightly to avoid over-measuring. This method is handy for quick recipes like <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/cookies>cookies</a> or muffins.</p>
