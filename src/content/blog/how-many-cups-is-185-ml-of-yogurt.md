<p>In the US, a standard cup holds 240 ml, while in the UK, it’s 284 ml. This means 185 ml of yogurt translates to roughly 0.78 US cups or 0.65 UK cups. If you’re using an air fryer for recipes like yogurt-marinated chicken or baked goods, these conversions ensure your dish turns out just right. Always measure yogurt in a liquid measuring cup for the most accurate results.</p>

<h2>Handy Tips for Measuring Yogurt</h2>  
<p>To measure 185 ml of yogurt accurately, use a liquid measuring cup with clear markings. Stirring the yogurt first ensures a consistent texture, especially if it’s been sitting in the fridge.</p>
