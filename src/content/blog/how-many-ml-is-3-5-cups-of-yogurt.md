<h2>Understanding the Conversion</h2>  
<p>Converting cups to millilitres can be tricky, but it’s straightforward once you know the basics. One standard US cup equals 236.588 ml, so 3.5 cups of yogurt translates to approximately 828 ml. This measurement is crucial for air fryer recipes, where exact quantities can make or break a dish. For example, a yogurt-based marinade or batter needs the right consistency to cook evenly in the air fryer.</p>  

<p>If you’re working with UK measurements, note that a UK cup is slightly larger at 284 ml. Always double-check which measurement system your recipe follows to avoid mishaps.</p>

<p>Air fryers rely on rapid air circulation, so ingredient proportions must be spot-on. Too much yogurt in a batter, for instance, can lead to soggy results, while too little might dry out your dish. Measuring 3.5 cups of yogurt in ml (828 ml) ensures consistency, whether you’re making a tangy marinade for chicken or a moist cake.</p>
