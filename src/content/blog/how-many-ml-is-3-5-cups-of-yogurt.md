<h2>Understanding the Conversion</h2>  
<p>Converting cups to millilitres can be tricky, but it’s straightforward once you know the basics. One standard US cup equals 236.588 ml, so 3.5 cups of yogurt translates to approximately 828 ml. This measurement is crucial for air fryer recipes, where exact quantities can make or break a dish. For example, a yogurt-based marinade or batter needs the right consistency to cook evenly in the air fryer.</p>  

<p>If you’re working with UK measurements, note that a UK cup is slightly larger at 284 ml.
