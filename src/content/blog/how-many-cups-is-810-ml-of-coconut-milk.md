<h2>UK vs. US Cup Conversions for 810 ml of Coconut Milk</h2>
<p>In the UK, 1 cup equals 284 ml, meaning 810 ml of coconut milk is roughly 3.42 cups. In the US, where 1 cup is 240 ml, 810 ml converts to about 3.38 cups. While the difference seems minor, it’s worth noting if you're following a precise recipe. Many air fryer recipes on <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>airfryerrecipe.co.uk</a> specify which measurement system they use, so always check before starting.</p>

<h3>How to Measure 810 ml of Coconut Milk Without a Measuring Cup</h3>
<p>If you don’t have a measuring cup, you can use a standard mug or glass, though this method is less precise. A typical UK mug holds about 300 ml, so 810 ml would be just under 3 mugs full.
