<h3>How to Measure 0.25 Cups of Coconut Milk Without a Measuring Cup</h3>  
<p>Don’t have a measuring cup handy? No problem. You can use a standard tablespoon to measure out 0.25 cups of coconut milk. Since 1 tablespoon is roughly 15 ml, you’ll need about 4 tablespoons to reach 59 ml. For thicker coconut milk, stir it well before measuring to ensure consistency. This method works perfectly for recipes like <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/dessert>air fryer desserts</a>, where precision matters.</p>

<h3>Common Mistakes When Measuring Coconut Milk</h3>  
<p>One frequent error is not shaking the coconut milk can before measuring, leading to inconsistent results. The thicker cream often separates from the liquid, so mixing ensures uniformity. Another mistake is using a liquid measuring cup for small amounts like 0.25 cups (59 ml)—a kitchen scale or tablespoon is often more accurate. For best results, always level off your measurements with a straight edge.</p>
