<h3>How to measure 250g of vinegar without a scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can still measure 250g of vinegar (8.82 oz) using measuring cups or spoons. One fluid ounce of vinegar weighs roughly 28.35 grams, so 250g translates to just under 9 fluid ounces. For most home cooks, this small difference won’t drastically affect your dish, but for precision, a scale is best.</p>
