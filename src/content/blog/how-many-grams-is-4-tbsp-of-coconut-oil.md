<h2>Common uses for coconut oil in air fryers</h2>  
<p>Coconut oil is a versatile ingredient in air fryer cooking, ideal for roasting vegetables, frying chicken, or baking goods. When converting 4 tbsp coconut oil to grams, you’ll find it’s lighter than butter, so adjustments may be needed in recipes.</p>  

<p>For example, if a recipe calls for 4 tbsp of butter (around 57g), substituting coconut oil (54g) requires a slight reduction. This ensures your dish isn’t overly greasy. <h3>Storing and handling coconut oil</h3>  
<p>Coconut oil solidifies below 24°C, so if your kitchen is cool, warm it slightly before measuring. This ensures an even 4 tbsp coconut oil to grams conversion. Always store it in an airtight container to prevent oxidation, which can affect flavour.
