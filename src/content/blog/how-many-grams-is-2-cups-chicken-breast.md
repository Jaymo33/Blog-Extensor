<h3>How to Measure Chicken Breast Correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate conversion, use a kitchen scale to weigh your chicken breast. If you don't have one, fluff the chicken lightly before scooping it into a measuring cup. Packed chicken will weigh more, leading to inconsistencies in your air fryer recipes. Remember, 2.0 cups of chicken breast in grams is roughly 280g, but slight variations can occur. Always check your recipe for specific weight requirements.</p>

<h3>Common Mistakes When Measuring Chicken</h3>  
<p>One of the biggest mistakes is using liquid measuring cups for solids like chicken breast. Dry measuring cups are designed for ingredients like 2.0 cups of chicken breast, ensuring a more accurate gram conversion. Another error is not accounting for cooked versus raw weight—chicken loses moisture during cooking, reducing its overall mass. Always measure raw chicken unless the recipe specifies otherwise.</p>
