<h3>How to measure minced onion correctly</h3>  
<p>Measuring 30g of minced onion is easiest with a digital kitchen scale. Simply place your bowl on the scale, tare it to zero, and add the minced onion until it reads 30g. If you don’t have a scale, 30g is roughly equivalent to 1.06 ounces or about three tablespoons. Keep in mind that minced onion can vary in density, so a scale is always best for precision.</p>
