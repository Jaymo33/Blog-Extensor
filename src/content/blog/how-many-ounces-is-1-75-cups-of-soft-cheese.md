<p>It is important to note that a UK cup is slightly different from a US cup, which could affect the conversion if you are using a recipe from a different region. A US customary cup holds 8 fluid ounces, which is the standard used for the 1.75 cups to 14 ounces soft cheese conversion. A UK imperial cup is larger, holding approximately 10 imperial fluid ounces. However, for dry and semi-solid ingredients like soft cheese, most modern UK recipes use the international metric standard (grams) or the US cup measurement for simplicity.</p>
<p>Therefore, when you encounter a recipe asking for 1.75 cups of soft cheese, it is almost certainly using the US cup measurement.
