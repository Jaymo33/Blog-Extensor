<h3>How to Measure 0.75 Cups of Almond Milk Correctly</h3>  
<p>Using a liquid measuring cup is the best way to measure 0.75 cups of almond milk accurately. Pour the milk to the 3/4 mark or use a metric jug for 177 ml. Avoid using dry measuring cups, as they can lead to slight inaccuracies. For sticky ingredients like almond milk, a clear measuring cup helps you check the meniscus at eye level. Proper tools ensure your air fryer recipes turn out perfectly every time.</p>
