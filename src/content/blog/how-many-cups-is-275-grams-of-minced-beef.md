<h2>How to Measure 275 Grams of Minced Beef Without a Scale</h2>
<p>No kitchen scale? No problem. Use a standard measuring cup to scoop lightly packed minced beef, filling it just above the rim for 275 grams. Avoid pressing down, as compacting the beef skews the measurement. If your recipe calls for 275 grams of minced beef in cups, this method gets you close enough for most dishes. For recipes like air-fryer burgers, consistency is key, so aim for uniformity in your cups.</p>
<p>Another trick is to visualise the amount: 275 grams of minced beef fills about two-thirds of a standard 2-cup measure. This works well for recipes where precision isn’t critical, such as chilli or bolognese. <h3>Adjusting Recipes for Different Minced Beef Quantities</h3>
<p>If your recipe uses 275 grams of minced beef but you need to scale up or down, keep the cup conversion in mind. The air fryer’s versatility shines here, whether you’re cooking a single serving or batch-prepping.</p>
