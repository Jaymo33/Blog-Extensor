<h3>How to measure 5g of brown sugar without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can still measure 5g of brown sugar using common household items. One level teaspoon of packed brown sugar is roughly 5g, which converts to 0.18 ounces. This method isn’t as precise as using scales but works in a pinch for recipes where exact measurements aren’t critical.</p>
