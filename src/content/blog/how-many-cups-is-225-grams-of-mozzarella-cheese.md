<h3>Shredded vs. Cubed Mozzarella: Does It Affect Cup Measurements?</h3>
<p>Yes! Shredded mozzarella packs more tightly into a cup than cubed or sliced cheese. If your recipe calls for 225 grams of mozzarella cheese in cups, shredded will measure closer to 2.25 cups, while cubed might fill closer to 2.5 cups. For air fryer recipes like stuffed peppers or cheesy garlic bread, shredded is often the better choice as it melts more evenly.</p>
