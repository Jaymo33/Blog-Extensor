<h2>Understanding Half And Half Measurements</h2>  
<p>Half and half is a dairy product made by combining equal parts whole milk and cream. It's commonly used in coffee, soups, and desserts, making accurate measurements crucial for recipe success. When converting 910 ml of half and half to cups, it's important to note that US and UK cup sizes differ slightly. A US cup holds 240 ml, while a UK cup is 250 ml. This means 910 ml converts to 3.85 US cups or 3.6 UK cups. For air fryer recipes, precise measurements ensure even cooking and perfect textures.</p>  

<p>If you're working with an air fryer, measuring liquids like half and half accurately can make or break your dish.
