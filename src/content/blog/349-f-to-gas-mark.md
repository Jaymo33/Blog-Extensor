<h3>Adjusting Air Fryer Recipes for Gas Mark 4</h3>
<p>When converting oven recipes to air fryer cooking at 349 F (Gas Mark 4), there are several factors to consider. For example, chicken breasts that take 25 minutes at Gas Mark 4 in an oven might only need 18-20 minutes in an air fryer at 349°F. Always check food 5 minutes before the suggested cooking time ends.</p>
