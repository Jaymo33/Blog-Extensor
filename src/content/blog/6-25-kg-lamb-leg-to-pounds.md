<p>The conversion from kilograms to pounds is straightforward: multiply the weight in kg by 2.20462. For a 6.25 kg lamb leg, that’s 6.25 x 2.20462 = 13.778875 pounds, which rounds to 13.78 pounds. This number is your guide for timing and temperature settings in the air fryer. Most air fryer recipes for lamb leg provide cook time per pound, so this accuracy is key.
