<h3>How to Measure 0.75 Cups of Mozzarella Cheese</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate results, use a kitchen scale to weigh 85 grams of mozzarella cheese. If you don’t have one, lightly pack the cheese into a measuring cup without pressing too hard. Shredded mozzarella fills the cup differently than cubed or fresh, so adjust accordingly. This method ensures you get the right amount for recipes like our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/chicken>cheesy chicken dishes</a> or crispy snacks.</p>
