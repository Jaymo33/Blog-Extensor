<h2>How to Convert 40g of Gelatin Powder to Ounces</h2>
<p>Converting grams to ounces is straightforward once you know the ratio. One gram is approximately 0.035 ounces, so 40g of gelatin powder works out to 1.41 ounces. This conversion is particularly useful for UK cooks who often use metric measurements but may encounter recipes in imperial units.</p>
