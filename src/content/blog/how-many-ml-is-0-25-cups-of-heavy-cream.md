<h3>How to Measure Heavy Cream Correctly</h3>  
<p>To measure 0.25 cups of heavy cream accurately, use a liquid measuring cup or a kitchen scale. Pour the cream slowly to avoid air bubbles, and check the measurement at eye level. If using a scale, 59 ml of heavy cream weighs roughly 60 grams. This method ensures consistency, whether you're making a creamy soup or an air fryer dessert.</p>
