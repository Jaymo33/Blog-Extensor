<h3>How to Measure 15g of Soy Sauce Without Scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can still measure 15g of soy sauce using tablespoons. One tablespoon of soy sauce is roughly 15g or 0.53 ounces. This makes it easy to measure even without specialised tools. Just remember that different brands of soy sauce may vary slightly in density, so this method is best for rough estimates.</p>
