<h3>How to Measure BBQ Sauce Correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate results, use a digital kitchen scale to weigh your BBQ sauce. Scoop the sauce into a tablespoon, levelling it off for consistency. If you don’t have scales, remember that 1 tbsp is roughly 15 grams, so 3 tbsp would be 45 grams. This method works for most thick sauces, including BBQ.</p>
