<h3>How to Measure 2.25 Cups of Rolled Oats Correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate conversion, lightly spoon the oats into your measuring cup without packing them down. Level off the top with a straight edge to avoid excess. If you’re using a kitchen scale, 225 grams will give you the perfect amount for air fryer cooking. This method works for both UK and US cup measurements, ensuring consistency.</p>

<h3>Adjusting Recipes for Different Oat Types</h3>  
<p>If you’re using quick oats or steel-cut oats instead of rolled oats, the weight may differ slightly. Always check your recipe notes for specifics, especially when adapting traditional oven recipes for the air fryer.</p>
