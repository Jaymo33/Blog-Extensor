<h2>Understanding the Conversion</h2>
<p>The difference between US and UK cup measurements can be confusing, but it's essential for accuracy. In the US, 1 cup equals 240 ml, making 235 ml of soy sauce just shy of a full cup. In the UK, 1 cup is 250 ml, so 235 ml translates to roughly 0.98 cups. This small variation matters when scaling recipes or adjusting flavours for air fryer dishes like teriyaki salmon or glazed tofu.</p>
