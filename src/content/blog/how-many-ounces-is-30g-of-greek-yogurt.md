<h3>How to measure 30g of greek yogurt accurately</h3>
<p>For perfect 30g portions of greek yogurt, use digital kitchen scales set to grams. If you only have measuring spoons, two level tablespoons equal roughly 30g or 1.06 ounces. Remember that greek yogurt's thickness means it won't pour like regular yogurt, so spoon it gently into your measuring tool.</p>

<h2>Converting other yogurt amounts between grams and ounces</h2>
<p>Once you've mastered converting 30g of greek yogurt to ounces (1.06 oz), you can apply the same ratio to other amounts. Simply multiply grams by 0.035 to get ounces. For example, 60g would be 2.12 ounces, and 100g equals 3.53 ounces.</p>
