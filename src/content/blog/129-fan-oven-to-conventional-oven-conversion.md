<h2>Why Convert 129 Fan Oven to Conventional Oven?</h2>
<p>Fan ovens circulate hot air, cooking food faster and more evenly than conventional ovens. When a recipe calls for 129 fan oven, it's designed for this efficient heat distribution. Converting to 150°C in a conventional oven compensates for the lack of air circulation. This adjustment is crucial for baking, roasting, and even air frying. <h2>How to Calculate 129 Fan Oven to Conventional Oven</h2>
<p>The general rule for converting fan oven to conventional oven is to increase the temperature by 20°C. Therefore, 129 fan oven becomes 150°C in a conventional oven. This simple calculation works for most recipes, from <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/cakes>cakes</a> to roasted meats.</p>
