<h3>How to Measure 150g of Soft Cheese in Ounces</h3>  
<p>To convert 150g of soft cheese to ounces, multiply the grams by 0.035274. This gives you roughly 5.29 ounces. If you don’t have a scale, use measuring spoons or cups—though weighing is always more accurate. Soft cheese can vary in density, so spooning it into a measuring cup may not give you the exact weight.</p>
