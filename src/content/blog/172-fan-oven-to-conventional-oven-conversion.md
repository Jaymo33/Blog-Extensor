<h2>172 Fan Oven vs Conventional Oven: Key Differences</h2>
<p>The main difference between 172 fan oven and conventional oven cooking is air circulation. Fan ovens distribute heat more efficiently, hence the lower temperature setting. When converting 172 fan oven to conventional oven, you're compensating for this difference in heat distribution. This ensures your food cooks at the same rate regardless of oven type.</p>

<p>Another factor to consider is oven positioning. In conventional ovens, the middle rack usually gives the most even heat, while fan ovens are more consistent throughout.
