<h2>Understanding Cup Measurements for Milk</h2>  
<p>When converting 935 ml of milk to cups, it's essential to know which cup measurement you're using. The US customary cup holds 240 ml, while the UK imperial cup is slightly larger at 284 ml. This means 935 ml translates to roughly 3.95 US cups or 3.3 UK cups. For air fryer recipes, especially when making custards or batters, this distinction matters.</p>  

<p>Many modern recipes, including those on <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>our recipe section</a>, specify which cup measurement they use.
