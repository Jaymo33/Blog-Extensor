<h3>How to Measure 635 ml of Almond Milk Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a measuring jug, you can use standard cups to measure 635 ml of almond milk. A UK metric cup holds 250 ml, so 2.5 cups would be 625 ml—just shy of 635 ml. For precision, add an extra tablespoon (about 15 ml) to reach the full amount. This method works well for most recipes, from pancakes to creamy pasta sauces.</p>
