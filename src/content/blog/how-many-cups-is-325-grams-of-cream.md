<p>Cream is a versatile ingredient used in everything from desserts to savoury dishes. When converting 325 grams of cream to cups, it's important to consider the density and type of cream. <h2>UK vs US Cup Measurements</h2>
<p>It's worth noting that UK and US cup measurements differ slightly. In the UK, 1 cup is 250 ml, while in the US, it's 240 ml. This means 325 grams of cream converts to 1.35 cups in the UK and 1.37 cups in the US. While the difference is minimal, it can matter in recipes where precision is key, such as baking or making sauces.</p>

<h2>Tips for Measuring Cream</h2>
<p>To measure 325 grams of cream accurately, use a kitchen scale for the most precise results.
