<h3>How to measure worcestershire sauce correctly</h3>  
<p>For best results, use a digital kitchen scale to weigh worcestershire sauce in grams. If you don’t have one, a standard UK tablespoon holds about 15ml, which roughly translates to 15g for worcestershire sauce. However, viscosity can vary, so weighing is always more accurate.
