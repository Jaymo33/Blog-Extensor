<h2>How to Measure 485 ml of Single Cream Correctly</h2>  
<p>To measure 485 ml of single cream accurately, use a liquid measuring cup with clear markings. Pour the cream slowly to the 485 ml line, checking at eye level to avoid over-pouring. If you don’t have a measuring cup, a kitchen scale can also help—485 ml weighs approximately 485 grams.</p>

<h3>US vs UK Cup Measurements</h3>  
<p>It’s worth noting that US and UK cup measurements differ slightly. A US cup is 240 ml, while a UK cup is 250 ml. For 485 ml of single cream, this means roughly 2 US cups or just under 2 UK cups. Always check your recipe’s origin to avoid discrepancies.</p>
