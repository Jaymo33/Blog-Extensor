<h2>How to Measure 210 ml of Half and Half</h2>  
<p>If you don’t have a measuring jug, you can use a standard cup to measure 210 ml of half and half. A US cup holds 240 ml, so 210 ml is just under a full cup. For UK measurements, a metric cup is 250 ml, making 210 ml roughly 0.84 cups. Always check your recipe’s origin to ensure the correct conversion.</p>
