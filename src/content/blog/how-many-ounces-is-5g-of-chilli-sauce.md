<h2>Why convert 5g of chilli sauce to ounces?</h2>
<p>Many UK recipes use grams, but ounces are still common in older cookbooks or international recipes. Converting 5g of chilli sauce to ounces (0.18 oz) helps you follow instructions precisely. <h2>How to measure 5g of chilli sauce accurately</h2>
<p>Measuring small amounts like 5g of chilli sauce can be tricky without the right tools. A digital kitchen scale is the most accurate way, but if you don’t have one, use a measuring spoon. Roughly, 5g of chilli sauce is about one teaspoon, but this can vary depending on the sauce’s thickness. For air fryer recipes, consistency matters, so weigh it if possible.</p>
<p>If you’re preparing a larger batch, scale up the measurement accordingly. For example, 10g of chilli sauce would be 0.35 ounces. This precision ensures your air fryer dishes turn out perfectly every time. <h3>Common uses for 5g of chilli sauce in air fryer recipes</h3>
<p>5g of chilli sauce is often used in marinades, glazes, or dips for air fryer dishes. It’s enough to add flavour without overwhelming the dish. Try it in a glaze for air-fried chicken wings or as a dipping sauce for spring rolls. The small quantity ensures the sauce doesn’t make the food soggy, which is crucial for air fryer cooking.</p>

<h3>Tips for storing chilli sauce</h3>
<p>To keep your chilli sauce fresh, store it in an airtight container in the fridge. This ensures the flavour remains intense, so 5g will still pack a punch. If you’re using homemade sauce, label it with the date to track freshness. Proper storage means your measurements will always be consistent.</p>
