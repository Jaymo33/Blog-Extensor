<h2>How to measure 15g of whipping cream accurately</h2>
<p>The most precise way to measure 15g of whipping cream is using digital kitchen scales. These give you exact gram measurements that you can then convert to ounces if needed. For those without scales, 15g is roughly one tablespoon of whipping cream, though this can vary slightly depending on the cream's thickness.</p>
