<h3>How to measure powdered milk accurately</h3>  
<p>To measure 4 tbsp of powdered milk in grams, use a digital kitchen scale for the most precise results. Spoon the powdered milk into the tablespoon without packing it down, then level it off with a knife. This method avoids over-measuring, which can make batters too dense or sauces too thick. For air fryer recipes requiring exact proportions, weighing ingredients is always the best approach.</p>
