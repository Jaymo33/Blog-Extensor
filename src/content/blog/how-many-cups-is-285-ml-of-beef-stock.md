<h3>US vs UK Cup Measurements for Beef Stock</h3>  
<p>It's important to note that US and UK cup measurements differ slightly. In the US, 1 cup equals 240 ml, making 285 ml roughly 1.2 cups. In the UK, 1 cup is 250 ml, so 285 ml is about 1.14 cups. This small difference can impact recipes, so always check which measurement system your recipe uses. For air fryer cooking, consistency is key, so stick to one system throughout.</p>

<h2>Common Mistakes When Measuring Beef Stock</h2>  
<p>One frequent error is confusing millilitres with fluid ounces, which can lead to incorrect measurements. 285 ml is about 9.6 fluid ounces, not to be mistaken with weight ounces. Another mistake is eyeballing the measurement, which often results in too much or too little liquid. For consistent air fryer results, always measure precisely, especially when working with rich ingredients like beef stock.</p>
