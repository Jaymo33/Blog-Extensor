<h3>US vs UK Cup Measurements for Beef Stock</h3>  
<p>It's important to note that US and UK cup measurements differ slightly. In the US, 1 cup equals 240 ml, making 285 ml roughly 1.2 cups. In the UK, 1 cup is 250 ml, so 285 ml is about 1.14 cups. This small difference can impact recipes, so always check which measurement system your recipe uses. For air fryer cooking, consistency is key, so stick to one system throughout.</p>
