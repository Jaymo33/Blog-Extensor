<h2>How to Measure 285 ml of Half And Half</h2>
<p>Measuring liquids like half and half can be tricky, but with the right tools, it’s straightforward. A liquid measuring cup is your best bet for accuracy. Simply pour the half and half up to the 285 ml mark, or use our conversion to measure out 1.2 cups. For more tips on measuring ingredients, check out our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-conversion-chart>air fryer conversion chart</a>.</p>
<p>If you don’t have a measuring cup, you can use a kitchen scale. Half and half weighs roughly 1.03 grams per ml, so 285 ml would be about 293 grams. This method is especially handy if you’re scaling recipes up or down.</p>

<p>In the UK, recipes often use millilitres, while in the US, cups are more common. Knowing that 285 ml of half and half equals 1.2 cups can help you follow recipes from either country. This is particularly useful if you’re using an air fryer, as many recipes come from international sources.</p>
