<p>Use a standard UK tablespoon (15ml) to measure your milk. Fill it to the brim without overflowing for an accurate 15ml per tablespoon. Three level tablespoons will give you roughly 45 grams of milk. For sticky ingredients, a digital kitchen scale is even more reliable.</p>
