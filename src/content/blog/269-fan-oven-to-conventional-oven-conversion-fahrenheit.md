<h2>Why Convert 269 Fan Oven to Fahrenheit?</h2>
<p>Fan ovens, also known as convection ovens, use a fan to circulate hot air, reducing cooking times and temperatures. A 269°F fan oven setting often requires a higher temperature in a conventional oven to achieve similar results. This is because the moving air in a fan oven transfers heat more efficiently. If you're using a recipe designed for a fan oven but only have a conventional oven, this conversion is essential. <h3>How to Adjust Cooking Times</h3>
<p>When converting 269 fan oven to Fahrenheit, remember that cooking times may also need adjustment. For example, if a recipe calls for 20 minutes in a fan oven at 269°F, try 22-23 minutes in a conventional oven at 300°F. Always use a timer and check for doneness with a skewer or thermometer.</p>
