<h3>How to Measure Oat Milk Correctly</h3>
<p>For the most accurate measurement, use a liquid measuring jug with millilitre markings. Pour the oat milk to the 473 ml line, and you’ve got the equivalent of 2.0 cups. If you don’t have a jug, a kitchen scale can help—1 ml of oat milk weighs roughly 1 gram. This method is handy when preparing <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/breakfast>breakfast</a> recipes like pancakes or smoothies.</p>
