<h3>How to Measure Evaporated Milk Correctly</h3>  
<p>To measure 2.75 cups of evaporated milk accurately, use a liquid measuring cup. Fill it to the 2.75-cup mark, ensuring the milk sits level for precision. If you don’t have a measuring cup, a kitchen scale can help—650 ml of evaporated milk weighs roughly 680 grams.
