<p>Using the right amount of vinegar in your air fryer recipes can impact flavour and texture. Too much can overpower the dish, while too little might leave it bland. <h2>How to convert 15g of vinegar to ounces</h2>
<p>Converting grams to ounces is straightforward. Since 1 gram equals roughly 0.035 ounces, multiplying 15g by this factor gives you 0.53 ounces. This conversion is particularly useful for UK cooks who might be following international recipes or using scales with different units.</p>

<h3>Tools for measuring vinegar</h3>
<p>A digital kitchen scale is the most accurate way to measure 15g of vinegar. If you don’t have one, a measuring spoon can work in a pinch. Just remember that 15g of vinegar is roughly one tablespoon, but this can vary slightly depending on the vinegar’s density.</p>

<p>Vinegar is a versatile ingredient in air fryer recipes. From marinades to glazes, 15g of vinegar can add a bright, acidic note to dishes like air fryer salmon or crispy vegetables. It’s also great for deglazing pans or adding a tangy finish to sauces.</p>
