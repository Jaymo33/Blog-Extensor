<h3>How to measure garlic paste accurately</h3>  
<p>Using a digital kitchen scale is the easiest way to measure 10g of garlic paste. If you don't have one, a standard teaspoon holds roughly 5g, so two level teaspoons will give you 10g (or 0.35 oz). For sticky ingredients like garlic paste, lightly oiling the spoon can help it slide off easily. Always level off the spoon for the most accurate measurement.</p>
