<h2>Converting Cups to Grams for Olive Oil</h2>  
<p>Converting 2.5 cups of olive oil to grams is straightforward once you know the standard weight per cup. One cup of olive oil typically weighs around 216 grams, so multiplying by 2.5 gives you 540 grams. This measurement is particularly useful for baking, where precision is key to achieving the perfect texture.</p>
