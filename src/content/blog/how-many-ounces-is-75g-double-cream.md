<h3>How to Measure 75g of Double Cream Without a Scale</h3>

<h3>Tips for Storing and Handling Double Cream</h3>  
<p>Double cream has a high fat content, so it’s prone to spoilage if not stored properly. Always keep it refrigerated and use it within a few days of opening. Thaw in the fridge before using, and give it a good stir to restore consistency.</p>
