<h3>US vs UK Cup Measurements</h3>  
<p>It’s important to note that US and UK cup measurements differ slightly. A US cup is 240 ml, while a UK cup is 284 ml. This means 135 ml of apple cider vinegar is about 0.57 US cups or 0.48 UK cups. Always check which measurement system your recipe uses to avoid mistakes.</p>
