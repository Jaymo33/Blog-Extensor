<h3>How to Measure Double Cream Correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate conversion, use a liquid measuring jug with ml markings. Scooping double cream with a cup measure can lead to inconsistencies due to its thickness. If you don’t have a jug, remember that 1 US cup equals 240 ml, while 1 UK cup is 284 ml. This means 3.25 US cups of double cream is 780 ml, whereas 3.25 UK cups is 923 ml—so always check which cup measurement your recipe uses.
