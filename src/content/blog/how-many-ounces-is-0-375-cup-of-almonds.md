<h2>How to measure 0.375 cup of almonds</h2>
<p>Measuring 0.375 cup of almonds is simple with the right tools. Use a dry measuring cup for the most accurate results, filling it just below the rim. If you don’t have a 0.375 cup measure, use 3/8 cup or 6 tablespoons instead. For weight, a kitchen scale will confirm you’ve got 1.5 ounces. This method works for whole, sliced, or slivered almonds.</p>
