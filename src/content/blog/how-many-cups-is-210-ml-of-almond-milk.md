<h2>Understanding Almond Milk Measurements</h2>  
<p>Almond milk is a popular dairy-free alternative used in many recipes, from baking to smoothies. Measuring it correctly ensures your dishes turn out as intended. For air fryer recipes, accuracy is key, especially when dealing with batters or sauces. A slight miscalculation can affect texture and taste.</p>  

<p>If you're using 210 ml of almond milk, it's helpful to know the exact cup equivalent. In the US, 1 cup equals 240 ml, making 210 ml approximately 0.89 cups. In the UK, where 1 cup is 250 ml, 210 ml converts to 0.88 cups. Always check your recipe's origin to avoid confusion.</p>
