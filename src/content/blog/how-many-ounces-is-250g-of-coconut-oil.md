<h3>How to Measure 250g of Coconut Oil Accurately</h3>  
<p>To measure 250g of coconut oil, use a digital kitchen scale for precision. If your scale displays ounces, it should read roughly 8.82 oz for 250g. Softened coconut oil is easier to measure than solid or melted oil, so let it sit at room temperature first. For sticky situations, lightly grease your measuring spoon or cup to avoid residue.</p>

<h3>Tips for Storing Coconut Oil for Air Frying</h3>  
<p>Store your 250g of coconut oil in a cool, dark place to maintain its quality. If it melts and solidifies repeatedly, it’s still safe to use but may lose some texture. For long-term storage, keep it in an airtight container away from heat sources. This ensures your coconut oil stays fresh for all your air fryer adventures.</p>
