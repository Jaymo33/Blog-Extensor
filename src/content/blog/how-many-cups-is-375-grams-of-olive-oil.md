<h2>Converting 375 Grams of Olive Oil to Cups</h2>  
<p>To convert 375 grams of olive oil to cups, divide the weight by the density of olive oil (224 grams per cup). The result is approximately 1.6 cups. This conversion is useful for scaling recipes or adjusting portion sizes. For instance, if a recipe calls for 750 grams of olive oil, you’d need about 3.2 cups. Always double-check measurements to avoid mistakes.</p>
