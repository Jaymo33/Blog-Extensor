<h3>How to measure minced onion for air fryer recipes</h3>  
<p>Measuring 500g of minced onion is straightforward with a kitchen scale. If your recipe calls for ounces, simply multiply the grams by 0.035274 to get the equivalent. For 500g minced onion to ounces, this calculation gives you 17.64 oz. This method works for all types of onions, whether finely minced or coarsely chopped.</p>
