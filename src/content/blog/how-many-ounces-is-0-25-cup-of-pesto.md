<h3>How to measure 0.25 cup of pesto</h3>  
<p>Use a liquid measuring cup for pesto, scraping it level with a spatula for accuracy. If you’re using a kitchen scale, 2 ounces is your target. For sticky ingredients like pesto, lightly greasing the measuring cup can help it slide out easily. This method works well for recipes like air fryer pesto salmon or stuffed mushrooms.</p>

<h2>Storing leftover pesto</h2>  
<p>If you’ve measured out 0.25 cup of pesto and have leftovers, store it in an airtight container with a thin layer of olive oil on top to prevent browning. It keeps in the fridge for up to a week or can be frozen for longer storage. Check out our guide on <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-cleaner>cleaning your air fryer</a> after cooking with oily ingredients like pesto.</p>
