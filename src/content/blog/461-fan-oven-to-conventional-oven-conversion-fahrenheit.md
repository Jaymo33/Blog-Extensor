<p>Fan ovens circulate hot air constantly, cooking food faster and more evenly than conventional ovens. The 461 fan oven to fahrenheit conversion reflects this efficiency difference. Most manufacturers recommend reducing temperatures by 20-25°C (36-45°F) when switching from fan to conventional ovens.
