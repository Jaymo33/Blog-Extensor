Unlike standard oven baking pans, these trays are sized specifically for air fryers, ensuring even heat circulation for perfect results every time. Whether you're using a small baking tray for individual portions or a full bake tray set for family meals, they're essential for air fryer enthusiasts. Unlike bulky baking baking pans meant for conventional ovens, these trays are designed with air fryer cooking mechanics in mind, ensuring crisper results with less oil. Many come as part of versatile bake tray sets including racks and dividers for multi-zone cooking.</p><p id="">For those who love meal prepping, small baking trays for air fryer allow you to cook multiple components simultaneously without flavour transfer. Imagine roasting vegetables on one level while baking chicken above - it's all possible with the right accessories. The raised edges prevent drips from messy foods like marinated meats or juicy fruits in desserts. Most standard trays measure 18-20cm in diameter for 4-5L appliances, while mini air fryers need 15cm trays. Depth matters too - opt for 2-3cm sides for roasting but shallower 1cm designs for delicate pastries. Stackable bake tray sets maximise capacity in larger models, letting you cook an entire meal's components at once without sacrificing texture or flavour.</p><h2 id="">Care and maintenance tips</h2><p id="">To prolong your air fryer baking tray's lifespan, always allow it to cool before washing to prevent warping. Hand washing preserves non-stick coatings better than dishwashers, using soft sponges rather than abrasive scrubbers. For stubborn residue, soak trays in warm soapy water before gently cleaning - never use metal utensils that could scratch surfaces. Store trays flat or hanging to prevent bending, and consider parchment liners for sticky foods like melted cheese or caramel.</p><p id="">Regular inspection for coating damage is crucial, as compromised non-stick surfaces can affect cooking performance and food safety.
