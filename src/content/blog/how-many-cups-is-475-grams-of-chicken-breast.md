<h3>How to Measure 475 Grams of Chicken Breast in Cups</h3>  
<p>To measure 475 grams of chicken breast in cups, start by dicing or shredding the meat. Pack it lightly into a measuring cup without pressing too hard, as this can affect accuracy. For best results, use a kitchen scale to weigh the chicken first, then transfer it to cups if needed.</p>
