<h3>UK vs. US Cup Measurements</h3>  
<p>It's important to note that UK and US cup measurements differ slightly. In the UK, 1 cup equals 284 ml, while in the US, it's 240 ml. This means 835 ml of lime juice converts to 3.5 UK cups or 3.52 US cups. For air fryer recipes, this small difference usually won't affect the outcome, but it's good to know for precision baking.</p>
