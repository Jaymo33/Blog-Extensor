<h3>How to Measure Almond Milk Correctly</h3>  
<p>To measure 3.75 cups of almond milk accurately, use a liquid measuring jug with clear ml markings. Fill it to the 887.5 ml line for perfect results. Avoid using dry measuring cups, as they can lead to slight variations. For sticky ingredients like almond milk, lightly grease the measuring cup to ensure all the liquid pours out smoothly.</p>

<h2>Tips for Storing Leftover Almond Milk</h2>  
<p>If you don’t use all 887.5 ml of almond milk at once, store the remainder in an airtight container in the fridge. It typically stays fresh for 4-5 days. Shake well before reusing, as separation is natural. For longer storage, consider freezing almond milk in ice cube trays for future recipes.</p>
