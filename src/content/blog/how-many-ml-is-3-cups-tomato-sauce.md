<h3>How to Measure Tomato Sauce Correctly</h3>  
<p>To measure 3.0 cups of tomato sauce accurately, use a liquid measuring cup with clear ml markings. Fill it to the 710 ml line for the perfect amount. If you don’t have a measuring cup, a kitchen scale can help—tomato sauce weighs roughly 240 grams per cup, so 3.0 cups would be about 720 grams. This small variance won’t significantly affect most air fryer recipes, but for baking, precision is crucial. Always level off the sauce with a spatula for the most accurate measurement.</p>

<h3>Tips for Storing Leftover Tomato Sauce</h3>  
<p>If you have leftover tomato sauce after measuring out 3.0 cups (710 ml), store it in an airtight container in the fridge for up to five days. Just place it in an oven-safe dish and heat at 180°C for a few minutes.
