<h3>How to Measure Rolled Oats Correctly</h3>
<p>To measure 275 grams of rolled oats in cups, use the spoon-and-level method for the most accuracy. Lightly spoon the oats into a measuring cup without packing them down, then level off the top with a straight edge. This ensures you don't end up with more oats than needed, which could throw off your recipe. For air fryer cooking, this method is particularly useful when making dishes like oat-crusted fish or crispy granola clusters.</p>

<h3>Common Mistakes When Measuring Rolled Oats</h3>
<p>One of the most frequent errors is packing the oats too tightly into the measuring cup, which can result in using significantly more than the recipe calls for. Another mistake is using a liquid measuring cup instead of dry measuring cups, which can affect the accuracy. When converting 275 grams of rolled oats to cups, always use proper dry measuring tools for the best results. This is especially important for air fryer recipes where precise ratios affect cooking performance.</p>
