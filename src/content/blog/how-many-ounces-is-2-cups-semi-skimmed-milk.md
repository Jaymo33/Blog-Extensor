<p>2 cups of semi skimmed milk equals approximately 16.9 ounces (or 480 millilitres). This conversion is essential for precise cooking and baking, especially when using an air fryer where measurements can impact texture and consistency. Whether you’re whipping up a creamy sauce or a fluffy cake, knowing the exact weight ensures perfect results every time.</p>  

<h2>Why Accurate Measurements Matter in Air Fryer Cooking</h2>  
<p>When using an air fryer, precise measurements are crucial because the cooking process relies heavily on even heat distribution. Too much or too little liquid, like semi skimmed milk, can affect how your dish turns out. For example, adding too much milk to a batter might make it too runny, while too little could leave it dry. Accurate conversions, such as 2 cups of semi skimmed milk to ounces, help maintain the right balance.</p>

<h3>How to Measure Semi Skimmed Milk Correctly</h3>  
<p>To measure 2 cups of semi skimmed milk accurately, use a liquid measuring cup with clear markings. Fill it to the 2-cup line without overpouring, as excess milk can throw off your recipe. If you don’t have a measuring cup, a kitchen scale can help—just weigh out 16.9 ounces for the perfect amount.</p>  

<h2>Common Uses for Semi Skimmed Milk in Air Fryer Recipes</h2>  
<p>Semi skimmed milk is a versatile ingredient in air fryer cooking. It’s often used in batters for dishes like crispy chicken or fish, adding moisture without making the coating soggy. It’s also great for creamy sauces, such as those in pasta bakes or gratins, which cook beautifully in an air fryer.</p>

Whether you’re meal prepping or cooking for a crowd, accurate measurements ensure consistent results.</p>  

<h3>Converting Other Milk Types to Ounces</h3>  
<p>If you’re using whole milk or skimmed milk instead of semi skimmed, the weight may vary slightly due to fat content. However, 2 cups of any milk will still be close to 16.9 ounces. Always double-check if a recipe specifies a particular type of milk, as substitutions can alter the final dish.</p>  

<h2>Tips for Storing and Using Semi Skimmed Milk</h2>  
<p>To keep semi skimmed milk fresh, store it in the fridge at or below 4°C. If you’re using it in air fryer recipes, let it come to room temperature first for better mixing. Cold milk can cause batters to thicken unevenly, affecting the cooking process.</p>
