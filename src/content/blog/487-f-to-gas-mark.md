<h3>Tips for Cooking at High Temperatures</h3>
<p>Cooking at 487°F or Gas Mark 9 requires attention to detail to avoid burning or uneven results. Always use a light coating of oil to prevent sticking and promote even crisping. Avoid overcrowding the air fryer basket, as this can lead to steaming instead of crisping. For larger items, consider flipping or shaking them halfway through cooking. These small adjustments make a big difference in achieving restaurant-quality results at home.</p>
