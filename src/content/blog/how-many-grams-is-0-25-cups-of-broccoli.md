<p>0.25 cups of broccoli equals approximately 22 grams. This measurement is key for air fryer recipes where precision ensures even cooking and perfect texture. Whether you're whipping up a quick side or adding broccoli to a main dish, knowing this conversion helps avoid guesswork.</p>

<p>Air fryers rely on hot air circulation to cook food evenly, so ingredient quantities matter. Too much broccoli can lead to steaming instead of crisping, while too little might burn. <h3>How to Measure 0.25 Cups of Broccoli</h3>  
<p>For accuracy, lightly pack chopped broccoli into a measuring cup without crushing it. If you don’t have cups, a kitchen scale simplifies things—just weigh out 22 grams. This method works for fresh or frozen broccoli, though frozen may need slight adjustments due to ice content.</p>
