<h3>How to Measure 0.33 Cup of Cranberries</h3>  
<p>Measuring 0.33 cup of cranberries is simple with the right tools. Use a standard measuring cup and fill it just under a third for accuracy. If you don’t have a 1/3 cup measure, you can eyeball it by filling a 1-cup measure to roughly one-third of its capacity. For even greater precision, a kitchen scale will confirm the weight as 1.41 oz.</p>
