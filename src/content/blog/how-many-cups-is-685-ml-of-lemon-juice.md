<h3>US vs. UK Cup Measurements</h3>
<p>Note that US and UK cup measurements differ slightly. In the UK, 1 cup is 284 ml, while in the US, it's 240 ml. For 685 ml of lemon juice, this translates to 2.9 US cups or 2.4 UK cups. Double-check your recipe's origin to avoid discrepancies.</p>
