<h3>UK vs. US Cup Measurements for Milk</h3>  
<p>In the UK, a standard metric cup holds 240 ml, while the US customary cup is slightly smaller at 236.59 ml. For 985 ml of milk, this means 4.16 UK cups or 4.23 US cups. The difference is minor but worth noting for exact recipes, especially in air fryer cooking where precision matters.</p>
