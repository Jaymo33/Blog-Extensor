<h2>How to Measure 210 ml of Heavy Cream</h2>  
<p>Measuring 210 ml of heavy cream is simple with the right tools. A liquid measuring cup with millilitre markings is the most accurate way. If you only have a standard cup measure, remember that 1 cup equals roughly 236.59 ml, so 210 ml is just under a full cup.</p>  

<h3>Using a Kitchen Scale</h3>  
<p>For even greater precision, a kitchen scale can help. Heavy cream weighs approximately 1 gram per millilitre, so 210 ml should weigh around 210 grams. This method eliminates any guesswork and ensures your recipes turn out perfectly every time.</p>
