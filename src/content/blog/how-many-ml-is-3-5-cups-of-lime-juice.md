<h2>How to Measure Lime Juice Correctly</h2>
<p>Measuring lime juice can be tricky, especially if you're using fresh limes. To get an accurate 3.5 cups (828 ml), squeeze the juice from fresh limes and pour it into a measuring cup. Avoid including pulp or seeds, as they can throw off your measurements. If you're using bottled lime juice, simply pour it directly into the measuring cup.</p>
