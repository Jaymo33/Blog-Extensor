<p>When working with a 4 kg turkey crown, food safety should always be your top priority, especially when using relatively new cooking methods like air frying. The internal temperature should reach at least 75°C in the thickest part of the meat to ensure any harmful bacteria are destroyed. Use separate cutting boards and utensils for raw turkey to avoid cross-contamination with other foods in your kitchen.
