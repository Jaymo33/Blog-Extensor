<h3>How to Measure 10g of Powdered Milk Accurately</h3>  
<p>Using a digital kitchen scale is the easiest way to measure 10g of powdered milk. If you don’t have one, a standard measuring spoon can help—roughly two tablespoons of powdered milk equal 10g.
