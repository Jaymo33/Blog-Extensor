<h3>How to Measure 10g of Powdered Milk Accurately</h3>  
<p>Using a digital kitchen scale is the easiest way to measure 10g of powdered milk. If you don’t have one, a standard measuring spoon can help—roughly two tablespoons of powdered milk equal 10g. However, for the most precise results, especially in baking, a scale is recommended. This ensures your air fryer recipes turn out perfectly every time.</p>

<h3>Tips for Storing Powdered Milk</h3>  
<p>To keep your powdered milk fresh, store it in an airtight container in a cool, dry place. This prevents clumping and preserves its quality. If you’re using it frequently, consider measuring out 10g portions in advance for convenience. Proper storage ensures your air fryer recipes always taste their best.</p>
