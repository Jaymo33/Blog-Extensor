<h3>How to measure 2.5 tbsp of porridge oats without a scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, use a standard UK tablespoon and level off the oats with a straight edge for accuracy. Scooping directly from the bag can lead to inconsistent amounts, so always spoon the oats into the measuring spoon. For 2.5 tbsp, fill the tablespoon twice, then half-fill it a third time.</p>
