<h3>How to Measure 10 ml of Coconut Oil Without a Scale</h3>
<p>If you don’t have a measuring jug or scale handy, you can still measure 10 ml of coconut oil accurately. One tablespoon equals roughly 15 ml, so 10 ml is just under two-thirds of a tablespoon. For those who prefer cups, remember that 0.042 cups is your target. This method is handy for quick adjustments, especially when you’re mid-recipe and need a fast solution.</p>

<h2>Tips for Storing and Using Coconut Oil in Your Air Fryer</h2>
