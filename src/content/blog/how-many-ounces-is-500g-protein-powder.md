<h2>How to Measure 500g of Protein Powder Accurately</h2>  
<p>To measure 500g of protein powder in ounces, you’ll need a reliable kitchen scale. Digital scales with both gram and ounce settings are ideal. Simply scoop your protein powder into a bowl on the scale until it hits 500g, then switch the unit to ounces to see the conversion. This method eliminates guesswork and ensures precision.</p>  

<p>If you don’t have a scale, you can use volume measurements, but these are less accurate. Protein powder can vary in density, so 500g might not always fill the same cup size.
