<h3>How to Measure 0.75 Cups of Milk Without a Measuring Cup</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a measuring cup, you can still measure 0.75 cups (177 ml) of milk using a metric jug or kitchen scale. One cup of milk weighs about 240 grams, so 0.75 cups would be roughly 180 grams. While this isn’t exact, it’s close enough for most recipes. Alternatively, use tablespoons—12 tablespoons equal 0.75 cups.
