<h3>How to measure Worcestershire sauce without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can still measure 25g (0.88 oz) of Worcestershire sauce using tablespoons. One tablespoon holds roughly 15g (0.53 oz), so 1.5 tablespoons will get you close to 25g. For even more precision, use a measuring spoon set or a graduated liquid measure. This method works well for recipes like <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/chicken>air fryer chicken wings</a>, where the sauce is a key ingredient.</p>
