<p>Air fryers rely on even cooking, and incorrect measurements can lead to uneven results. Peanut butter, being dense, needs precise weighing to avoid overly sticky or dry outcomes. A slight variation in weight can affect texture, especially in baked goods like cookies or cakes. <p>Using a kitchen scale is the best way to measure 125g of peanut butter accurately. Scooping it directly from the jar can lead to inconsistencies due to air pockets. For sticky ingredients like peanut butter, lightly greasing your measuring spoon or cup helps release it easily. This small trick ensures you get the right amount every time.</p>  

<h3>How to convert 125g peanut butter to ounces</h3>  
<p>The conversion from grams to ounces is straightforward: 1 gram equals 0.035 ounces. Multiplying 125g by 0.035 gives you 4.41 ounces. This measurement is particularly useful for UK cooks following American recipes, which often use ounces. If you're preparing a <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/cookies>cookie recipe</a>, this precision ensures your treats turn out perfectly.</p>
