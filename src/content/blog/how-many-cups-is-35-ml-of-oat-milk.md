<h3>How to Measure 35 ml of Oat Milk Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>Not everyone has a kitchen scale, but you can still measure 35 ml of oat milk accurately. Use a liquid measuring cup with metric markings, or if you only have imperial cups, remember that 35 ml is just under 1/7 of a cup. A tablespoon holds about 15 ml, so 2 tablespoons plus 1 teaspoon will get you close to 35 ml.</p>
