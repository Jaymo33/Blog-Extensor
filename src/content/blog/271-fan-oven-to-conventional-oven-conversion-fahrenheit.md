<h2>Why Convert 271 Fan Oven to Conventional Oven?</h2>
<p>Fan ovens circulate hot air around the food, reducing cooking time and temperature. A conventional oven, on the other hand, relies on radiant heat, which is less efficient. Converting 271°F in a fan oven to 300°F in a conventional oven ensures your dish cooks properly without burning or undercooking. This is especially useful if you're following a recipe designed for a fan oven but only have a conventional one.
