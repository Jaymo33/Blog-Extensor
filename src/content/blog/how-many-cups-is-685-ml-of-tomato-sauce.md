<h3>How to Measure 685 ml of Tomato Sauce Without a Measuring Cup</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a measuring cup, use a standard mug, which typically holds about 240 ml (roughly 1 cup). For 685 ml of tomato sauce, fill the mug just under three times. Alternatively, weigh the sauce—1 ml of tomato sauce weighs roughly 1 gram, so 685 grams equals 685 ml. This method works well for sticky sauces that cling to measuring tools.</p>
