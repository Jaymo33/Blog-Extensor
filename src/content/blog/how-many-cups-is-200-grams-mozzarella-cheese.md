<h3>How to Measure Mozzarella Cheese Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t own a kitchen scale, use the spoon-and-level method for shredded mozzarella. Lightly fill your measuring cup with cheese, then level it off with a knife for accuracy. For cubed mozzarella, gently press the pieces into the cup without compacting them too much. Remember, 200 grams of mozzarella cheese in cups is about 2 cups when shredded, but slightly less if cubed. <h3>Does Mozzarella Type Affect the Cup Measurement?</h3>  
<p>Yes, the type of mozzarella cheese can slightly change the cup measurement. Fresh mozzarella, packed in water, is denser and may weigh more per cup compared to low-moisture shredded varieties. For 200 grams of fresh mozzarella, you’ll need about 1.5 cups when diced. Pre-shredded bagged cheese often includes anti-caking agents, which can make it fluffier—so 200 grams might fill closer to 2.5 cups.
