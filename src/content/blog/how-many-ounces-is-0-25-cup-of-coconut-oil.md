<h2>How to Measure 0.25 Cup of Coconut Oil</h2>
<p>Measuring coconut oil can be tricky since it changes texture based on temperature. At room temperature, coconut oil is solid, but it melts easily when heated. To measure 0.25 cup (2 oz) accurately, you can use a liquid measuring cup for melted oil or pack it firmly into a dry measuring cup if solid.
