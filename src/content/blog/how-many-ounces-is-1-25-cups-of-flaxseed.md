<p>Getting the right amount of flaxseed ensures your dishes turn out perfectly every time. Too much or too little can affect texture, taste, and even cooking time in your air fryer. <h3>How to Measure Flaxseed Correctly</h3>  
<p>To measure 1.25 cups of flaxseed, spoon it lightly into a measuring cup without packing it down. Level off the top with a straight edge for accuracy. If you’re converting to ounces, remember that 1 cup of flaxseed weighs roughly 3.6 ounces, so 1.25 cups equals about 4.5 ounces. This method works for UK measurements too, making it easy to follow any recipe.</p>
