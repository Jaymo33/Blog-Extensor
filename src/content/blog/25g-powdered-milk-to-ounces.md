<h3>How to measure 25g of powdered milk accurately</h3>  
<p>For the most precise measurement, use digital kitchen scales set to grams. Scoop the powdered milk into a bowl until the scale reads 25g. If you don’t have scales, 25g is roughly 5 level teaspoons, but this can vary slightly depending on how tightly packed the powder is. For air fryer recipes, accuracy ensures even cooking and perfect texture, whether you’re making a creamy sauce or a fluffy cake.</p>
