<h3>How to Measure 0.25 Cups of Broccoli</h3>  
<p>For accuracy, lightly pack chopped broccoli into a measuring cup without crushing it. If you don’t have cups, a kitchen scale simplifies things—just weigh out 22 grams. This method works for fresh or frozen broccoli, though frozen may need slight adjustments due to ice content.</p>
