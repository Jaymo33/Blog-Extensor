<h3>How to Slice Mushrooms for Consistent Measurements</h3>  
<p>Uniform slices ensure even cooking in the air fryer. Aim for ¼-inch thickness to match standard cup-to-ounce conversions. Rinse mushrooms briefly and pat dry before slicing to prevent excess moisture, which can skew weight. For best results, use a sharp knife or mandoline for precision.</p>
