<h3>How to measure 2.5 cups of canned beans</h3>  
<p>Start by draining the beans thoroughly in a colander to remove excess liquid. Gently shake off any remaining moisture before transferring them to a measuring cup. Fill the cup to the brim without packing the beans down too tightly. Level off the top with a straight edge for accuracy. Repeat until you’ve measured 2.5 cups, which should weigh roughly 20 ounces.</p>

<h3>Common mistakes when measuring canned beans</h3>  
<p>Avoid measuring beans straight from the can without draining, as the liquid adds unnecessary weight. Similarly, packing beans too tightly into the cup leads to inaccuracies.
