<p>Getting the right amount of chopped spinach ensures your air fryer dishes cook evenly. Too much can lead to soggy results, while too little might leave your meal lacking flavour. <h3>How to measure chopped spinach correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate 3 cups to ounces conversion, lightly pack the spinach into your measuring cup. Don't press it down too firmly, as this can compress the leaves and throw off your measurement. If you're using frozen chopped spinach, thaw and drain it thoroughly before measuring.
