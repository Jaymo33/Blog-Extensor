<h3>How to measure chopped garlic correctly</h3>  
<p>For consistent results, lightly pack the chopped garlic into a measuring cup without pressing too hard. A heaped cup can skew the weight, so level it off with a knife. If you’re scaling recipes, a kitchen scale is handy for verifying 2.5 cups of chopped garlic to ounces.</p>
