<h2>How to measure 1.5 cups of cream without a scale</h2>
<p>If you don't have a kitchen scale, you can still measure 1.5 cups of cream accurately. Use a liquid measuring cup and fill it to the 1.5-cup mark, ensuring the cream is level with the measurement line. Avoid packing the cream down, as this can lead to over-measuring. For thicker creams like double cream, gently spoon it into the cup to avoid air pockets. Remember, 1.5 cups of cream to oz is always 12 ounces, regardless of the cream's thickness.</p>
