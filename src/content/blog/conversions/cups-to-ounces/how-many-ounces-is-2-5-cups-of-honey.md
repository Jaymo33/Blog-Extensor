<p>2.5 cups of honey equals approximately 30 ounces (oz). This conversion is essential for air fryer recipes, baking, or meal prep where precision matters. Honey’s thick consistency means volume-to-weight conversions can vary slightly, but 2.5 cups reliably translates to 30 oz in both UK and US measurements.</p>

<h2>Why Accurate Honey Measurements Matter</h2>  
<p>When cooking with an air fryer, exact measurements ensure even cooking and perfect texture. Too much honey can make dishes overly sticky, while too little might leave them dry. For example, glazing <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/chicken>air fryer chicken</a> requires just the right balance—30 oz of honey for 2.5 cups guarantees consistency. Check out our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-conversion-chart>air fryer conversion chart</a> for other common ingredient swaps.</p>  

<p>Honey’s density means it weighs more than water—12 oz per cup versus 8 oz for water. This difference is crucial for recipes like <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/dessert>air fryer desserts</a>, where sticky sweetness needs careful portioning. Always use liquid measuring cups for honey, and level off the top with a spatula for accuracy.</p>  

<h3>Converting 2.5 Cups of Honey to Ounces</h3>  
<p>To convert 2.5 cups of honey to ounces, multiply the cup measurement by honey’s weight per cup (12 oz). The math is simple: 2.5 x 12 = 30 oz. This applies universally, whether you’re making <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>honey-glazed salmon</a> or baking treats. For sticky ingredients like honey, lightly greasing your measuring cup helps it slide out cleanly.</p>

<h3>Tips for Measuring Honey Perfectly</h3>  
<p>Use a clear liquid measuring cup placed on a flat surface for accuracy. Pour honey slowly to avoid air bubbles, and check at eye level. If your recipe calls for melted honey, measure it after melting—volume can expand slightly when heated. For sticky situations, keep a damp cloth nearby to wipe spills immediately.</p>
