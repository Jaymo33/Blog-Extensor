<h2>Tips for baking with yeast in an air fryer</h2>  
<p>Air fryers circulate hot air, which can dry out dough faster than a conventional oven. To prevent this, lightly cover your dough with foil during the first half of cooking. This helps retain moisture and ensures even rising. Always preheat your air fryer for yeast-based recipes to avoid a gummy texture.</p>
