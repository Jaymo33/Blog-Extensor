<h3>How to Measure Minced Onion Correctly</h3>  
<p>To measure 1 cup of minced onion, pack it lightly into a measuring cup without pressing too hard. Overpacking can lead to more onion than needed, skewing your recipe. If you’re using pre-minced onion from a jar, drain excess liquid first. Freshly minced onion offers the best flavour, so consider chopping your own for optimal results.</p>
