<h3>How to Measure Chia Seeds Correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate 1.25 cups to ounces conversion, use a kitchen scale. Scoop chia seeds lightly into a measuring cup without packing them down. Level off the top with a straight edge for consistency. If you don’t have a scale, remember that 1 cup of chia seeds weighs roughly 4.2 ounces, so 1.25 cups equals about 5.3 ounces. This method works for UK and US measurements alike.</p>
