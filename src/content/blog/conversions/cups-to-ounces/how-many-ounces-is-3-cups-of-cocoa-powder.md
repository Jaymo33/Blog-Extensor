<h3>How to measure cocoa powder correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate measurement, spoon the cocoa powder into your measuring cup and level it off with a knife. Avoid packing it down, as this can lead to using more cocoa powder than the recipe intends. If you're converting 3 cups of cocoa powder to ounces, this method ensures you get the correct 24-ounce equivalent.</p>
