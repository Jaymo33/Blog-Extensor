<h3>How to measure 2.5 cups of olive oil accurately</h3>  
<p>For the most precise measurement, use a liquid measuring cup with clear markings. Fill it to the 2.5-cup line, ensuring the oil sits level for an accurate read. If you’re using a kitchen scale, 2.5 cups of olive oil weighs roughly 20 oz (or 567 grams). This method is ideal for recipes where precision matters, like baking or marinades.</p>
