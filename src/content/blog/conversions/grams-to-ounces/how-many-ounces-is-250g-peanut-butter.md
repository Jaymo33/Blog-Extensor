<h2>How to measure peanut butter for air fryer recipes</h2>  
<p>Measuring sticky ingredients like peanut butter can be tricky. For the most accurate results, use a kitchen scale set to grams or ounces. If you don’t have one, spoon the peanut butter into a measuring cup and level it off. Avoid packing it down, as this can lead to over-measuring.</p>  

<h3>Converting grams to ounces for peanut butter</h3>  
<p>The general rule is that 1 gram equals 0.035 ounces, so 250g of peanut butter is roughly 8.82 ounces. This conversion is useful for recipes from the US or UK, where measurements may vary.
