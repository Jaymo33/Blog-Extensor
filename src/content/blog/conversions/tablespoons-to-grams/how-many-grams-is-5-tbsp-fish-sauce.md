<h3>How to measure fish sauce correctly</h3>  
<p>Use a liquid measuring spoon for tablespoons, not a dinner spoon. Pour fish sauce to the brim, then level it off with a knife. For sticky sauces, lightly grease the spoon first. If your recipe calls for 5 tbsp of fish sauce (75g), a kitchen scale removes guesswork.
