<h3>UK vs. US Cup Measurements</h3>  
<p>In the UK, 1 cup equals 284 ml, while in the US, it's 240 ml. This means 585 ml of vinegar is roughly 2.47 UK cups or 2.48 US cups. The difference is minimal but worth noting for precision. If you're using an American recipe with 585 ml of vinegar, stick to US cups for accuracy. For UK recipes, our conversion ensures you won’t go wrong.</p>
