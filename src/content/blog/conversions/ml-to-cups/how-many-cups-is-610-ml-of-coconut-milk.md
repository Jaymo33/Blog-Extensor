<h3>UK vs. US Cup Measurements</h3>  
<p>In the UK, 1 cup equals 284 ml, while in the US, it's 236.59 ml. This means 610 ml of coconut milk translates to roughly 2.57 UK cups or 2.58 US cups. The slight difference might not seem significant, but for baking or sauces, precision matters. Always check which measurement system your recipe uses to avoid mishaps.</p>
