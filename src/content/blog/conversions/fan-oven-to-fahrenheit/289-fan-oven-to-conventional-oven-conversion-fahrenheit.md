<h2>Why Convert 289 Fan Oven to Conventional Oven?</h2>
<p>Fan ovens and conventional ovens heat differently, which affects cooking times and results. A fan oven’s circulating air means it cooks about 20°C (or 36°F) cooler than a conventional oven. So, 289°F in a fan oven equates to 325°F in a conventional oven. This adjustment ensures your baked goods don’t overcook or dry out.
