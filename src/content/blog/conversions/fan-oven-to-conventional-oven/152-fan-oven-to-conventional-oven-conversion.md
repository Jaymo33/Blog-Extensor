<h2>Understanding Fan Oven vs Conventional Oven Temperatures</h2>
<p>Fan ovens, also known as convection ovens, operate differently from conventional ovens. The fan circulates hot air around the food, creating more even heat distribution. This means fan ovens typically cook food about 20°C faster than conventional ovens. When a recipe calls for 152 fan oven, you'll need to adjust the temperature for a conventional oven. The general rule is to add 20°C when converting from fan to conventional.</p>
