<h2>How to Measure 100 Grams of Cream Without a Scale</h2>
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can still measure 100 grams of cream using standard cups. A UK metric cup holds about 240ml, while a US cup holds 237ml. For 100 grams of cream, you’ll need just under half a cup in either system. Stir the cream lightly before measuring to ensure consistency. This method works well for recipes like <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>air fryer cakes</a> where precision matters.</p>
