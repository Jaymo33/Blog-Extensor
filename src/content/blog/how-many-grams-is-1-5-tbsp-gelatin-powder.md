<h3>How to measure gelatin powder correctly</h3>  
<p>To measure 1.5 tbsp of gelatin powder accurately, use a flat-edged utensil to level off the powder. Scoop the gelatin lightly to avoid compacting it, as this can lead to over-measuring. For the most precise results, a digital kitchen scale is best—simply weigh out 10.5 grams. If you don’t have a scale, spoon the gelatin into a tablespoon and level it carefully.
