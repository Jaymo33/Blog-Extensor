<h3>How to Measure 30g of Protein Powder Accurately</h3>  
<p>To measure 30g of protein powder in ounces, use a digital kitchen scale for precision. Set the scale to grams, scoop the powder until it reads 30g, then switch to ounces to see the equivalent (about 1.06 oz). If your scale only measures in ounces, aim for just over 1 ounce. For air fryer recipes like protein muffins or pancakes, this ensures the right balance of dry and wet ingredients.
