<h2>How to Measure 5g of Oats Without Scales</h2>
<p>Don't have digital scales? You can estimate 5g of oats using household measures. Approximately 1 tablespoon of oats weighs about 5g (0.176 oz). This method works well for quick measurements when preparing <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/breakfast>breakfast recipes</a> in your air fryer.</p>

<h3>Types of Oats and Their Weights</h3>
<p>All oat varieties weigh roughly the same per gram, but their volumes differ. Steel-cut oats are denser than rolled oats, so 5g (0.176 oz) takes up less space. This matters when converting between weight and volume measurements for air fryer recipes.</p>

<h2>Using 5g of Oats in Air Fryer Recipes</h2>
<p>5g of oats (0.176 oz) might seem small, but it's perfect for coating individual items like fish fingers or chicken strips in your air fryer. This light coating gives crunch without overwhelming the main ingredient. It's also ideal for sprinkling over <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/dessert>desserts</a> before air frying for texture.</p>

<h3>Storing Leftover Oats</h3>
