Measuring 1.5 tbsp of agave syrup in grams ensures consistency, especially in recipes like glazes or marinades. <h2>How to measure 1.5 tbsp of agave syrup in grams</h2>
<p>The easiest way to measure 1.5 tbsp of agave syrup in grams is by using a digital kitchen scale. Simply tare your scale with the container on it, then pour the syrup until it reaches 31.5 grams. If you don’t have a scale, remember that 1 tbsp of agave syrup weighs roughly 21 grams, so 1.5 tbsp equals 31.5 grams. <p>Agave syrup is versatile in air fryer recipes, from glazing chicken wings to sweetening baked goods. A 1.5 tbsp (31.5g) portion is perfect for marinades, dressings, or drizzling over roasted vegetables. Its mild sweetness complements spices and herbs, making it a staple in many <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/chicken>chicken recipes</a>. <h3>Tips for working with agave syrup in the air fryer</h3>
<p>Agave syrup can burn at high temperatures, so use it sparingly in air fryer recipes. Mixing 1.5 tbsp (31.5g) with oils or acids like lemon juice creates a balanced glaze that won’t scorch.
