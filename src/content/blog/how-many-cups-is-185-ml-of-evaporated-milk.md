<h2>How to Measure 185 ml of Evaporated Milk</h2>  
<p>If you don't have a measuring jug with ml markings, you can still measure 185 ml of evaporated milk accurately. One standard US cup is 240 ml, so 185 ml is roughly three-quarters of a cup plus one tablespoon. For UK measurements, it's slightly different due to metric variations.</p>  

<h3>Using a Kitchen Scale</h3>  
<p>For ultimate precision, weigh your evaporated milk. Since 185 ml of evaporated milk weighs about 195 grams, a kitchen scale can help you measure it perfectly. This method is especially useful for baking, where exact proportions are key.</p>
