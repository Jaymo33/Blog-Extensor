<h2>How to measure 30g of chilli sauce accurately</h2>
<p>Measuring 30g of chilli sauce can be tricky if you don’t have a kitchen scale. One tablespoon of chilli sauce typically weighs around 15g, so two tablespoons would give you roughly 30g (1.06 oz). However, sauces vary in density, so a scale is the most accurate tool. If you’re using a measuring spoon, level it off for consistency. For sticky sauces, lightly grease the spoon to help it slide off easily.</p>

<h2>Common mistakes when measuring 30g of chilli sauce</h2>
