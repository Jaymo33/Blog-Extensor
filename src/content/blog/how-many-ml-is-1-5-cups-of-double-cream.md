<h3>How to Measure Double Cream Correctly</h3>  
<p>To measure 1.5 cups of double cream accurately, use a liquid measuring cup with clear millilitre markings. Pour the cream slowly to avoid air bubbles, and check the measurement at eye level. If you don’t have a measuring cup, a kitchen scale can also help—1.5 cups of double cream weighs roughly 360 grams.</p>

<h2>Tips for Storing and Handling Double Cream</h2>  
<p>Double cream is perishable, so always store it in the fridge and use it within a few days of opening. If your recipe calls for whipped cream, chill the cream and your mixing bowl beforehand for best results.
