<h2>How to Convert 500g of Porridge Oats to Ounces</h2>
<p>To convert 500g of porridge oats to ounces, you can use a simple formula: multiply the grams by 0.035274. This gives you the weight in ounces. So, 500g x 0.035274 = 17.64 ounces. If you don’t want to do the math, you can also use a kitchen scale that switches between grams and ounces.</p>
