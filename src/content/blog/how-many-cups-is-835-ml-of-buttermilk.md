<h2>UK vs US Cup Measurements for 835 ml of Buttermilk</h2>
<p>It’s important to note that UK and US cup measurements differ slightly. In the UK, 1 cup equals 284 ml, while in the US, it’s 240 ml. This means 835 ml of buttermilk converts to 3.5 cups in the UK and 3.52 cups in the US. For air fryer recipes, this small difference usually won’t affect the outcome, but it’s good to be aware of.</p>

<h3>How to Measure 835 ml of Buttermilk Without a Measuring Cup</h3>
<p>If you don’t have a measuring cup handy, you can use a kitchen scale to measure 835 ml of buttermilk. Buttermilk weighs roughly 1.03 grams per ml, so 835 ml would be about 860 grams. Alternatively, use a standard mug (about 300 ml) and fill it just under three times for an approximate measurement.</p>
