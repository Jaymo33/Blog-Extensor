<h2>How to measure 10g of yeast without scales</h2>  
<p>Not everyone has a kitchen scale, but there are ways to measure 10g of yeast without one. One standard UK teaspoon holds about 3g of yeast, so 10g would be roughly 3 and a third teaspoons.
