<h2>How to Convert 735 ml of Vinegar to Cups</h2>  
<p>Converting millilitres to cups is straightforward once you know the ratio. In the UK, 1 cup equals 284 ml, while in the US, it's 236.6 ml. For 735 ml of vinegar, this works out to roughly 3.1 cups in both systems. This makes it easy to scale recipes up or down, whether you're cooking for one or a crowd.</p>
