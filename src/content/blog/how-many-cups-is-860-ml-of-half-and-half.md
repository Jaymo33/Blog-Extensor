<h2>Understanding Half And Half Measurements</h2>  
<p>Half and half is a dairy product made by combining equal parts whole milk and cream. It’s commonly used in recipes to add richness without being as heavy as pure cream. When converting 860 ml of half and half to cups, it’s important to note whether you’re using US or UK measurements. In the US, 1 cup equals 240 ml, while in the UK, 1 cup is 284 ml. This difference can affect your recipe, so always double-check which measurement system you’re using.</p>  
<p>For air fryer recipes, precise measurements are crucial. Too much or too little half and half can alter the texture and taste of your dish. If you’re unsure, refer to our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-conversion-chart>air fryer conversion chart</a> for quick reference. Whether you’re making a creamy pasta sauce or a decadent dessert, accurate measurements ensure consistent results every time.</p>

<h2>Converting 860 ml of Half And Half to Cups</h2>  
<p>To convert 860 ml of half and half to US cups, divide the milliliters by 240. This gives you approximately 3.63 cups. For UK cups, divide by 284, resulting in roughly 3.02 cups. These conversions are particularly useful when following recipes from different regions. If you’re using an air fryer, precise measurements help avoid soggy or overly dry dishes.</p>  
<p>Many air fryer recipes, like those found on <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>our recipes page</a>, call for half and half to add creaminess without overwhelming the dish. Whether you’re making a creamy mushroom sauce or a rich custard, knowing how to measure 860 ml of half and half in cups ensures your dish turns out perfectly. Always use a measuring jug for accuracy, especially when dealing with liquids.</p>

<h3>Why Accuracy Matters in Air Fryer Cooking</h3>  
<p>Air fryers cook food quickly and evenly, but they rely on precise ingredient measurements to work effectively. Too much liquid, like half and half, can lead to steaming instead of crisping. On the other hand, too little can result in dry, unappetizing dishes. By converting 860 ml of half and half to cups correctly, you ensure your air fryer recipes turn out as intended.</p>
