If you’re working with a recipe that calls for 1.33 cups of cream, knowing it’s 10.64 ounces helps you measure with confidence.</p>

<h2>How to measure 1.33 cups of cream accurately</h2>  
<p>Measuring liquids like cream can be tricky, especially when dealing with fractions like 1.33 cups. Here’s a simple method: use a liquid measuring cup with clear markings. Fill it to the 1-cup line, then add another 1/3 cup to reach 1.33 cups total. For even more precision, a kitchen scale set to ounces will confirm you’ve got the right amount (10.64 oz).</p>  
<p>If you’re scaling up a recipe, remember that 1.33 cups of cream to ounces is a fixed ratio. Doubling the recipe? You’ll need 21.28 ounces of cream. This consistency is key for dishes like <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/dessert>air fryer desserts</a>, where texture is everything.</p>
