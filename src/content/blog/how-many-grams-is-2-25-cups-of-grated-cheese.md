<h3>How to Measure Grated Cheese Correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate results, use a digital kitchen scale to weigh your cheese. If you don’t have one, lightly spoon grated cheese into a measuring cup without pressing down. Overpacking can add up to 20% more cheese, throwing off your recipe. For air fryer dishes like crispy cheese bites or cheesy garlic bread, consistency is crucial for even cooking.</p>
