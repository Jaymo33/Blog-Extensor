<h3>How to Measure Honey Correctly</h3>  
<p>To measure 3.5 cups of honey accurately, lightly grease your measuring cup first—this helps the honey slide out easily. Pour slowly and level off the top with a straight edge. For sticky ingredients like honey, a liquid measuring cup with a spout works best. <h2>Converting 3.5 Cups of Honey to ml</h2>  
<p>One US cup of honey is roughly 236.6 ml, so 3.5 cups equal about 828 ml. UK cups are slightly larger at 284 ml per cup, meaning 3.5 UK cups would be approximately 994 ml. Always check which cup measurement your recipe uses—especially if following international guides. Our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/dessert>dessert recipes</a> often include both measurements for clarity.</p>  

<p>If you don’t have a measuring cup, use a kitchen scale: 1 ml of honey weighs about 1.42 grams. For 828 ml, you’d need roughly 1,175 grams.
