<h2>Why Accurate Measurements Matter in Air Frying</h2>  
<p>Using the right amount of coconut oil ensures your air-fried dishes turn out crispy and golden. Too little oil can leave food dry, while too much can make it greasy. For example, when making air-fried chicken or roasted vegetables, 535 ml of coconut oil (or 2.26 cups) provides the perfect balance. Check out our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-conversion-chart>air fryer conversion chart</a> for more handy measurement tips.</p>  

<p>Coconut oil is a popular choice for air frying due to its high smoke point and health benefits. Unlike butter, it doesn’t burn easily, making it ideal for high-heat cooking. Whether you're preparing <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/chicken>air-fryer chicken</a> or crispy tofu, measuring 535 ml correctly ensures consistent results every time.</p>  

<h3>How to Measure 535 ml of Coconut Oil Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a measuring jug, use standard cups to measure 535 ml of coconut oil. One cup equals roughly 237 ml, so 2.26 cups will give you the correct amount. For sticky coconut oil, lightly grease the measuring cup first for easy pouring. This trick works perfectly for recipes like <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/dessert>air-fryer desserts</a> or savoury dishes.</p>  

<h2>Converting 535 ml of Coconut Oil for Different Recipes</h2>  
<p>Whether you're following a UK or US recipe, knowing how many cups 535 ml of coconut oil equals is crucial. In the UK, metric measurements are standard, while US recipes often use cups. For air fryer cooking, this conversion ensures you never over or under-oil your ingredients. Visit <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>our recipe hub</a> for more measurement guides and inspiration.</p>  

<p>If a recipe calls for melted coconut oil, measure it after melting for accuracy. Solid coconut oil takes up less space, so 535 ml melted will fill slightly more than 2.26 cups. This is especially important for baking, where exact ratios affect texture. For cleaning sticky residue, try our recommended <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-cleaner>air fryer cleaner</a> to keep your appliance in top shape.</p>  

<h3>Common Mistakes When Measuring Coconut Oil</h3>  
<p>Avoid packing coconut oil into the cup, as this leads to excess oil. Instead, spoon it in loosely for an accurate 535 ml (2.26 cups) measurement. Also, ensure your measuring tools are at room temperature—cold utensils can cause the oil to harden unevenly. These small adjustments make a big difference in air fryer cooking.</p>
