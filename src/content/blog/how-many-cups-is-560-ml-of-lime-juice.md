<h2>How to Measure 560 ml of Lime Juice</h2>
<p>Measuring lime juice accurately is simple with the right tools. A liquid measuring cup with millilitre markings is ideal, but if you only have standard cups, 560 ml equals about 2.37 cups. For best results, pour the juice into the measuring cup at eye level to avoid over- or under-pouring. If you’re using fresh limes, remember that one medium lime yields roughly 30 ml of juice, so you’d need around 18-19 limes for 560 ml.</p>
