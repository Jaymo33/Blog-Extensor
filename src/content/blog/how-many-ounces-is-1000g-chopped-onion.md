<h2>Why convert 1000g chopped onion to ounces?</h2>
<p>Many UK recipes, especially those for air fryers, use metric measurements like grams. However, some kitchen scales or older cookbooks might still use ounces. Converting 1000g of chopped onion to ounces (about 35.27 oz) helps maintain accuracy in your cooking. This is particularly important when preparing dishes where onion is a key ingredient, like French onion soup or caramelised onion tarts.</p>

<h2>How to measure 1000g of chopped onion</h2>
<p>When converting 1000g chopped onion to oz, it's helpful to know how to measure your ingredients properly. Start by peeling and roughly chopping your onions, then use digital kitchen scales for the most accurate measurement. If you don't have scales, 1000g is roughly equivalent to 4 large onions or 6 medium ones.</p>
<p>For air fryer cooking, uniformly chopped onions cook more evenly. Our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>recipe collection</a> includes many dishes where precise onion measurements matter, from fajitas to onion bhajis. The moisture content in onions can affect weight, so it's always best to measure after chopping rather than estimating with whole onions.</p>

<h3>Does onion type affect the weight?</h3>
<p>Different onion varieties have slightly different densities. While 1000g of chopped onion equals about 35.27 ounces regardless of type, red onions tend to be slightly heavier than white or brown onions when measured by volume. This is why weight measurements (grams or ounces) are more reliable than cup measurements for accuracy.</p>

The water content means frozen onion may soften slightly when thawed, but it's still perfect for cooked dishes.</p>
