<h2>Understanding the Conversion: 185 ml to Cups</h2>  
<p>Converting 185 ml of water to cups depends on whether you're using the US or UK measurement system. In the US, 1 cup is 240 ml, so 185 ml is roughly 0.78 cups. In the UK, where 1 cup is 250 ml, 185 ml translates to about 0.74 cups. This small difference matters in baking, where precision is key. For air fryer recipes, especially those involving batters or liquids, getting this right ensures even cooking.</p>

<h3>Why Accurate Measurements Matter in Air Fryer Cooking</h3>  
<p>Air fryers circulate hot air rapidly, so incorrect liquid measurements can lead to uneven cooking or soggy results. For example, adding too much water to a batter might prevent crisping, while too little could dry out your dish. Measuring 185 ml of water correctly ensures your recipes turn out as intended. This is especially crucial for delicate dishes like cakes or steamed puddings.</p>
