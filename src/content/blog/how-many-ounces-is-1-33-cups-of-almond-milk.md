<h3>How to measure almond milk correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate conversion, use a kitchen scale to weigh 1.33 cups of almond milk in ounces. If you don’t have one, a liquid measuring cup will suffice, but ensure it’s levelled off. Almond milk’s density is close to water, so 1 cup equals roughly 8 ounces, making 1.33 cups equal to 10.64 ounces. This method works for both homemade and store-bought almond milk.</p>
