You can roast a 1.5kg chicken, cook 4-6 salmon fillets, or prepare enough vegetables for 4 people in one go. Despite their modest size, they pack in that all-important 5 litre cooking capacity that makes them so versatile.</p><p id="">When not in use, many 5 litre air fryers can be tucked away in cupboards thanks to their manageable size and weight. This makes them particularly economical for everyday cooking compared to using your main oven for smaller meals.</p><h3 id="">Cost-effective cooking</h3><p id="">By cooking complete meals in one go, a 5 litre air fryer helps reduce energy bills compared to using multiple appliances or the main oven.
