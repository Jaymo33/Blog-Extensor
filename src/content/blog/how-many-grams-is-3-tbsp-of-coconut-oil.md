<h3>How to measure coconut oil correctly</h3>  
<p>Measuring coconut oil can be tricky since it changes consistency with temperature. For solid coconut oil, pack it firmly into the tablespoon and level it off. If it’s melted, pour it into a liquid measuring spoon. Either way, 3 tbsp of coconut oil in grams will always be around 42g. For best results, weigh it on a digital scale.</p>
