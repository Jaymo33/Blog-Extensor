<h3>How to measure sunflower oil correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate results, use a digital kitchen scale to weigh your sunflower oil. If you don’t have one, a standard UK tablespoon holds roughly 13.6 grams of sunflower oil. Multiply this by 3, and you’ll get the 41-gram measurement. Always level off your tablespoons for consistency.</p>  

<h2>Common uses for sunflower oil in air fryers</h2>  
<p>Sunflower oil works brilliantly for roasting vegetables, frying chicken, or even baking cakes in your air fryer. Its light texture ensures food doesn’t become overly greasy. <h3>Sunflower oil vs other cooking oils</h3>  
<p>While sunflower oil is versatile, other oils like olive or coconut may alter flavour profiles. However, sunflower oil’s neutral taste makes it ideal for most air fryer dishes. If you’re experimenting with different oils, always check their smoke points to avoid burning.</p>
