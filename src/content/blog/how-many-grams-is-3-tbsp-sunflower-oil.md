<h3>How to measure sunflower oil correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate results, use a digital kitchen scale to weigh your sunflower oil. If you don’t have one, a standard UK tablespoon holds roughly 13.6 grams of sunflower oil. Multiply this by 3, and you’ll get the 41-gram measurement. Always level off your tablespoons for consistency.</p>
