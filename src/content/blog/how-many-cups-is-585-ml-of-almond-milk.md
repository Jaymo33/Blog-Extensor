<h2>How to Measure 585 ml of Almond Milk Without a Measuring Cup</h2>
<p>If you don’t have a measuring cup handy, you can still measure 585 ml of almond milk accurately. A standard UK mug holds about 284 ml, so you’d need just over two mugs to reach 585 ml. For US measurements, remember that 1 cup is roughly 240 ml.</p>
<p>Another handy trick is using a kitchen scale. Since 1 ml of almond milk weighs approximately 1 gram, 585 ml would be around 585 grams.
