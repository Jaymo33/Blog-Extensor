<h3>Tips for Storing Leftover Garlic Paste</h3>  
<p>If you’ve measured 100g of garlic paste but only need half, proper storage keeps it fresh. Transfer leftovers to an airtight container and refrigerate for up to two weeks. Always label containers with dates to track freshness and avoid waste.</p>
