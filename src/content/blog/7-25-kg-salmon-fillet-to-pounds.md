<h2>Practical tips for cooking large salmon fillets</h2>
<p>When working with a 7.25 kg salmon fillet, preparation is key to air fryer success. First, ensure your air fryer basket is large enough to accommodate the fillet or plan to cut it into smaller portions. Pat the salmon dry with paper towels to help achieve a crispy skin, and season generously according to your recipe. Preheat your air fryer to ensure even cooking from the moment the salmon goes in. Cooking times will vary based on your air fryer model, but a good starting point is 12-15 minutes at 180°C for every 2.5 cm of thickness. Always use a meat thermometer to check the internal temperature reaches 63°C for perfect doneness.</p>
