<h3>How to Measure 0.75 Cups of Vegetable Oil Without a Measuring Cup</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a measuring cup handy, you can still measure 177 ml of vegetable oil using a kitchen scale. One ml of vegetable oil weighs roughly 0.92 grams, so 177 ml would be about 163 grams. Alternatively, use a tablespoon—15 ml per tablespoon means 0.75 cups is roughly 12 tablespoons.
