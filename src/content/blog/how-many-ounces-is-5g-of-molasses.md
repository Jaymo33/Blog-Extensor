<h3>How to measure 5g of molasses without scales</h3>  
<p>Not everyone has a kitchen scale handy, but don’t worry—there are other ways to measure 5g of molasses. A standard teaspoon holds about 7g of molasses, so just under a teaspoon will give you roughly 5g. If you’re converting 5g of molasses to ounces, remember that 0.18 ounces is just a smidge over half a teaspoon. This method isn’t as precise as using scales, but it’ll do in a pinch for most recipes.</p>
