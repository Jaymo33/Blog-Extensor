<h3>How to Measure 1000g of Lemon Juice</h3>
<p>Measuring 1000g of lemon juice accurately requires a kitchen scale. Place a measuring jug on the scale, tare it to zero, then pour the juice until it reaches 1000g. If you don’t have a scale, 1000g roughly equals 35.27 ounces or about 4.2 cups. For air fryer recipes, precision is key, so a scale is highly recommended. Lemon juice can vary in density, so weighing it ensures consistency.</p>
