<h3>How to Measure 0.33 Cup of Evaporated Cream</h3>  
<p>Measuring evaporated cream accurately is simple with the right tools. Use a liquid measuring cup for the most precise results, filling it just below the 1/3 cup mark. Alternatively, a kitchen scale set to ounces will give you the exact weight of 2.64 ounces. This method is especially helpful for recipes where precision is key, like air fryer custards or sauces.</p>
