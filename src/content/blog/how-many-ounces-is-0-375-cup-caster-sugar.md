<p>0.375 cup of caster sugar equals approximately 2.65 ounces. This conversion is essential for baking, especially when using an air fryer, where precise measurements can affect texture and taste. <h2>Why accurate sugar measurements matter</h2>
<p>Using the correct amount of caster sugar is crucial for achieving the right sweetness and texture in your baked goods. Too much sugar can make desserts overly sweet or cause them to brown too quickly in the air fryer. Too little sugar might result in a bland taste or affect the structure of your recipe. For air fryer recipes, where cooking times are shorter, precision is even more important.</p>

<h2>How to measure 0.375 cup of caster sugar</h2>
<p>Measuring 0.375 cup of caster sugar can be tricky if you don’t have a 1/3 cup measure. One easy way is to use a 1/4 cup plus 2 tablespoons, which equals 0.375 cup. Alternatively, you can weigh it for accuracy—2.65 ounces ensures you’re spot-on. A digital kitchen scale is ideal for this, especially when working with air fryer recipes where small adjustments matter.</p>
<p>For those who prefer volume measurements, levelling off the sugar with a straight edge ensures consistency.
