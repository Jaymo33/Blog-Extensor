<h2>How to measure rolled barley correctly</h2>
<p>For the most accurate conversion of 2 cups of rolled barley to ounces, use a kitchen scale. Scoop the barley into a measuring cup without packing it down, then level it off. This method ensures you’re not accidentally adding extra weight. If you don’t have a scale, remember that 2 cups of rolled barley weigh roughly 6.35 ounces.
