<h2>How to Convert 435 ml of Vegetable Oil to Cups</h2>  
<p>The standard conversion for liquid ingredients is 1 cup = 236.59 ml. To find how many cups 435 ml of vegetable oil equals, divide 435 by 236.59. The result is roughly 1.84 cups. For most recipes, rounding to 1 ¾ cups works perfectly.</p>  

<h3>Measuring Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don't have a measuring jug with ml markings, use standard cups. Fill a cup to just below the rim for accuracy. For 435 ml of vegetable oil, you'll need almost two full cups. This method is handy when preparing <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>air fryer recipes</a> on the fly.</p>
