<h3>How to measure 20g of almond milk without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can use volume measurements to approximate 20g of almond milk. One tablespoon of almond milk weighs roughly 15g, so 20g is just over 1.3 tablespoons. For UK cooks, this translates to about 0.71 ounces, a handy reference when using air fryer recipes that list ingredients in grams or ounces.</p>
