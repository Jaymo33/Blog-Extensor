<h3>How to measure 200g of cocoa powder without a scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can still measure 200g of cocoa powder using volume. One tablespoon of cocoa powder weighs about 7g, so 200g would be roughly 28-29 tablespoons. However, this method isn’t as precise as weighing, so for best results, invest in a digital scale.
