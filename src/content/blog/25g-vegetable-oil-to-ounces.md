<h3>How to measure 25g of vegetable oil without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have digital scales, you can measure 25g (0.88 oz) of vegetable oil using tablespoons. This method works well for recipes like air-fried tofu or mushrooms, where precision isn’t as critical.</p>
