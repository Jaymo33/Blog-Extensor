<h3>The Science Behind Water Measurements</h3>
<p>Water has a density of 1g per millilitre, making weight-to-volume conversions straightforward. This means 5g of water occupies 5ml, which converts to 0.176 ounces. Understanding this relationship helps when scaling recipes up or down for your air fryer.</p>
