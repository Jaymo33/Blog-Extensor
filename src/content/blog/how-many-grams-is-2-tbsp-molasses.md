<h3>How to measure molasses correctly</h3>  
<p>To measure 2 tbsp of molasses accurately, use a kitchen scale for the most reliable results. Spoon the molasses into the tablespoon without packing it down, then level it off. Since molasses is sticky, lightly greasing the spoon can help it slide off easily. If you don’t have a scale, remember that 2 tbsp equals roughly 40 grams in UK measurements. For sticky ingredients like this, a <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-cleaner>good cleaner</a> is a must!</p>
