<h3>How to Convert 1.66 Cups of Rice to Ounces</h3>
<p>The conversion from cups to ounces for rice is thankfully quite straightforward once you know the basic weight per cup. As a general rule, one US cup of uncooked long-grain white rice weighs approximately 8 ounces. Therefore, to find out how many ounces is 1.66 cups of rice, you simply multiply 1.66 by 8, which gives you 13.28 ounces. This calculation provides a reliable figure for most standard cooking and baking purposes. It is always best to use a digital kitchen scale for the most accurate measurement, especially when precision is key for a recipe's success.</p>

<h3>UK vs US Cup Measurements for Rice</h3>
<p>It is important to note that a UK cup is slightly larger than a US cup, which can affect your conversion if you are using a recipe from a different country. A UK cup holds 10 UK fluid ounces, whereas a US cup holds 8 US fluid ounces. However, when measuring weight for dry ingredients like rice, the US standard of 8 ounces per cup is widely accepted and used. So, for the question of 1.66 cups of rice in ounces UK, the answer remains approximately 13.28 ounces if you are following a US-based recipe. Always check the origin of your recipe to be certain, but for most practical purposes, the US standard is the common reference point.</p>
