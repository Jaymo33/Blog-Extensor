<h3>How to Measure 75g of Protein Powder Without Scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have scales, use tablespoons to approximate 75g of protein powder. One level tablespoon holds roughly 15g, so 5 tablespoons equal 75g (or 2.65 oz).
