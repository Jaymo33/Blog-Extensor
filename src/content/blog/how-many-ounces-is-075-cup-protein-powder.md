<h3>How to Measure Protein Powder Correctly</h3>  
<p>To measure 0.75 cup of protein powder accurately, use a dry measuring cup and level it off with a straight edge. Avoid packing the powder down, as this can lead to an incorrect weight. If you don’t have a measuring cup, 6 ounces is roughly the weight you’re aiming for.
