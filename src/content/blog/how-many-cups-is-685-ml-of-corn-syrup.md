<h2>How to Measure 685 ml of Corn Syrup in Cups</h2>
<p>Converting 685 ml of corn syrup to cups is straightforward. Since 1 cup equals approximately 236.6 ml, dividing 685 by 236.6 gives you roughly 2.9 cups. For most recipes, rounding to 3 cups works fine, but for precision, stick to 2.9 cups. This is particularly useful for desserts like pecan pie or sticky buns, where the syrup’s consistency is key.</p>
