<h3>How to Measure 35 ml of Single Cream Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a measuring jug, use a tablespoon for small quantities. One tablespoon holds about 15 ml, so 35 ml is just over two tablespoons. For air fryer recipes, this method works well for small batches of sauces or toppings.</p>  

<h2>Converting 35 ml of Single Cream for UK and US Recipes</h2>  
<p>In the UK, single cream has a fat content of around 18%, while in the US, light cream is the closest substitute at 20% fat. When converting 35 ml of single cream to cups, the measurement remains the same (0.15 cups), but the slight fat difference may affect richness in recipes.</p>
