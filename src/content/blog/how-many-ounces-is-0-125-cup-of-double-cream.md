<h3>Why Double Cream Measurements Matter</h3>  
<p>Double cream has a high fat content, usually around 48%, which affects its weight and volume. This makes it denser than other dairy products, so measuring by weight (ounces) is often more reliable than volume (cups). For air fryer recipes, where even small discrepancies can alter outcomes, using the correct amount of double cream ensures your dish isn’t too runny or thick. If you're unsure, a kitchen scale is the best tool for precise measurements.</p>
