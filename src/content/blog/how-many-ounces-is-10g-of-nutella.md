<h3>How to measure 10g of Nutella without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have digital scales, you can still measure 10g of Nutella (0.35 ounces) using household items. A standard teaspoon holds roughly 5g, so two level teaspoons will give you approximately 10g. For more precise measurements, consider investing in a small kitchen scale—it’s a game-changer for air fryer cooking.</p>
