<h3>Converting Cups to ml for Soy Sauce</h3>  
<p>The conversion from cups to millilitres is straightforward: 1 cup equals 236.59 ml, so 0.25 cups is a quarter of that. This means 0.25 cups of soy sauce in ml is precisely 59 ml. This measurement is universal, whether you're in the UK or the USA.</p>
