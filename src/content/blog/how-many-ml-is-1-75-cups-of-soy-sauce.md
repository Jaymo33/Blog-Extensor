<h3>How to Measure Soy Sauce for Air Fryer Recipes</h3>  
<p>To measure 1.75 cups of soy sauce in ml, use a liquid measuring jug with clear ml markings. Pour the soy sauce slowly to avoid spills and check at eye level for accuracy.
