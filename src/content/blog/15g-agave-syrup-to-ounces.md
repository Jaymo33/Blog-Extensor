<h2>How to measure 15g of agave syrup accurately</h2>
<p>Measuring 15g of agave syrup can be tricky due to its sticky consistency. Using a digital kitchen scale is the most accurate method. Simply place your container on the scale, tare it to zero, then pour the agave syrup until it reaches 15g. If you don't have a scale, you can use measuring spoons, but this method is less precise.</p>
<p>For those who prefer volume measurements, 15g of agave syrup is roughly equivalent to 1 tablespoon. However, we recommend using weight measurements for better accuracy, especially when working with air fryer recipes where consistency is key. Check out our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>recipe collection</a> for more ideas on how to use agave syrup in your air fryer.</p>

<h3>The importance of accurate measurements in air fryer cooking</h3>
<p>Air fryers cook food quickly and evenly, which means small measurement errors can have noticeable effects. When converting 15g of agave syrup to ounces (0.53 oz), precision ensures your glazes and marinades have the right consistency. Too much syrup can make food overly sweet or cause burning, while too little might result in lacklustre flavour.</p>
