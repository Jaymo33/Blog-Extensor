<h2>How to Measure 250g of Agave Syrup in Ounces</h2>  
<p>To measure 250g of agave syrup in ounces, you can use a kitchen scale set to imperial units. Alternatively, you can use a conversion formula: 1 gram equals 0.035 ounces. Multiply 250g by 0.035 to get 8.82 ounces. This method is handy if your scale only displays grams.</p>  
<p>For those without a scale, agave syrup’s viscosity means it’s tricky to measure by volume. However, 250g roughly translates to ¾ cup, but weighing is far more accurate. Always opt for weight measurements when precision is key, especially in air fryer cooking where even small deviations can impact results.</p>
