<h3>US vs. UK Cup Measurements</h3>  
<p>It’s worth noting that cup sizes differ between the US and UK. A US cup is 240 ml, while a UK cup is 284 ml. For this conversion, we’ve used the US standard (3.0 cups = 710 ml). If you’re following a UK recipe, 3.0 cups would be 852 ml. Always check the recipe’s origin to avoid confusion.</p>
