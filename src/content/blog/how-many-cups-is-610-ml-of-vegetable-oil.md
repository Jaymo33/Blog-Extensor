<h2>Converting 610 ml of Vegetable Oil to Cups</h2>  
<p>To convert 610 ml of vegetable oil to cups, divide the millilitres by the cup size you're using. For US cups: 610 ÷ 240 ≈ 2.57 cups. For UK cups: 610 ÷ 284 ≈ 2.15 cups. This conversion is handy when following international recipes or adjusting portion sizes.</p>
