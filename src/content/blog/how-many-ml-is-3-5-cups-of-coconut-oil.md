<h3>How to Measure Coconut Oil for Air Fryer Recipes</h3>  
<p>Measuring coconut oil can be tricky, especially if it’s solid at room temperature. To get 830 ml (3.5 cups), melt the oil first for an accurate liquid measurement. Use a measuring jug with millilitre markings for precision. If your recipe calls for solid coconut oil, 3.5 cups weigh approximately 735 grams. Always level off the measuring cup to avoid excess.</p>
