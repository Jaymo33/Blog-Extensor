<h2>Why accurate lime juice measurements matter</h2>  
<p>Using the correct amount of lime juice ensures your air fryer dishes aren’t overly acidic or bland. Too much lime can overpower other flavours, while too little might leave your dish lacking brightness. Precision is especially important in baking, where acidity affects texture. <h3>How to measure lime juice correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate conversion of 2.5 tbsp lime juice to grams, use a digital kitchen scale. Pour the juice directly into a measuring spoon or jug, then weigh it. Freshly squeezed lime juice may vary slightly in density, but 37.5g is a reliable benchmark. If you’re using bottled lime juice, check the label for consistency.
