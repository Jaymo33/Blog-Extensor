<h2>How to Measure 760 ml of Corn Syrup Without a Measuring Cup</h2>
<p>If you don't have a measuring cup, you can still measure 760 ml of corn syrup using other tools. A standard metric measuring jug will show millilitres clearly, making it easy to pour the exact amount needed. Alternatively, you can use a kitchen scale since 760 ml of corn syrup weighs roughly 1040 grams.</p>
<p>For those who prefer visual cues, 760 ml fills about three-quarters of a standard litre jug. This method is handy when you're in a pinch and need a quick estimate. Remember, accuracy matters, especially when adapting recipes for your air fryer.</p>

<h3>US vs UK Cup Measurements</h3>
<p>It's worth noting that US and UK cup measurements differ slightly. In the US, 1 cup equals 240 ml, while in the UK, it's 250 ml. This means 760 ml of corn syrup is about 3.17 US cups or 3.04 UK cups. For most recipes, this small difference won't matter, but it's good to be aware of when precision is key.</p>

<h3>Storing Leftover Corn Syrup</h3>
<p>If you have leftover corn syrup after measuring out 760 ml, store it properly to maintain freshness. Keep it in an airtight container at room temperature, away from direct sunlight. Proper storage ensures your syrup stays ready for your next air fryer adventure.</p>

<p>Corn syrup can be sticky and messy, but a few tricks make handling it easier. Lightly grease your measuring cup or spoon before pouring to help the syrup slide out cleanly. When using it in air fryer recipes, mix it thoroughly with other ingredients to ensure even distribution.</p>
