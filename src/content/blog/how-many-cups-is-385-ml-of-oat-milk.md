<h3>US vs. UK Cup Measurements</h3>  
<p>The difference between US and UK cup measurements can be confusing. In the US, 1 cup equals 240 ml, while in the UK, it’s 250 ml. This means 385 ml of oat milk translates to roughly 1.63 US cups or 1.52 UK cups. If you're following a recipe from <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/dessert>dessert category</a>, double-check which measurement system it uses to avoid errors.</p>  

<h2>How to Measure 385 ml of Oat Milk Without a Measuring Cup</h2>  
<p>Don’t have a measuring cup? No problem! You can use a standard mug or glass to estimate 385 ml of oat milk. A typical UK mug holds about 284 ml, so filling it just over halfway will get you close. Alternatively, use a kitchen scale—oat milk weighs roughly 1 gram per ml, so 385 grams equals 385 ml. For more air fryer measurement hacks, explore our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-cleaner>cleaning and usage guides</a>.</p>  

<p>Another trick is to use tablespoons: 1 US tablespoon holds 15 ml, so 385 ml would be around 25.6 tablespoons. While not as precise as a measuring cup, this method works in a pinch. Just remember, accuracy is key for baking, so invest in a good set of measuring tools if you cook often.</p>
