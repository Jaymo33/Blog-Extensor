<h2>How to Measure 435 ml of Olive Oil in Cups</h2>  
<p>To convert 435 ml of olive oil to cups, divide the millilitres by 236.59 (the number of ml in a US cup). This gives you roughly 1.84 cups. For UK measurements, note that a UK cup is 284 ml, so adjust accordingly if following British recipes.</p>
