<h3>How to measure 50g of baking powder without scales</h3>
<p>If you don’t have scales, you can still measure 50g of baking powder (1.76 ounces) using tablespoons. One level tablespoon of baking powder weighs about 15g, so 3 tablespoons plus 1 teaspoon will give you roughly 50g. This method isn’t as precise as using scales, but it works in a pinch. For best results, especially with air fryer recipes, we recommend investing in a digital scale.</p>

<h3>Tips for storing baking powder after measuring</h3>
<p>After measuring out 50g of baking powder (1.76 ounces), it’s important to store the rest properly. Keep it in an airtight container in a cool, dry place to maintain its potency. Proper storage ensures your next batch of air fryer treats rises perfectly every time.</p>
