<h3>How to Measure 160 ml of Orange Juice Without a Measuring Cup</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a measuring cup handy, you can still measure 160 ml of orange juice accurately. A standard UK tablespoon holds 15 ml, so roughly 10.5 tablespoons will give you 160 ml. <h3>Converting 160 ml of Orange Juice for US and UK Recipes</h3>  
<p>It’s worth noting that cup sizes differ between the US and UK. A US cup is 240 ml, while a UK cup is 250 ml. For 160 ml of orange juice, this means approximately 0.68 US cups or 0.64 UK cups. Most modern recipes specify which measurement system they use, but when in doubt, stick to millilitres for accuracy. This is especially important for air fryer recipes, where even small discrepancies can affect cooking times and results.</p>
