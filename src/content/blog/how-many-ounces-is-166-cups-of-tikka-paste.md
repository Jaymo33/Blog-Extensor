<h3>How to Measure 1.66 Cups of Tikka Paste</h3>  
<p>Measuring 1.66 cups of tikka paste can be tricky if you don’t have a measuring jug with precise markings. A kitchen scale is the most accurate tool, as it directly converts cups to ounces. Simply pour the paste until it reaches 13.28 ounces, and you’re good to go. For those without a scale, using a standard measuring cup and levelling off the excess works too.</p>

<h3>Tips for Storing Leftover Tikka Paste</h3>  
<p>If you don’t use all 1.66 cups of tikka paste at once, store the remainder in an airtight container in the fridge. It will stay fresh for up to a week, ready for your next air fryer adventure. You can also freeze it in ice cube trays for portioned use later. This way, you’ll always have the right amount on hand without needing to measure again.</p>
