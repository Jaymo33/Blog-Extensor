<h2>How to measure 0.75 tbsp of olive oil accurately</h2>
<p>Measuring 0.75 tbsp of olive oil can be tricky if you don’t have the right tools. A standard tablespoon holds 15 ml, so 0.75 tbsp is just over half (11.25 ml). For grams, this translates to roughly 11.25 grams, as olive oil weighs about 0.9 grams per ml. A kitchen scale is the most accurate way to measure, but if you don’t have one, use a measuring spoon and fill it three-quarters full.</p>
