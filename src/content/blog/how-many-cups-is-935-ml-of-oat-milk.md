<h2>UK vs. US Cup Measurements</h2>
<p>In the UK, 1 cup equals 284 ml, making 935 ml roughly 3.95 cups. In the US, 1 cup is 236.59 ml, so 935 ml is about 3.96 cups. The difference is minimal but worth noting for precision.
