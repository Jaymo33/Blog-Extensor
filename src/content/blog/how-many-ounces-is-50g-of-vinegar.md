<h3>How to measure 50g of vinegar without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can still measure 50g of vinegar using volume. One tablespoon of vinegar weighs about 15 grams, so 50g would be roughly 3.3 tablespoons. This method isn’t as precise as weighing, but it’s handy in a pinch. Just remember that different types of vinegar may have slightly varying densities.</p>

<h3>Converting other vinegar amounts</h3>  
<p>Need to convert a different amount of vinegar? The same ratio applies—1 gram equals roughly 0.035 ounces. So, 100g of vinegar would be about 3.53 ounces, and 25g would be approximately 0.88 ounces. This makes scaling recipes up or down a breeze.</p>
