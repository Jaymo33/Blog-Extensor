<h3>How to measure garlic paste without a scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, use measuring cups with a spatula to level off the garlic paste. Pack it lightly to avoid air gaps, which can affect the weight. For 0.375 cup, fill a 1/4 cup measure and then add half of another 1/4 cup. This method gets you close to 3 ounces, though a scale is always more precise.</p>
