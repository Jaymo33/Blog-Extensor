<h3>How to Measure 40g of Milk Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>No scale? No problem! One tablespoon of milk weighs roughly 15g, so 40g is just under 3 tablespoons. For liquids, a metric measuring jug marked in millilitres can also help—40g of milk is about 39ml. Keep in mind that milk’s density can vary slightly with fat content, but this method works for most recipes.</p>
