<h2>How to measure 250g of condensed milk accurately</h2>
<p>Using a digital kitchen scale is the most accurate way to measure 250g of condensed milk. Simply place your mixing bowl on the scale, tare it to zero, and pour the condensed milk until it hits 250g. If you don’t have a scale, 250g is roughly 8.82 ounces, which can be measured using a liquid measuring cup. Just remember that condensed milk is thick, so level it off with a spatula for precision.</p>
