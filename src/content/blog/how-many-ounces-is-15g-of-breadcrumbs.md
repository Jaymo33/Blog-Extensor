<h3>How to measure 15g of breadcrumbs without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can still measure 15g of breadcrumbs using tablespoons. One tablespoon of breadcrumbs weighs about 7-8g, so two lightly packed tablespoons will give you roughly 15g (0.53 oz). This method isn’t as precise as weighing, but it works in a pinch for most air fryer recipes.</p>
