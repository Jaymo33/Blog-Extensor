<h3>How to measure wholemeal flour correctly</h3>  
<p>To convert 5 tbsp of wholemeal flour into grams accurately, fluff the flour first with a fork before spooning it into the tablespoon. Level it off with a knife for consistency. This method prevents compacting, which can lead to using more flour than intended.</p>
