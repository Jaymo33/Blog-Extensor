<h2>How to Measure 30g of Cornflour Accurately</h2>
<p>For the most precise measurement, use a digital kitchen scale set to grams. Scoop the cornflour into a bowl until the scale reads 30g. If you don’t have a scale, 30g is roughly 2 tablespoons, but this can vary slightly depending on how packed the flour is. Always level off the top for consistency.</p>
