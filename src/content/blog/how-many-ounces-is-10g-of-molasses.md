<h3>How to measure 10g of molasses without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can still measure 10g of molasses (0.35 oz) using a tablespoon. One level tablespoon of molasses weighs roughly 20g, so half a tablespoon will give you approximately 10g. For even more precision, use a teaspoon—about 2 teaspoons equal 10g. This method is handy when you're in a pinch or working with smaller quantities. Just remember, molasses is sticky, so lightly greasing your measuring spoon can help it slide off easily.</p>
