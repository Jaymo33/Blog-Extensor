<h3>How to measure ginger paste without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can still measure 150g of ginger paste using tablespoons. One tablespoon of ginger paste weighs roughly 15g, so 10 tablespoons will give you 150g (or 5.29 ounces). This method isn’t as precise as using scales, but it works in a pinch. <h3>Storing ginger paste for future use</h3>  
<p>Ginger paste can be stored in the fridge for up to two weeks or frozen for longer shelf life. Portion it into ice cube trays for easy measuring—each cube holds about 15g (0.53 ounces), so 10 cubes equal 150g. This trick saves time and reduces waste, making meal prep a breeze.</p>
