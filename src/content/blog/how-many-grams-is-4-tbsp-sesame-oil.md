<h2>How to measure sesame oil for air fryer recipes</h2>  
<p>For the most accurate results, use a digital kitchen scale to weigh 4 tbsp sesame oil in grams. If you don’t have one, remember that 1 tbsp of sesame oil is about 13.5g, so 4 tablespoons equal 54g. This method eliminates inconsistencies caused by varying spoon sizes or oil density.</p>

<p>To preserve its flavour, store sesame oil in a cool, dark place. Properly sealed, it lasts up to a year. Always check for rancidity before using—fresh oil should smell nutty, not bitter.</p>
