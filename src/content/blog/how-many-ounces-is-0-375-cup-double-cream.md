<h3>How to Measure 0.375 Cup of Double Cream</h3>  
<p>Measuring 0.375 cup of double cream can be tricky if you don’t have a 1/3 cup measure. A simple solution is to use a 1/4 cup plus 2 tablespoons, which equals 0.375 cup. Alternatively, weighing it at 3 ounces ensures accuracy. A kitchen scale is a handy tool for air fryer enthusiasts who want consistent results.</p>
