<h3>How to Measure 1000g of Mayonnaise Accurately</h3>  
<p>Using a digital kitchen scale is the easiest way to measure 1000g of mayonnaise. Set your scale to grams, tare the container, and scoop until you hit 1000g. If your scale only shows ounces, aim for 35.27 ounces. For smaller batches, divide the measurement accordingly. This method works for all types of mayonnaise, whether store-bought or homemade.</p>
