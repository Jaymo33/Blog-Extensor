<h2>How to Measure 560 ml of Olive Oil in Cups</h2>  
<p>The standard conversion for liquid measurements is 1 cup = 236.59 ml. Therefore, 560 ml of olive oil equals roughly 2.37 cups. For practical purposes, you can round this to 2 and 1/3 cups. If you don’t have a measuring cup, a kitchen scale can help—560 ml of olive oil weighs approximately 504 grams.</p>
