<h3>How to measure 250g of water accurately</h3>  
<p>Using a digital kitchen scale is the easiest way to measure 250g of water. Set your scale to grams, place a container on it, and tare the weight. Then, pour water until it reaches 250g. If your scale only shows ounces, aim for 8.82 oz. For those without scales, 250g of water is roughly 250ml, as water's density is 1g per ml. Always double-check for accuracy, especially when baking.</p>
