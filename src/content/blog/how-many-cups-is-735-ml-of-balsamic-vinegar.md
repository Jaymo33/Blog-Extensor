<h2>How to Measure 735 ml of Balsamic Vinegar Without a Scale</h2>
<p>Not everyone has a kitchen scale, but most have measuring cups handy. To measure 735 ml of balsamic vinegar in cups, fill a liquid measuring cup to the 3-cup mark, then add another tablespoon (roughly 0.1 cup). This method gets you close enough for most recipes. For baking or reductions where precision matters, consider using a scale or millilitre markings on a jug.</p>
