<h2>How to Measure Yogurt Correctly</h2>
<p>Measuring yogurt isn’t as straightforward as measuring liquids like water or milk. Its thickness means you need to scoop and level it properly to get an accurate 3.25 cups (768 ml). Use a dry measuring cup for best results, filling it to the brim before levelling off with a knife. If you’re using a liquid measuring cup, be mindful of air pockets that can throw off the measurement. For air fryer recipes, precision ensures even cooking and perfect textures.</p>

<p>If you don’t have measuring cups, a kitchen scale can be a great alternative. Since 3.25 cups of yogurt is roughly 768 ml, it weighs about 768 grams due to yogurt’s similar density to water.
