<h3>How to measure 0.125 cup of lentils</h3>  
<p>Use a dry measuring cup for accuracy. Fill it to the 0.125 cup mark (or 2 tablespoons) and level it off with a straight edge. If you don’t have a measuring cup, a kitchen scale set to ounces will do the trick—just weigh out 1 ounce. This method ensures you nail the 0.125 cup lentils to ounces conversion every time.</p>
