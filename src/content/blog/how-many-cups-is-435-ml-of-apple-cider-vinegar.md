<h2>How to Measure 435 ml of Apple Cider Vinegar</h2>  
<p>If you don’t have a measuring jug with millilitre markings, you can still measure 435 ml of apple cider vinegar using standard cups. Since 1 cup equals roughly 236.6 ml, 435 ml works out to just under 2 cups. For greater accuracy, use 1 full cup plus another ¾ cup, or weigh it on a kitchen scale (1 ml of vinegar weighs about 1 gram).</p>
