<h2>How to Measure 100g of Cooked Rice Accurately</h2>  
<p>Using a digital kitchen scale is the easiest way to measure 100g of cooked rice. If your scale displays ounces, you'll see it’s roughly 3.53 oz. For those without scales, a standard UK measuring cup holds about 200g of cooked rice, so half a cup will give you approximately 100g (or 3.53 oz).</p>
