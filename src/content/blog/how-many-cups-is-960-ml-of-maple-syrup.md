<h2>Converting 960 ml of Maple Syrup to Cups</h2>
<p>To convert 960 ml of maple syrup to cups, you’ll need to know that 1 cup equals roughly 236.59 ml. Dividing 960 by 236.59 gives you approximately 4.06 cups. This measurement works for both baking and air fryer recipes, ensuring your dishes turn out just right.</p>
