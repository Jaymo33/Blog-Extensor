<h3>How to Measure 760 ml of Coconut Milk</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a measuring jug, you can use standard cups to measure 760 ml of coconut milk. Fill a cup to the 240 ml mark three times, then add another 40 ml (roughly 3 tablespoons) to reach 760 ml. This method works well for both canned and homemade coconut milk, ensuring you get the right consistency for your air fryer recipes.</p>
