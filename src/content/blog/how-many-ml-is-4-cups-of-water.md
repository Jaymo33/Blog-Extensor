<h3>US vs UK Cup Measurements</h3>  
<p>It's important to note that US and UK cup measurements differ. A US cup is 236.59 ml, while a UK metric cup is 284.13 ml. This means 4.0 cups of water in the US is 946.35 ml, whereas in the UK, it's 1136.52 ml. Always check which system your recipe uses to avoid mistakes.</p>
