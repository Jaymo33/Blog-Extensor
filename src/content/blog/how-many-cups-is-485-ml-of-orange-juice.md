<h3>US vs UK Cup Measurements</h3>
<p>It’s worth noting that US and UK cup measurements differ slightly. In the US, 1 cup is 240 ml, while in the UK, it’s 250 ml. For 485 ml of orange juice, this means approximately 2.02 US cups or 1.94 UK cups. Always check which standard your recipe uses.</p>
