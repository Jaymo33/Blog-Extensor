<h2>Understanding the Conversion</h2>  
<p>Converting 685 ml of heavy cream to cups depends on whether you’re using US or UK measurements. In the US, 1 cup equals 240 ml, so 685 ml works out to approximately 2.9 cups. In the UK, where 1 cup is 284 ml, 685 ml translates to about 2.4 cups. This distinction is crucial for air fryer recipes, especially when precision matters. If you’re unsure, always double-check your measurements to avoid recipe mishaps.</p>
