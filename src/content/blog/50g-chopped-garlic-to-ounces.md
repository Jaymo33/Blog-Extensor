<h3>How to measure 50g of chopped garlic without a scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a scale, you can estimate 50g of chopped garlic by volume. One medium-sized garlic clove weighs about 5g, so you’d need around 10 cloves to reach 50g. Once chopped, this fills roughly 3-4 tablespoons. For recipes requiring precise amounts, like garlic butter or marinades, accuracy is key. <h3>Storing leftover chopped garlic</h3>  
<p>If you’ve chopped more garlic than needed, store it properly to maintain freshness. Place 50g of chopped garlic in an airtight container with a splash of olive oil to prevent drying.
