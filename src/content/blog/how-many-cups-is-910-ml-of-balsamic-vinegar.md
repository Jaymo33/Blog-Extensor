<h3>How to Measure 910 ml of Balsamic Vinegar Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a measuring jug, use standard cup measurements for 910 ml of balsamic vinegar. One UK cup holds 250 ml, so 3 full cups plus ¾ of another will get you close. For US cups (240 ml), you’ll need 3 cups and 3 tablespoons. This method works well for marinades or dressings in air fryer recipes.</p>
