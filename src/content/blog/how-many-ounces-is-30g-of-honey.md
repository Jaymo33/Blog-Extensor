<h2>How to measure honey without scales</h2>  
<p>Not everyone has kitchen scales handy, but there are easy ways to measure 30g of honey (1.06 oz) without them. A standard tablespoon holds about 21g of honey, so 1.5 tablespoons will get you close to 30g. For a more precise method, use a measuring jug with fluid ounces—30g of honey is roughly 1.5 fluid ounces. Just remember, honey’s thickness means it won’t pour as easily as water.</p>
