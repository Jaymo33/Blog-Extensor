<h3>How to Measure Worcestershire Sauce Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can still measure 200g of Worcestershire sauce using volume conversions. Since 1 fluid ounce of Worcestershire sauce weighs roughly 28.35 grams, 200g translates to about 7.05 fluid ounces. Use a liquid measuring cup for accuracy, and remember that viscosity can slightly affect the measurement. For sticky sauces like Worcestershire, lightly greasing the measuring cup can help it pour smoothly.</p>
