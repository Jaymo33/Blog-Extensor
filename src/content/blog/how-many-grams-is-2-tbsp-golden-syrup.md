<p>2 tablespoons (tbsp) of golden syrup equals approximately 42 grams in the UK. This conversion is essential for air fryer recipes where precision matters, ensuring your dishes turn out perfectly every time. Whether you’re baking sticky toffee pudding or glazing roasted vegetables, knowing the exact weight of golden syrup helps avoid over-sweetening or under-measuring.</p>  

<h2>Why accurate golden syrup measurements matter</h2>  
<p>Golden syrup is a staple in many British desserts and savoury dishes, especially those prepared in an air fryer. Using too much can make your dish overly sweet, while too little might leave it lacking flavour. For example, if you’re making <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>air fryer cookies</a>, 2 tbsp of golden syrup (42g) ensures the right chewy texture without being cloying. Always measure golden syrup by weight for consistency, as its sticky nature makes volume measurements tricky.</p>  

<p>Many air fryer recipes, like <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/dessert>desserts</a>, rely on golden syrup for moisture and sweetness. A digital kitchen scale is the best tool for this, as it eliminates the guesswork of spoon measurements. If you’re converting from US recipes, note that UK tablespoons are slightly larger (15ml vs. 14.79ml), but the difference in grams is minimal for home cooking.</p>  

<h3>How to measure golden syrup without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have scales, use the spoon-and-level method for 2 tbsp of golden syrup. Fill the spoon to the brim, then level it off with a straight edge. This method isn’t as precise as weighing, but it’s a handy backup. For sticky ingredients like golden syrup, lightly greasing the spoon helps it slide off easily, ensuring you get the full 42g.</p>

<h3>Storing golden syrup for accurate measurements</h3>  
<p>Keep golden syrup in a cool, dry place to maintain its consistency. If it crystallises, gently warm the jar in hot water to return it to a liquid state. This ensures easy pouring and accurate measurements for your air fryer recipes. Always wipe the rim of the jar after use to prevent sticking and spoilage.</p>
