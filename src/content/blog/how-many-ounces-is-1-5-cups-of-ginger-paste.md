<p>Using the right amount of ginger paste can make or break your dish, especially in an air fryer where flavours intensify. <h3>How to Measure Ginger Paste Correctly</h3>  
<p>Ginger paste is denser than liquids, so measuring it in cups can be tricky. For 1.5 cups of ginger paste, use a kitchen scale to confirm it’s 12 ounces. If you don’t have a scale, spoon the paste into a measuring cup and level it off for accuracy. This method works well for pastes and thick ingredients.</p>
