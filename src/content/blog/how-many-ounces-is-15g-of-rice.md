<h2>Why Convert 15g of Rice to Ounces?</h2>  
<p>Many air fryer recipes, especially those from the UK, use grams for measurements. However, some kitchen scales or recipes might list ingredients in ounces. Converting 15g of rice to ounces (0.53 oz) helps you follow recipes accurately. This is particularly useful for dishes like air-fried rice cakes or crispy rice snacks.</p>  
<p>Precision matters in air frying because even small variations can affect texture and cooking time. For example, 15g of rice might be the perfect amount for a single serving of air-fried rice balls. Explore more precise measurements on our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-conversion-chart>air fryer conversion chart</a>.</p>  

<h2>How to Measure 15g of Rice Without a Scale</h2>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can estimate 15g of rice using household items. A tablespoon of uncooked rice typically weighs around 10-12g, so 1.5 tablespoons will be close to 15g. This method isn’t exact but works in a pinch for air fryer recipes.</p>  
<p>For more accurate results, consider investing in a digital scale. It’s a game-changer for air fryer cooking, ensuring perfect portions every time. Check out our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>recipe collection</a> for ideas on using 15g of rice in creative ways.</p>  

<h3>Types of Rice and Their Weights</h3>  
<p>Different types of rice have slightly varying weights. For example, 15g of basmati rice might look slightly different in volume compared to jasmine rice. However, the weight in ounces (0.53 oz) remains the same. This consistency makes weight-based measurements ideal for air fryer cooking.</p>  

<h2>Using 15g of Rice in Air Fryer Recipes</h2>  
<p>15g of rice is a versatile measurement for small-batch air fryer dishes. It’s perfect for making crispy rice crackers, mini rice cakes, or as a base for air-fried vegetable stir-fries. The key is to spread the rice evenly in the air fryer basket for even cooking.</p>  
<p>For inspiration, browse our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/vegetables>vegetable recipes</a> that pair well with rice. Whether you’re meal prepping or cooking for one, 15g of rice can be the foundation of a delicious air fryer meal.</p>  

<h3>Common Mistakes When Measuring Rice</h3>  
<p>Avoid packing rice tightly when measuring by volume, as this can lead to inaccurate weights. Instead, lightly spoon the rice into your measuring tool. For 15g of rice, precision ensures your air fryer dishes turn out perfectly every time.</p>
