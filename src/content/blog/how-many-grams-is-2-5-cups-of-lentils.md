<h2>How to Measure 2.5 Cups of Lentils Correctly</h2>  
<p>For the most accurate 2.5 cups of lentils in grams, use a kitchen scale. Scoop the lentils loosely into a measuring cup without packing them down—this avoids overestimating. If you don’t have a scale, remember that 1 cup of lentils is roughly 200 grams, so 2.5 cups equal 500 grams. This method works for both UK and US cup measurements, so no need to adjust.
