<h3>How to Measure 50g of Breadcrumbs Accurately</h3>  
<p>Using a digital kitchen scale is the easiest way to measure 50g of breadcrumbs. Simply place your bowl on the scale, tare it to zero, and add the breadcrumbs until you hit 50g. If you don’t have a scale, 50g is roughly equivalent to ½ cup of fine breadcrumbs or ⅔ cup of coarse ones. However, weighing is always more precise, especially for air fryer recipes where consistency matters.</p>
