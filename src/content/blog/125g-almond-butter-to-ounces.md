<h3>How to measure 125g of almond butter without scales</h3>  
<p>No kitchen scales? A standard tablespoon holds about 16g of almond butter, so 125g is roughly 7.5 tablespoons. For ounces, that’s just under 4.5 oz. Keep in mind that almond butter’s thickness varies by brand, so this method is a close estimate. For best results, we recommend investing in digital scales—they’re a game-changer for air fryer cooking.</p>

<h3>Storing almond butter for optimal freshness</h3>  
<p>Once opened, almond butter can separate or dry out. After measuring your 125g (4.41 oz), store the rest in an airtight container in the fridge. Stirring it occasionally maintains consistency. Proper storage ensures your next air fryer recipe tastes just as good as the first.</p>
