<h3>How to Measure Honey Correctly</h3>  
<p>To measure 3.25 cups of honey accurately, use a liquid measuring cup with clear ml markings. Lightly coat the cup with oil to help the honey slide out easily. For sticky ingredients like honey, a digital kitchen scale can also be handy—just remember that 1 ml of honey weighs roughly 1.42 grams.</p>
