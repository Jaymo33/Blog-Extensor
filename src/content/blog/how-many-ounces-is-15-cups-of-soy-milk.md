<h2>Why Accurate Measurements Matter in Air Fryer Cooking</h2>  
<p>Air fryers rely on precise ingredient ratios to achieve crispy, evenly cooked dishes. Using the correct amount of soy milk—like 1.5 cups (or 12 ounces)—ensures your recipes turn out perfectly. Too much liquid can make food soggy, while too little may dry it out. For more air fryer tips, check out our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-conversion-chart>air fryer conversion chart</a>.</p>

<h3>How to Measure 1.5 Cups of Soy Milk Accurately</h3>  
<p>Use a liquid measuring cup for the most precise results. Pour soy milk to the 1.5-cup mark, ensuring it sits level. For ounces, a kitchen scale can confirm the weight (12 oz). This method avoids guesswork, especially for air fryer dishes like tofu scrambles or vegan pancakes.</p>
