<h3>How to Measure Icing Sugar Correctly</h3>  
<p>Spoon the icing sugar into your measuring cup lightly without packing it down, then level it off with a knife. This avoids over-measuring, which can make frostings too sweet or dense. For air fryer baking, where even heat distribution is key, accuracy ensures your desserts cook evenly.</p>
