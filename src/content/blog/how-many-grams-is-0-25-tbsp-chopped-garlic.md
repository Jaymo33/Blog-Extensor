<h2>How to measure 0.25 tbsp of chopped garlic</h2>
<p>Measuring small quantities like 0.25 tbsp can be tricky without the right tools. A set of measuring spoons is indispensable, but if you don’t have one, here’s a practical tip: fill a 1 tbsp measure halfway, then halve that portion. This rough estimate gets you close to 0.25 tbsp (3.75g) of chopped garlic.</p>
<p>For even greater accuracy, consider using a kitchen scale.
