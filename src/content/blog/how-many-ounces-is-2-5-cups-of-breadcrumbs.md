<h3>How to Measure Breadcrumbs Correctly</h3>  
<p>To get the most accurate measurement, lightly spoon breadcrumbs into a measuring cup and level it off with a knife. Packing them down can lead to using more than needed, throwing off your recipe. If you’re converting 2.5 cups of breadcrumbs to ounces frequently, consider investing in a kitchen scale for effortless precision.</p>
