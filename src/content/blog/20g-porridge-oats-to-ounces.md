<h3>How to Measure 20g of Porridge Oats Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a scale, you can use tablespoons to measure 20g of porridge oats. Roughly 3 level tablespoons equal 20g, which is about 0.71 ounces. This method isn’t as precise as weighing, but it works in a pinch. For best results, fluff the oats lightly before scooping to avoid compacting them.</p>

<h3>Common Mistakes When Measuring Porridge Oats</h3>  
<p>One mistake is packing oats too tightly into measuring cups, which leads to more than 20g. Always spoon oats lightly and level off the top for accuracy. Another error is confusing fluid ounces with weight ounces—20g is a weight measurement, not volume. Stick to scales or our conversion for foolproof results.</p>
