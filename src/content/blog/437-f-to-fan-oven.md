<h3>How Fan Ovens Differ From Conventional Ovens</h3>
<p>Fan ovens cook food faster and more evenly than conventional ovens because they circulate hot air. This means you typically need to reduce the temperature by about 20 C when converting from Fahrenheit. For 437 F, this brings you to 225 C in a fan oven. The same principle applies to air fryers, which are essentially mini fan ovens.</p>
