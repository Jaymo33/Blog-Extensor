<h2>Using Hoisin Sauce in Your Air Fryer</h2>  
<p>Hoisin sauce is incredibly versatile in the air fryer. From glazing salmon to marinating tofu, its sweet and savoury profile enhances countless dishes. <h3>Common Mistakes When Measuring Hoisin Sauce</h3>  
<p>One frequent error is using a heaped tablespoon instead of a level one, which can throw off the 0.125 cup measurement.
