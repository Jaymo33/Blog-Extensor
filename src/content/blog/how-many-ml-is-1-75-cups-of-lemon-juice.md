<h3>How to Measure 1.75 Cups of Lemon Juice Correctly</h3>  
<p>To measure 1.75 cups of lemon juice (414 ml), use a liquid measuring cup with clear ml markings. Pour the juice slowly to avoid spills and check at eye level for accuracy. If you don’t have a measuring cup, a kitchen scale can help—1 ml of lemon juice weighs roughly 1 gram, so 414 ml would be about 414 grams. Freshly squeezed lemon juice is best for flavour, but bottled works too—just ensure it’s pure juice with no additives.</p>
