<h3>How to measure 75g of treacle without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have scales, you can still measure 75g of treacle (2.65 oz) using tablespoons. One tablespoon of treacle weighs roughly 25g, so three tablespoons will give you close to 75g. For sticky ingredients like treacle, lightly grease the spoon first for easy release. This method isn’t as precise as weighing but works in a pinch. Always level off the spoon for the best accuracy.</p>
