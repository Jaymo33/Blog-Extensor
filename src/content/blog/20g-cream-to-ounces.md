<h3>How to measure 20g of cream without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have digital scales, you can still measure 20g of cream using tablespoons. One tablespoon of cream weighs roughly 15g, so 20g is just over 1.3 tablespoons. This method isn’t as precise as weighing but works in a pinch for recipes like <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/breakfast>breakfast</a> bakes or creamy pasta sauces.</p>
