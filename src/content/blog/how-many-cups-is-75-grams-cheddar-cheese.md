<h3>How to Measure 75 Grams of Cheddar Cheese</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate measurement, use a kitchen scale to weigh 75 grams of cheddar cheese. If you don't have one, lightly packed grated cheese will roughly fill 0.75 cups. Keep in mind that shredded cheese takes up more volume than a solid block, so adjust accordingly.
