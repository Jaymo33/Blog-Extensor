<h3>How to measure minced garlic without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don't have digital scales, a tablespoon can help. One tablespoon of minced garlic weighs roughly 10g, so 2.5 tablespoons equal 25g (or 0.88 ounces). This method isn't as precise but works in a pinch. For perfectly measured ingredients, invest in a kitchen scale—it's a game-changer for air fryer cooking.</p>

<h3>Storing minced garlic for freshness</h3>  
<p>To keep minced garlic fresh, store it in an airtight container in the fridge for up to a week. For longer storage, freeze it in ice cube trays—each cube holds about 10g (0.35 ounces), so 2.5 cubes equal 25g. This way, you’ll always have pre-measured garlic ready for your air fryer creations.</p>
