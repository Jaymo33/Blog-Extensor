<h3>How to Measure Worcestershire Sauce Correctly</h3>  
<p>Liquid measurements can be tricky, especially with thick sauces like Worcestershire. For the most accurate results, use a clear measuring cup designed for liquids. Fill it to the 1.33-cup mark, then check the weight on a kitchen scale for confirmation. If you don’t have a scale, remember that 1 cup of Worcestershire sauce equals 8 ounces, so 1.33 cups is roughly 10.64 ounces. This method works for all air fryer recipes, whether you’re cooking frozen foods or fresh vegetables.</p>
