<h3>How to measure evaporated cream accurately</h3>  
<p>To measure 2 cups of evaporated cream, use a liquid measuring cup for the most accurate results. Fill it to the 2-cup mark, ensuring the cream is level with the measurement line. If you're converting to ounces, remember that 1 cup of evaporated cream is roughly 8 ounces, so 2 cups equal 16 ounces. This method works perfectly for air fryer recipes that demand precision.</p>
