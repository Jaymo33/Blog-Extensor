<h3>How to Measure 15g of Golden Syrup Accurately</h3>  
<p>Measuring 15g of golden syrup can be tricky due to its sticky consistency. A digital kitchen scale is the most accurate tool, but if you don't have one, use a measuring spoon. One tablespoon of golden syrup is roughly 20g, so 15g would be about three-quarters of a tablespoon. For air fryer recipes, precision matters, so always aim for the closest measurement possible.</p>
