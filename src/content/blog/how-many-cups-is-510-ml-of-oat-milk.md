<h2>Understanding the Conversion from 510 ml of Oat Milk to Cups</h2>

In the UK, where 1 cup is 284 ml, 510 ml translates to about 1.8 cups. This difference is crucial for air fryer recipes, where precision can affect texture and consistency.
