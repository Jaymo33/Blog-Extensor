<p>Measuring raisins by volume can be tricky due to their irregular shape. To get an accurate 0.25 cup, lightly pack the raisins into a measuring cup without pressing too hard. If you have a kitchen scale, weighing 1.25 ounces (35 grams) is even more precise.</p>
