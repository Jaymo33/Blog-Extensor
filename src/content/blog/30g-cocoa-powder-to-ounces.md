<h3>How to measure 30g cocoa powder without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can still measure 30g of cocoa powder using tablespoons. One level tablespoon of cocoa powder weighs roughly 7g, so 30g equals about 4.3 tablespoons.
