<h3>UK vs. US Cup Measurements</h3>  
<p>In the UK, a standard cup holds 250ml, while in the US, it's 240ml. This small difference means 450 grams of rice converts to 2.25 cups in the UK and 2.5 cups in the US. If you're following a recipe from another country, always check which measurement system it uses. A kitchen scale is the most reliable tool, but cup measurements work well for quick estimates.</p>  

<h2>How to Measure 450 Grams of Rice Without a Scale</h2>  
<p>If you don't have a kitchen scale, you can still measure 450 grams of rice using cups. Fill a UK measuring cup to the brim with rice, level it off, and repeat until you've used 2.25 cups. For US measurements, use 2.5 cups instead. This method isn't as precise as weighing but works in a pinch. <h3>Common Mistakes When Measuring Rice</h3>  
<p>One of the biggest mistakes is using the wrong cup size, leading to incorrect amounts. Another is scooping rice directly from the bag, which can compress it and throw off measurements. Always spoon rice into the cup gently for the most accurate results. If you're unsure, err on the side of slightly less rice to avoid overcooking.</p>
