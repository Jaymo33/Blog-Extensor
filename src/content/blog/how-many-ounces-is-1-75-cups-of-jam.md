<h2>How to measure 1.75 cups of jam accurately</h2>
<p>Measuring sticky ingredients like jam can be tricky, but a few simple tricks make it easier. First, lightly grease your measuring cup with oil to help the jam slide out cleanly. This ensures you get the full 1.75 cups (14 ounces) without leaving half stuck to the cup.</p>

<h3>Using kitchen scales for precision</h3>
