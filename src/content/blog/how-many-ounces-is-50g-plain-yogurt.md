<h2>How to measure 50g of plain yogurt accurately</h2>  
<p>Using a digital kitchen scale is the easiest way to measure 50g of plain yogurt (1.76 oz). Simply place your bowl on the scale, tare it to zero, and spoon in the yogurt until you hit 50g. If you don’t have a scale, 50g is roughly 3 tablespoons, but a scale is more precise for air fryer recipes. For more tips on measuring ingredients, explore our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>recipe collection</a>.</p>
