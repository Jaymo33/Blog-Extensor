<h3>How to Measure 2.75 Cups of Plain Flour Correctly</h3>  
<p>To measure 2.75 cups of plain flour accurately, use the spoon-and-level method. Lightly spoon flour into a dry measuring cup without packing it down, then level the top with a straight edge. This avoids over-measuring, which is common when scooping directly from the bag. For air fryer recipes, especially delicate ones like <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/cookies>cookies</a>, precision ensures even cooking and perfect texture.</p>
