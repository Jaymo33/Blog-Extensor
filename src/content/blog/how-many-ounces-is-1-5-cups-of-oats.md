<h3>Types of oats and their weights</h3>  
<p>Not all oats weigh the same per cup. Rolled oats, quick oats, and steel-cut oats have different densities, which can slightly affect the conversion. For most recipes, 1.5 cups of rolled oats will weigh around 12 ounces, but steel-cut oats may be slightly heavier. Always check your recipe for specific oat types to ensure accuracy.</p>  

<h2>How to measure 1.5 cups of oats accurately</h2>  
<p>To measure 1.5 cups of oats correctly, use the spoon-and-level method. Gently spoon the oats into a measuring cup without packing them down, then level off the top with a straight edge. Avoid tapping or shaking the cup, as this compacts the oats and leads to inaccurate measurements. For best results, consider using a kitchen scale to weigh out 12 ounces directly.</p>
