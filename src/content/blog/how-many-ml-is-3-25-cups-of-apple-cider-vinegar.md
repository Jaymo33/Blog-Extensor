<h2>How to Convert 3.25 Cups of Apple Cider Vinegar to ml</h2>  
<p>The conversion from cups to millilitres is straightforward once you know the basics. One US cup equals 236.59 ml, so multiplying this by 3.25 gives you 768.91 ml. If you’re using UK measurements, note that a UK cup is slightly larger at 284.13 ml, but most recipes default to US cups unless specified.</p>  

<h3>Using Measuring Tools for Accuracy</h3>  
<p>For the best results, use a liquid measuring cup or a digital kitchen scale. Pour the apple cider vinegar to the 3.25-cup mark, or weigh it (1 ml of vinegar weighs roughly 1 gram). This method eliminates errors and ensures your air fryer dishes are consistently delicious.</p>
