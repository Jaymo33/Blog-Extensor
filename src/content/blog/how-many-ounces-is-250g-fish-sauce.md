<h3>How to Measure 250g of Fish Sauce Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a scale, you can still measure 250g of fish sauce (8.82 oz) using volume. One fluid ounce of fish sauce weighs roughly 28.35g, so 250g is close to 8.82 fluid ounces. Use a liquid measuring cup for accuracy, but remember that viscosity can affect the measurement slightly.
