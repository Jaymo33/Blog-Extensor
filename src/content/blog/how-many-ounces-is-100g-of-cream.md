<h3>How to Measure 100g of Cream Accurately</h3>  
<p>A kitchen scale is the best tool for measuring 100g of cream. If you don’t have one, use a measuring jug with metric and imperial markings. Remember, 100g of cream equals roughly 3.53 ounces. For sticky ingredients like cream, lightly greasing your measuring spoon or cup can help with clean pouring.</p>

<h3>Tips for Storing Leftover Cream</h3>  
<p>If you have leftover cream, store it in an airtight container in the fridge for up to three days. For longer storage, freeze it in ice cube trays and thaw as needed. This way, you’ll always have the right amount for your next air fryer recipe.</p>
