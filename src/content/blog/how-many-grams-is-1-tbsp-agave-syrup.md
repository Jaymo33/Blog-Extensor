<h3>How to measure agave syrup without a scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a scale, use the spoon-and-level method for accuracy. Fill your tablespoon just until it overflows, then level it off with a straight edge. Remember, 1 tbsp of agave syrup equals roughly 21 grams, but slight variations can occur. For sticky ingredients like agave, lightly greasing the spoon helps it slide off cleanly.</p>
