<h2>Understanding the Conversion: 460 ml to Cups</h2>
<p>Converting millilitres to cups can be tricky, as the US and UK use different cup measurements. In the US, 1 cup equals 240 ml, while in the UK, 1 cup is 284 ml. This means 460 ml of orange juice converts to about 1.94 US cups or 1.62 UK cups. For air fryer recipes, where precision matters, using the correct measurement ensures your dishes cook evenly and taste perfect.</p>
