<h2>How to Calculate 115 Fan Oven to Conventional</h2>
<p>The general rule for converting 115 fan oven to conventional oven is adding 20°C. This adjustment accounts for the fan's efficient heat distribution. So 115°C fan becomes 135°C conventional. This conversion works for most baking scenarios, from cakes to roasted vegetables.</p>

<p>When using an air fryer, you might need slightly different adjustments. Air fryers are essentially mini convection ovens, so their 115 fan oven equivalent might vary.
