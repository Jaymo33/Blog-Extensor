<h3>UK vs. US Cup Measurements</h3>
<p>The difference between UK and US cup measurements might seem small, but it can affect your recipe. A UK cup holds 250ml, while a US cup is about 240ml. This means 500 grams of cream equals 2.1 UK cups or 2.4 US cups. <h2>Tips for Measuring Cream</h2>
<p>Measuring cream by weight (grams) is more accurate than using cups, as cream’s consistency can vary. If you don’t have a scale, spoon the cream into a measuring cup and level it off with a knife for the closest approximation. For air fryer recipes, where precision is often key, weighing ingredients like cream ensures consistent results every time.</p>
<p>Another handy tip is to check the fat content of your cream. Heavy cream (double cream in the UK) is denser than light cream, so 500 grams might take up slightly less volume.
