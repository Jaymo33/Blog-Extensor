<h3>How to Measure Rolled Oats Correctly</h3>  
<p>To measure 475 grams of rolled oats accurately, use a kitchen scale for the best results. If you don’t have one, fluff the oats with a spoon before scooping them into a measuring cup. Avoid packing them down, as this can lead to an incorrect measurement. For air fryer recipes, consistency is key, so always measure carefully.</p>  

<h2>Converting 475 Grams of Rolled Oats to Cups</h2>  
<p>The exact conversion of 475 grams of rolled oats to cups can vary slightly depending on how the oats are packed. Generally, 475 grams equals about 5.5 cups, but it’s always best to double-check if precision matters. For baking, even a small discrepancy can affect the outcome, so weigh your ingredients whenever possible.</p>
