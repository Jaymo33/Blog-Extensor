<h2>Understanding the Conversion from Millilitres to Cups</h2>
<p>Converting millilitres to cups can be tricky, especially when dealing with ingredients like cream. The standard conversion is 1 cup equals 240 ml, but this can vary slightly depending on the country. In the UK, 1 cup is typically 250 ml, while in the USA, it's 240 ml. For 735 ml of cream, the conversion works out to roughly 3.1 cups in the UK or 3.06 cups in the USA.</p>

<h3>Tips for Measuring Cream</h3>
<p>When measuring cream, always use a liquid measuring cup for accuracy. Fill the cup to the desired level and check at eye level to avoid over- or under-pouring. If your recipe calls for whipped cream, note that 735 ml of liquid cream will yield more volume once whipped. For best results, chill the cream and your mixing bowl before whipping.</p>
