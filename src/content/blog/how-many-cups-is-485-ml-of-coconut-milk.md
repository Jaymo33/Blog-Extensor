<h3>How to Measure Coconut Milk Correctly</h3>  
<p>To measure 485 ml of coconut milk, use a liquid measuring cup for the most accurate results. Stir the coconut milk well before measuring, as it can separate in the can. If you don’t have a measuring cup, remember that 485 ml is roughly 2.05 cups, or just over 2 cups. This measurement works for both UK and US cup sizes, making it versatile for any recipe.</p>
