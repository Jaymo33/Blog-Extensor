<h3>How to measure treacle without a scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can estimate 250g of treacle using tablespoons. One tablespoon of treacle weighs roughly 20g, so 250g equals about 12.5 tablespoons. However, this method is less accurate than weighing, especially for sticky ingredients like treacle. For consistent results, we recommend investing in a digital scale.</p>

<h3>Storing treacle for long-term use</h3>  
<p>Proper storage keeps treacle fresh for months. After measuring out your 250g (8.82 ounces), ensure the lid is tightly sealed to prevent drying. Store in a cool, dark place—the pantry is ideal. If your treacle crystallises, gently warm the jar in hot water to restore its smooth texture.</p>
