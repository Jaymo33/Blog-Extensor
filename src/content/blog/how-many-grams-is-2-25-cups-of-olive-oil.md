<h2>How to Measure 2.25 Cups of Olive Oil Correctly</h2>  
<p>Measuring liquids like olive oil can be tricky due to meniscus curves in cups. For 2.25 cups, use a clear measuring jug at eye level for accuracy. Alternatively, weigh 510 grams directly on a scale for foolproof results. This method is faster and avoids spills, keeping your <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-cleaner>air fryer cleaner</a>.</p>
