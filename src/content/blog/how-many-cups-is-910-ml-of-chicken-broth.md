<h2>How to Measure 910 ml of Chicken Broth</h2>  
<p>If you don't have a measuring jug with millilitre markings, don't worry. You can easily measure 910 ml of chicken broth using standard cup measurements. Since 1 cup equals roughly 236.59 ml, dividing 910 ml by this number gives you 3.85 cups. For practical purposes, you can round this to 3 and 3/4 cups.</p>  
<p>For recipes where precision matters, like sauces or gravies, sticking to the exact 3.85 cups ensures the right consistency. <p>It's worth noting that US and UK cup measurements differ slightly. In the UK, 1 cup is 250 ml, meaning 910 ml would be 3.64 cups.
