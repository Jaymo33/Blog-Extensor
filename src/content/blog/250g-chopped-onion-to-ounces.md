<h3>How to measure chopped onion</h3>  
<p>To measure 250g of chopped onion, use a kitchen scale for accuracy. If you don’t have one, roughly 2 medium-sized onions yield about 250g when chopped. Pack the onions lightly into a measuring cup to avoid overfilling. Remember, 250g of chopped onion to ounces is 8.82, so keep this in mind for recipes requiring precise amounts.</p>
