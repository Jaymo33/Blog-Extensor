<h2>How to Measure 485 ml of Oat Milk</h2>
<p>Measuring 485 ml of oat milk is straightforward if you have the right tools. A liquid measuring cup with millilitre markings is ideal, but if you only have a standard cup measure, you'll need to convert. Remember, 1 cup equals roughly 237 ml, so 485 ml is just over 2 cups.</p>
