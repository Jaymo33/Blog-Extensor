<h2>Converting 310 ml of Water to Cups: UK vs. US Measurements</h2>
<p>In the UK, 1 cup equals 284 ml, so 310 ml of water is roughly 1.09 UK cups. However, in the US, 1 cup is 240 ml, making 310 ml about 1.31 US cups. This difference might seem minor, but it can impact recipes, especially in baking. For air fryer users, sticking to one measurement system avoids confusion. Most modern recipes, including those on <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/dessert>our dessert category</a>, use US cup measurements for consistency.</p>
