<h3>How to measure 125g of yeast without a scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can still measure 125g of yeast using volume. One teaspoon of active dry yeast weighs about 3 grams, so 125g would be roughly 42 teaspoons. However, this method isn’t as precise as weighing, so we recommend investing in a scale for accuracy.
