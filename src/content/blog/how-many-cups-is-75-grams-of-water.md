<h2>Why Accurate Water Measurements Matter</h2>  
<p>In air frying and baking, even small variations in liquid quantities can affect texture and cooking times. Too much water can make batters runny, while too little may dry out your dish. <h3>How to Measure 75 Grams of Water Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a scale, use a liquid measuring cup. Since 1 cup of water is 240 grams, 75 grams is roughly 1/3 cup minus 1 teaspoon. For small quantities like this, a <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>detailed recipe guide</a> can help avoid guesswork. Note that UK and US cup sizes differ slightly, but the variance here is minimal.</p>
