<h2>How to measure 0.75 cup of chopped walnuts</h2>
<p>Measuring chopped walnuts can be tricky due to their irregular shape. For the most accurate 0.75 cup measurement, lightly pack the walnuts into a measuring cup without pressing too hard. This method ensures you get close to the 3-ounce mark. If you’re using a kitchen scale, simply weigh out 85 grams for precision.</p>

<h3>Tips for chopping walnuts evenly</h3>
<p>Unevenly chopped walnuts can lead to inconsistent measurements. Use a sharp knife and a steady cutting board to achieve uniform pieces. Smaller chops are ideal for coatings, while larger pieces work well in salads or as toppings. <h3>Storing chopped walnuts for future use</h3>
<p>Chopped walnuts can go rancid if not stored properly. Keep them in an airtight container in a cool, dark place for up to a month. For longer storage, freeze them in portions equivalent to 0.75 cup (3 ounces) for easy use in future recipes.</p>
