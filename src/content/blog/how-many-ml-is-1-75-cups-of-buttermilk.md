<h2>How to Measure Buttermilk Correctly</h2>
<p>For the most accurate results, use a liquid measuring cup or a digital scale set to millilitres. If you don’t have a scale, remember that 1 cup equals 237 ml, so 1.75 cups is 414 ml (237 x 1.75). This method works for both UK and US measurements, though cup sizes can vary slightly. Always level off your measuring cup to avoid over-pouring.</p>
