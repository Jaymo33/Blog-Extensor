<h3>US vs. UK Cup Measurements</h3>  
<p>It's important to note that US and UK cup measurements differ slightly. A US cup holds 240 ml, while a UK cup is 284 ml. This means 910 ml converts to about 3.85 US cups or 3.2 UK cups. If your recipe originates from a different region, double-check which standard it uses to avoid errors. <h3>Tips for Measuring Lemon Juice</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate results, use a liquid measuring cup rather than dry measuring cups. Fill it to the 910 ml mark or use a kitchen scale for precision. Freshly squeezed lemon juice offers the best flavour, but bottled works in a pinch—just check for additives. If your air fryer recipe calls for zest too, remember that one lemon yields about 1 tablespoon of juice and 1 teaspoon of zest.</p>
