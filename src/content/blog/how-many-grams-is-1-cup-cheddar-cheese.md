<p>Different brands of cheddar cheese may vary slightly in density, but 113 grams per 1.0 cups is a reliable standard. <p>If you don’t have a scale, you can still measure 1.0 cups of cheddar cheese accurately. Shredded cheese should fill the cup loosely, while grated cheese can be lightly packed.
