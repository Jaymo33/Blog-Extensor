<h3>UK vs. US Cup Measurements</h3>  
<p>In the UK, 1 cup of icing sugar weighs 120g, making 0.25 cups equal to 30g. In the US, 1 cup is lighter at 112g, so 0.25 cups equals 28g. This slight difference matters in recipes where precision is key. Always specify which measurement system your recipe uses to avoid inconsistencies.</p>

<h2>How to Measure 0.25 Cups of Icing Sugar Without Scales</h2>  
<p>If you don’t have scales, spoon the icing sugar into a measuring cup and level it off with a knife. Avoid packing it down, as this can lead to over-measuring. For 0.25 cups, fill a 1-cup measure to the quarter mark. Sifting the sugar beforehand ensures uniformity, especially for delicate <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/dessert>air fryer desserts</a>.</p>
