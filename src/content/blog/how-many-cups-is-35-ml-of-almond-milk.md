<h3>How to Measure 35 ml of Almond Milk Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a measuring jug with millilitre markings, use a tablespoon for accuracy. One tablespoon holds about 15 ml, so 35 ml is just over two tablespoons (2 tbsp + 1 tsp). For cup measurements, fill a 1/4 cup slightly less than halfway. This method works well for recipes like air-fryer pancakes or custards where almond milk’s consistency is crucial.</p>
