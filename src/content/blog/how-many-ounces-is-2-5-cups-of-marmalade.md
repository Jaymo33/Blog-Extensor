<h2>Understanding the conversion from cups to ounces</h2>
<p>Converting cups to ounces can be tricky because it depends on the ingredient’s density. Marmalade, being a thick, sticky spread, weighs more than liquids like water. One cup of marmalade typically weighs around 8 ounces, so 2.5 cups would be 20 ounces. This measurement is crucial for air fryer recipes where precision affects texture and cooking time. For more conversion tips, visit our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-conversion-chart>air fryer conversion chart</a>.</p>

<h3>Tips for measuring marmalade accurately</h3>
<p>To measure 2.5 cups of marmalade (20 ounces) correctly, use a liquid measuring cup for sticky ingredients. Lightly grease the cup with oil to help the marmalade slide out easily. Whether you’re baking or glazing, accurate measurements ensure perfect results every time.</p>
