<h2>How to Measure 150g of Protein Powder Without Scales</h2>  
<p>If you don’t have kitchen scales, you can still measure 150g of protein powder using volume. One level tablespoon of most protein powders weighs around 15g, so 10 tablespoons would give you roughly 150g (or 5.29 ounces). However, this method isn’t as precise as weighing, so for best results, invest in a good set of scales.
