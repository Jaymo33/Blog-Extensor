<h2>Why Convert 338 Fan Oven to Conventional Oven?</h2>

<h3>Common Conversion Mistakes</h3>
<p>Avoid simply using the same temperature when converting 338 fan oven to conventional oven. The reduced heat in conventional ovens means dishes need more time, not necessarily higher temperatures. Overcompensating with heat can lead to burnt exteriors and raw interiors. This principle applies doubly for air fryers, where the intense heat requires careful monitoring.</p>
