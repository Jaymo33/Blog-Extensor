<h3>How to measure ground cumin correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate results, use a kitchen scale to weigh 1.75 cups of ground cumin in ounces. Scooping directly from the bag can compress the spice, leading to inconsistent measurements. Instead, spoon the cumin lightly into a measuring cup and level it off. This method ensures you get the correct 14 oz for your recipe.</p>
