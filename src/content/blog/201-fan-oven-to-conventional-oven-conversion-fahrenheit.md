<h2>Understanding Fan Oven vs Conventional Oven Temperatures</h2>
<p>The key difference between a 201 fan oven and conventional oven lies in heat distribution. Fan ovens circulate hot air constantly, cooking food more evenly at slightly lower temperatures. When converting 201 fan oven to Fahrenheit for conventional baking, you'll typically add about 25-50°F to compensate for the less efficient heat distribution. This principle applies whether you're using a traditional oven or adapting recipes for your air fryer.</p>
