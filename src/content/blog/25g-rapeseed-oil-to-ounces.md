<h3>Converting between grams and ounces</h3>
<p>The general conversion rate for rapeseed oil is 1 gram equals approximately 0.035 ounces. Therefore, to convert 25g of rapeseed oil to ounces, you multiply 25 by 0.035, giving you 0.88 ounces. This conversion is standard for rapeseed oil, though other oils might have slightly different densities.</p>
