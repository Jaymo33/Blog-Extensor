<h3>UK vs. US Cup Measurements</h3>  
<p>It's worth noting that cup measurements can vary slightly between the UK and the US. In the UK, a standard cup holds about 250ml, while in the US, it's roughly 240ml. For rolled oats, this difference is minimal, but for other ingredients, it can matter. Always double-check if you're following a recipe from another country.</p>  

<h2>How to Measure 250 Grams of Rolled Oats Without Scales</h2>  
<p>If you don't have kitchen scales, you can still measure 250 grams of rolled oats using cups. Simply fluff the oats with a spoon to loosen them, then lightly spoon them into a measuring cup. Level off the top with a straight edge for accuracy. This method ensures you get as close to 2.5 cups as possible without weighing.</p>
