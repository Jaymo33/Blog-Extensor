<p>The weight of your cod fillet significantly impacts how it cooks in the air fryer. Air fryers work by circulating hot air, so ensuring even thickness across the fillet promotes uniform cooking. If you're working with a large 4.75 kg cod fillet to pounds conversion, you might decide to cut it into individual servings before cooking. <h2>How to cook a large cod fillet in your air fryer</h2>
<p>Cooking a 4.75 kg cod fillet in your air fryer is entirely possible with the right approach. Preheat your air fryer to around 200°C to ensure it starts cooking immediately upon contact. If your fillet is too large for the basket in one piece, cutting it into manageable portions is wise. This prevents overcrowding and promotes even browning and crisping on all sides. For a 4.75 kg cod fillet to pounds, which is over 10 pounds, you'll likely have several good-sized pieces to work with. <p>Timing is crucial when air frying a substantial amount of cod like a 4.75 kg cod fillet. As a general rule, cook cod for about 10-12 minutes per inch of thickness at 200°C.
