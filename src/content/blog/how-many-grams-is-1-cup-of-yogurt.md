<h3>How to Measure 1.0 Cups of Yogurt Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>No scale? No problem. Use the spoon-and-level method: spoon yogurt into a measuring cup, then level it off with a knife for an approximate 245 grams. For thicker yogurts, gently press to remove air pockets. This trick works wonders for <a href="https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/dessert">air fryer desserts</a> like cheesecake or parfaits.</p>
