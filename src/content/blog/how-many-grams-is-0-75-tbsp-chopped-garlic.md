<h2>How to measure 0.75 tbsp chopped garlic accurately</h2>
<p>Measuring chopped garlic can be tricky due to its irregular shape and tendency to clump. For the most accurate 0.75 tbsp (11 gram) measurement, use these professional techniques. First, ensure your garlic is evenly chopped - larger chunks will leave gaps in your tablespoon measure while finely minced garlic packs more densely. The best method is to lightly spoon the chopped garlic into your measuring spoon without pressing down, then level off the top with a straight edge.</p>

<h3>Using kitchen scales for perfect precision</h3>
