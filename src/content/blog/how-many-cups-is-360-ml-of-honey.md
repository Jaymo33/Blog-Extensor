<h2>How to Measure 360 ml of Honey in Cups</h2>
<p>Measuring honey can be messy, but a few tricks make it easier. First, lightly grease your measuring cup with oil to help the honey slide out smoothly. Pour the honey slowly, levelling it off at the 1.5-cup mark for accuracy. If you’re using a scale, 360 ml of honey weighs roughly 504 grams, but cups are often more convenient for home cooks.</p>

<h3>Tools for Measuring Honey</h3>
