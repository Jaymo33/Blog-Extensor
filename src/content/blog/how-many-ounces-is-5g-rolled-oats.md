<h3>How to Measure 5g of Rolled Oats</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a digital scale, use a measuring spoon to approximate 5g of rolled oats. One level tablespoon is about 5-6g, so slightly underfilled will get you close. For air fryer recipes, consistency is key, so investing in a small kitchen scale is worthwhile.</p>
