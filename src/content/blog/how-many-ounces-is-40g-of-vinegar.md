<h3>How to measure 40g of vinegar without scales</h3>  
<p>No scales? No problem. One tablespoon of vinegar weighs about 15g, so 40g is roughly 2.5 tablespoons. For ounces, that’s just under 1.5 fluid ounces (since 1 fluid ounce ≈ 29.57ml, and vinegar’s density is close to water). A shot glass or small measuring jug can help eyeball it. Just avoid using teaspoons—they vary too much for precise conversions.</p>
