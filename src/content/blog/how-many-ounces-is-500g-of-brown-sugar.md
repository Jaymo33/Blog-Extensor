<h2>How to Convert 500g Brown Sugar to Ounces</h2>  
<p>The conversion from grams to ounces is straightforward: 1 gram equals 0.035274 ounces. For 500g brown sugar, multiply 500 by 0.035274 to get 17.64 ounces. This works for both light and dark brown sugar, as their densities are nearly identical.</p>
