<h3>How to Measure Ground Coriander Without Scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, use the spoon-and-level method for 1.66 cups of ground coriander. Lightly spoon the spice into a measuring cup and level it off with a knife. This avoids compacting, which could lead to more than 5.3 ounces. For smaller quantities, tablespoons or teaspoons work—just refer to our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-conversion-chart>conversion chart</a>.</p>

<h3>Storing Leftover Ground Coriander</h3>  
<p>After measuring 1.66 cups (5.3 oz), store leftovers in an airtight container away from heat and light. Ground coriander loses potency over time, so label the container with the date.
