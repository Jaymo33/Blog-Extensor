<h2>How to measure 0.75 tbsp of whipping cream without scales</h2>
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can still measure 0.75 tbsp accurately. A standard UK tablespoon holds 15 ml, so 0.75 tbsp is roughly 11.25 ml. Use a measuring spoon and fill it three-quarters full for the correct amount. This method works well for liquids like whipping cream, where volume and weight are closely related.</p>
<p>For thicker creams or recipes requiring absolute precision, we recommend investing in digital scales. Many affordable options are available, and they’re invaluable for air fryer cooking.
