<p>If you don't have a kitchen scale, you can still measure 500g of rice using cups or spoons. One cup of uncooked rice typically weighs around 185g, so 500g is roughly 2.7 cups. For ounces, this means 17.64 oz is about 2.7 standard cups. Keep in mind that rice varieties like basmati or jasmine may differ slightly in density.
