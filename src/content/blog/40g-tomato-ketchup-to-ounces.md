<h3>How to Measure 40g of Tomato Ketchup</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a digital scale, you can use tablespoons to measure 40g of tomato ketchup. Roughly 2.5 tablespoons will give you the equivalent of 40g or 1.41 ounces. This method is handy when you're in a rush but still want to stick to the recipe. Just remember, ketchup’s thickness can vary slightly by brand, so a scale is always best for precision.</p>

<h3>Does Brand Affect the Weight of Tomato Ketchup?</h3>  
<p>While most tomato ketchups have a similar density, slight variations exist between brands. Thicker ketchups may weigh slightly more per tablespoon, but 40g will still be close to 1.41 ounces. If precision is key, like in baking or marinades, always use a scale. For everyday cooking, the tablespoon method works well.</p>
