<h3>How to measure 3 tbsp of cottage cheese correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate 3 tbsp cottage cheese to grams conversion, use a kitchen scale. Scoop the cheese lightly into a tablespoon without packing it down, then level it off. If you don’t have a scale, remember that 1 tbsp equals roughly 15 grams in the UK.
