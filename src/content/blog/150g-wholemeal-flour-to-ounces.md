<h3>How to measure 150g wholemeal flour without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have scales, use the spoon-and-level method for 150g (5.29 oz) of wholemeal flour. Gently spoon the flour into a measuring cup, then level it off with a knife. Avoid scooping directly from the bag, as this compacts the flour and adds extra weight. One UK metric cup of wholemeal flour holds about 120g, so 150g is roughly 1¼ cups. For best results, invest in a digital scale—it’s a game-changer for air fryer baking.</p>
