<h3>How to Measure Sugar Syrup Correctly</h3>  
<p>To measure 2 cups of sugar syrup accurately, use a liquid measuring cup with clear markings. Pour the syrup slowly to avoid air bubbles, and check the measurement at eye level. Remember, 2 cups of sugar syrup to ounces is 16, so double-check your measurements before adding it to your air fryer recipe.</p>
