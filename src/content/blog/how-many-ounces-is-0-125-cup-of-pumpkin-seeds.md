<h2>Why Accurate Measurements Matter in Air Fryer Cooking</h2>
<p>Using the correct amount of ingredients, like 0.125 cup of pumpkin seeds, ensures even cooking and optimal flavour. Air fryers rely on hot air circulation, so overcrowding or underfilling can affect texture and taste. For best results, always measure ingredients like pumpkin seeds accurately before adding them to your air fryer basket.</p>

<h2>How to Measure 0.125 Cup of Pumpkin Seeds</h2>
<p>Measuring 0.125 cup of pumpkin seeds is simple with the right tools. Use a standard measuring cup and fill it halfway to the 1/4 cup mark. Alternatively, a kitchen scale can provide the most accurate measurement, showing you the exact 0.5 ounces needed for your recipe.</p>

<h2>Converting Other Pumpkin Seed Measurements</h2>
<p>If your recipe calls for a different amount of pumpkin seeds, here’s a quick reference guide. One full cup of pumpkin seeds weighs roughly 4 ounces, so halving or quartering this measurement is easy. For example, 0.25 cup would be 1 ounce, and 0.5 cup would be 2 ounces.</p>

<h3>Tips for Storing Pumpkin Seeds</h3>
<p>To keep your pumpkin seeds fresh, store them in an airtight container in a cool, dark place. This ensures they retain their flavour and crunch for future recipes. If you’ve got leftover seeds after measuring out 0.125 cup, they’ll stay fresh for weeks when stored properly.</p>
