<h2>Why Accurate Garlic Measurements Matter</h2>  
<p>Garlic is a powerhouse of flavour, and using the right amount can make or break a dish. Too little, and your meal might taste bland; too much, and it can overpower other ingredients. For air fryer recipes, consistency is key—especially when preparing batch meals or meal prepping. A precise 0.66 cup to ounces conversion ensures your garlic chicken or garlic-roasted vegetables turn out perfectly every time. <h3>How to Measure Chopped Garlic Correctly</h3>  
<p>To get an accurate 0.66 cup of chopped garlic, lightly pack the garlic into a measuring cup without crushing it. Avoid heaping the cup, as this can lead to excess weight. If you’re using pre-chopped garlic from a jar, note that it may have added moisture, slightly altering the weight. For best results, fresh garlic is ideal—especially in air fryer recipes where texture matters.</p>
