<h3>How to Measure 1000g of Baking Soda Accurately</h3>  
<p>For best results, use a digital kitchen scale set to grams when measuring baking soda. If your scale only displays ounces, simply input 35.27 oz for 1000g. Scoop the baking soda gently to avoid compacting it, which could lead to over-measuring. Level off the top with a knife for precision, especially when using cups.
