<h3>How to Measure 3.5 Cups of Coconut Milk Correctly</h3>
<p>For consistent results, always stir your coconut milk well before measuring, as it naturally separates. Use a liquid measuring cup for accuracy, filling it to the 3.5 cup mark (828 ml). If you're using metric measurements, a kitchen scale set to millilitres works brilliantly too. This method is particularly helpful when preparing <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/dessert>air fryer desserts</a> where precision matters.</p>
