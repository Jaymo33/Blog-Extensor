<h3>UK vs. US Cup Measurements for Cream</h3>
<p>In the UK, 1 cup equals 284 ml, while in the US, it’s 240 ml. This means 985 ml of cream is roughly 4.16 cups in the UK and 4.17 cups in the US. The difference might seem small, but it can matter in precise recipes.
