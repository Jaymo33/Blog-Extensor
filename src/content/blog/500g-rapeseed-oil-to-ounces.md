This makes 500g rapeseed oil to ounces a straightforward calculation that any home cook can master.</p>

<h3>Why measure rapeseed oil by weight?</h3>
<p>Measuring ingredients by weight rather than volume is more accurate, especially for liquids like oil. While you might see some recipes calling for millilitres or fluid ounces, grams and ounces (weight measurements) give consistent results regardless of temperature or container shape. This is particularly important when using your air fryer, where even small variations can affect cooking times and results.</p>
