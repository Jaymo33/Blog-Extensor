<p>Turmeric is a powerful spice, and even small variations can affect flavour and colour. Too much turmeric might overpower your dish, while too little could leave it bland. <h3>How to Measure 0.66 Cup of Turmeric</h3>  
<p>For best results, use a dry measuring cup and level off the turmeric with a straight edge. If you don’t have a 0.66 cup measure, you can use a 1/3 cup twice (totalling 0.66 cup). Alternatively, a kitchen scale set to ounces will give you the exact 2.64 oz equivalent. This method is foolproof for air fryer cooking, where precision matters.</p>
