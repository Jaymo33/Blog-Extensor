<p>To measure 5 tbsp of Nutella in grams, use a kitchen scale for the most precise results. Scoop the Nutella into a bowl or onto parchment paper, then weigh it until you reach 85 grams. If you don't have a scale, lightly spoon the Nutella into a tablespoon and level it off for a close approximation. Avoid packing it down, as this can lead to over-measuring.
