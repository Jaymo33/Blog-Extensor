<h2>Why accurate breadcrumb measurements matter</h2>  
<p>Using the right amount of breadcrumbs is crucial for texture and flavour in air fryer cooking. Too few, and your coating won’t crisp up properly; too many, and your dish might turn out dry. For recipes like air-fried chicken or fish, 1.75 cups of breadcrumbs (7 ounces) is often the sweet spot. Check out our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-conversion-chart>air fryer conversion chart</a> for more handy measurement tips.</p>  

<p>Different types of breadcrumbs can vary slightly in weight, but standard dry breadcrumbs will generally weigh around 4 ounces per cup. This means 1.75 cups equals roughly 7 ounces. If you’re using fresh breadcrumbs, they’ll be slightly heavier due to moisture content. Always measure for best results.</p>  

<h3>How to measure breadcrumbs correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate measurement, use a kitchen scale to weigh your breadcrumbs in ounces. If you don’t have one, spoon the breadcrumbs into a measuring cup and level it off with a knife. Never pack the breadcrumbs down, as this will give you more than the recipe calls for.
