<h3>How to Measure 510 ml of Coconut Oil Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, use a liquid measuring cup for accuracy. Coconut oil solidifies at cooler temperatures, so melt it first for a precise measurement. Pour the liquid oil into the cup until it reaches the 510 ml mark, which is roughly 2.15 cups. For sticky ingredients like coconut oil, a silicone spatula helps scrape every last bit into your mix. This method ensures you’re not wasting a drop of this versatile oil.</p>
