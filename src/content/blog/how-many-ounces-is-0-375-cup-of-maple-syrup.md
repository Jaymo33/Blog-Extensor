<h2>Why accurate measurements matter in air fryer cooking</h2>

<h3>How to measure 0.375 cup of maple syrup</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate measurement, use a digital kitchen scale set to ounces. Pour the syrup until it reaches 3 oz, and you’ve got the perfect amount. If you don’t have a scale, a liquid measuring cup marked in fractions will work—just be sure to level it off. This method is especially useful when preparing <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>air fryer recipes</a> that require precise sweetness levels.</p>
