<h2>How to measure 0.25 tbsp of honey in grams</h2>
<p>To measure 0.25 tbsp of honey in grams, you’ll need a digital kitchen scale. Simply place your measuring spoon on the scale, tare it to zero, and then add honey until it reaches 5.3 grams. This method eliminates guesswork and ensures you’re using the exact amount required.</p>

<h3>Tips for working with honey</h3>
<p>Honey can be sticky and difficult to measure, so here are a few tips. Lightly coat your measuring spoon with oil to help the honey slide off easily. Alternatively, warm the honey slightly to make it more pourable. These small tricks can make measuring 0.25 tbsp of honey in grams much simpler.</p>
