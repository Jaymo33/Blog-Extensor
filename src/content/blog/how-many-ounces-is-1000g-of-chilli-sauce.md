<h2>How to Measure 1000g of Chilli Sauce</h2>
<p>Measuring 1000g of chilli sauce is straightforward if you have a kitchen scale. Simply place your container on the scale, tare it to zero, and pour the sauce until it reaches 1000g. If your scale displays ounces, you’ll see it’s roughly 35.27 ounces. For those without a scale, 1000g of chilli sauce is roughly equivalent to 4 cups, though this can vary slightly depending on the sauce’s thickness.</p>
<p>Using the right amount of chilli sauce is key to balancing flavours in your air fryer dishes. Too much can overpower other ingredients, while too little might leave your dish lacking. If you’re experimenting with new recipes, start with the recommended 1000g (or 35.27 ounces) and adjust to taste. <p>If you don’t use all 1000g of chilli sauce at once, proper storage is key to maintaining its freshness. Transfer the sauce to an airtight container and refrigerate it for up to two weeks.
