<h3>How Fan Ovens Differ From Conventional Ovens</h3>
<p>Fan ovens use a circulating fan to distribute heat, much like air fryers. This means 274 F in a fan oven cooks food faster than in a conventional oven. The forced air eliminates cold spots, ensuring uniform cooking. When converting 274 F to fan oven, you might lower the temperature by 10-15 C compared to conventional settings. This prevents burning while achieving perfect doneness.</p>
