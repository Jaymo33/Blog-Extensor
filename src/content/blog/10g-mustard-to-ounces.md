<h3>How to measure 10g of mustard without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have digital scales, you can still measure 10g of mustard (0.35 oz) using spoons. Roughly, one level tablespoon of mustard weighs around 15g, so two-thirds of a tablespoon will give you 10g. For smaller amounts, a teaspoon holds about 5g, so two teaspoons will get you close. Remember, mustard’s thickness can vary, so these are approximate measures.</p>
