<h2>Comparing 326 F Across Cooking Methods</h2>
<p>Traditional ovens without fans would typically require about 340 F to achieve similar results to 326 F in a fan oven. Air fryers, being compact convection ovens, can often use the same 326 F fan oven setting with excellent results. The key difference is cooking time - air fryers generally cook faster due to their smaller capacity and more intense air circulation.</p>

<p>When using 326 F in an air fryer, you might need to reduce cooking times by 20-30% compared to fan oven instructions. Always check food a few minutes early to prevent overcooking.
