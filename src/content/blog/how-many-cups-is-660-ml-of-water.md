<h2>UK vs US Cup Measurements</h2>
<p>It's worth noting that UK and US cup measurements differ slightly. In the UK, 1 cup equals 250 ml, while in the US, it's 240 ml. This means 660 ml of water converts to 2.79 cups in the UK and 2.8 cups in the US. While the difference seems small, it can add up in recipes requiring multiple cup measurements.</p>
<p>For air fryer recipes, especially those involving batters or sauces, sticking to one measurement system is best. If you're using a recipe from <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>our collection</a>, note whether it follows UK or US measurements to ensure accuracy.</p>
