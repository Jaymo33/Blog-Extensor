<h3>How to Measure 200g of Mayonnaise Accurately</h3>
<p>Using a digital kitchen scale is the most accurate way to measure 200g of mayonnaise. Simply place your container on the scale, tare it to zero, and spoon in the mayonnaise until you hit 200g. If you don't have a scale, 200g is roughly 7.05 ounces, or about 13 tablespoons. Remember, mayonnaise can vary in density, so weighing is best for precision.</p>

<h3>Tips for Storing Leftover Mayonnaise</h3>
<p>If you have leftover mayonnaise after measuring out 200g, store it in an airtight container in the fridge. Homemade mayonnaise lasts about a week, while store-bought varieties can last up to two months. Always check for signs of spoilage, like an off smell or separation, before using.</p>
