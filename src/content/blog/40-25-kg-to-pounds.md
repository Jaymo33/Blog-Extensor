Doing this calculation gives you the precise figure of 88.73555 pounds, which is typically rounded to 88.74 pounds for most practical kitchen purposes. <p>In the UK, body weight and sometimes large food items are still often discussed in stones and pounds. If you need to express 40.25 kg in this traditional unit, the conversion is a two-step process. First, know that one stone is equal to 14 pounds. You already know that 40.25 kg is 88.74 pounds. To find the number of stones, divide 88.74 by 14, which gives you 6.33 stones. The whole number is the stones, so 6 stone. Then, multiply the decimal part (0.33) by 14 to find the remaining pounds: 0.33 x 14 = 4.62 pounds. So, 40.25 kg is approximately 6 stone and 4.62 pounds.</p>
