<h3>How to measure tomato paste for air fryer recipes</h3>  
<p>Measuring tomato paste can be tricky due to its thick consistency. To accurately measure 3 cups of tomato paste in ounces, use a kitchen scale for the most precise results. <h3>Tips for storing leftover tomato paste</h3>
