<h3>How to measure 10g minced garlic without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can estimate 10g of minced garlic using household spoons. One tablespoon of minced garlic roughly equals 10g or 0.35 ounces. This trick works well for quick recipes like garlic bread or marinades. For more precision, invest in a digital scale—it’s a game-changer for air fryer cooking.</p>

<h3>Storing minced garlic for future use</h3>  
<p>If you’ve minced extra garlic, store it in an airtight container in the fridge for up to a week. Freezing minced garlic in ice cube trays is another great option—each cube holds about 10g (0.35 oz), perfect for future recipes. Just pop one out whenever you need it!</p>
