<h3>How to Measure 435 ml of Soy Sauce Without a Measuring Cup</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a measuring cup, you can use a standard mug or glass. A typical UK mug holds about 250 ml, so 435 ml would be roughly 1.75 mugs. For more accurate results, consider investing in a kitchen scale or measuring jug.</p>  

<h2>Converting 435 ml of Soy Sauce to Other Units</h2>  
<p>Besides cups, you might need to convert 435 ml of soy sauce to tablespoons or fluid ounces. Here’s a quick breakdown: 435 ml equals 29.4 tablespoons or 14.7 fluid ounces. These conversions are handy for smaller adjustments in recipes.</p>
