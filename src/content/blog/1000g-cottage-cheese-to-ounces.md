<h2>How to measure 1000g of cottage cheese</h2>
<p>For the most accurate measurement, use digital kitchen scales set to grams. If your scale only shows ounces, look for 35.27 oz to measure 1000g of cottage cheese. This precision matters in air fryer cooking, where even small quantity differences can affect how dishes crisp up.</p>
<p>When measuring cottage cheese, remember it's a semi-solid dairy product. Use the spoon-and-level method: spoon the cottage cheese into your measuring container without packing it down, then level off the top. For recipes from <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/breakfast>breakfast category</a>, this technique ensures you don't accidentally use too much, which could make dishes soggy.</p>
