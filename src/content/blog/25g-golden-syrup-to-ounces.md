<h3>How to measure 25g of golden syrup without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don't have scales, two level tablespoons of golden syrup will roughly equal 25g (0.88 oz). Lightly grease your spoon first to help the syrup slide off easily. <h3>Storing golden syrup for air fryer use</h3>  
<p>Keep your golden syrup in an airtight container at room temperature. If it crystallises, gently warm the jar in hot water before measuring out your 25g (0.88 oz) portion. This ensures easy pouring and accurate measurements for all your air fryer creations.</p>
