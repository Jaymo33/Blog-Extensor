<h2>Converting 100g of cornflour to ounces</h2>
<p>The conversion from grams to ounces is straightforward once you know the ratio. One gram equals 0.035 ounces, so 100g of cornflour works out to 3.53 ounces. This measurement is particularly useful for UK cooks who might be following American recipes or vice versa.</p>
<p>For quick reference, here’s a simple breakdown of common cornflour measurements:</p>
<ul>
<li>50g cornflour = 1.76 ounces</li>
<li>100g cornflour = 3.53 ounces</li>
<li>150g cornflour = 5.29 ounces</li>
<li>200g cornflour = 7.05 ounces</li>
</ul>

<h3>Tips for measuring cornflour in your air fryer recipes</h3>
<p>When measuring 100g of cornflour (or 3.53 ounces), always spoon it into your measuring cup and level it off with a knife. Scooping directly from the bag can compact the flour, leading to inaccurate measurements. This is especially crucial for air fryer baking, where precision affects texture and rise.</p>
