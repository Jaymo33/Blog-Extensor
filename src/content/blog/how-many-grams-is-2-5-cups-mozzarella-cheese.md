<p>Using the right amount of mozzarella cheese ensures your dish isn’t too dry or overly greasy. Too little cheese, and your air fryer pizza might lack that irresistible stretch. <h3>How to Measure Mozzarella Cheese Correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate results, use a kitchen scale. Scoop the shredded cheese loosely into a measuring cup without packing it down. If you don’t have a scale, fluff the cheese with a fork before measuring to avoid compaction. This method ensures you’re as close to 283 grams as possible.</p>

<h3>Common Mistakes When Measuring Cheese</h3>  
<p>Packing the cheese tightly into the cup is a common error—it can add up to 20% more weight, throwing off your recipe. Another mistake? Using block cheese without shredding it first. Cubes or slices won’t measure the same as shredded. For best results, always shred and measure loosely.</p>
