<h3>How to measure 15g of ginger paste without a scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a scale, you can still measure 15g of ginger paste (0.53 ounces) using common kitchen tools. A standard teaspoon holds about 5g, so three level teaspoons will give you roughly 15g. Alternatively, a tablespoon can hold around 15g when slightly heaped. For sticky ingredients like ginger paste, lightly oil the spoon first to help it slide off easily. This method isn’t as precise as a scale, but it works in a pinch for most <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/vegetables>vegetable-based recipes</a>.</p>

<h3>Storing ginger paste for future use</h3>
