<h3>How to Measure Flaxseed Correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate conversion, use a kitchen scale. Scooping flaxseed directly with a cup can lead to inconsistencies due to settling. Lightly spooning it into the measuring cup and levelling it off works best. If you’re adjusting a recipe, remember that 1.33 cups of flaxseed to ounces is roughly 5.3 oz.</p>
