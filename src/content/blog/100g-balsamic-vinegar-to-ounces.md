<h3>How to measure 100g of balsamic vinegar without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don't have a kitchen scale, use a liquid measuring cup. Since 100g of balsamic vinegar is roughly 3.53 ounces, it translates to just under half a US cup (0.42 cups) or about 7 tablespoons. For UK cooks, this is slightly less than 100ml due to density differences. Always opt for weight measurements when precision matters.</p>
