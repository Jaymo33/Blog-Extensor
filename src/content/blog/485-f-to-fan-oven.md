<p>When converting 485 F to fan oven, it's helpful to know the general rule: subtract about 25 degrees from conventional oven settings. This accounts for the fan's efficient heat distribution. So 485 F (250 C) in a fan oven gives similar results to 500 F (260 C) in a conventional oven.</p>
