<h2>How to Measure 485 ml of Maple Syrup</h2>
<p>Measuring 485 ml of maple syrup can be tricky due to its thick consistency. The easiest way is to use a liquid measuring cup with clear markings. Pour the syrup slowly to avoid air bubbles, which can distort the measurement. If you don’t have a measuring cup, a kitchen scale can also do the trick. Just remember that 485 ml of maple syrup weighs roughly 625 grams.</p>
