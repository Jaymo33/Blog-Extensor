<h2>Converting 350 Grams of Plain Flour to Cups</h2>
<p>To convert 350 grams of plain flour to cups, you'll need to consider how the flour is measured. The most accurate method is to use a kitchen scale, but if you don't have one, the spoon-and-level technique works well. Gently spoon flour into your measuring cup without packing it down, then level off the top with a knife. This method typically gives you about 125 grams per cup, making 350 grams roughly 2.8 cups. For air fryer recipes like crispy coatings or batters, this precision ensures perfect texture.</p>

<p>Remember that different types of flour have different densities. Plain flour, also known as all-purpose flour, is what we're converting here.
