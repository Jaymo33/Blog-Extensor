<h2>Converting Cups to Grams for Milk</h2>  
<p>Milk’s density means 1 cup typically weighs around 240 grams, so 2.5 cups equal 600 grams. However, factors like fat content (whole vs. skim) can cause slight variations. For most recipes, especially those using an air fryer, 600 grams is a safe bet. If precision is critical, weighing your milk with a kitchen scale is best. Need more conversion help? <h3>Does Milk Type Affect the Weight?</h3>  
<p>Whole milk, skim milk, and plant-based alternatives have slightly different densities, but the difference is minimal for most recipes.
