<h2>How to Measure 1.25 Cups of Milk Correctly</h2>  
<p>To measure 1.25 cups of milk accurately, use a liquid measuring cup for the best results. Fill it to the 1-cup mark, then add another quarter cup. If you’re converting to grams, a kitchen scale is your best friend—simply pour until it hits 300 grams. This method eliminates errors, especially when working with air fryer recipes that demand precision.</p>  

<h3>UK vs. US Cup Measurements</h3>
