<p>1.66 cups of treacle equals approximately 13.28 ounces. This conversion is essential for precise baking or cooking, especially when using an air fryer where measurements matter. Treacle, a thick syrup, can vary slightly in weight, but this standard conversion ensures accuracy for your recipes.</p>  

<h2>Understanding Treacle Measurements</h2>  
<p>Treacle is a staple in British baking, adding rich sweetness to desserts like sticky toffee pudding or treacle tart. When converting 1.66 cups of treacle to ounces, it’s important to note that treacle’s density affects the weight. For most recipes, 1 cup of treacle weighs around 8 ounces, making 1.66 cups roughly 13.28 ounces. This measurement ensures your air fryer recipes turn out perfectly every time.</p>  

<p>If you’re working with an air fryer, precise measurements like this help avoid overly sticky or dry results. For more air fryer-friendly recipes, check out our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>recipes section</a>, where we share tips for perfecting desserts and savoury dishes alike.</p>  

<h3>Why Accurate Measurements Matter</h3>  
<p>Using the correct amount of treacle ensures your baked goods have the right texture and sweetness. Too much can make desserts overly dense, while too little might leave them lacking flavour. This is especially crucial for air fryer cooking, where even heat distribution relies on balanced ingredients.</p>  

<h2>Converting Cups to Ounces for Treacle</h2>  
<p>To convert 1.66 cups of treacle to ounces, multiply the cup measurement by the weight per cup. Since 1 cup of treacle is about 8 ounces, 1.66 cups x 8 ounces equals 13.28 ounces. This simple calculation works for most syrups and thick liquids used in baking.</p>

<h3>Tips for Measuring Treacle</h3>  
<p>Treacle’s sticky consistency can make measuring tricky. Lightly greasing your measuring cup or spoon with oil helps it slide out easily. Alternatively, warm the treacle slightly to loosen it before measuring. These small adjustments ensure you get the exact 13.28 ounces needed for your recipe.</p>  

<h2>Using Treacle in Air Fryer Recipes</h2>  
<p>Treacle works wonderfully in air fryer desserts like gingerbread or flapjacks. Its deep flavour pairs well with spices, and its sticky texture helps bind ingredients. When using 1.66 cups (13.28 ounces) in a recipe, ensure even distribution for consistent cooking.</p>
