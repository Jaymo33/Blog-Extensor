<h3>How to Measure 110 ml of Tomato Sauce Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a measuring jug, you can use a standard cup to estimate 110 ml of tomato sauce. Fill the cup halfway, leaving a small gap at the top for accuracy. Alternatively, use tablespoons—110 ml equals about 7.5 tablespoons. This method works well for smaller quantities in air fryer cooking.</p>
