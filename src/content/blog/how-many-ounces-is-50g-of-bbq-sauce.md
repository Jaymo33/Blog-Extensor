<h2>Why Convert 50g of BBQ Sauce to Ounces?</h2>  
<p>Many UK recipes, especially those for air fryers, use grams for measurements. However, some kitchen scales or international recipes might list ingredients in ounces. Converting 50g of BBQ sauce to ounces (1.76 oz) helps avoid confusion and ensures your dish turns out perfectly. <h2>How to Measure 50g of BBQ Sauce</h2>  
<p>Using a digital kitchen scale is the easiest way to measure 50g of BBQ sauce. Simply place your container on the scale, tare it to zero, and pour the sauce until it hits 50g. If you don’t have a scale, 50g is roughly three tablespoons, but this can vary slightly depending on the sauce’s thickness.</p>  

<h3>Adjusting Recipes for Your Air Fryer</h3>  
<p>When converting recipes, remember that air fryers cook faster than conventional ovens. If a recipe calls for 50g of BBQ sauce, you might need to adjust cooking times. For example, air fryer chicken wings coated in 50g of sauce will crisp up quicker than oven-baked ones. <p>If you have leftover BBQ sauce after measuring 50g, store it in an airtight container in the fridge. It’ll stay fresh for up to two weeks, ready for your next air fryer adventure.</p>
