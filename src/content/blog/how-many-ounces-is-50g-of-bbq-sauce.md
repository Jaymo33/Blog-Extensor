<h2>How to Measure 50g of BBQ Sauce</h2>  
<p>Using a digital kitchen scale is the easiest way to measure 50g of BBQ sauce. Simply place your container on the scale, tare it to zero, and pour the sauce until it hits 50g. If you don’t have a scale, 50g is roughly three tablespoons, but this can vary slightly depending on the sauce’s thickness.</p>
