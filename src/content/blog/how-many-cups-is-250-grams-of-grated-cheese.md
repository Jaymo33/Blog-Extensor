<p>When converting grams to cups, the density and texture of the ingredient play a significant role. Grated cheese, for instance, can vary in volume based on how tightly it's packed or how finely it's shredded. A loosely packed cup of grated cheese will weigh less than a tightly packed one, which is why weight measurements like grams are often more accurate. For air fryer recipes, where even cooking is key, using the correct amount of cheese ensures your dishes aren't overly greasy or dry.</p>

<h3>UK vs. US Cup Measurements</h3>
<p>It's worth noting that UK and US cup measurements differ slightly. A UK cup is 250ml, while a US cup is 240ml. This small difference can add up when measuring ingredients like grated cheese. For 250 grams of grated cheese, the conversion is roughly 2.5 cups in both systems, but if precision is crucial, it's best to stick to one system throughout your recipe.
