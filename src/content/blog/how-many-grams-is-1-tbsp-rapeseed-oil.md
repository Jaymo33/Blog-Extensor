<p>Air fryers rely on hot air circulation to cook food, so using the right amount of oil is crucial. Too much oil can make dishes greasy, while too little may leave them dry. <h3>How to measure rapeseed oil without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don't have digital scales, you can still measure 1 tablespoon of rapeseed oil accurately. Use a standard UK tablespoon (15ml capacity) and fill it to the brim without overflowing. For sticky ingredients, lightly grease the spoon first. Remember that 1 tbsp rapeseed oil in grams is roughly 14g, whether measured by volume or weight. This method works perfectly for <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/chicken>chicken recipes</a> where oil helps achieve golden skin.</p>
