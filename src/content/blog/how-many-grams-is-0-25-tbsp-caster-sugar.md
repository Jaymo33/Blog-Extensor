<h3>How to measure 0.25 tbsp of caster sugar without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a digital scale, you can still measure 0.25 tbsp of caster sugar accurately. Use a standard UK tablespoon and fill it to just under a quarter of its capacity. Alternatively, use a teaspoon—0.25 tbsp is equivalent to 0.75 tsp. For best results, level off the sugar with a straight edge to ensure precision. This method works well for small batches of air fryer desserts, like mini muffins or shortbread.</p>

<h3>Tips for storing and handling caster sugar</h3>  
<p>Keep caster sugar in an airtight container to prevent clumping, especially in humid kitchens. If your sugar has hardened, a quick blitz in a blender can restore its fine texture.
