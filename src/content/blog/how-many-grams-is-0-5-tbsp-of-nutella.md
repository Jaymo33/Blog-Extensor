<h2>How to Convert 0.5 Tbsp of Nutella to Grams</h2>  
<p>Converting tablespoons to grams is simple with Nutella. Since 1 tbsp of Nutella weighs around 18 grams, halving that gives you 9 grams for 0.5 tbsp. This conversion is consistent across UK and international measurements, so you can trust it for any recipe.</p>  
<p>If you’re working with larger quantities, remember that 1 tablespoon equals 18 grams, and 1 teaspoon is roughly 6 grams. <h3>Tips for Measuring Nutella Accurately</h3>  
<p>To measure 0.5 tbsp of Nutella precisely, use a digital kitchen scale for the best results. Scoop the Nutella into a spoon, then level it off with a knife before weighing. This method avoids excess and ensures you’re using exactly 9 grams.</p>

<h3>Storing Nutella for Freshness</h3>  
<p>Keep your Nutella fresh by storing it in a cool, dry place. Avoid refrigeration, as it hardens the spread and makes measuring tricky. If separation occurs, a quick stir restores its smooth texture, ready for your next air fryer creation.</p>
