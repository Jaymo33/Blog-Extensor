<h3>UK vs. US Cup Measurements for 175 Grams of Milk</h3>  
<p>In the UK, 1 cup equals 250ml, so 175 grams of milk is approximately 0.74 cups. In the US, where 1 cup is 240ml, it’s about 0.73 cups. This small difference won’t drastically affect most air fryer recipes, but it’s good to know for precision baking.</p>  

<h2>How to Measure 175 Grams of Milk Without Scales</h2>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can use a measuring jug to approximate 175 grams of milk. Since milk’s density is close to water, 175ml is nearly equivalent to 175 grams. For best results, use a clear liquid measuring cup and check at eye level. Avoid using dry measuring cups, as they can lead to inaccuracies.</p>

<h3>Tips for Measuring Milk in Air Fryer Recipes</h3>  
<p>Always stir milk before measuring, as it can separate. Pour slowly to avoid bubbles, which can distort the measurement. For sticky ingredients like condensed milk, lightly grease the measuring cup first.
