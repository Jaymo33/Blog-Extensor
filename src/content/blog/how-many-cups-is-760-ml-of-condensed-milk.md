<h3>How to Measure 760 ml of Condensed Milk</h3>  
<p>Measuring condensed milk can be tricky due to its thick consistency. For 760 ml, use a liquid measuring cup and fill it to the 3-cup mark, then add an additional 1/5 cup (or roughly 2 tablespoons) to reach 3.2 cups. Alternatively, weigh it—760 ml of condensed milk weighs about 800 grams.</p>

<h3>Converting Other Condensed Milk Measurements</h3>  
<p>If your recipe calls for a different amount, here’s a quick reference: 1 cup = 237 ml, so 380 ml is roughly 1.6 cups, and 950 ml is about 4 cups. Keep these conversions handy for seamless baking and air frying.</p>
