<h2>How to Measure 250g of Tomato Paste</h2>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can still measure 250g of tomato paste using volume. One tablespoon of tomato paste weighs roughly 15g, so 250g would be about 16-17 tablespoons. However, weighing is far more accurate, especially for air fryer recipes where consistency is key.</p>  

<h3>Using a Kitchen Scale</h3>  
<p>Place a bowl on your kitchen scale, tare it to zero, then spoon in the tomato paste until you reach 250g. This method eliminates guesswork and ensures your dish turns out perfectly.
