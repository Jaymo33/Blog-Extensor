<h2>Why Convert Grams to Ounces for Breadcrumbs?</h2>  
<p>Many UK recipes use grams, but some cooks prefer ounces for measuring dry ingredients like breadcrumbs. Converting 200g of breadcrumbs to ounces (7.05 oz) ensures consistency, especially in air fryer cooking where even distribution matters. Breadcrumbs help create that golden, crispy texture we all love. <h3>How to Measure 200g of Breadcrumbs Accurately</h3>  
<p>For precise measurements, use a digital kitchen scale set to grams. If your scale only shows ounces, aim for 7.05 oz when measuring 200g of breadcrumbs. Lightly packed breadcrumbs work best for even coating. Too tightly packed, and you might end up with clumpy results.</p>  

<h2>Best Uses for 200g of Breadcrumbs in Your Air Fryer</h2>  
<p>200g of breadcrumbs (7.05 oz) is perfect for coating 4-6 chicken breasts or a batch of crispy fish fillets. It’s also ideal for making air-fried mozzarella sticks or vegetable fritters. The key is to spread the breadcrumbs evenly for uniform crispiness. Discover more tips in our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/chicken>chicken recipe category</a>.</p>  

<p>For gluten-free options, you can substitute regular breadcrumbs with almond flour or crushed cornflakes. The same 200g (7.05 oz) measurement applies, ensuring your coatings stay light and crispy. Always preheat your air fryer for the best results.</p>  

<h3>Storing Leftover Breadcrumbs</h3>  
<p>If you don’t use all 200g of breadcrumbs, store them in an airtight container to keep them fresh. They’ll last up to a month in the cupboard or longer in the freezer. For more storage tips, visit <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk>airfryerrecipe.co.uk</a>.</p>
