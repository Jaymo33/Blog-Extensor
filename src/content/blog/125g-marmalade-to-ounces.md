<p>Measuring sticky ingredients like marmalade can be messy business. For the most accurate 125g to ounces conversion, we recommend using digital kitchen scales. <h3>The science behind the 125g marmalade to ounces conversion</h3>
<p>The conversion from grams to ounces follows a standard ratio where 1 ounce equals 28.35 grams. Therefore, to convert 125g of marmalade to ounces, you divide 125 by 28.35, giving you approximately 4.41 ounces. This conversion remains consistent whether you're measuring marmalade, jam or other preserves for your air fryer recipes.</p>
