<h3>How to Measure 1.5 Cups of Milk Correctly</h3>
<p>To measure 1.5 cups of milk accurately, use a liquid measuring cup with clear markings. Fill it to the 1.5-cup line, ensuring the milk sits level for an exact 12-ounce measurement.
