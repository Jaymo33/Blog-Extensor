<h3>How to measure tahini without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a scale, you can use tablespoons to measure tahini. One tablespoon of tahini weighs roughly 15g, so 50g would be just over three tablespoons. However, tahini’s thick consistency means it’s best to stir it well before measuring for accuracy. For sticky ingredients like tahini, a silicone spatula helps scrape every bit from the spoon. This method isn’t as precise as weighing but works in a pinch for most <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/vegetables>vegetable-based recipes</a>.</p>
