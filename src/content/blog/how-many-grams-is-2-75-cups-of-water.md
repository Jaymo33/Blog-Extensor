<h2>How to Convert Cups to Grams for Water</h2>  
<p>Water has a standard conversion rate, making it easy to switch between cups and grams. One cup of water weighs roughly 236.59 grams, so 2.75 cups of water in grams is calculated by multiplying 236.59 by 2.75, resulting in approximately 650 grams. This conversion is consistent whether you’re in the UK or the USA, though always double-check your cup measurements for accuracy.</p>
