<h3>How to measure brown sugar without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a scale, you can still measure 0.5 tbsp of brown sugar accurately. Use a standard UK tablespoon and fill it halfway, ensuring the sugar is loosely packed. Remember that 1 UK tablespoon holds about 12.5 grams of brown sugar, so halving it gives you roughly 6.25 grams. For sticky sugars, lightly grease the spoon to help it slide off easily. This method works well for quick recipes like <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/dessert>air fryer desserts</a> where small adjustments matter.</p>
