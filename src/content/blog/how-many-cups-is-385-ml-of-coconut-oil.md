<h2>How to Measure 385 ml of Coconut Oil</h2>
<p>Measuring 385 ml of coconut oil accurately is easier with the right tools. A liquid measuring cup with millilitre markings is ideal, but if you only have cups, 1.63 cups will do the trick. Remember, coconut oil can be solid or liquid depending on temperature, so adjust your method accordingly.</p>

<p>For solid coconut oil, packing it into a measuring cup works best. If it's melted, pour it slowly to avoid spills. Either way, 385 ml of coconut oil equals roughly 1.63 cups.
