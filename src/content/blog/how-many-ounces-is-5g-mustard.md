<h3>How to Measure 5g of Mustard Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a scale, 5g of mustard is roughly half a tablespoon. However, this can vary depending on the mustard’s consistency—smooth mustard will weigh slightly less than grainy varieties. <h3>Does Mustard Type Affect the Weight?</h3>  
<p>Yes, thicker mustards like wholegrain may weigh slightly more per gram than smoother varieties. However, 5g of any mustard will still be close to 0.18 ounces.
