<h2>Understanding the 0.125 cup to ounces conversion</h2>
<p>When converting 0.125 cup of pesto to ounces, it's helpful to know that 1 cup of pesto typically weighs about 8 ounces. Therefore, 0.125 cup (which is 1/8 of a cup) equals exactly 1 ounce. This conversion is particularly useful for air fryer recipes where you might need smaller, precise amounts of ingredients. Many of our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>air fryer recipes</a> call for pesto in small quantities to enhance flavour without overwhelming the dish.</p>

<p>Pesto's density can vary slightly depending on ingredients like cheese and nuts, but 0.125 cup to oz remains fairly consistent.
