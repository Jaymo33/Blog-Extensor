<h3>How to measure almond butter without a scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a scale, levelling off tablespoons is the next best method. Scoop almond butter into a measuring spoon and scrape off the excess with a flat edge for accuracy. Remember, 5 tbsp of almond butter to grams is roughly 75g, but slight variations can occur depending on brand density. For sticky nut butters, lightly greasing the spoon helps it slide off easily.</p>
