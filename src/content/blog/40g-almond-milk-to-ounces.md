<h2>How to Measure 40g of Almond Milk Accurately</h2>  
<p>Using a digital kitchen scale is the easiest way to measure 40g of almond milk. Simply place your measuring cup on the scale, tare it to zero, and pour until you hit 40g. If your scale displays ounces, it should read around 1.41 oz. No scale? A tablespoon of almond milk weighs roughly 15g, so 2.5 tablespoons will get you close to 40g.</p>
