<h2>Understanding the Conversion: 510 ml to Cups</h2>  
<p>Converting millilitres to cups depends on which measurement system you're using. In the US, 1 cup equals 240 ml, meaning 510 ml of olive oil is about 2.15 cups. In the UK, where 1 cup is 284 ml, 510 ml works out to roughly 1.8 cups. This difference is crucial when following recipes, especially for air fryer dishes where oil quantities affect texture and crispiness.
