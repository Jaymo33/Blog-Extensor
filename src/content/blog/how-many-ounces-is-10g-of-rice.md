Converting 10g of rice to ounces (0.35 oz) helps you follow recipes precisely, whether you’re making a side dish or a main course. <h3>How to measure 10g of rice without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can estimate 10g of rice using a tablespoon. One level tablespoon of uncooked rice is roughly 10g or 0.35 ounces. This method isn’t as precise as weighing, but it works in a pinch. For best results, invest in a digital scale for your air fryer cooking.
