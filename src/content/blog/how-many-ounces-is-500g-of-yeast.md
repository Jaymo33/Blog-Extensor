<h3>How to Measure 500g of Yeast Accurately</h3>  
<p>Using a digital kitchen scale is the easiest way to measure 500g of yeast. If your scale displays ounces, you’ll see it’s roughly 17.64 oz. For those without a scale, 500g of yeast is roughly equivalent to 10 standard UK tablespoons, though weighing is always more precise. When working with air fryer recipes, consistency is key—so stick to weighed measurements for best results.</p>
