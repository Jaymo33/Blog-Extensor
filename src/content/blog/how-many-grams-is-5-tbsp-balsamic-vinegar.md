<h2>Why accurate measurements matter in air fryer cooking</h2>
<p>Air fryers rely on even cooking, and incorrect ingredient quantities can lead to uneven results. Too much balsamic vinegar might make your food soggy, while too little could leave it bland. Grams provide a more accurate measurement than tablespoons, especially for liquids like balsamic vinegar. This is why many professional recipes, including those on <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>airfryerrecipe.co.uk</a>, use weight measurements. Consistency is key to perfect air fryer meals every time.</p>
