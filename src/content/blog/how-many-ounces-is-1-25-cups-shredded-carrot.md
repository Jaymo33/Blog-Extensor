<h2>Why accurate measurements matter</h2>  
<p>Getting the right amount of shredded carrot ensures your air fryer recipes cook evenly. Too much or too little can affect texture and cooking time. For example, roasting 1.25 cups of shredded carrot (4.5 oz) at 180°C gives a crispy, caramelised result. If you're prepping meals in advance, precise measurements save time and reduce waste. <p>Carrots shrink slightly when cooked, so starting with the correct weight is crucial. A kitchen scale is handy, but if you don’t have one, measuring cups work too. Just remember that 1.25 cups of shredded carrot is roughly 4.5 ounces. This conversion is especially useful for <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/vegetables>vegetable-based dishes</a> in your air fryer.</p>  

<h3>How to measure shredded carrot</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate results, lightly pack the shredded carrot into your measuring cup. Don’t press too hard, or you’ll end up with more than 4.5 ounces. If you’re using a scale, tare it first, then add the carrot until it hits 4.5 oz. This method works for all <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-conversion-chart>air fryer conversions</a>.</p>
