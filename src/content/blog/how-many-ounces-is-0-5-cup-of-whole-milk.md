<h3>How to Measure 0.5 Cup of Whole Milk Correctly</h3>  
<p>To measure 0.5 cup of whole milk accurately, use a liquid measuring cup with clear markings. Pour the milk to the 0.5 cup line, ensuring it sits level for an exact measurement. If you don’t have a measuring cup, a kitchen scale can help—simply weigh out 4.23 ounces of whole milk. This method is particularly useful for air fryer recipes where precision matters, like when making <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/dessert>creamy desserts</a>.</p>
