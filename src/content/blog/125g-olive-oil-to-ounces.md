<h3>How to measure 125g of olive oil without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a scale, use a liquid measuring cup to convert 125g of olive oil to ounces. One fluid ounce of olive oil weighs roughly 28.35g, so 125g is about 4.41 fluid ounces. Pour slowly and check the markings for accuracy. This method works well for air fryer recipes like <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/vegetables>roasted vegetables</a>, where even distribution matters. Remember, olive oil’s density can vary slightly, so a scale is still the gold standard.</p>
