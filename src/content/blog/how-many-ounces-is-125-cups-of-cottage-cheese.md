<h3>How to Measure Cottage Cheese Correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate results, use a liquid measuring cup for cottage cheese. Spoon it in gently without packing it down, then level it off. If you're using a scale, tare it first to account for the container. This method ensures you get exactly 10 ounces from 1.25 cups. It’s a simple step that guarantees your <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/dessert>desserts</a> or mains turn out perfectly.</p>
