<h3>How to Measure 810 ml of Olive Oil Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a measuring jug with millilitre markings, you can still measure 810 ml of olive oil using cups. Since 1 cup equals roughly 237 ml, dividing 810 by 237 gives you about 3.42 cups. A standard measuring cup will do the trick, but for best results, use a liquid measuring cup for accuracy. This method works well for both UK and US cup measurements, though slight variations exist. For more precision, consider using a kitchen scale alongside your cups.</p>
