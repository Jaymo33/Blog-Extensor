<h2>How to Measure 20g of Apple Cider Vinegar Without Scales</h2>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can still measure 20g of apple cider vinegar using volume. One tablespoon of apple cider vinegar weighs roughly 15g, so 20g is just over 1.3 tablespoons. For air fryer recipes, this small difference can be adjusted based on taste preferences. <h3>Common Uses for 20g of Apple Cider Vinegar in Air Fryer Recipes</h3>  
<p>Apple cider vinegar is versatile in air fryer cooking. From tenderising meats to adding zest to roasted veggies, 20g (0.71 oz) is often the perfect amount. Try it in a marinade for <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/vegetables>vegetables</a> or as part of a glaze for salmon. Its acidity also helps reduce excess oil, making it a healthier choice for air frying.</p>
