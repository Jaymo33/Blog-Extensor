<h3>How to Measure Cottage Cheese for Air Fryer Recipes</h3>  
<p>To measure 1.5 cups of cottage cheese accurately, use a liquid measuring cup for the creamiest consistency. For a firmer texture, gently press the cheese into the cup without compacting it. If you’re converting to ounces, a kitchen scale is your best friend. This method ensures your air fryer dishes, like cheesy stuffed peppers or protein muffins, turn out flawless.</p>
