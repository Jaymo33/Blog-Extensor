<h3>How to measure 50g of garlic paste without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have scales, you can estimate 50g of garlic paste using tablespoons. One tablespoon of garlic paste weighs around 15g, so 3 heaped tablespoons will give you roughly 50g (or 1.76 ounces). This method isn’t as precise as weighing, but it works in a pinch. For best results, invest in a small kitchen scale—it’s a game-changer for air fryer cooking.</p>

<h3>Storing leftover garlic paste</h3>  
<p>If you’ve measured out 50g of garlic paste but only need half, store the rest properly. Transfer it to an airtight container and refrigerate for up to two weeks. You can also freeze small portions in ice cube trays—each cube holds about 15g (0.53 oz), making future measurements a breeze. Proper storage ensures your garlic paste stays fresh and ready for your next air fryer adventure.</p>
