<h3>How to Measure 2.5 Cups of Olive Oil in ml</h3>  
<p>To measure 591.47 ml of olive oil, use a liquid measuring jug with clear ml markings. Pour the oil slowly to avoid spills and check at eye level for accuracy. If your jug only has cup measurements, remember that 1 cup equals 236.59 ml, so 2.5 cups will be 591.47 ml.</p>

<h2>Differences Between UK and US Cup Measurements</h2>
