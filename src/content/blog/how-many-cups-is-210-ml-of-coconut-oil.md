<h3>How to Measure 210 ml of Coconut Oil Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, use a liquid measuring cup for melted coconut oil. Pour slowly until the oil reaches the 210 ml mark, which is just shy of a full cup. For solid coconut oil, pack it into a dry measuring cup and level it off. Remember, 210 ml equals roughly 0.89 cups, so aim for just under the 1-cup line.
