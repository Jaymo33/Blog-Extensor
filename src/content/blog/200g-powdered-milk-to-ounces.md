<h3>How to Measure 200g of Powdered Milk Accurately</h3>  
<p>To measure 200g of powdered milk, use a digital kitchen scale for the most accurate results. If your scale only shows ounces, set it to metric mode if possible, or manually convert 200g to 7.05 oz. Scoop the powdered milk gently into your measuring cup or spoon, levelling it off for consistency. Avoid packing it down, as this can lead to over-measuring.</p>
