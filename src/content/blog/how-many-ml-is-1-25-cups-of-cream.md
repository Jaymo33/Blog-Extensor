<h3>UK vs. US Cup Measurements for Cream</h3>  
<p>In the UK, 1 cup of cream is typically 237 ml, making 1.25 cups roughly 296 ml. In the US, 1 cup equals 236.6 ml, so 1.25 cups is about 295.7 ml. While the difference is minimal, it’s worth noting for recipes requiring exact measurements. For most home cooking, rounding to 296 ml works perfectly.</p>  

<h2>How to Measure 1.25 Cups of Cream Without a Measuring Cup</h2>  
<p>If you don’t have a measuring cup, you can use a kitchen scale to weigh the cream. Heavy cream weighs roughly 240 grams per cup, so 1.25 cups would be about 300 grams. Alternatively, use a liquid measuring jug with ml markings for accuracy. This method is handy for air fryer recipes where precise liquid ratios matter.</p>
