<h2>How to Measure 660 ml of Balsamic Vinegar</h2>

To measure 660 ml, you’d need just under 2 and 3/4 cups. For UK cooks, a metric cup holds 250 ml, so 660 ml would be roughly 2.64 cups.</p>
