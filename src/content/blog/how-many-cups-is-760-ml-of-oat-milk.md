<h2>Understanding the Conversion: 760 ml of Oat Milk to Cups</h2>  
<p>Converting 760 ml of oat milk to cups is straightforward once you know the standard measurements. In the UK, 1 cup equals 250 ml, making 760 ml roughly 3.04 cups. In the US, 1 cup is 240 ml, so 760 ml is about 3.17 cups. For most recipes, rounding to 3.2 cups works perfectly. This conversion is especially useful for air fryer recipes, where precision matters.</p>

<h3>UK vs. US Cup Measurements for Oat Milk</h3>

For UK bakers, 760 ml is 3.04 cups, while US bakers will measure it as 3.17 cups. Most recipes account for this slight variance, but if precision is crucial, stick to one system.</p>
