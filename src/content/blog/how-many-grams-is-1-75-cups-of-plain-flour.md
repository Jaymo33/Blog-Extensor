<h3>How to Measure 1.75 Cups of Plain Flour Correctly</h3>  
<p>To measure 1.75 cups of plain flour accurately, fluff the flour with a spoon before lightly scooping it into a measuring cup. Level it off with a knife for consistency. For even better precision, use a kitchen scale—219 grams is the weight you’re aiming for. This method is especially useful for air fryer baking, where small deviations can affect texture.</p>
