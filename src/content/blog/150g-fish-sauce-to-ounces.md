<h3>How to Measure 150g of Fish Sauce Accurately</h3>  
<p>Using a digital kitchen scale is the easiest way to measure 150g of fish sauce. If you don’t have one, a liquid measuring jug marked in millilitres can help—150g of fish sauce is roughly 150ml, as its density is close to water. For sticky ingredients like fish sauce, lightly greasing the measuring spoon or cup ensures easy pouring and cleanup. Need more tips? <h3>Storing Leftover Fish Sauce After Measuring</h3>  
<p>Once you’ve measured 150g of fish sauce, store the remainder in a cool, dark place to preserve its flavour. The bottle’s original packaging is ideal, as it’s designed to prevent oxidation. Fish sauce lasts for years unopened and up to a year after opening, making it a pantry staple for air fryer enthusiasts.</p>
