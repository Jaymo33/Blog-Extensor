<h2>How to Convert 75g of Almond Butter to Ounces</h2>  
<p>The conversion from grams to ounces is straightforward: 1 gram equals roughly 0.035 ounces. For 75g of almond butter, multiply 75 by 0.035 to get 2.65 ounces. This method works for any nut butter, making it a handy kitchen hack.</p>  

<h3>Using a Kitchen Scale for Precision</h3>  
<p>A digital kitchen scale is the best tool for measuring almond butter. Simply tare your scale with the container, then spoon in the almond butter until you hit 75g. If you don’t have a scale, use tablespoons—about 5 tablespoons equal 75g (or 2.65 ounces).</p>
