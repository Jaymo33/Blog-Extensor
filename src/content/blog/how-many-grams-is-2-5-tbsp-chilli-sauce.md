<h3>How to measure chilli sauce correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate results, use a digital kitchen scale. Spoon the sauce into a measuring tablespoon, level it off, and then weigh it. If you don’t have scales, remember that 2.5 tbsp of chilli sauce is roughly 37.5g.
