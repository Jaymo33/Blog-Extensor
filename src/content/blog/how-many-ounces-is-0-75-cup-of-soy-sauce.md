<p>Using the right amount of soy sauce—or any liquid—can significantly impact your air fryer results. Too little, and your dish might lack depth; too much, and it could become overly salty or soggy. Air fryers circulate hot air, so liquids evaporate faster than in traditional cooking. That’s why converting 0.75 cup of soy sauce to ounces (6 fl oz) helps maintain consistency.
