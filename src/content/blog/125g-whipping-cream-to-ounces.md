<h3>How to Measure 125g of Whipping Cream Without Scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can still measure 125g of whipping cream using volume conversions. One tablespoon of whipping cream weighs roughly 15g, so 8 tablespoons will give you close to 125g (or 4.41 oz). This method isn’t as precise as weighing, but it works in a pinch.
