<p>Gas Mark 9 represents one of the highest temperature settings on British ovens, equivalent to 247 C or 477 F. <p>The Gas Mark system was developed specifically for UK gas ovens, but modern electric ovens and air fryers often include both Celsius and Gas Mark references. When converting 247 degrees C to Gas Mark, it's worth noting this falls at the upper end of most domestic oven scales. For those using fan-assisted ovens, you'll typically reduce the temperature by 20 C while keeping the same Gas Mark.</p>
