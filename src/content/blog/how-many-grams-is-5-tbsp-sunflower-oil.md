<h3>How to measure sunflower oil accurately</h3>  
<p>To measure 5 tbsp of sunflower oil in grams, use a digital kitchen scale for precision. Place your measuring jug or bowl on the scale, tare it to zero, then pour the oil until it reaches 68g. If you don’t have a scale, remember that 1 tbsp of sunflower oil weighs roughly 13.6g, so 5 tbsp equals 68g. For best results, always use level tablespoons—no heaping! This method ensures your air fryer dishes turn out perfectly every time.</p>
