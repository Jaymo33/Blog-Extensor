<h3>How to measure bbq sauce for air fryer recipes</h3>
<p>To measure 1.33 cups of bbq sauce in ounces, use a liquid measuring cup or a kitchen scale. A scale is more precise, as bbq sauce’s thickness can make volume measurements inconsistent. If you don’t have a scale, spoon the sauce into a measuring cup and level it off. For sticky sauces, lightly greasing the cup can help it slide out easily.</p>
