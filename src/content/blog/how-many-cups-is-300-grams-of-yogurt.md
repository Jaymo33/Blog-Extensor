<h2>Converting 300 Grams of Yogurt to Cups</h2>
<p>The exact cup measurement for 300 grams of yogurt depends on its type and density. For standard yogurt, 300g equals approximately 1.25 cups. Greek yogurt might be closer to 1 cup due to its thickness. When preparing <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>air fryer recipes</a>, always spoon yogurt into measuring cups rather than scooping to avoid compacting it.</p>
<p>For best results when converting 300 grams of yogurt to cups, use kitchen scales for accuracy. If you don't have scales, remember that 1 UK cup of yogurt weighs about 250 grams, making 300 grams roughly 1.2 cups. <h3>Tips for Measuring Yogurt in Air Fryer Recipes</h3>
<p>When using 300 grams of yogurt in air fryer cooking, lightly grease your measuring cup first - the yogurt will slide out easily. For sticky Greek yogurt, use the displacement method: fill a measuring cup with water to 1 cup, add yogurt until the water reaches 1.25 cups, then drain the water. This gives you perfect 300g measurements every time.</p>
