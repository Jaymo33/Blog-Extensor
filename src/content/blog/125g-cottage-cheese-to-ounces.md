<h3>How to Measure 125g of Cottage Cheese</h3>  
<p>Using a digital scale is the easiest way to measure 125g of cottage cheese. If you don’t have one, a standard UK tablespoon holds about 15g, so roughly 8 tablespoons will get you close. However, for baking or crispy air fryer dishes, weighing is always best.</p>
