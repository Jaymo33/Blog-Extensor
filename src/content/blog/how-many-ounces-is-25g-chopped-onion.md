<h3>How to measure 25g of chopped onion</h3>
<p>Using a digital kitchen scale is the most accurate way to measure 25g of chopped onion. If you don’t have one, roughly ¼ cup of finely chopped onion equals 25g (0.88 oz). Keep in mind that onion density can vary based on chop size, so a scale is best for precision. This small amount is perfect for adding flavour to air-fried dishes without overwhelming them.</p>
