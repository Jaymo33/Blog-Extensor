<h2>Why converting 75g of soft cheese to ounces matters</h2>
<p>Accurate measurements are crucial in cooking, especially when using an air fryer. Soft cheese, like cream cheese or ricotta, is often measured in grams in the UK, but many recipes use ounces. <h2>How to measure 75g of soft cheese accurately</h2>
<p>Using a digital kitchen scale is the easiest way to measure 75g of soft cheese. Simply place your bowl or container on the scale, tare it to zero, and add the cheese until it reads 75g. If you don’t have a scale, 75g of soft cheese is roughly 1/3 cup, but a scale is more precise for air fryer recipes.</p>
