<h2>How to Measure 560 ml of Buttermilk</h2>
<p>If you don’t have a measuring jug with millilitre markings, don’t worry. You can use a standard cup measure to get 560 ml of buttermilk. Since 1 cup equals 236.59 ml, 560 ml is just over 2 and 1/3 cups. For best results, fill the cup to the 2-cup mark, then add another 1/3 cup.</p>
<p>For those who prefer weighing ingredients, 560 ml of buttermilk weighs approximately 580 grams. This method is handy if you’re scaling recipes up or down, especially for <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>air fryer recipes</a> that require precision.</p>
