<h3>How to Measure Chilli Sauce Correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate conversion of 2.5 cups of chilli sauce to ounces, use a kitchen scale. Liquid measuring cups can vary slightly, but a scale removes guesswork. If you don’t have one, remember that 1 cup of chilli sauce typically weighs 8 ounces, so 2.5 cups would be 20 ounces. This method works for most thick sauces used in air fryer cooking.</p>

<p>If you’ve measured out 2.5 cups (20 ounces) but don’t use it all, proper storage keeps it fresh. Transfer leftovers to an airtight container and refrigerate for up to two weeks.
