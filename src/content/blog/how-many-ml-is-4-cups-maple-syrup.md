<p>Maple syrup is a staple in many kitchens, especially for those who love sweet and sticky dishes. Converting 4.0 cups of maple syrup to millilitres ensures you get the right consistency in recipes. If you're working with UK measurements, note that a UK cup is slightly larger at 284 ml, but most recipes default to US cups unless specified.
