<h2>How to measure soy sauce for air fryer cooking</h2>  
<p>When converting 2.5 cups of soy sauce to ounces, it’s important to note whether you’re using fluid ounces (volume) or weight ounces. In this case, 2.5 cups equal 20 fluid ounces, which is the standard measurement for liquids like soy sauce. If you’re working with a recipe that calls for weight, the density of soy sauce means 20 fluid ounces weigh roughly the same.</p>
