<h3>How to Measure 235 ml of Corn Syrup Without a Measuring Cup</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a measuring cup, you can use a kitchen scale. Corn syrup weighs roughly 312 grams per cup, so 235 ml would be close to 308 grams. <h3>Common Mistakes When Measuring Corn Syrup</h3>  
<p>One mistake is not accounting for stickiness—corn syrup clings to measuring tools, leading to inaccurate pours. To avoid this, lightly grease your measuring cup or spoon with oil. Another error is using liquid and dry cups interchangeably; always use liquid measuring cups for syrups.</p>
