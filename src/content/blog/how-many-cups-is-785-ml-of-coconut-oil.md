<h3>How to Measure 785 ml of Coconut Oil Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, use a liquid measuring cup to measure 785 ml of coconut oil. Since 1 cup equals 237 ml, you’ll need roughly 3 cups plus 3 tablespoons for accuracy.
