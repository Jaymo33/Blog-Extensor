<h3>How to Measure 425 Grams of Broccoli Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can estimate 425 grams of broccoli by filling a measuring cup. One cup of chopped broccoli typically weighs around 90-100 grams, so 4.5 to 5 cups should give you roughly 425 grams. Keep in mind that tightly packed broccoli will weigh more, so fluff it lightly for accuracy. This method works well for most air fryer recipes, ensuring you get the right portion every time.</p>
