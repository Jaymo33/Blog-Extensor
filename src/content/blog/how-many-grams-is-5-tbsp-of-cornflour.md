<h3>How to Measure 5 Tbsp of Cornflour Without Scales</h3>  
<p>No scales? No problem. Use the spoon-and-level method: lightly spoon cornflour into a tablespoon and level it off with a knife. Five level tablespoons should give you roughly 40 grams. For best results, avoid packing the cornflour down, as this can lead to over-measuring. If you’re working with larger quantities, consider investing in a digital scale for precision.</p>
