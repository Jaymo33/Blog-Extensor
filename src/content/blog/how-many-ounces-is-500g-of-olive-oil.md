<h2>How to Measure 500g of Olive Oil Accurately</h2>  
<p>Using a digital kitchen scale is the easiest way to measure 500g of olive oil. Simply place your container on the scale, tare it to zero, and pour the oil until it reaches 500g. If your scale displays ounces, it should show around 17.64 oz. For those without scales, 500g of olive oil is roughly 2.1 cups, but weight measurements are always more reliable.</p>  
<p>Olive oil’s density can vary slightly depending on temperature, so weighing it ensures consistency.
