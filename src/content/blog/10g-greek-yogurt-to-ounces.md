<h2>How to Measure 10g of Greek Yogurt Accurately</h2>
<p>For the most precise measurement, use digital kitchen scales set to grams. If your scales only display ounces, simply multiply the grams by 0.035 to get the equivalent in ounces. This method works perfectly for 10g of Greek yogurt or any other ingredient.</p>
