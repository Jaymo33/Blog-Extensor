<h2>How to Measure 250g of Milk Accurately</h2>
<p>To measure 250g of milk, you can use a kitchen scale for the most accurate results. Simply pour the milk into a measuring jug on the scale until it hits 250g. If you don't have a scale, 250g of milk is roughly 250ml, as milk's density is close to water.</p>
