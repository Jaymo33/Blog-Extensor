<h3>How to Measure Sunflower Oil for Air Fryer Recipes</h3>  
<p>When converting 1.33 cups of sunflower oil to ounces, use a kitchen scale for the most accurate results. If you don’t have one, remember that 1 cup of sunflower oil weighs about 8 ounces, so 1.33 cups is roughly 10.64 ounces. This method works for all types of oils, making it a handy rule of thumb.</p>
