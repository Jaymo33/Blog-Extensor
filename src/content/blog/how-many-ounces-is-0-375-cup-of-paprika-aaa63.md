<h3>How to measure 0.375 cup of paprika</h3>  
<p>Measuring 0.375 cup of paprika is simple with the right tools. Use a dry measuring cup and level off the top with a straight edge for accuracy. If you don’t have a 0.375 cup measure, you can use 6 tablespoons (since 1 cup = 16 tablespoons). For those who prefer weight, a kitchen scale set to ounces will give you the exact 1.5 ounces needed.</p>
