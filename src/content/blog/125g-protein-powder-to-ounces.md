<h3>How to measure 125g of protein powder without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can use measuring cups to approximate 125g of protein powder. One level cup of most protein powders weighs around 90–100g, so 125g would be roughly 1¼ cups.
