<h2>How to Measure 75g of Milk Accurately</h2>
<p>Using a digital kitchen scale is the easiest way to measure 75g of milk. Simply pour the milk into a measuring jug on the scale until it hits 75g. If you don’t have a scale, 75g is roughly 75ml, as milk’s density is close to water.
