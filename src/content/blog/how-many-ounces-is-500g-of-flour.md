<h2>How to Convert 500g Flour to Ounces</h2>  
<p>To convert 500g of flour to ounces, multiply the grams by 0.035274. This gives you roughly 17.64 ounces. For air fryer baking, rounding to 17.5 or 18 ounces is usually fine, but precision matters for delicate recipes like soufflés or sponges.</p>
