<h2>Understanding Cup Measurements</h2>  
<p>Cup measurements vary between the UK and the US, which can affect your recipes. In the US, 1 cup equals 237 ml, making 2.0 cups 473 ml. In the UK, 1 cup is 284 ml, so 2.0 cups equal 568 ml. This difference is essential when following recipes from different regions. Always check whether your recipe uses UK or US measurements to avoid mishaps.</p>
