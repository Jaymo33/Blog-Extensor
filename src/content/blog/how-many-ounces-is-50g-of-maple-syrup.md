<h2>How to measure 50g of maple syrup without a scale</h2>  
<p>No scale? No problem. Roughly 2 tablespoons of maple syrup weigh close to 50g (1.76 oz), though density can vary slightly. For sticky ingredients like syrup, lightly greasing your measuring spoon helps it slide off easily. This hack is handy when whipping up quick air-fryer treats like cinnamon rolls or glazed carrots.
