<h3>Converting grams to ounces for mustard</h3>  
<p>The conversion from grams to ounces is straightforward: 1 gram equals 0.035 ounces. For 200g of mustard, simply multiply 200 by 0.035 to get 7.05 ounces. This calculation works for any ingredient, but mustard’s density means it’s a reliable benchmark. If you’re scaling a recipe, this conversion ensures accuracy.
