<h3>How to measure 0.25 cup of molasses accurately</h3>
<p>Measuring sticky ingredients like molasses can be tricky. To get exactly 0.25 cup (or 3 ounces), lightly grease your measuring cup with oil first. This helps the molasses slide out easily, giving you a more accurate measurement for your air fryer recipes.</p>
