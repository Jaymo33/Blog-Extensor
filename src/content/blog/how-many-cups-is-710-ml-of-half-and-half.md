<h2>Understanding Half and Half Measurements</h2>  
<p>Half and half is a dairy product made by combining equal parts whole milk and cream. It's commonly used in recipes to add richness without being as heavy as pure cream. When converting 710 ml of half and half to cups, it's essential to know that 1 cup equals roughly 237 ml. This means 710 ml translates to just under 3 cups, making it easy to measure for your air fryer recipes.</p>
