<h2>How to measure 250g of gelatin powder accurately</h2>
<p>To measure 250g of gelatin powder, use a digital kitchen scale for precision. If your scale only displays ounces, you’ll now know that 250g translates to roughly 8.82 oz. Scoop the powder lightly into your measuring container to avoid compacting it, which could lead to over-measuring. For air fryer recipes, accuracy is key—especially with ingredients like gelatin, which set the texture of your dish.</p>
<p>If you don’t have a scale, you can use volume measurements, though they’re less precise. One tablespoon of gelatin powder weighs about 9g, so 250g would be roughly 28 tablespoons. However, for best results in air fryer cooking, we recommend sticking to weight measurements.
