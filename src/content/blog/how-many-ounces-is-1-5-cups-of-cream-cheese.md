<h3>How to measure cream cheese correctly</h3>  
<p>Measuring cream cheese can be tricky due to its thick, spreadable consistency. For the most accurate results, use a kitchen scale to weigh out 12 ounces for 1.5 cups. If you don’t have a scale, spoon the cream cheese into a measuring cup and level it off with a knife. Avoid packing it down, as this can lead to over-measuring.
