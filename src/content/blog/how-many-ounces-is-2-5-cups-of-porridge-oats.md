<h3>How to Measure Porridge Oats Correctly</h3>  
<p>To measure 2.5 cups of porridge oats accurately, use a dry measuring cup and level off the top with a straight edge. Scooping directly from the bag can lead to packed cups, resulting in more oats than needed. For best results, weigh your oats using kitchen scales—10 ounces is the ideal weight for 2.5 cups.</p>

<h2>Tips for Cooking with Porridge Oats</h2>  
<p>For air fryer recipes, lightly toasting oats before use enhances their nutty flavour. Spread 2.5 cups (10 oz) evenly in the basket and air fry at 160°C for 3-4 minutes, shaking halfway. This step works wonders for granola or crumble toppings.</p>
