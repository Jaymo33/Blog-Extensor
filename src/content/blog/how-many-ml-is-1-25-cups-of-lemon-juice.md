<h3>How to Measure Lemon Juice Correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate conversion of 1.25 cups of lemon juice to ml, use a liquid measuring cup. Fill it to the 1.25-cup mark, then pour into a metric measuring jug to confirm it’s 295.74 ml. Freshly squeezed lemon juice may vary slightly in volume due to pulp, so straining ensures consistency. If you’re using bottled juice, shake well before measuring.</p>
