<h2>How to measure 0.25 tbsp of gelatin powder</h2>
<p>If you don’t have a scale, you can still measure 0.25 tbsp of gelatin powder using a standard tablespoon. Fill it lightly without packing the powder, then divide it into four equal parts. One of those parts will be roughly 0.25 tbsp, or 2.5 grams. For best results, level off the powder with a knife to avoid over-measuring.</p>
