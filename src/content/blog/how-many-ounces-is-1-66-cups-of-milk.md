<h2>Why Convert Cups to Ounces?</h2>  
<p>Converting cups to ounces is crucial for recipes that require precision, such as air fryer desserts or sauces. Cups measure volume, while ounces measure weight, and milk's density means the two aren't interchangeable. For example, 1.66 cups of milk to ounces ensures you add the right amount for a creamy texture. Many UK recipes, especially those on <a href="https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes">airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes</a>, use ounces for consistency. This avoids over- or under-measuring, which can affect taste and texture.</p>

<h3>How to Measure 1.66 Cups of Milk Accurately</h3>  
<p>To measure 1.66 cups of milk, use a liquid measuring cup with clear markings. Fill it just below the 1 and 2/3 cup line for accuracy. If you don’t have a 1.66 cup measure, use 1 cup plus 2/3 cup. For ounces, a digital scale is ideal—simply pour until it hits 13.28 oz. This method is especially useful for air fryer recipes where precision matters.</p>
