<h3>How to measure 1.25 cups of milk without a measuring cup</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a measuring cup, you can still measure 1.25 cups of milk accurately. Since 1 cup equals 8 oz, 1.25 cups is 10 oz. Use a kitchen scale to weigh the milk directly, or pour it into a liquid measuring jug marked in ounces. For smaller quantities, tablespoons can help—1.25 cups is equivalent to 20 tablespoons. This method works well for <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/breakfast>breakfast recipes</a> like French toast or scrambled eggs.</p>
