<h3>How to Measure 5g of Cream Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a scale, you can use a teaspoon to measure 5g of cream. One level teaspoon holds roughly 5g (0.176 oz) of liquid cream. This method isn’t as precise as a scale but works in a pinch. For thicker creams, like clotted or whipped, fluffing it lightly before measuring ensures accuracy.</p>
