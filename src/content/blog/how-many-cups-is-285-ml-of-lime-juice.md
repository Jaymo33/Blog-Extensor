<h3>Tips for Measuring Lime Juice Without a Scale</h3>
<p>If you don't have measuring cups handy, there are clever ways to estimate 285 ml of lime juice. A standard UK tablespoon holds 15 ml, so 19 tablespoons equal roughly 285 ml.
