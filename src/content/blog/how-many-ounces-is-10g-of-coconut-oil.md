<h3>How to measure 10g of coconut oil without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can still measure 10g of coconut oil using tablespoons. One tablespoon of coconut oil weighs roughly 14g, so 10g is just under three-quarters of a tablespoon. Melted coconut oil can also be measured by volume—10g equals about two teaspoons.
