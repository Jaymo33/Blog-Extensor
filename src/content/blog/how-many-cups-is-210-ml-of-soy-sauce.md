<h3>How to Measure 210 ml of Soy Sauce Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a measuring jug or scale, you can still measure 210 ml of soy sauce using standard cups. A US customary cup holds about 240 ml, so 210 ml is just under one full cup. For UK cooks, a metric cup is 250 ml, making 210 ml roughly 0.84 cups. Keep in mind that soy sauce’s viscosity means it pours differently than water, so always measure at eye level for accuracy.</p>
