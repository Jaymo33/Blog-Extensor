<h3>How to Measure 125g of Tomato Paste Accurately</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can use volume measurements. 125g of tomato paste is roughly 8 tablespoons or half a US cup. However, weighing it is more accurate, especially for air fryer recipes where consistency matters. A digital scale that switches between grams and ounces is a great investment for any home cook.</p>
