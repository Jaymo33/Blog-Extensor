<h3>How to Measure 75 Grams of Minced Beef Without Scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, use a measuring cup and lightly spoon the minced beef into it. Avoid packing it down, as this increases the density. Fluff it with a fork to keep it loose, then level it off. This method gives you the closest approximation to 75 grams, or about ⅓ cup.
