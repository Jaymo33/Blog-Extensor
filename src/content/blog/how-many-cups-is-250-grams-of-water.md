<h3>UK vs. US Cup Measurements for 250 Grams of Water</h3>  
<p>In the UK, 250 grams of water is roughly 1.06 cups, while in the US, it's about 1.05 cups. This slight difference stems from varying cup sizes—the UK uses 284ml per cup, and the US uses 240ml. For air fryer recipes, this minor variance usually doesn’t affect outcomes, but it’s good to know if precision is critical. Always check whether your recipe follows UK or US measurements to avoid confusion.</p>  

<h2>How to Measure 250 Grams of Water Without Scales</h2>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can still measure 250 grams of water using cups. Fill a UK measuring cup to just above the 1-cup line (or a US cup to the 1-cup mark) for an approximate 250g measurement. For better accuracy, use a liquid measuring jug with millilitre markings—250ml of water equals 250 grams, as water’s density is 1g/ml. This trick works perfectly for <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/dessert>air fryer desserts</a> or sauces where precision matters.</p>  

<p>Another method is to use tablespoons—16.67 UK tablespoons (15ml each) or 16.91 US tablespoons (14.79ml each) equal 250 grams of water.
