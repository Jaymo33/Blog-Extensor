<h2>Why accurate measurements matter in air fryer cooking</h2>  
<p>Getting the right amount of rolled barley is crucial for air fryer recipes. Too much or too little can affect texture and cooking time. <h3>How to measure 0.125 cup of rolled barley without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have scales, use the spoon-and-level method for dry ingredients. Fill your measuring cup with rolled barley, then level it off with a straight edge. For 0.125 cup (which is 2 tablespoons), measure carefully to avoid packing the barley down. This ensures you get the correct 0.5-ounce weight for your recipe.</p>
