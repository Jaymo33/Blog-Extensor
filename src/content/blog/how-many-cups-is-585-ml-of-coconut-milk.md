<h2>US vs UK Cup Measurements for Coconut Milk</h2>  
<p>The difference between US and UK cup sizes means 585 ml of coconut milk converts differently. In the US, 1 cup is 240 ml, so 585 ml equals about 2.47 cups. In the UK, 1 cup is 284 ml, making 585 ml roughly 2.06 cups. Always check which measurement system your recipe uses.</p>  
<p>If you're following a recipe from <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes</a>, it will typically specify the measurement standard. For baking, even small discrepancies can affect texture, so precision matters. When in doubt, use a kitchen scale alongside measuring cups for the best accuracy.</p>  

<h3>How to Measure 585 ml of Coconut Milk Without a Measuring Cup</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a measuring cup, you can use a standard mug or glass, though results may vary. A typical UK mug holds about 250 ml, so 585 ml would be just over 2 mugs. For US measurements, it’s closer to 2.5 mugs. However, for air fryer recipes, we recommend using proper measuring tools for consistency.</p>
