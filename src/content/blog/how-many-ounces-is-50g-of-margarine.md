<h3>How to Measure 50g of Margarine Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a scale, you can use tablespoons to measure 50g of margarine. One tablespoon of margarine weighs roughly 14g, so 3.5 tablespoons equal 50g (or about 1.76 oz).
