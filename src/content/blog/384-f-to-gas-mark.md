<h2>When to Use 384 F (Gas Mark 5) in Your Air Fryer</h2>
<p>Gas Mark 5 (384 F) is ideal for a variety of air fryer dishes. It's hot enough to crisp foods without burning them, while still allowing thorough cooking. This temperature works perfectly for roasting potatoes, baking cookies, or cooking chicken pieces. The hot air circulation in air fryers means food often cooks faster than in conventional ovens at the same temperature.</p>

<p>Baking at 384 F (Gas Mark 5) in an air fryer requires some adjustments.
