<h3>How to measure 4 tbsp cocoa powder without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have scales, lightly spoon cocoa powder into a tablespoon and level it off for accuracy. Avoid packing it down, as this can add extra grams unintentionally. Four level tablespoons should give you roughly 24 grams, but for precision, scales are best. This method works well for air fryer cookies and other treats where consistency matters.</p>
