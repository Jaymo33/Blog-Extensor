<p>Using the correct amount of cheddar cheese ensures your air fryer recipes have the right texture and flavour. <p>Different grating methods can affect the volume of cheese. Finely grated cheddar will pack more densely into a cup, while coarsely shredded cheese takes up more space. <h3>How to Measure 200 Grams of Cheddar Cheese Without Scales</h3>  
<p>If you don't have scales, use the general rule that 100 grams of grated cheddar cheese equals roughly 1 cup. This means 200 grams of cheddar cheese would be about 2 cups. Keep in mind that pre-shredded supermarket cheese often contains anti-caking agents, which can slightly alter the volume compared to freshly grated cheese. For best results in your air fryer, freshly grate your cheddar and lightly pack it into measuring cups.</p>
