<h2>How to measure 200g of sunflower oil in ounces</h2>
<p>Converting 200g of sunflower oil to ounces is straightforward. One gram is approximately 0.035 ounces, so 200g x 0.035 gives you 7.05 ounces. For most home cooking purposes, you can round this to 7 ounces without affecting the recipe. This conversion is particularly useful when following American recipes that use ounces instead of grams.</p>
<p>If you don't have a scale, you can use measuring spoons or cups. 200g of sunflower oil is roughly 14 tablespoons or just under a cup. However, for the most accurate results, especially in air frying, we recommend using a digital kitchen scale. <h3>The benefits of sunflower oil in air frying</h3>
<p>Sunflower oil is ideal for air frying because it can withstand high temperatures without breaking down. Its light flavour won't overpower your food, making it versatile for various dishes. When using 200g (7 ounces) of sunflower oil in your air fryer, you'll achieve that perfect crispy texture without excess grease.</p>
