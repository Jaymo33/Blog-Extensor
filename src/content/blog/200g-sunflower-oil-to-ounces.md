<h2>How to measure 200g of sunflower oil in ounces</h2>
<p>Converting 200g of sunflower oil to ounces is straightforward. One gram is approximately 0.035 ounces, so 200g x 0.035 gives you 7.05 ounces. For most home cooking purposes, you can round this to 7 ounces without affecting the recipe. This conversion is particularly useful when following American recipes that use ounces instead of grams.</p>
<p>If you don't have a scale, you can use measuring spoons or cups. 200g of sunflower oil is roughly 14 tablespoons or just under a cup.
