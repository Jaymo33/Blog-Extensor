<h3>How to Measure Ground Cumin Correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate conversion of 3 cups of ground cumin to ounces, use a kitchen scale. Scoop the cumin into a measuring cup without packing it down, then level it off with a knife. If you don’t have a scale, remember that 1 cup of ground cumin weighs roughly 8 ounces, so 3 cups would be 24 ounces. This method works for UK and US measurements alike.</p>
