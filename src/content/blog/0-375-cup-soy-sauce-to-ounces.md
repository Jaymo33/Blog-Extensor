<h3>How to measure 0.375 cup of soy sauce</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a 0.375 cup measure, you can use tablespoons instead. Six tablespoons equal 0.375 cup, which converts to 3 ounces of soy sauce. This method is great for smaller quantities and ensures accuracy without specialised tools. For larger batches, a liquid measuring cup with clear markings will do the trick.</p>
