<h3>The science behind whipping cream measurements</h3>
<p>Whipping cream contains about 30-36% fat content, which affects its density and measurement accuracy. The 250g whipping cream to ounces conversion remains constant, but the volume changes when aerated. This is why weight measurements are more reliable than volume for baking in your air fryer.</p>
