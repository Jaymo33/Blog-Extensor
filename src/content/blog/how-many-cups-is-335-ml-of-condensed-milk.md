<h3>How to Measure 335 ml of Condensed Milk Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a measuring jug, use a standard cup. One cup holds about 240 ml, so 335 ml is just over 1 and 1/3 cups. For sticky ingredients like condensed milk, lightly grease your measuring cup for easy pouring. Always level off the top for accuracy.</p>
