<h2>Understanding Milk Measurements</h2>
<p>Converting 585 ml of milk to cups depends on whether you're using US or UK measurements. In the US, one cup equals 240 ml, so 585 ml works out to roughly 2.47 cups. In the UK, where a cup is 284 ml, 585 ml converts to about 2.06 cups. This difference is crucial for recipes, especially when using an air fryer, where precision can affect cooking times and results.</p>

<h3>US vs. UK Cup Measurements</h3>
<p>The difference between US and UK cup sizes stems from historical measurement systems. While the US uses 240 ml per cup, the UK uses 284 ml. This means 585 ml of milk translates to slightly different amounts depending on where your recipe originates. Always check the source of your recipe to avoid mishaps.</p>
