<h2>UK vs. US Cup Measurements</h2>  
<p>Did you know that UK and US cup measurements differ slightly? In the UK, 1 cup equals 284 ml, while in the US, it's 240 ml. This means 860 ml of apple cider vinegar converts to approximately 3.63 cups in the UK and 3.64 cups in the US. While the difference is minimal, it's worth noting for precision.</p>  
<p>If you're using an air fryer, these small variations can matter, especially in baking.
