<h2>US vs UK Cup Measurements</h2>
<p>In the United States, 1 cup equals 240 ml, while in the UK, 1 cup is 284 ml. This difference might seem small, but it can significantly impact your baking results. For 585 ml of condensed milk, you'd use about 2.47 US cups or 2.06 UK cups. Most modern recipes specify which measurement system they use, but when in doubt, US measurements are more commonly used in international recipes.</p>
