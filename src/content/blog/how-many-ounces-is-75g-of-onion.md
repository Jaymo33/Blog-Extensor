<h2>How to convert 75g of onion to ounces</h2>
<p>The conversion from grams to ounces is straightforward: 1 gram equals roughly 0.035 ounces. For 75g of onion, simply multiply 75 by 0.035 to get 2.65 ounces. This small but crucial detail can help you follow recipes more accurately, especially those from <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>our recipe collection</a>.</p>
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, visual cues can help. A medium-sized onion typically weighs around 150g, so half of that would be roughly 75g. However, for the most precise results, especially in air fryer cooking, weighing your ingredients is always best.</p>
