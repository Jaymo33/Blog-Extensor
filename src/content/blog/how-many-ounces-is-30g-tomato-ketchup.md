<h2>How to Measure 30g of Tomato Ketchup Without a Scale</h2>
<p>If you don't have a kitchen scale, you can still measure 30g of tomato ketchup using tablespoons. One tablespoon of ketchup weighs roughly 15g, so two tablespoons equal 30g. This method is handy for quick recipes like <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/vegetables>air fryer vegetables</a>.</p>
