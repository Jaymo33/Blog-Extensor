<p>Measuring lemon juice by weight (25g) is more precise than using volume (ounces or millilitres), as it accounts for variations in juiciness. To measure 25g of lemon juice, use a digital kitchen scale for the best accuracy. Alternatively, 25g is roughly equivalent to 1.5 tablespoons or 0.88 ounces. Freshly squeezed lemon juice is ideal, but bottled works in a pinch—just check for additives.
