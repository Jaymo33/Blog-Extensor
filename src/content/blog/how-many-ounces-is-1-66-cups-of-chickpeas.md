<h2>Why accurate measurements matter in air fryer cooking</h2>  
<p>Precision is key when using an air fryer, as unevenly sized ingredients can cook at different rates. Chickpeas, for example, need even distribution to crisp up properly. Measuring by weight (ounces) rather than volume (cups) ensures uniformity. This is especially useful for recipes like spiced air-fried chickpeas, where consistency affects texture. Check out our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-conversion-chart>air fryer conversion chart</a> for more measurement tips.</p>  

<p>Using 1.66 cups of chickpeas in ounces (14.5 oz) helps avoid over or undercooking. Air fryers rely on hot air circulation, so overcrowding or uneven portions can lead to soggy results. For perfect crispy chickpeas, stick to the recommended weight. Explore our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/beans>bean-based recipes</a> for inspiration on using chickpeas in your air fryer.</p>  

<h3>How to measure chickpeas for air frying</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate results, use a kitchen scale to weigh your chickpeas. If you don’t have one, fluff the chickpeas in the cup before measuring to avoid compaction. Level off the top with a straight edge for consistency. Remember, 1.66 cups of chickpeas equals roughly 14.5 ounces, but slight variations can occur based on how they’re packed.</p>  

<h2>Common uses for chickpeas in air fryers</h2>  
<p>Chickpeas are incredibly versatile in air fryer cooking. From crunchy snacks to protein-packed additions, they’re a staple in many recipes. Roasting 1.66 cups (14.5 oz) of chickpeas with spices creates a delicious, healthy snack. They can also be used in salads, wraps, or even blended into hummus after air frying.</p>  

<p>For a quick meal, try tossing 1.66 cups of chickpeas with olive oil and your favourite seasonings. Air fry at 190°C for 12-15 minutes, shaking halfway. This method ensures even cooking and maximum crispiness. Find more ideas in our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>recipe collection</a>.</p>  

<h3>Tips for storing cooked chickpeas</h3>  
<p>Once air-fried, chickpeas can be stored in an airtight container for up to 3 days. To maintain crispiness, reheat them in the air fryer for a few minutes before serving. Avoid refrigeration if possible, as moisture can make them soggy. For longer storage, freeze them and reheat straight from frozen.</p>
