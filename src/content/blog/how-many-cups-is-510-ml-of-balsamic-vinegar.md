<h3>How to Measure 510 ml of Balsamic Vinegar Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a measuring jug, use standard cup measurements. Since 1 cup holds roughly 237 ml, 510 ml is just over 2 cups (2.15 cups, to be exact). For smaller quantities, tablespoons work—15 ml per tablespoon means 510 ml equals 34 tablespoons.
