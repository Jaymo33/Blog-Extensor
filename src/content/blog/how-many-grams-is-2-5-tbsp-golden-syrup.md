<p>Air fryers rely on even heat distribution, so ingredient ratios must be spot-on. <h3>How to measure golden syrup without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have scales, lightly grease your measuring spoon to help the syrup slide out cleanly. Fill it to the brim without overflowing, then level off with a straight edge. Remember, 2.5 tbsp golden syrup in grams is roughly 53g, but slight variations can occur based on temperature and brand.</p>
