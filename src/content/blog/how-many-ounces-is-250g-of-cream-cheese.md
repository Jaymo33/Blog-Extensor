<h3>How to measure 250g of cream cheese without scales</h3>  
<p>Don’t have a kitchen scale? No problem. One standard block of cream cheese in the UK is typically 200g, so 250g would be roughly 1 and 1/4 blocks. Alternatively, 8.82 ounces is just under 1 cup (since 1 cup of cream cheese is about 8 ounces). For air fryer-friendly desserts like cheesecake bites, this method works well in a pinch.</p>
