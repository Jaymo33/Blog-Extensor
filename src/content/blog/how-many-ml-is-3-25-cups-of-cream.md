<h2>How to Measure 3.25 Cups of Cream Correctly</h2>
<p>Measuring liquids like cream can be tricky, but using the right tools makes all the difference. A liquid measuring cup with clear markings is ideal for accuracy. Pour the cream slowly to avoid spills and check the measurement at eye level. If you don’t have a measuring cup, a kitchen scale can also help—just remember that 1 ml of cream weighs about 1 gram.</p>
