<h2>How to Measure 1.75 Cups of Pasta</h2>
<p>To measure 1.75 cups of pasta accurately, use a dry measuring cup designed for solids. Fill the cup loosely with pasta, then level it off with a straight edge. Avoid packing the pasta down, as this can lead to an inaccurate measurement. For best results, weigh the pasta afterward to confirm it's around 210 grams.</p>
<p>If you don't have a measuring cup, you can estimate using common household items. For instance, a standard mug holds roughly 1 cup, so 1.75 cups would be just under two mugs.
