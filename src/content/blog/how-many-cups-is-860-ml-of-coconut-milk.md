<h3>US vs. UK Cup Measurements for Coconut Milk</h3>  
<p>The difference between US and UK cup measurements can catch cooks off guard. A US cup holds 240 ml, while a UK cup holds 284 ml. This means 860 ml of coconut milk converts to 3.63 US cups but only 3.02 UK cups. Always check which system your recipe uses to avoid mishaps. In the UK, it's roughly 3 cups plus a tablespoon. Shake the coconut milk well before measuring, as it separates in the can. For more precision, consider using a liquid measuring cup with millilitre markings.</p>
