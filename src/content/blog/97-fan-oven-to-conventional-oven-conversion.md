<h2>Why Convert Fan Oven to Conventional Oven Temperatures?</h2>

<h2>How to Use 97°C Fan Oven Conversion in an Air Fryer</h2>
<p>Air fryers operate like compact fan ovens, making temperature conversions essential. If a recipe calls for 97°C in a fan oven, you’d typically use the same temperature in your air fryer. However, cooking times may be shorter due to the air fryer’s smaller size and more intense air circulation. For example, roasting vegetables at 97°C fan oven equivalent (117°C conventional) might take 20% less time in an air fryer.</p>
