<h2>Understanding the Conversion</h2>  
<p>When converting 360 ml of milk to cups, it's important to note that UK and US cup measurements differ slightly. In the UK, 1 cup equals 250 ml, making 360 ml roughly 1.5 cups. In the US, 1 cup is 240 ml, so 360 ml works out to about 1.52 cups. This small difference might not matter for some recipes, but for baking, precision is key.
