<h2>Why Convert 266 F to Fan Oven?</h2>
<p>Fan ovens circulate hot air, cooking food more evenly and quickly than conventional ovens. This means temperatures often need adjusting. Converting 266 F to fan oven settings ensures your dishes aren’t over or undercooked. Many air fryer recipes also use fan oven temperatures for consistency. Check out our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-conversion-chart>air fryer conversion chart</a> for more details.</p>

<p>Fan ovens are particularly useful for baking and roasting. At 130 C (266 F), you can achieve crispy exteriors and moist interiors. This temperature works well for dishes like roasted vegetables or baked goods. If you're unsure, always start with a lower temperature and adjust as needed. <h3>How to Adjust Cooking Times</h3>
<p>When converting 266 F to fan oven, cooking times may need slight adjustments. Fan ovens cook faster, so reduce the time by 10-15%. For example, if a recipe calls for 30 minutes at 266 F, check at 25 minutes. Air fryers also cook quickly, so keep an eye on your food.
