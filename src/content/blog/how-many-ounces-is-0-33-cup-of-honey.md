<h2>Converting 0.33 cup of honey to ounces</h2>
<p>To convert 0.33 cup of honey to ounces, you’ll need to know that 1 cup of honey weighs roughly 12 ounces. This means 0.33 cup of honey is about 3.9 ounces. This conversion is consistent whether you’re using UK or US measurements, making it easy to follow any recipe.</p>

<p>If you’re working with smaller quantities, like 0.33 cup of honey, a digital scale is your best friend. However, if you’re in a pinch, remember that 1 tablespoon of honey is approximately 0.7 ounces. So, 0.33 cup (which is about 5.3 tablespoons) would be close to 3.9 ounces.</p>

<h3>How to measure honey without a scale</h3>
<p>If you don’t have a scale, use the spoon-and-level method for accuracy. Lightly coat your measuring cup with oil to help the honey slide out easily. This trick works wonders for sticky ingredients like honey and ensures you’re not leaving half of it stuck to the cup. For more tips, explore our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/dessert>dessert recipes</a>, where honey is often a star ingredient.</p>
