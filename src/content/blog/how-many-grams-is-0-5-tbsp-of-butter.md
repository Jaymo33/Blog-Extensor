<h3>How to measure 0.5 tbsp of butter without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can still measure 0.5 tbsp of butter using a tablespoon. Fill it halfway, or use the markings on butter packaging (most UK butter blocks have tablespoon indicators). This method is handy for quick conversions, like 0.5 tbsp butter to grams, when you're in a pinch.</p>
