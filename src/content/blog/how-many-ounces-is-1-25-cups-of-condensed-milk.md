<p>1.25 cups of condensed milk equals approximately 10.6 ounces (or 10.58 oz to be precise). This conversion is essential for UK bakers and air fryer enthusiasts who need precise measurements for recipes. Whether you're making a creamy dessert or a rich sauce, knowing the exact weight ensures perfect results every time.</p>

<h2>Why Accurate Measurements Matter in Air Fryer Cooking</h2>  
<p>Using the right amount of condensed milk can make or break your air fryer recipes. Too little, and your dish might lack richness; too much, and it could become overly sweet or soggy. For example, our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>air fryer caramel drizzle</a> relies on precise condensed milk measurements for the perfect consistency. Accurate conversions also help when scaling recipes up or down, ensuring your treats turn out just right.</p>  
<p>Many UK cooks prefer weighing ingredients rather than using cup measurements for better precision. A kitchen scale takes the guesswork out of recipes, especially when working with sticky ingredients like condensed milk. If you're adapting a US recipe, our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-conversion-chart>conversion chart</a> can help you switch between cups and ounces effortlessly.</p>

<h3>How to Measure 1.25 Cups of Condensed Milk</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate measurement, use a liquid measuring cup specifically designed for sticky ingredients. Lightly grease the cup with oil to help the condensed milk slide out easily. Alternatively, weigh out 10.6 ounces on a digital scale for foolproof results. This method works perfectly for our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/dessert>air fryer dessert recipes</a> where precision matters.</p>

<h2>Converting Other Condensed Milk Measurements</h2>  
<p>Need to adjust your recipe? Here's a quick reference for common condensed milk conversions: 1 cup = 8.45 ounces, ½ cup = 4.23 ounces, and 2 cups = 16.9 ounces. These conversions are particularly useful when preparing multiple batches of <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/cakes>air fryer cakes</a> or desserts for gatherings. Always check whether your recipe uses US or UK cup measurements, as they can vary slightly.</p>  
<p>When substituting condensed milk in recipes, remember that its thick, sweet consistency can't be replicated by regular milk. For cleaning sticky measuring cups afterwards, our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-cleaner>air fryer cleaning guide</a> offers tips for tackling tough residues. Proper measurement and cleanup ensure your kitchen stays organised and your recipes turn out perfectly every time.</p>
