<h3>How to measure 150g of peanut butter without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have scales, you can use tablespoons to measure 150g of peanut butter. Roughly 10 level tablespoons equal 150g, but this can vary slightly depending on the brand and consistency. For sticky ingredients like peanut butter, lightly greasing the spoon helps with accuracy.</p>
