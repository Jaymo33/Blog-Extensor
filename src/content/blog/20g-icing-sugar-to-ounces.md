<h3>How to Measure 20g of Icing Sugar Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a digital scale, you can use tablespoons to measure 20g of icing sugar. One level tablespoon holds roughly 10g, so two tablespoons will give you close to 20g (0.71 oz). However, for best results, a scale is recommended, especially for air fryer baking where precision matters.</p>

<h2>Tips for Storing Icing Sugar</h2>  
<p>Keep your icing sugar fresh by storing it in an airtight container away from moisture. Humidity can cause clumping, making it harder to measure accurately. If your sugar has hardened, sieve it before use to restore its fine texture—especially important for air fryer recipes where even distribution matters.</p>
