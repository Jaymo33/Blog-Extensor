<h2>Why Convert 125g of Mayonnaise to Ounces?</h2>  
<p>Many air fryer recipes, especially those from the UK, use grams, while others rely on ounces. Converting 125g of mayonnaise to ounces (4.41 oz) helps you follow recipes without guesswork. <h3>How to Measure 125g of Mayonnaise</h3>  
<p>Using a kitchen scale is the easiest way to measure 125g of mayonnaise. If you don’t have one, a tablespoon holds about 15g, so roughly 8 tablespoons equal 125g. For ounces, this translates to just over 4.4 oz. Precision matters in air frying, so investing in a good scale is worth it. <h2>Common Uses for 125g of Mayonnaise in Air Fryers</h2>

You can also mix it with herbs for a quick dip or sauce. Our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/chicken>chicken recipes</a> often use this amount for marinades or glazes.</p>
