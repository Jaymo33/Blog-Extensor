<h3>How to Measure 125g of Mayonnaise</h3>  
<p>Using a kitchen scale is the easiest way to measure 125g of mayonnaise. If you don’t have one, a tablespoon holds about 15g, so roughly 8 tablespoons equal 125g. For ounces, this translates to just over 4.4 oz. Precision matters in air frying, so investing in a good scale is worth it.
