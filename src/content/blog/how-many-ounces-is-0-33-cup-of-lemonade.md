<h3>How to Measure 0.33 Cup of Lemonade Accurately</h3>  
<p>To measure 0.33 cup of lemonade, use a liquid measuring cup with clear markings. Fill it just below the 1/3 cup line for precision. If you’re converting 0.33 cup of lemonade to ounces, a kitchen scale set to fluid ounces will give you the exact 2.64 oz needed. This method is especially useful for recipes like glazes or dressings where balance is key.</p>
