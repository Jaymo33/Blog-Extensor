<p>100g of water equals approximately 3.53 ounces. This conversion is essential for precise cooking, especially when using an air fryer where accurate measurements can make all the difference. Whether you're following a recipe or adjusting portion sizes, knowing how to convert grams to ounces ensures perfect results every time.</p>  

<h2>Why Convert 100g of Water to Ounces?</h2>  
<p>Many air fryer recipes, especially those from the UK or Europe, list ingredients in grams. If your kitchen scale measures in ounces, converting 100g of water to ounces (3.53 oz) helps maintain accuracy. This is particularly useful for liquids like water, where even small discrepancies can affect texture and cooking times.</p>  

<p>For example, when making air fryer cakes or steamed dishes, precise water measurements ensure the right consistency. A slight miscalculation could lead to dry or soggy results. That’s why <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-conversion-chart>our conversion chart</a> is a handy tool for quick reference.</p>  

<h3>The Science Behind the Conversion</h3>  
<p>Water has a density of 1 gram per millilitre, making it a straightforward substance to measure. Since 1 ounce is approximately 28.35 grams, dividing 100g by 28.35 gives you 3.53 ounces. This ratio remains consistent, whether you're measuring water for air fryer recipes or other cooking methods.</p>  

<h2>Practical Uses in Air Fryer Cooking</h2>  
<p>Converting 100g of water to ounces (3.53 oz) is especially useful for air fryer recipes that require steam or moisture. Dishes like air fryer salmon or vegetables often need a splash of water to prevent drying out. Knowing the exact measurement ensures your food stays juicy and perfectly cooked.</p>

<h3>Common Mistakes to Avoid</h3>  
<p>One of the biggest mistakes is confusing fluid ounces with weight ounces. While 100g of water converts to 3.53 weight ounces, fluid ounces measure volume and can vary slightly. Always use a kitchen scale for the most accurate results, especially when working with air fryer recipes.</p>  

<h2>Tools for Accurate Conversions</h2>  
<p>A digital kitchen scale is the best tool for converting 100g of water to ounces. Look for one that switches between grams and ounces effortlessly. This is particularly helpful when preparing international recipes or experimenting with new dishes in your air fryer.</p>

<h3>Converting Other Ingredients</h3>  
<p>While 100g of water is 3.53 ounces, other ingredients may differ due to density. For example, 100g of flour converts to roughly 3.5 ounces, but it’s best to weigh each ingredient separately. This precision is crucial for air fryer baking, where ratios matter.</p>
