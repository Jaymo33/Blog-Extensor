<h2>How to Measure 1.5 tbsp of Rapeseed Oil Without Scales</h2>
<p>If you don’t have kitchen scales, you can still measure 1.5 tbsp of rapeseed oil accurately. A standard UK tablespoon holds 15ml of liquid, so 1.5 tbsp would be 22.5ml. Since rapeseed oil has a density of about 0.92g/ml, this translates to roughly 20 grams. For a visual guide, 1.5 tbsp is about the size of a ping-pong ball. This method is handy when you’re in a pinch, but investing in a set of digital scales will give you the most precise results for your air fryer cooking.</p>
