<h2>Why Accurate Measurements Matter in Air Fryer Cooking</h2>  
<p>Air fryers rely on even cooking, and incorrect measurements can lead to uneven results. For recipes like coconut chicken tenders or air-fried banana bites, 0.25 cup of shredded coconut (0.7 oz) is often the perfect amount. <h3>How to Measure Shredded Coconut Correctly</h3>  
<p>When measuring shredded coconut, lightly spoon it into the cup without packing it down. Overpacking can lead to using more than 0.7 ounces, altering your recipe’s texture. For best results, fluff the coconut with a fork before measuring.</p>
