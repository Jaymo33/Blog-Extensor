<h3>How to measure 1.66 cups of plain flour correctly</h3>
<p>To measure 1.66 cups of plain flour in ounces accurately, fluff the flour first with a spoon before lightly scooping it into your measuring cup. Level it off with a straight edge for consistency. Remember, 1.66 cups equals 7.3 ounces, but slight variations can occur based on humidity and packing.</p>
