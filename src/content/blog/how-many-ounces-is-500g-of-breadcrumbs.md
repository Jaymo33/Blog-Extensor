<p>Air fryers rely on even cooking, and the right amount of breadcrumbs ensures a crispy, golden finish. Too little, and your food won’t crisp up properly. Too much, and you risk uneven cooking or a dry texture. For recipes like air-fried chicken tenders or breaded shrimp, 500g of breadcrumbs (17.64 oz) is often the perfect amount for a family-sized portion.</p>

<h3>How to Measure 500g of Breadcrumbs Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>No kitchen scale? No problem. One cup of breadcrumbs typically weighs around 50g, so 500g would be roughly 10 cups. However, this can vary slightly depending on the breadcrumb texture (fine or coarse). For the most accurate results, we recommend using a digital scale, especially when preparing air fryer recipes where precision matters.</p>
