<h2>Converting 1.5 cups of water to ounces</h2>
<p>The conversion from cups to ounces is simple: 1 cup of water equals 8 ounces, so 1.5 cups of water is 12 ounces. This applies to both US and UK measurements, though it’s worth noting that UK cups are slightly larger (284ml vs. 240ml). However, for water, the weight in ounces remains consistent because water’s density is uniform. This makes it easy to measure liquids for air fryer cooking without needing complex calculations.</p>
<p>If you’re working with other liquids like oil or milk, their densities vary, so the weight in ounces might differ slightly. But for water, 1.5 cups will always be 12 ounces.
