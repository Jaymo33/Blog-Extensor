<h3>How to measure flaxseed for air fryer recipes</h3>
<p>Using a kitchen scale is the most accurate way to measure 0.25 cup of flaxseed in ounces. Simply place your bowl on the scale, tare it to zero, and add the flaxseed until it reaches 1 ounce. If you don’t have a scale, fluff the flaxseed with a fork before lightly spooning it into a measuring cup. Avoid packing it down, as this can lead to over-measuring.
