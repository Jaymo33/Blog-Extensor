<h2>How to measure tahini for air fryer recipes</h2>
<p>Measuring tahini can be tricky due to its thick, sticky consistency. To accurately measure 0.25 tbsp of tahini in grams, use a spoon to scoop it lightly without packing it down. This method is especially useful when preparing air fryer recipes like tahini-glazed chicken or roasted vegetables with tahini sauce.</p>
