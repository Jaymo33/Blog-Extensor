<h3>How to Measure 185 ml of Olive Oil Without a Measuring Cup</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a measuring cup, you can use a standard tablespoon to measure 185 ml of olive oil. Since 1 tablespoon holds about 15 ml, 185 ml equals roughly 12 tablespoons (or ¾ cup). This method works well for smaller quantities in air fryer recipes, such as dressings or marinades.</p>
