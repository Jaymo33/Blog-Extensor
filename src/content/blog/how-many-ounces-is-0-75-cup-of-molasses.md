<h3>How to Measure Molasses Without the Mess</h3>  
<p>Measuring sticky ingredients like molasses can be frustrating, but a few tricks make it easier. Lightly greasing your measuring cup or spoon helps the molasses slide out cleanly. Alternatively, warm the molasses slightly to make it less viscous. Always use liquid measuring cups for accuracy, and avoid packing it down like you would with flour or sugar.</p>
