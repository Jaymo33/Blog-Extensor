<h3>How to measure 0.66 cup of mayonnaise accurately</h3>
<p>Using a liquid measuring cup is the easiest way to measure 0.66 cup of mayonnaise. Fill the cup to the two-thirds mark, then level it off with a straight edge. For even more accuracy, a kitchen scale can confirm the weight in ounces. This method eliminates errors and ensures your air fryer dishes are consistently delicious.</p>
