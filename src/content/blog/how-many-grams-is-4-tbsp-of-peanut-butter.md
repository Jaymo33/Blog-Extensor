<h3>How to measure peanut butter for air fryer recipes</h3>  
<p>The easiest way to measure peanut butter is by using a kitchen scale. Scoop your peanut butter into a bowl and zero out the scale for an exact 64-gram measurement. If you don’t have a scale, lightly pack the peanut butter into a tablespoon and level it off. Avoid compacting it too tightly, as this can skew the weight.</p>
