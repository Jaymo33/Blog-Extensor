<h2>How to Measure 885 ml of Coconut Milk Without a Scale</h2>
<p>No measuring jug? No problem! A standard US cup holds 240 ml, so 885 ml is just shy of 4 cups. For UK cooks, note that a metric cup is 250 ml, making 885 ml equal to 3.54 cups. If you’re using canned coconut milk, one 400 ml can plus half another gets you close to 885 ml. Stir well before measuring, as the cream and liquid often separate.</p>
