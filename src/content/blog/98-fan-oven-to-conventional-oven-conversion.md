<h2>How to Calculate 98 Fan Oven to Celsius</h2>
<p>Converting 98 fan oven to celsius involves a simple formula. The general rule is to add 20°C to the fan oven temperature. This accounts for the more intense heat circulation in fan-assisted cooking. For 98 fan oven, this becomes 118°C conventional, though we round to 120°C for practicality.</p>
<p>This calculation works similarly for air fryers, which also use fan-assisted cooking.
