<h3>How to Measure 15g of Lime Juice</h3>  
<p>Measuring lime juice by weight (15g) is more accurate than using volume, as it accounts for variations in juiciness. To measure 15g of lime juice, use a digital kitchen scale for precision. If you don’t have a scale, roughly 0.53 ounces (or just over half an ounce) will do the trick. <h3>Tips for Storing Lime Juice</h3>  
<p>Fresh lime juice is best used immediately, but if you have leftover 15g portions, store them in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 3 days. This way, you’ll always have the perfect amount on hand for your air fryer adventures.
