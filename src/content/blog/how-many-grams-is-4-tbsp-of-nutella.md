<h3>How to Measure 4 Tbsp of Nutella</h3>  
<p>To measure 4 tablespoons of Nutella, use a standard UK tablespoon (15ml per tbsp). Level off each spoonful with a knife for accuracy. If you’re using a kitchen scale, 64 grams is your target. This method works for all Nutella-based recipes, from air-fried pastries to no-bake treats.</p>
