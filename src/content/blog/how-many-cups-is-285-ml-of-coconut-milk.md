<h2>How to Measure 285 ml of Coconut Milk Without a Measuring Cup</h2>
<p>If you don't have a measuring cup, you can still measure 285 ml of coconut milk accurately. A standard metric measuring jug will do the trick, or you can use a kitchen scale. One ml of coconut milk weighs roughly 1 gram, so 285 ml would be about 285 grams.</p>

<h3>US vs UK Cup Measurements</h3>
<p>It's worth noting that US and UK cup measurements differ slightly. In the US, 1 cup is 240 ml, while in the UK, it's 250 ml. This means 285 ml of coconut milk is about 1.19 US cups or 1.14 UK cups. For most recipes, this small difference won't matter, but it's good to know for precision baking.</p>
