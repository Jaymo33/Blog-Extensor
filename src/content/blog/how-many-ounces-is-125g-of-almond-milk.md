<h3>How to Measure 125g of Almond Milk Accurately</h3>  
<p>To measure 125g of almond milk, use a digital kitchen scale for the most accurate results. Place your measuring jug on the scale, tare it to zero, then pour the almond milk until it reaches 125g. If you don’t have a scale, remember that 125g is roughly 4.41 ounces or half a US cup. However, scales are always more precise, especially for air fryer recipes where consistency is key.
