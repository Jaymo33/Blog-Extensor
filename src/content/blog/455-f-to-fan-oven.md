<h3>How Fan Ovens Differ From Conventional Ovens</h3>
<p>Fan ovens cook about 20°C faster than conventional ovens due to air circulation. This means 455 F converts to 220 C rather than the standard 235 C. The even heat distribution makes fan ovens ideal for air fryer-style cooking. When using an air fryer, you're essentially working with a compact fan oven. This similarity explains why the 455 F to fan oven conversion works so well.</p>
