<h3>How to measure 10g of margarine without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have kitchen scales, you can still measure 10g of margarine (0.35 oz) using tablespoons. One level tablespoon of margarine is roughly 15g, so two-thirds of a tablespoon will give you 10g. For smaller quantities, a teaspoon holds about 5g, so two teaspoons equal 10g. This method is handy when you’re mid-recipe and realise your scales are out of batteries. Just remember, these are approximations—investing in digital scales guarantees accuracy.</p>
