<h2>How to measure 250g of soy sauce without scales</h2>
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can still measure 250g of soy sauce (8.82 ounces) using volume measurements. One tablespoon of soy sauce weighs about 15g, so 250g would be roughly 16-17 tablespoons. However, this method is less precise than weighing, so we recommend using scales for accuracy. For more precise measurements, consider investing in a digital scale, which is a must-have for air fryer enthusiasts.</p>
