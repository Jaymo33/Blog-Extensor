<h3>How to Measure Balsamic Vinegar Accurately</h3>  
<p>For the most precise measurement, use a liquid measuring cup or kitchen scale. Pour the balsamic vinegar slowly to avoid spills and check at eye level. If using a scale, note that 1 ml of balsamic vinegar weighs roughly 1.04 grams due to its density. This means 709.76 ml (3.0 cups) weighs approximately 738 grams.</p>
