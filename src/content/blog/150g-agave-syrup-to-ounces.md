<h2>How to Measure 150g of Agave Syrup Accurately</h2>
<p>Measuring 150g of agave syrup can be tricky due to its sticky consistency. Using a digital kitchen scale is the most accurate method. Simply tare your scale with the container, then pour the syrup until it reaches 150g (or 5.29 ounces). If you don’t have a scale, a measuring spoon can work in a pinch, but it’s less precise.</p>
