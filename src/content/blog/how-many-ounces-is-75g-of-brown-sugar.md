<h3>How to Measure 75g of Brown Sugar Accurately</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate conversion, use a digital kitchen scale set to grams. Scoop the brown sugar loosely into a measuring cup, then level it off without packing it down. If your scale only shows ounces, 75g translates to roughly 2.65 oz. Keep in mind that brown sugar’s moisture can cause slight variations, so measuring by weight is always best.
