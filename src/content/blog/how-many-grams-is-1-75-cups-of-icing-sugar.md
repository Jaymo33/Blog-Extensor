<h3>How to Measure Icing Sugar Correctly</h3>
<p>To measure 1.75 cups of icing sugar accurately, fluff the sugar first to avoid compaction. Spoon it lightly into your measuring cup, then level it off with a knife. This method ensures perfect consistency for air fryer baking every time.</p>

<h3>Common Mistakes When Measuring Icing Sugar</h3>
<p>One common mistake is scooping icing sugar directly from the bag with a measuring cup. This compacts the sugar, leading to an inaccurate measurement.
