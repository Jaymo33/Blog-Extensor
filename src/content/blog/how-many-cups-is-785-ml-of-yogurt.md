<h3>How to Measure 785 ml of Yogurt Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a measuring jug, use standard cups to portion 785 ml of yogurt. Fill a cup to the brim and level it off for accuracy—this equals roughly 240 ml per cup. For 785 ml, you’ll need just over 3 cups (3.3 cups). Stir the yogurt first, as thickness can affect volume. This method works for both UK and US cup measurements.</p>
