<h3>How to Measure 1.75 Cups of Oats Correctly</h3>  
<p>To measure 1.75 cups of oats accurately, use a dry measuring cup and level off the excess with a straight edge. Scooping directly from the bag can compress the oats, leading to an incorrect measurement. For best results, fluff the oats with a fork before measuring to ensure an even distribution. If you’re converting 1.75 cups of oats to ounces, a kitchen scale is the most reliable tool—simply weigh out 5.6 oz. This method is especially useful for air fryer recipes where precision affects cooking time and texture.</p>
