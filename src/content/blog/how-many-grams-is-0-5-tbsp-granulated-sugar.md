<h3>How to measure 0.5 tbsp granulated sugar without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a scale, lightly spoon granulated sugar into a tablespoon and level it off for accuracy. Half of this will give you roughly 0.5 tbsp granulated sugar how many grams. However, this method isn’t as precise as weighing, so for critical recipes like macarons or meringues, invest in a scale. Air fryer baking often requires tighter tolerances, so when in doubt, opt for grams over volume.</p>
