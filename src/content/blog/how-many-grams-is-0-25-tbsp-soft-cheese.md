<p>0.25 tbsp of soft cheese equals approximately 3.75 grams. This measurement is handy for air fryer recipes where precision matters, ensuring your dishes turn out perfectly creamy and balanced. Whether you're making a dip, a sauce, or a topping, knowing the exact weight helps avoid over- or under-seasoning.</p>  

<h2>Why accurate measurements matter in air fryer cooking</h2>  
<p>Air fryers rely on even cooking, and incorrect ingredient quantities can throw off textures and flavours. Soft cheese, like cream cheese or ricotta, adds richness to dishes but must be measured correctly. Too much can make a dish overly heavy, while too little might leave it lacking creaminess. For the best results, always weigh ingredients like soft cheese when following recipes from <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>airfryerrecipe.co.uk</a>.</p>

<h3>How to measure 0.25 tbsp of soft cheese without a scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a scale, you can still measure 0.25 tbsp of soft cheese using a standard tablespoon. Fill it halfway and then divide that portion in half again for a rough estimate. However, for recipes where precision is key—like air fryer cheesecakes or stuffed mushrooms—weighing is the better option.</p>
