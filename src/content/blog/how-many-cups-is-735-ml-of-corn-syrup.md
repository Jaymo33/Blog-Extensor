<h3>Converting 735 ml of Corn Syrup to Cups</h3>  
<p>To convert 735 ml of corn syrup to cups, divide the millilitres by 236.59 (the volume of one US cup). This gives you approximately 3.1 cups. However, UK cups differ slightly, holding 284 ml, meaning 735 ml equals about 2.6 UK cups. Always check which measurement system your recipe uses to avoid confusion.
