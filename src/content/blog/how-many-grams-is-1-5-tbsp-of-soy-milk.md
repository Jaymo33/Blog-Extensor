<h3>How to measure soy milk correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate results, use a digital kitchen scale to weigh your soy milk. If you don’t have one, ensure your tablespoon is levelled off—no heaping scoops! This is especially important for baking, where even small discrepancies can affect the final product. Keep in mind that 1.5 tbsp of soy milk equals 22.5 grams, a handy figure to remember.</p>
