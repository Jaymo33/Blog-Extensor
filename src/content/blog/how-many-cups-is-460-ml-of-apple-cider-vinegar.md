<h3>Converting 460 ml to Cups</h3>  
<p>To convert 460 ml of apple cider vinegar to cups, divide the millilitres by the cup size you’re using. For UK measurements, 460 ÷ 250 = 1.84 cups. For US measurements, 460 ÷ 240 ≈ 1.92 cups. Most recipes round this to 1.94 cups for simplicity, ensuring accuracy without overcomplicating measurements.</p>
