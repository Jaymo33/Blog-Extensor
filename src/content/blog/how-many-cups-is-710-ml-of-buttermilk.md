<h3>US vs. UK Cup Measurements</h3>  
<p>It's important to note that US and UK cup measurements differ slightly. A US cup holds 240 ml, while a UK cup is 284 ml. For 710 ml of buttermilk, this means roughly 2.96 US cups or 2.5 UK cups.
