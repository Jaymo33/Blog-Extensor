<p>475 grams of water equals approximately 2 cups in US measurements or 1.9 cups in UK measurements. This conversion is essential for precise cooking, especially when using an air fryer where ingredient ratios matter. Whether you're baking, making sauces, or preparing air fryer recipes, getting the measurement right ensures perfect results every time.</p>

<h2>Understanding the Conversion</h2>
<p>Converting grams to cups can be tricky because it depends on the ingredient's density. Water, however, has a straightforward conversion since its density is 1 gram per millilitre. This means 475 grams of water is roughly 475 millilitres. In US cups, which are 240 ml each, 475 grams of water translates to just under 2 cups. In UK cups, which are 284 ml, it's about 1.9 cups.</p>
<p>For air fryer recipes, accuracy is key. Too much or too little water can affect texture and cooking times. <h3>US vs UK Cup Measurements</h3>
<p>It's worth noting that US and UK cup measurements differ slightly. A US cup is 240 ml, while a UK cup is 284 ml. This means 475 grams of water is 2 US cups but only 1.9 UK cups. Always check which standard your recipe uses, especially if it's from an international source like <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk>airfryerrecipe.co.uk</a>.</p>
