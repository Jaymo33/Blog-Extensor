<h3>US vs UK Cup Measurements for 635 ml of Vegetable Oil</h3>  
<p>In the US, 635 ml converts to about 2.68 cups (635 ÷ 240). For UK cooks, it's roughly 2.23 cups (635 ÷ 284). This 0.45 cup difference shows why specifying your measurement system is vital.
