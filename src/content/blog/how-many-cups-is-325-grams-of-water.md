<h2>Why Accurate Water Measurements Matter in Air Fryers</h2>

For example, when making air fryer cakes or steamed dishes, 325 grams of water (about 1.37 cups) is often the sweet spot. <h3>Converting 325 Grams of Water to Cups</h3>
<p>To convert 325 grams of water to cups, remember that 1 cup of water equals roughly 237 grams. This means 325 grams is about 1.37 cups. However, this can vary slightly depending on factors like temperature and altitude. For air fryer cooking, where precision is key, sticking to grams is often more reliable. Explore our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>recipe collection</a> for dishes that use this measurement.</p>

<h3>Tips for Measuring 325 Grams of Water Without a Scale</h3>
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can still measure 325 grams of water using cups. Fill a measuring cup to the 1.37-cup mark, or use 1 cup plus 1/3 cup for a close approximation. Just remember that this method isn’t as precise as weighing. For air fryer recipes where accuracy is critical, investing in a scale is worth it.
