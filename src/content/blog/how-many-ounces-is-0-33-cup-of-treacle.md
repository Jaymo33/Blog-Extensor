<p>Using the correct amount of treacle is crucial for achieving the right texture and flavour in your bakes. <h2>How to measure 0.33 cup of treacle</h2>  
<p>Measuring treacle can be messy, but a few simple tricks make it easier. First, lightly grease your measuring cup or spoon with oil to help the treacle slide out cleanly. For 0.33 cup (roughly 5 tablespoons + 1 teaspoon), fill the cup just under one-third full. Alternatively, use a digital kitchen scale to weigh out 2.93 ounces for pinpoint accuracy.</p>
