<h3>How to Measure 450 Grams of Brown Sugar Correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate 450g brown sugar to cups conversion, lightly spoon the sugar into your measuring cup without packing it down. Brown sugar is often sold compacted, so fluff it with a fork first. If your recipe calls for firmly packed sugar, press it gently into the cup and level it off. This method ensures consistency across all your <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/dessert>air fryer dessert recipes</a>.</p>

<p>UK and US cup measurements differ slightly, which matters when converting 450 grams of brown sugar. A UK cup holds 250ml, while a US cup is 240ml. For 450 grams of brown sugar, this means roughly 2.25 cups in the US and 2.2 cups in the UK. Always check your recipe’s origin to avoid discrepancies, especially for air fryer baking where precision counts.</p>
