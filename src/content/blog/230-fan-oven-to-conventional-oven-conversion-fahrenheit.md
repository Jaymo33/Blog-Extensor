<h2>How to Convert 230 Fan Oven to Conventional Oven</h2>
<p>The standard conversion formula for fan ovens to conventional ovens is simple. For 230 fan oven, subtract 20°C from the temperature, then convert to Fahrenheit. This accounts for the fan oven's efficiency. The calculation looks like this: (230°C - 20°C) × 9/5 + 32 = 446°F.</p>
