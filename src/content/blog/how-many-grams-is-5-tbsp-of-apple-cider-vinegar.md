<p>5 tablespoons of apple cider vinegar equals approximately 75 grams. <p>Using the right amount of ingredients is crucial for air fryer recipes. Too much liquid, like apple cider vinegar, can affect texture and cooking times. <h3>How to Measure Apple Cider Vinegar Correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate results, use a digital kitchen scale to weigh 75 grams of apple cider vinegar. If you don’t have one, fill a tablespoon to the brim without spilling—this ensures you’re close to the 15g per tablespoon standard. Avoid guessing, as small discrepancies can alter your dish’s outcome.</p>
