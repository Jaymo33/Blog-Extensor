<h3>The exact conversion for 500g wholemeal flour</h3>
<p>One gram equals 0.035274 ounces, making 500g of wholemeal flour equivalent to 17.637 ounces. For practical purposes, most bakers round this to 17.64 ounces. This conversion applies to all types of flour, but remember that wholemeal flour is denser than white flour, so always measure by weight rather than volume for accuracy.</p>

<h2>How to measure 500g of wholemeal flour</h2>
<p>For the most accurate measurement of 500g wholemeal flour, use digital kitchen scales set to grams. If your scales only show ounces, simply multiply the grams by 0.035274. Scooping flour directly from the bag can compact it, leading to inaccurate measurements - always spoon flour into your measuring container for best results.</p>
