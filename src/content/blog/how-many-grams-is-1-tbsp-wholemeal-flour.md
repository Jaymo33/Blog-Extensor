<h3>How to measure wholemeal flour without a scale</h3>
<p>If you don’t have a scale, use the spoon-and-level method for accuracy. Gently spoon wholemeal flour into the tablespoon without packing it down, then level it off with a knife. This prevents over-measuring, which is common when scooping directly from the bag.
