<h3>Measuring 510 ml of Buttermilk Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don't have a measuring jug or scale, you can still measure 510 ml of buttermilk using standard cups. One UK metric cup holds about 250 ml, so 510 ml would be just over 2 cups.
