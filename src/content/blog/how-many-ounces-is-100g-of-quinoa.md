<h2>How to Measure 100g of Quinoa in Ounces</h2>  
<p>Measuring 100g of quinoa in ounces is straightforward with a kitchen scale. Simply weigh your quinoa in grams and convert it using the ratio 1g = 0.0353 oz. This means 100g of quinoa is roughly 3.53 ounces. If you don’t have a scale, a standard measuring cup can help, but weights are more accurate for perfect results.</p>  
<p>For air fryer cooking, accuracy matters. Always fluff your quinoa after cooking for the best texture.</p>

<h3>Tips for Cooking Quinoa in an Air Fryer</h3>  
<p>Cooking quinoa in an air fryer is quick and easy. Rinse 100g (3.53 oz) of quinoa thoroughly to remove bitterness, then combine it with water or broth in an air fryer-safe dish. Cook at 180°C for 12-15 minutes, stirring occasionally. The result? Light, fluffy quinoa ready for any recipe.</p>

<h3>Storing Cooked Quinoa</h3>  
<p>Cooked quinoa keeps well in the fridge for up to 5 days. Store 100g portions in airtight containers for quick meals. Reheat in the air fryer for 3-4 minutes at 160°C to restore its fluffy texture.</p>
