The decimal represents a fraction of a pound, which we can convert to ounces.</p>

<h3>3.5 kg Lamb Leg to US Pounds vs Imperial Pounds</h3>
<p>It's a common question: is there a difference between US pounds and imperial pounds for converting 3.5 kg lamb leg? The short answer is no. Both the US customary system and the British imperial system use the avoirdupois pound, which is defined as exactly 0.45359237 kilograms. Therefore, whether you are in the UK, the US, or elsewhere, the conversion from 3.5 kg lamb leg to pounds will always yield the same result: approximately 7.7 pounds.
