<h3>How to Measure 0.25 Cups of Chicken Breast</h3>  
<p>Use a kitchen scale for the most accurate gram measurement (56g). If you don’t have one, lightly pack diced chicken into a measuring cup. Avoid compacting it, as this can skew results. For shredded chicken, fluff it lightly before measuring. This method works well for <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/meal-prep>meal prep</a> or salads.</p>
