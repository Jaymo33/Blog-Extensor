<h3>How to measure hoisin sauce correctly</h3>
<p>For the most accurate conversion, use a kitchen scale to weigh your hoisin sauce directly in ounces. If you don’t have one, a standard US cup holds 8 fluid ounces, so 1.66 cups would be roughly 13.28 ounces. Remember, hoisin sauce is dense, so spoon it into your measuring cup without packing it down. For sticky sauces like hoisin, lightly greasing your measuring cup can help it slide out easily.
