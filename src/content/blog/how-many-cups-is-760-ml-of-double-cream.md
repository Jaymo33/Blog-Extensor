<h3>UK vs. US Cup Measurements</h3>  
<p>In the UK, 1 cup equals 250 ml, so 760 ml of double cream is roughly 3.2 cups. In the US, 1 cup is 240 ml, making 760 ml about 3.1 cups. This small difference matters in recipes requiring exact proportions. If you're using an air fryer for baking, always check whether the recipe follows UK or US measurements.</p>  

<h2>How to Measure 760 ml of Double Cream Without a Scale</h2>  
<p>If you don’t have a measuring jug, you can use standard cups to measure 760 ml of double cream. Fill a UK cup (250 ml) three times, then add another 10 ml (about two teaspoons) to reach 760 ml. For US cups, use three full cups (720 ml) plus an extra 40 ml (roughly two tablespoons). This method works well for air fryer recipes like custards or creamy pasta sauces.</p>
