<h3>Common Mistakes to Avoid</h3>

Always use a meat thermometer to confirm doneness rather than relying solely on time, as air fryers can vary in performance. Finally, allow the chicken to rest after cooking; cutting it too soon will cause the juices to run out, leaving the meat dry. Follow these tips for a flawless 3.5 kg whole chicken every time.</p>
