<p>4 tablespoons (tbsp) of breadcrumbs weigh approximately 28 grams. This measurement is essential for air fryer recipes, ensuring your coatings and toppings are perfectly portioned. Whether you're making crispy chicken or a golden fish fillet, knowing the exact weight helps achieve consistent results every time.</p>  

<h2>Why Accurate Measurements Matter in Air Fryer Cooking</h2>  
<p>Precision is key when using an air fryer, as uneven coatings can lead to undercooked or burnt spots. Breadcrumbs are a staple for creating that desirable crunch, and measuring them correctly ensures even cooking. Too little, and your food won’t crisp up; too much, and it might dry out. <h3>How to Measure Breadcrumbs Correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate results, use a kitchen scale to weigh your breadcrumbs. Scooping directly from the bag can compress them, leading to inconsistent measurements. Lightly spooning the breadcrumbs into the tablespoon and levelling off the excess ensures you get the right amount. This method is especially useful for recipes requiring precise ratios, like <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>air fryer coatings</a>.</p>
