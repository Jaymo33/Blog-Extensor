<h2>How to Measure 535 ml of Condensed Milk Without a Scale</h2>  
<p>Not everyone has a kitchen scale, but measuring cups are a common alternative. To measure 535 ml of condensed milk, use a liquid measuring cup for accuracy. Fill it to the 2-cup mark, then add another quarter cup to reach 2.25 cups. This method ensures you get the right consistency for your air fryer recipes.</p>  

<h3>UK vs. US Cup Measurements</h3>  
<p>It's worth noting that UK and US cup measurements differ slightly. In the UK, 1 cup is 284 ml, while in the US, it's 240 ml. For 535 ml of condensed milk, the conversion is roughly 2.25 US cups or 1.88 UK cups. Always check which measurement system your recipe uses to avoid errors.</p>
