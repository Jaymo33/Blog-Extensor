<h3>How to measure maple syrup accurately</h3>  
<p>To measure 2 tbsp of maple syrup in grams, use a digital kitchen scale for precision. Pour the syrup into a tablespoon until it’s level, then weigh it. If you don’t have a scale, remember that 2 tbsp maple syrup equals roughly 40 grams. This method works for UK measurements and ensures your air fryer recipes turn out perfectly.</p>
