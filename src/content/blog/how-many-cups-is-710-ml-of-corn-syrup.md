<h3>UK vs. US Cup Measurements</h3>  
<p>While 710 ml of corn syrup is roughly 3 cups in both systems, slight variations exist. A UK cup holds 284 ml, whereas a US cup is 240 ml. For most recipes, the difference is negligible, but for precision baking, it’s worth noting. If your air fryer recipe originates from the US, stick to their standard for consistency.</p>  

<h2>How to Measure 710 ml of Corn Syrup Without a Measuring Cup</h2>

Alternatively, use a standard mug (about 300 ml) and fill it just under two and a half times.
