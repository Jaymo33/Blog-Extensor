<h3>How to Measure Fish Sauce Correctly</h3>  
<p>Using a liquid measuring cup is the easiest way to measure fish sauce. Pour it slowly to avoid spills and check at eye level for accuracy. For smaller quantities, a kitchen scale set to ounces ensures precision. If your recipe calls for 1.33 cups, weighing out 10.64 ounces guarantees consistency. This method is especially useful for air fryer marinades and sauces.</p>

<h3>Tips for Storing Fish Sauce</h3>  
<p>Fish sauce has a long shelf life but should be stored in a cool, dark place to maintain quality. Once opened, keeping it tightly sealed prevents oxidation and odours. If you use it frequently, transferring it to a smaller bottle can make measuring easier. Always wipe the rim clean to avoid sticky residues that could affect future measurements.</p>
