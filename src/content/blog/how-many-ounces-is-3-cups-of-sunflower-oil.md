<h3>How to Measure Sunflower Oil Correctly</h3>  
<p>To measure 3 cups of sunflower oil accurately, use a liquid measuring cup with clear markings. Fill it to the 3-cup line, ensuring the oil sits level for an exact measurement. If you’re converting to ounces, remember that 1 cup of sunflower oil equals 8 fluid ounces, so 3 cups would be 24 oz.</p>
