<h3>How to Measure 25g of Protein Powder Accurately</h3>  
<p>To measure 25g of protein powder (0.88 oz), use a digital kitchen scale for the most accurate results. Scoop the powder lightly without packing it down to avoid over-measuring. If you don’t have a scale, a standard tablespoon holds roughly 15g of protein powder, so 1.5 tablespoons will get you close to 25g. For more precision, refer to your protein powder’s packaging, as densities vary by brand.</p>
