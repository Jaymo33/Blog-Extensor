<h2>Common uses for 100g of bbq sauce in air fryer cooking</h2>

<h3>Tips for storing leftover bbq sauce</h3>
<p>If you have leftover bbq sauce after measuring out 100g, store it in an airtight container in the fridge. It should last for up to two weeks. You can also freeze it in small portions for future use. When reheating, gently warm it in a saucepan or microwave before adding it to your air fryer dishes. This ensures the consistency stays perfect for glazing or dipping.</p>
