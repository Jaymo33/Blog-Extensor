<h3>How to Measure 185 ml of Half And Half Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a measuring jug, use standard cup measurements: 185 ml is just under 4/5 of a cup. For smaller increments, a tablespoon holds about 15 ml, so 12 tablespoons plus 1 teaspoon will get you close.
