<h3>How to Measure 40g of Cream Cheese Accurately</h3>  
<p>Using a digital kitchen scale is the easiest way to measure 40g of cream cheese. Simply place your bowl or container on the scale, tare it to zero, and scoop the cream cheese until it reads 40g. If you don’t have a scale, 40g is roughly two tablespoons, but this can vary slightly depending on the cream cheese’s density. For air fryer recipes, precision is key, so we recommend sticking to weight measurements.</p>

<h3>Tips for Storing Leftover Cream Cheese</h3>  
<p>If you’ve measured out 40g but don’t use it all, store the remainder in an airtight container in the fridge. Cream cheese can last up to two weeks if sealed properly. For longer storage, consider freezing it in 40g portions—just thaw it in the fridge before using it in your next air fryer creation.</p>
