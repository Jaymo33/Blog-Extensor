<h3>How to Measure Maple Syrup Correctly</h3>  
<p>When measuring sticky ingredients like maple syrup, use a liquid measuring cup for accuracy. Lightly grease the cup or spoon with oil to help the syrup slide out easily. For 0.5 cups of maple syrup in ml, fill the cup halfway (118 ml) and level it off. This method ensures you’re not shortchanging your recipe or making it overly sweet.</p>
