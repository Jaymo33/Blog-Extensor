For 3.25 kg, the calculation is 3.25 multiplied by 2.20462, which gives you roughly 7.17 pounds. This conversion is particularly useful when you're dealing with recipes that use different measurement systems. Many air fryer enthusiasts find this helpful when scaling recipes up or down.
