<h2>How to measure 0.25 tbsp of yeast</h2>
<p>Measuring small amounts of yeast can be tricky without the right tools. For 0.25 tablespoon of yeast, we recommend using measuring spoons specifically designed for baking. <h3>Using measuring spoons</h3>
<p>If you're using measuring spoons, fill a 1 tablespoon measure halfway to get approximately 0.5 tbsp, then divide that portion in half again. <h3>Using digital scales</h3>
