<h3>How to measure shredded carrot correctly</h3>  
<p>To get an accurate 0.25 cup (1 ounce) of shredded carrot, use the spoon-and-level method. Gently fill your measuring cup without packing the carrot down, then level it with a straight edge. Avoid pressing the shreds, as this compacts them and skews the weight. For larger batches, a kitchen scale is ideal—simply tare your bowl and add until you hit 28 grams. This method works perfectly for <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>air fryer recipes</a> requiring precise veggie portions.</p>
