<h3>How to Measure 2.25 Cups of Vegetable Oil Correctly</h3>
<p>To measure 2.25 cups of vegetable oil accurately, use a liquid measuring cup with clear ml markings. Pour the oil slowly to avoid spills and check the measurement at eye level for precision. If you don’t have a measuring cup, a kitchen scale can help—1 ml of vegetable oil weighs roughly 0.92 grams. For smaller quantities, tablespoons and teaspoons can be used, but for 2.25 cups, a large measuring cup is best.
