<h3>How to Measure 385 ml of Corn Syrup</h3>  
<p>To measure 385 ml of corn syrup, use a liquid measuring cup for accuracy. Pour the syrup slowly to avoid air bubbles, which can skew the measurement. If you don’t have a measuring cup, a kitchen scale can also help—385 ml weighs roughly 515 grams.
