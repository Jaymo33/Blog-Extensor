<h3>How to Measure 585 ml of Balsamic Vinegar Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a measuring jug, you can use standard cups to measure 585 ml of balsamic vinegar. Alternatively, use a tablespoon (15 ml) to measure smaller increments if precision is key.</p>
