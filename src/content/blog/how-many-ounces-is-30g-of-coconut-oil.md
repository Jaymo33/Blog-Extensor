<h3>How to measure 30g of coconut oil without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can still measure 30g of coconut oil using tablespoons. One tablespoon of coconut oil weighs roughly 14g, so 30g is just over two tablespoons. This method isn’t as precise as weighing, but it works in a pinch. For best results, we recommend investing in a digital scale, especially if you frequently use your air fryer.</p>
