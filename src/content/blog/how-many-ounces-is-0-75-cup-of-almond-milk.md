<h3>How to Measure 0.75 Cup of Almond Milk Accurately</h3>
<p>To measure 0.75 cup of almond milk, use a liquid measuring cup with clear markings. Fill it to the 3/4 line for accuracy. If you’re weighing it, 5.3 ounces is the target. A kitchen scale is your best friend here, especially for air fryer recipes where precision matters. Shake the almond milk carton well before pouring, as separation can affect volume. For sticky or thick almond milk varieties, lightly grease the measuring cup for easy pouring.</p>
