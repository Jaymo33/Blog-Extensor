<h2>UK vs US Measurements</h2>
<p>It's important to note that cup measurements can vary between the UK and the US. In the UK, 1 cup is typically 284 ml, while in the US, it's 240 ml. However, for honey, the difference is minimal due to its density. Regardless of where you are, 1.75 cups of honey will still be close to 414 ml. This makes it easy to follow recipes from <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/>airfryerrecipe.co.uk</a> without confusion.</p>

<h3>How to Measure Honey Accurately</h3>
<p>Measuring honey can be tricky because of its sticky consistency. To get an accurate 414 ml (1.75 cups) of honey, lightly grease your measuring cup with oil or spray. This helps the honey slide out easily, ensuring you don’t leave any behind. Alternatively, use a kitchen scale for even more precision—honey weighs about 340 grams per cup.
