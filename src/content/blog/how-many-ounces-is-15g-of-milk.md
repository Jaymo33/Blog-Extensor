<h3>How to Measure 15g of Milk Accurately</h3>  
<p>Using a digital kitchen scale is the easiest way to measure 15g of milk. Set your scale to grams, pour the milk until it hits 15g, and you’re done. If your scale only shows ounces, aim for 0.53 ounces instead. For those without a scale, a tablespoon of milk is about 15g, but this can vary slightly depending on the milk’s density. Always double-check for accuracy, especially in baking.</p>
