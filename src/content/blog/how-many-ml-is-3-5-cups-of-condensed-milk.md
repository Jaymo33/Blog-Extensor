<h3>How to Measure Condensed Milk Correctly</h3>  
<p>Using a liquid measuring cup is the best way to measure condensed milk accurately. Pour it slowly to avoid air bubbles, and level it off for precision. If you don’t have a measuring cup, a kitchen scale can help—condensed milk weighs roughly 312 grams per cup, so 3.5 cups would be about 1092 grams.
