<h3>How to Measure 3.25 Cups of Olive Oil Accurately</h3>  
<p>For the best results, use a liquid measuring cup with clear ml markings. Pour the olive oil slowly to avoid spills and check the measurement at eye level. If you don’t have a measuring cup, a kitchen scale can help—1 ml of olive oil weighs approximately 0.92 grams. This method is handy when scaling recipes up or down, ensuring your air fryer dishes turn out perfectly every time.</p>
