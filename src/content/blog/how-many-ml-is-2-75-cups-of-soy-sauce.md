<h3>How to Measure Soy Sauce for Air Fryer Cooking</h3>  
<p>To measure 2.75 cups of soy sauce in ml, use a liquid measuring jug with millilitre markings for accuracy. Pour the soy sauce slowly to avoid spills and check the measurement at eye level.
