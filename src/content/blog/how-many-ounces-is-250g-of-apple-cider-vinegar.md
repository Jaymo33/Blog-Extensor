<h2>How to convert grams to ounces</h2>
<p>Converting grams to ounces is straightforward once you know the ratio. One gram is approximately 0.035 ounces, so to convert 250g of apple cider vinegar to ounces, you multiply 250 by 0.035. This gives you the 8.82 ounces mentioned earlier.</p>
