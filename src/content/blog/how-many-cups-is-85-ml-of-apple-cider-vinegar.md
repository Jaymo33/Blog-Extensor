<h3>How to Measure 85 ml of Apple Cider Vinegar Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a measuring jug with ml markings, you can still measure 85 ml of apple cider vinegar using standard cups. Since 1 cup equals 240 ml, 85 ml is roughly a third of a cup. For even more precision, use tablespoons—85 ml is about 5.7 tablespoons. This method works well for small-batch recipes, like air fryer sauces or glazes.</p>
