<h3>How to Measure 385 ml of Chicken Broth Without a Measuring Cup</h3>  
<p>If you don't have a measuring cup, you can still measure 385 ml of chicken broth accurately. A standard UK metric cup holds 250 ml, so 385 ml is roughly 1 and a half cups plus 2 tablespoons. For US cups, which are 240 ml, it's about 1.6 cups. Always double-check to avoid over-pouring.</p>
