<h3>The science behind porridge oats measurements</h3>
<p>Porridge oats have a density of about 0.38 g/ml, which means 0.25 tbsp (roughly 3.75ml) weighs approximately 1.5 grams. This conversion remains consistent whether you're using jumbo oats, rolled oats, or instant varieties. The key is using proper measuring spoons for accuracy.</p>

<h2>How to measure 0.25 tbsp porridge oats without scales</h2>
<p>If you don't have digital scales, you can still measure 0.25 tbsp porridge oats accurately. Fill a tablespoon measure halfway, then divide that portion in half again. This visual method gets you close to the 1.5 gram mark, though scales are always more precise.</p>
