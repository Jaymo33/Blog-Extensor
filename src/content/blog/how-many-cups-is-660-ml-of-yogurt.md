<h3>How to Measure Yogurt Correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate results, use a liquid measuring cup for yogurt. Spoon the yogurt into the cup and level it off with a knife. Avoid packing it down, as this can lead to over-measuring. If you're using Greek yogurt, note that it’s denser than regular yogurt. Adjust slightly if your recipe calls for a specific consistency.</p>
