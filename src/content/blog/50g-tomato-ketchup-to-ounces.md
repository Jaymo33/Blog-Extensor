<h3>How to measure 50g of tomato ketchup without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can use tablespoons to measure 50g of tomato ketchup. Roughly three level tablespoons equal 50g or 1.76 ounces. For sticky ingredients like ketchup, lightly grease the spoon for easier release. This method is handy for quick adjustments in recipes. Always level off the spoon for accuracy.</p>
