<h2>Why Convert Fan Oven to Conventional Oven Temperatures?</h2>
<p>Fan ovens circulate hot air around the food, reducing cooking time and often requiring lower temperatures. This efficiency means recipes designed for conventional ovens may overcook in a fan oven if not adjusted. Converting 286°F fan oven to 266°F conventional ensures your dishes cook evenly without drying out. This is especially useful for baking delicate items like cakes or cookies, where precision matters.
