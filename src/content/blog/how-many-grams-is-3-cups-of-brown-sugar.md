<h3>How to Measure Brown Sugar Correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate conversion, lightly pack brown sugar into your measuring cup before levelling it off. Unlike granulated sugar, brown sugar compacts easily, which can lead to over-measuring if not done carefully. A kitchen scale eliminates this issue entirely—simply tare your bowl and add 600 grams for 3.0 cups.</p>
