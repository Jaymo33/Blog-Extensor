<h3>Why Grams Are More Accurate Than Cups</h3>  
<p>Measuring by weight (grams) is far more reliable than using cups, as potato pieces can settle differently in a measuring cup. A tightly packed cup will weigh more than a loosely filled one, leading to inconsistencies in your cooking. For best results, invest in a digital kitchen scale—it’s a game-changer for air fryer enthusiasts who want perfectly cooked meals every time.</p>
