<h3>How to Measure Minced Onion Correctly</h3>  
<p>To measure minced onion accurately, lightly pack the tablespoon without pressing down too hard. Overpacking can result in more than 10 grams, while underpacking may leave you short. <h3>Converting Other Onion Measurements</h3>  
<p>If your recipe calls for diced or sliced onion instead of minced, the weight per tablespoon will vary. Diced onion is slightly heavier, at around 12 grams per tbsp, due to larger pieces. Always adjust measurements based on the onion's form to ensure accuracy in your air fryer recipes.</p>
