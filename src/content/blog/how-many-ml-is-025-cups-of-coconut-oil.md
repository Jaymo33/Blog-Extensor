<h3>How to Measure Coconut Oil for Air Fryer Recipes</h3>  
<p>Measuring coconut oil can be tricky due to its solid state at room temperature. To get an accurate 59 ml (0.25 cups), gently melt the oil before measuring. Alternatively, use a kitchen scale for precision—1 ml of coconut oil weighs roughly 0.92 grams.</p>  

<h2>Common Uses for 0.25 Cups of Coconut Oil in Air Fryers</h2>  
<p>From crispy chicken wings to golden roasted vegetables, 0.25 cups (59 ml) of coconut oil is a versatile ingredient. It adds a subtle sweetness and helps achieve that perfect crunch. <p>Coconut oil is also great for greasing air fryer baskets, preventing sticking without overpowering flavours. Just a light coating of 59 ml (0.25 cups) is enough for most dishes. Remember, a little goes a long way in an air fryer!</p>

<h2>Tips for Storing and Using Coconut Oil in Air Fryers</h2>  
<p>Store coconut oil in a cool, dark place to maintain its quality. When using 0.25 cups (59 ml) in your air fryer, warm it slightly for even distribution.
