<h2>How to measure 2.5 tbsp of flour accurately</h2>  
<p>Measuring flour by volume (tablespoons) can be tricky due to settling and packing. For the most accurate conversion of 2.5 tbsp of flour into grams, use a kitchen scale. Spoon the flour lightly into the tablespoon without packing it down, then level it off with a knife. Repeat until you have 2.5 tbsp, then weigh it for the exact gram equivalent.</p>  

<h3>Using a kitchen scale for precision</h3>  
<p>A digital kitchen scale eliminates guesswork and ensures your 2.5 tbsp of flour translates to the correct 19.5 grams. This method is especially useful for air fryer baking, where consistency is key. If you don’t have a scale, fluff the flour in its container before scooping to avoid compacting it, which can lead to over-measuring.</p>
