<h3>Tips for measuring vegetable oil in your air fryer recipes</h3>
<p>When measuring 0.25 cup vegetable oil (2 ounces) for air frying, use liquid measuring cups for accuracy. Pour the oil to just below the 1/4 cup line, as vegetable oil's meniscus can make precise measurement tricky. For even distribution, consider using an oil sprayer to lightly coat food rather than pouring - this gives great coverage with less oil.</p>

<h3>Storing vegetable oil for air fryer use</h3>
<p>To ensure your vegetable oil stays fresh for accurate 0.25 cup measurements, store it in a cool, dark place. Consider buying smaller bottles if you don't use oil frequently, as opened vegetable oil is best used within 3-6 months.</p>
