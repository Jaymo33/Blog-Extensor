<h2>How to measure 150g of oats without scales</h2>  
<p>Not everyone has a kitchen scale, but you can still measure 150g of oats (5.29 oz) using household items. A standard UK metric cup holds about 90g of oats, so 150g would be roughly 1 and 2/3 cups. For ounces, this translates to just over 5 ounces, which you can measure using a liquid measuring jug marked in fluid ounces. While not as precise as scales, these methods work in a pinch.</p>
