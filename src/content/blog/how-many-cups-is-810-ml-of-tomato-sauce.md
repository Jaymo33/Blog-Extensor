<h2>US vs UK Cup Measurements</h2>  
<p>The US and UK use slightly different cup measurements, which can affect your recipe. In the US, 1 cup equals 240 ml, while in the UK, it’s 250 ml. For 810 ml of tomato sauce, this means 3.42 US cups or 3.56 UK cups. This small difference might not seem significant, but it can impact baking or sauces requiring precision.</p>  
<p>If you're using an air fryer, sticking to one measurement system avoids confusion. Most <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/>airfryerrecipe.co.uk</a> recipes specify which cup measurement to use. Always double-check if you're adapting a recipe from another source to ensure the best results.</p>

<h3>How to Measure 810 ml of Tomato Sauce Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a measuring jug, you can use standard cups to measure 810 ml of tomato sauce. For US cups, fill 3 cups completely and add about 1/3 of another cup. For UK cups, use 3 full cups and roughly half of another. This method works well for most <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/potatoes>potato-based dishes</a> or marinades.</p>
