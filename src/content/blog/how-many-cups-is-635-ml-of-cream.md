<h2>UK vs. US Cup Measurements</h2>  
<p>It's important to note that UK and US cup measurements differ slightly. In the UK, 1 cup equals 284 ml, while in the US, it's 236.59 ml. This means 635 ml of cream converts to 2.68 cups in the UK and 2.69 cups in the US. For air fryer recipes, always check whether the recipe uses UK or US measurements to avoid mistakes.</p>  

<h3>How to Measure Cream Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don't have a measuring jug or scale, you can still measure 635 ml of cream using standard cups. Fill a UK cup (284 ml) twice and add roughly two-thirds of another cup to reach 635 ml. For US cups, fill one cup (236.59 ml) twice and add about half of a third cup. This method works well for air fryer recipes like <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/dessert>desserts</a> or creamy casseroles.</p>
