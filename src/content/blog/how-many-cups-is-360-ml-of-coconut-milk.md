<h3>How to Measure 360 ml of Coconut Milk Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a scale, use a liquid measuring cup marked in millilitres. Fill it to the 360 ml line for accuracy. Alternatively, use a standard cup and measure 1.52 cups of coconut milk. <h3>Tips for Storing Leftover Coconut Milk</h3>  
<p>After measuring 360 ml of coconut milk, store the rest in an airtight container. It keeps for up to 4 days in the fridge or 2 months in the freezer.
