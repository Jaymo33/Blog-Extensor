<h3>How to measure 1.5 cups of raisins correctly</h3>  
<p>To get an accurate 1.5 cups of raisins, lightly spoon them into a measuring cup without packing them down. Raisins can vary in size, so this method prevents over-measuring. If you’re using a kitchen scale, aim for 7.5 ounces for perfect precision.
