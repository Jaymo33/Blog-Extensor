<h2>Why Convert 50g of Mustard to Ounces?</h2>  
<p>Many UK recipes use grams, while others rely on ounces, especially older cookbooks or international guides. Converting 50g of mustard to ounces (1.76 oz) helps maintain accuracy in your cooking. This is particularly important for air fryer recipes, where precise measurements can affect texture and flavour. Mustard is a common ingredient in marinades, like those for chicken or lamb, so getting the measurement right matters. <p>Mustard adds depth to air fryer dishes, from glazes for salmon to coatings for crispy potatoes. 50g (1.76 oz) is enough for a marinade serving 2-4 people.
