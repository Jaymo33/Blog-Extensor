<p>Using the correct amount of BBQ sauce can affect the texture and flavour of your dish. Too much sauce might make your food soggy, while too little could leave it dry. <h3>How to Measure BBQ Sauce Without Scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have kitchen scales, use standard UK tablespoons for measuring. A level tablespoon holds about 15 grams of BBQ sauce, so 5 tbsp equals roughly 75 grams. Avoid heaped spoonfuls, as this can throw off your measurements. For sticky sauces, lightly grease the spoon first to help it slide off easily. This trick works well for other condiments too, like ketchup or honey.</p>

<h3>Tips for Cooking with BBQ Sauce in an Air Fryer</h3>  
<p>BBQ sauce can caramelise quickly in the air fryer, so keep an eye on your food to prevent burning. For even coverage, toss your ingredients in the sauce before cooking.
