<p>If you don’t have a scale, use a liquid measuring cup for chilli sauce. Fill it to the 2-cup mark, ensuring the sauce settles evenly. Thicker sauces may require a gentle stir to remove air pockets. For sticky sauces, lightly grease the cup first.
