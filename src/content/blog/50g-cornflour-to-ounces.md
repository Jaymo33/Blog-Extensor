<h2>How to Measure 50g of Cornflour Without a Scale</h2>
<p>No scale? No problem. Roughly 50g of cornflour fills a 1/4-cup measure, but this can vary slightly. For absolute precision, invest in a digital scale—it’s a game-changer for air fryer cooking. Scooping directly from the bag can compact the flour, leading to inaccurate amounts.</p>
<p>If you’re mid-recipe and realise your scales are missing, don’t panic. Fluff the cornflour with a fork before spooning it into your measuring cup.
