<h3>How to measure 50g of baking soda without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can still measure 50g of baking soda using volume. One tablespoon of baking soda weighs about 10g, so 5 tablespoons equal roughly 50g (or 1.76 oz). However, this method isn’t as precise as weighing, so invest in a scale for best results.
