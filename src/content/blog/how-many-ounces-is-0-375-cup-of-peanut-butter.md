<h3>How to Measure 0.375 Cup of Peanut Butter</h3>  
<p>Use a dry measuring cup for peanut butter, scraping off the excess for accuracy. Alternatively, a kitchen scale set to ounces (3 oz for 0.375 cup) is foolproof.
