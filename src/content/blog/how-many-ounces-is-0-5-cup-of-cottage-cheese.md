<h3>How to measure 0.5 cup of cottage cheese</h3>
<p>For the most accurate 0.5 cup cottage cheese to ounces conversion, use a kitchen scale. Scoop the cottage cheese into a measuring cup, then transfer to the scale to confirm it's 4 ounces. If you don't have scales, lightly pack the cottage cheese into a dry measuring cup without pressing too firmly, as this can compress the curds and lead to inaccurate measurements.</p>
