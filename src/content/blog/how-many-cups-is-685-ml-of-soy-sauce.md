<h2>How to Convert 685 ml of Soy Sauce to Cups</h2>  
<p>The standard conversion for liquids is 1 cup = 236.588 ml. Dividing 685 ml by this figure gives you roughly 2.9 cups. For air fryer cooking, this measurement is particularly useful when scaling recipes up or down. If you're working with a <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-conversion-chart>conversion chart</a>, you’ll find this ratio handy.</p>  
<p>Keep in mind that soy sauce’s viscosity can slightly affect measurements. For best results, use a liquid measuring cup rather than dry cups. This ensures you’re getting the exact 685 ml of soy sauce in cups (2.9 cups) needed for your dish.</p>
