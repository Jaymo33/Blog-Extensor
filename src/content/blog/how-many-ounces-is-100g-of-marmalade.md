<h2>How to measure 100g of marmalade in ounces</h2>
<p>Converting 100g of marmalade to ounces is straightforward once you know the ratio. One gram is roughly 0.035 ounces, so 100g translates to 3.53 ounces. If you don’t have a scale, you can use measuring spoons or cups, though a scale is more accurate. For air fryer recipes, where precision is key, we recommend using a digital scale. This ensures your marmalade-based dishes, like glazes or desserts, turn out perfectly every time.
