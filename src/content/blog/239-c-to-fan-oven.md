<h2>Why Convert 239 C to Fan Oven?</h2>
<p>Fan ovens circulate hot air around the food, reducing cooking time and often requiring a lower temperature than conventional ovens. When a recipe calls for 239°C in a standard oven, dropping it to 219°C in a fan oven prevents overcooking. This is particularly useful for baked goods like cakes or cookies, where precision matters. For air fryers, which work similarly to fan ovens, this conversion ensures crispy results without burning.
