<h2>How to Convert 350 Grams of Cream to Cups</h2>
<p>Converting grams to cups isn’t always straightforward, as cream’s density can vary. A general rule is that 350 grams of cream equals about 1.48 US cups or 1.23 UK cups. For the most accurate measurement, use a kitchen scale. If you don’t have one, a liquid measuring cup will work, but be sure to level it off properly.</p>

<h3>US vs UK Cup Measurements for Cream</h3>
<p>In the US, a standard cup is 240ml, while in the UK, it’s 250ml. This small difference means 350 grams of cream will measure slightly differently depending on where your recipe originates. For US recipes, 350g is about 1.48 cups, whereas UK recipes will call for roughly 1.23 cups. Always double-check the origin of your recipe to avoid measurement mishaps.</p>
