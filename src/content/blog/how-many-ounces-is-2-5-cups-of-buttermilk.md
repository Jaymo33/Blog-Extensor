<h2>Why accurate buttermilk measurements matter</h2>
<p>Using the right amount of buttermilk can make or break your air fryer recipes. Too little, and your batter might be too thick; too much, and it could become runny. For 2.5 cups of buttermilk, which is 20 ounces, you’ll achieve the ideal consistency for most recipes. This measurement is particularly crucial for air fryer baking, where even small deviations can affect texture and cooking time.</p>
<p>Many of our favourite <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>air fryer recipes</a>, from buttermilk biscuits to marinated chicken, rely on this precise ratio. <h3>Liquid vs weight measurements for buttermilk</h3>
<p>While 2.5 cups of buttermilk equals 20 fluid ounces, the weight in ounces can vary slightly depending on the buttermilk’s thickness. Most standard buttermilk weighs about 8.5 ounces per cup, making 2.5 cups approximately 21.25 ounces by weight. For most home cooking purposes, especially in air fryer recipes, the 20-fluid-ounce measurement works perfectly.</p>

<h2>Converting buttermilk for UK measurements</h2>
<p>For our UK readers, it’s worth noting that 2.5 cups of buttermilk converts to about 600 millilitres. However, since many UK recipes still use ounces for liquid measurements, the 20-ounce equivalent remains useful. This conversion is particularly handy when trying American recipes in your air fryer or when following guides from <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk>airfryerrecipe.co.uk</a>.</p>

<h3>Tips for measuring buttermilk accurately</h3>
<p>To measure 2.5 cups (20 ounces) of buttermilk perfectly, use a clear liquid measuring cup placed on a level surface. Pour until the meniscus (the curve at the liquid’s surface) reaches the 20-ounce mark. For sticky buttermilk, lightly grease your measuring cup first to ensure all the liquid pours out cleanly.</p>

<p>If you don’t have buttermilk for your 20-ounce requirement, you can make a substitute by adding 1 tablespoon of lemon juice or vinegar to every cup of milk (2.5 tablespoons for 2.5 cups).
