<h2>How to Measure Almond Milk for Air Fryer Recipes</h2>  
<p>Measuring liquids like almond milk can be tricky, especially when recipes call for ounces instead of cups. To measure 1.25 cups (10 oz) accurately, use a liquid measuring cup with clear markings. Pour the almond milk to the 1.25-cup line, or weigh it on a kitchen scale set to ounces for precision.</p>  

<h3>Using a Kitchen Scale for Best Results</h3>  
<p>A digital kitchen scale is the most reliable way to measure 1.25 cups of almond milk in ounces. Simply place your measuring cup on the scale, tare it to zero, then pour the almond milk until it reaches 10 oz. This method eliminates guesswork and ensures consistency in your air fryer dishes.</p>
