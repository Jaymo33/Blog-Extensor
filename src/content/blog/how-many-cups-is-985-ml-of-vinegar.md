<h2>How to Measure 985 ml of Vinegar Without a Measuring Cup</h2>
<p>Don’t have a measuring cup handy? No problem! You can still measure 985 ml of vinegar using common kitchen items. A standard UK mug holds about 250 ml, so roughly 4 mugs will give you close to 985 ml. Alternatively, use a tablespoon (15 ml) – you’d need around 65 tablespoons to reach 985 ml.</p>

<h3>Using a Kitchen Scale for Precision</h3>
<p>For ultimate accuracy, weigh your vinegar. Since vinegar has a similar density to water, 985 ml weighs roughly 985 grams. Set your kitchen scale to grams, pour until you hit 985 g, and you’ve got the perfect amount. This method is especially useful for baking or making dressings where proportions are critical.</p>

<h2>Converting 985 ml of Vinegar in US vs UK Cups</h2>
<p>It’s worth noting that US and UK cup measurements differ slightly. In the US, 1 cup equals 240 ml, so 985 ml of vinegar is about 4.1 US cups. In the UK, 1 cup is 250 ml, making 985 ml approximately 3.94 UK cups. Always check which standard your recipe follows to avoid measurement mishaps.</p>
