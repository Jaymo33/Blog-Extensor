<h3>How to Measure 20g of Tomato Paste Without Scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can still measure 20g of tomato paste (0.71 ounces) using tablespoons. One tablespoon of tomato paste typically weighs around 15g, so 20g is roughly 1 and 1/3 tablespoons. For sticky ingredients like tomato paste, lightly oiling the spoon helps it slide off easily.
