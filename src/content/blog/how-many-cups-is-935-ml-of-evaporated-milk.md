<h3>How to Measure 935 ml of Evaporated Milk</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a measuring jug, you can use standard cups to measure 935 ml of evaporated milk. Since 1 cup is roughly 237 ml, 935 ml works out to just under 4 cups. For the most accurate results, use a kitchen scale or a liquid measuring cup with millilitre markings.</p>
