<h3>How to Measure Buttermilk Correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate results, use a kitchen scale to weigh your buttermilk. If you don't have one, a liquid measuring cup will do—just ensure you're measuring at eye level. Remember, 1.33 cups of buttermilk is roughly 10.64 ounces, a measurement that works perfectly for most air fryer dishes.</p>

<h3>Tips for Storing Buttermilk</h3>  
<p>Buttermilk has a limited shelf life, so storing it properly is key. Keep it refrigerated and use it within a week of opening. If you don't use it often, consider freezing it in measured portions—just thaw it before using in your air fryer recipes.</p>
