<h3>How to Measure Double Cream Correctly</h3>  
<p>To measure 785 ml of double cream accurately, use a liquid measuring cup with clear markings. Pour the cream slowly to avoid air bubbles, which can distort the measurement. If you don’t have a measuring cup, a kitchen scale can help—just remember that 785 ml of double cream weighs about 800 grams.</p>
