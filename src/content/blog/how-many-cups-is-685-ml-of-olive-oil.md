<h2>How to Measure 685 ml of Olive Oil Without a Scale</h2>
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, measuring 685 ml of olive oil in cups is straightforward. A standard US cup holds 240 ml, so 685 ml divided by 240 ml gives you roughly 2.9 cups. In the UK, a metric cup is 250 ml, making 685 ml equal to about 2.74 cups. This small difference might not matter for most recipes, but for baking, precision is key.</p>
