<h3>How to Measure 5g of Sunflower Oil</h3>  
<p>To measure 5g of sunflower oil, use a digital kitchen scale set to grams. If your scale only displays ounces, aim for 0.18 oz. Alternatively, 5g of sunflower oil is roughly one teaspoon, though weighing is more precise. For sticky ingredients like oil, a small silicone bowl or measuring spoon can help avoid mess.
