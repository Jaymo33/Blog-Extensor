<h2>How to Measure 475 Grams of Granulated Sugar</h2>
<p>If you don't have a kitchen scale, measuring 475 grams of granulated sugar in cups is straightforward. Use a standard measuring cup and fill it to the brim, then level it off with a straight edge. Repeat this process until you've measured out 2.38 cups. For more accuracy, consider using a digital scale, especially for recipes where precision is key, like those found on <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes</a>.</p>

<h3>UK vs. US Cup Measurements</h3>
<p>It's worth noting that cup measurements can vary slightly between the UK and the US. In the UK, a standard cup holds 250ml, while in the US, it's 240ml. This difference means that 475 grams of granulated sugar might be a tad more or less depending on where your recipe originates. Always check the source of your recipe to ensure you're using the correct measurement.</p>
