<h3>How to Measure 125g of Icing Sugar Accurately</h3>  
<p>Using a digital kitchen scale is the most accurate way to measure 125g icing sugar in ounces. Scoop the sugar into a bowl on the scale until it hits 125g, which translates to roughly 4.41 oz. If you don’t have a scale, spoon the sugar into a measuring cup and level it off, but note that this method is less precise. For air fryer recipes, especially delicate bakes, precision matters—so weighing is best.</p>
