<h3>How to measure 0.5 cup of ground cumin</h3>  
<p>Measuring spices like ground cumin can be tricky due to their fine texture. For 0.5 cup, use a dry measuring cup and level it off with a straight edge for accuracy. If you don’t have a measuring cup, a kitchen scale set to ounces will give you the exact 1.5 ounces needed. This method is especially useful for air fryer cooking, where precision matters.</p>

<h3>Storing ground cumin for freshness</h3>  
<p>To keep your ground cumin fresh, store it in an airtight container away from heat and light. Proper storage ensures the spice retains its flavour, so your 0.5 cup measurements stay consistent over time. Fresh cumin makes all the difference in air fryer dishes, so always check your stock before cooking.</p>
