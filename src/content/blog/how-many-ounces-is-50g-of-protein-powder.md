<h2>How to Measure 50g of Protein Powder</h2>
<p>Measuring 50g of protein powder correctly ensures consistency in your air fryer recipes. The easiest way is to use a digital kitchen scale set to grams. Simply scoop the powder into a bowl on the scale until it hits 50g. If you don’t have a scale, you can use a measuring spoon, but this method is less precise.</p>
<p>One heaped tablespoon of protein powder usually weighs around 15g, so 50g would be roughly three and a half tablespoons. However, this can vary depending on the brand and how densely packed the powder is. For best results, always weigh your protein powder when possible, especially for air fryer recipes where precision is key.</p>
