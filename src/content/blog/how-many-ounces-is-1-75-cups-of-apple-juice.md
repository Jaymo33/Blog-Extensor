<h2>Why Accurate Measurements Matter in Air Fryer Cooking</h2>  
<p>Using the right amount of liquid, like apple juice, can make or break your air fryer recipes. Too much liquid can lead to soggy results, while too little might dry out your food. <h3>How to Measure 1.75 Cups of Apple Juice</h3>  
<p>To measure 1.75 cups of apple juice accurately, use a liquid measuring cup with clear markings. Fill it to the 1.75-cup line, or measure 14 ounces directly using a kitchen scale. This method eliminates guesswork and ensures your recipes turn out as intended.</p>

<h2>Tips for Storing and Using Apple Juice</h2>  
<p>Once opened, apple juice should be refrigerated and used within 7–10 days. If you’re using 1.75 cups (14 oz) in a recipe, ensure the juice is fresh for the best flavour. For longer storage, freeze apple juice in measured portions for future air fryer recipes.</p>
