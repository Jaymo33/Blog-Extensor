<h3>How to measure minced onion correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate conversion of 5 tbsp minced onion to grams, lightly pack the onion into the spoon without compressing it. Overpacking can add extra weight, skewing your recipe. If you don’t have a scale, remember that 1 tbsp of minced onion is roughly 6 grams. This rule of thumb works well for most <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/vegetables>vegetable-based recipes</a>.</p>
