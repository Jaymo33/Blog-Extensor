<h3>How to Measure Corn Syrup Accurately</h3>  
<p>Measuring thick liquids like corn syrup can be tricky. To get an exact 3.75 cups (887.21 ml), use a liquid measuring jug with clear markings. For air fryer recipes, precision ensures even cooking and perfect results every time.</p>
