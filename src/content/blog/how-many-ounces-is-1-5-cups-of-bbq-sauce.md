<h2>Understanding the conversion from cups to ounces</h2>
<p>Converting 1.5 cups of bbq sauce to ounces is straightforward once you know the basic ratio. In general, 1 cup of bbq sauce weighs about 8 ounces, making 1.5 cups equal to 12 ounces. This measurement is particularly useful when following air fryer recipes that specify weights rather than volumes. Many professional recipes use weight measurements for greater accuracy, especially when dealing with thick sauces like bbq. The consistency of bbq sauce can vary between brands, which is another reason weight measurements are often more reliable than volume.</p>

Weight measurements eliminate variables like how densely you packed the sauce into the measuring cup. This precision is especially important when adapting traditional recipes for air fryer use.</p>
