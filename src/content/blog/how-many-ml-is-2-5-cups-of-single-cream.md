<p>To measure 2.5 cups of single cream accurately, use a liquid measuring jug with clear millilitre markings. Pour the cream slowly to avoid air bubbles, and check at eye level for precision. If you don’t have a measuring jug, a standard metric cup holds 240 ml, so 2.5 cups would be 600 ml.
