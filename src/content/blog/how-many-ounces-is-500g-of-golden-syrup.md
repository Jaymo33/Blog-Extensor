<h3>How to Measure 500g of Golden Syrup Accurately</h3>  
<p>Measuring sticky ingredients like golden syrup can be tricky. Use a digital kitchen scale for grams or a liquid measuring cup for ounces. Lightly grease your spoon or cup to help the syrup slide off easily. For air fryer recipes, consistency matters, so avoid eyeballing measurements.</p>

<h3>Tips for Storing and Handling Golden Syrup</h3>  
<p>Store golden syrup in a cool, dry place to prevent crystallization. If it hardens, warm the jar in hot water to loosen it. Always wipe the rim after use to keep it clean.
