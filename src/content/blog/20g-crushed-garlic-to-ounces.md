<h3>How to Measure Crushed Garlic Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can estimate 20g of crushed garlic. Roughly, one medium garlic clove yields about 5g when crushed. So, 20g would be equivalent to four medium cloves. This method isn’t as precise as weighing but works in a pinch. For best results, invest in a digital scale—it’s a game-changer for air fryer cooking.</p>
