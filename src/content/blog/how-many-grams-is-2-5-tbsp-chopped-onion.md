<h2>Tips for working with onions in air fryers</h2>  
<p>Onions can sometimes stick to air fryer baskets due to their natural sugars. To prevent this, lightly coat your 2.5 tbsp (20g) of chopped onion with oil before cooking. This ensures even crisping and easy release. The moisture in onions also affects cooking times, so adjust accordingly based on your recipe.</p>  

<p>For caramelised onions in the air fryer, you might start with more than 2.5 tbsp as they reduce significantly. The concentrated sweetness adds incredible depth to burgers or steaks.
