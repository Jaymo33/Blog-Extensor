<p>Using the right amount of Nutella can make or break your air fryer recipes. Too much can cause spills or uneven cooking, while too little might leave your dish lacking flavour. <h3>How to Measure Nutella Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a scale, levelling off your tablespoons is the next best thing. Use the back of a knife to scrape off any excess for an accurate 2.5 tbsp measurement. Remember, 1 tbsp of Nutella is roughly 15 grams, so 2.5 tbsp comes to about 38 grams. This method works well for most <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/dessert>dessert recipes</a>.</p>

<h3>Storing Nutella for Optimal Freshness</h3>  
<p>To keep your Nutella at the perfect consistency for measuring, store it in a cool, dry place. Avoid refrigeration, as it hardens and becomes difficult to scoop. If separation occurs, a quick stir brings it back to its smooth, spreadable state—ready for your next air fryer creation.</p>
