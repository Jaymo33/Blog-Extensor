<h2>Converting Cups to Millilitres</h2>
<p>Converting cups to millilitres isn't always straightforward, as it depends on the ingredient's density. However, for single cream, 0.25 cups reliably translates to 59 ml. This measurement is standard in both UK and US recipes, though slight variations can occur based on brand or fat content. Always check your cream's packaging for specific details.</p>

<p>A liquid measuring cup or kitchen scale is the best way to measure 0.25 cups of single cream accurately.
