<h2>Why Convert 125g of Plain Yogurt to Ounces?</h2>  
<p>Many UK recipes, especially those for air fryers, use grams, while others may reference ounces. Converting 125g of plain yogurt to ounces (4.41 oz) ensures you follow recipes correctly. <h2>How to Measure 125g of Plain Yogurt Accurately</h2>  
<p>Using a digital kitchen scale is the easiest way to measure 125g of plain yogurt. Simply place your bowl on the scale, tare it to zero, and spoon in the yogurt until it hits 125g. If you don't have a scale, 125g is roughly half a standard UK yogurt pot (250g).</p>  
<p>For those who prefer volume measurements, 125g of plain yogurt is about ½ cup. However, weight is always more accurate, especially for air fryer recipes where consistency matters. Explore our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>recipe collection</a> for yogurt-based dishes that make the most of this versatile ingredient.</p>  

<h3>Common Uses for 125g of Plain Yogurt in Air Fryer Cooking</h3>  
<p>Plain yogurt is a staple in many air fryer recipes, from marinades to baked goods. A 125g portion works perfectly for tenderising chicken or adding moisture to cakes. It's also great for making creamy dips or dressings to accompany air-fried veggies.</p>  

<h3>Does Yogurt Weight Change with Brand or Fat Content?</h3>  
<p>While 125g of plain yogurt will always weigh 125g, density can vary slightly between brands or fat percentages. Full-fat yogurt may appear thicker, but the weight remains the same. For best results, stick to the weight measurement rather than volume.</p>
