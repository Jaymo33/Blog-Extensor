<h3>Shredded vs. Grated Mozzarella: Does It Affect the Measurement?</h3>
<p>Shredded mozzarella cheese tends to be fluffier and take up more space, while grated mozzarella is denser. <h2>How to Measure 100 Grams of Mozzarella Cheese Without Scales</h2>
<p>If you don’t have kitchen scales, you can still measure 100 grams of mozzarella cheese in cups using simple household items. A standard US cup holds about 240ml, while a UK cup is 250ml. For shredded mozzarella, 100 grams is roughly 0.4 cups, which is just under half a cup. You can also use tablespoons – 100 grams equals about 6-7 tablespoons of shredded mozzarella.</p>
