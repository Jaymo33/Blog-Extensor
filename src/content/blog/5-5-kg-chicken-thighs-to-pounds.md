<h2>How to convert 5.5 kg chicken thighs to pounds</h2>
<p>The conversion from kilograms to pounds is straightforward: multiply the weight in kilograms by 2.20462. For 5.5 kg chicken thighs, that means 5.5 multiplied by 2.20462, which equals approximately 12.125 pounds. This calculation gives you the precise weight in pounds, useful for recipes that require exact measurements. Remember that this is the weight including bones if your chicken thighs are bone-in, which is common for many recipes. If your recipe specifies boneless chicken thighs, you may need to adjust slightly, but the conversion factor remains the same.</p>
