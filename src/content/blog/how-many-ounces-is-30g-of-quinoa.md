<h2>Quinoa in air fryer cooking</h2>  
<p>Quinoa works brilliantly in air fryers, from crispy toppings to protein-packed sides. When converting 30g of quinoa to ounces for air frying, remember that 1.06 ounces is the dry weight. Cooked quinoa absorbs water, tripling in volume—so adjust portions accordingly. For perfect texture, spread quinoa evenly in the air fryer basket and shake occasionally.</p>

<h3>Common quinoa measurement mistakes</h3>  
<p>One frequent error is confusing cooked and uncooked quinoa weights. 30g of uncooked quinoa yields about 90g cooked—a crucial difference when following air fryer recipes. Another pitfall is packing quinoa into measuring cups, which leads to inaccurate amounts. For consistent results, always weigh quinoa or use the spoon-and-level method with measuring cups.</p>
