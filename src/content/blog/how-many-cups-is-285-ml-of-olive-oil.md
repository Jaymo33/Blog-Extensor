<h2>Why Accurate Olive Oil Measurements Matter</h2>  
<p>Using the right amount of olive oil in your air fryer recipes prevents soggy or overly dry dishes. Too much oil can lead to greasy results, while too little may cause sticking or uneven cooking. Converting 285 ml of olive oil to cups helps maintain balance, especially in recipes like roasted vegetables or fried chicken. <p>Olive oil is a staple in Mediterranean and healthy cooking, often used in air fryers for its high smoke point and rich flavour. Whether you're making <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/chicken>air fryer chicken</a> or drizzling over roasted veggies, 285 ml of olive oil translates to just over 1 cup, making it easy to scale recipes. Always measure carefully for the best results.</p>  

<h3>UK vs. US Cup Measurements</h3>  
<p>In the UK, 1 cup equals 250 ml, so 285 ml of olive oil is roughly 1.2 cups. In the US, 1 cup is 240 ml, making 285 ml about 1.18 cups. This small difference can matter in baking, where precision is key. If your air fryer recipe calls for 285 ml of olive oil, use a measuring jug for accuracy.</p>  

<h2>How to Measure 285 ml of Olive Oil Without a Scale</h2>  
<p>If you don’t have a measuring jug, you can use tablespoons to approximate 285 ml of olive oil. One tablespoon holds about 15 ml, so 19 tablespoons equal roughly 285 ml. This method works well for recipes like <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/vegetables>air fryer vegetables</a>, where a little extra oil won’t hurt.</p>

<p>Many air fryer recipes, like marinades or dressings, use 285 ml of olive oil as a base. <h2>Tips for Storing and Using Olive Oil in Your Air Fryer</h2>  
<p>Store olive oil in a cool, dark place to maintain its quality. When measuring 285 ml of olive oil for air fryer recipes, use fresh oil for the best taste. Avoid overheating, as olive oil can smoke at high temperatures, affecting your dish’s flavour.</p>  

<p>For even cooking, lightly coat ingredients with oil instead of drowning them. This technique works well with 285 ml of olive oil in cups, especially for crispy snacks like air fryer kale chips or sweet potato fries. Experiment with different oils for varied flavours.</p>
