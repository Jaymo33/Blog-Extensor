<h3>How to Measure 50g of Jam Without Scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have scales, use tablespoons to measure 50g of jam. One tablespoon holds about 20g, so 2.5 tablespoons equal roughly 50g (1.76 oz). Stir the jam first to ensure consistency, as thicker jams may vary. This method works well for recipes like <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>air fryer thumbprint cookies</a>, where precision matters. <h3>Tips for Storing Leftover Jam</h3>  
<p>After measuring 50g of jam, store the rest in an airtight container in the fridge. Proper storage keeps it fresh for weeks, ready for your next air fryer creation.
