<h2>How to Measure 3 Cups of Chopped Onion Correctly</h2>
<p>Chopping onions uniformly ensures accurate measurements. If the pieces are too large or small, the weight can vary. For 3 cups of chopped onion, aim for pieces roughly 1 cm in size. This consistency helps when converting cups to ounces, giving you a reliable 12-ounce result. Pack the onions lightly into the measuring cup—don’t press down, as this compacts them and skews the weight.</p>
