<h2>How to measure 0.33 cup of wheatgerm accurately</h2>
<p>Measuring 0.33 cup of wheatgerm doesn’t require special tools, but a few tips help. First, fluff the wheatgerm with a fork to avoid compacting it. Then, spoon it lightly into your measuring cup and level it off with a straight edge. For even more precision, use a kitchen scale to weigh out 1.5 ounces. This method eliminates guesswork and ensures consistency across batches.</p>

<p>If you're scaling up recipes, remember that 0.33 cup wheatgerm to ounces stays proportional. Doubling the recipe? You’ll need 3 ounces (or 0.66 cups).
