<h2>How to Convert 150g of Baking Soda to Ounces</h2>  
<p>The conversion from grams to ounces is straightforward. One gram equals roughly 0.035 ounces, so multiplying 150g by this factor gives you 5.29 ounces. This is particularly useful for UK cooks who might be following American recipes, which often use ounces instead of grams.
