<h3>The science behind converting grams to ounces</h3>
<p>The conversion from grams to ounces is based on the international avoirdupois ounce, where 1 ounce equals 28.35 grams. Therefore, to convert 250g of chilli sauce to ounces, you divide 250 by 28.35, giving you approximately 8.82 ounces. This precise calculation ensures your air fryer recipes maintain their intended flavour balance and texture.</p>
