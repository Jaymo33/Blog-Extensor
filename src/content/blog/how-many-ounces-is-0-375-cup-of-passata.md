<h3>How to measure passata for air fryer recipes</h3>  
<p>Use a liquid measuring cup for passata, as its consistency is thicker than water but not as dense as paste. Fill the cup to the 0.375 mark, then level it off with a spatula. If you’re using a kitchen scale, 3 ounces is your target. This method works for all <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/vegetables>vegetable-based sauces</a> in your air fryer.</p>  

<h2>Common mistakes when converting cups to ounces</h2>  
<p>Many home cooks assume all liquids weigh the same, but passata’s density means 0.375 cup isn’t equal to 3 fluid ounces—it’s 3 weight ounces. This distinction is crucial for air fryer cooking, where excess moisture can affect texture. Always double-check conversions, especially for ingredients like passata that vary in thickness.</p>
