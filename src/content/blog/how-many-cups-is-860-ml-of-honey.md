<h3>How to Measure 860 ml of Honey Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, use liquid measuring cups for accuracy. Fill the cup to the 3.6-cup mark, or use multiple cups if needed. For thicker honey, warm the container slightly to help it pour smoothly. Avoid packing honey down, as this can lead to over-measuring. A quick trick: coat your spoon or cup with oil to prevent sticking.</p>
