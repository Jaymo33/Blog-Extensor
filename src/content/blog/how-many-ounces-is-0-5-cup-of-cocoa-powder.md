<h2>Why accurate cocoa powder measurements matter</h2>  
<p>Cocoa powder is a finicky ingredient—too much can make your baked goods bitter, while too little can leave them bland. In an air fryer, where cooking times are shorter, precision is even more critical. A slight mismeasurement can lead to dry or undercooked results. That’s why converting 0.5 cup of cocoa powder to ounces (1.5 oz) is so helpful. <h3>How to measure 0.5 cup of cocoa powder correctly</h3>  
<p>To measure 0.5 cup of cocoa powder accurately, fluff the powder first with a spoon to avoid compaction. Then, lightly spoon it into your measuring cup and level it off with a straight edge. Never pack it down, as this can lead to using more cocoa than needed. If you’re converting 0.5 cup of cocoa powder to ounces, remember it’s roughly 1.5 oz.</p>  

<h2>Common cocoa powder conversions for air fryer baking</h2>  
<p>Beyond 0.5 cup, here are other handy cocoa powder conversions for air fryer recipes: 1 cup equals 3 oz (85 grams), while 1 tablespoon is about 0.3 oz (8.5 grams). These measurements are crucial for scaling recipes up or down. For more baking tips, visit <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk>airfryerrecipe.co.uk</a>.</p>
