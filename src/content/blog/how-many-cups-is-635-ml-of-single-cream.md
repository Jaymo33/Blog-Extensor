<h2>Why Accurate Measurements Matter in Air Fryer Cooking</h2>  
<p>Precision is key when using an air fryer, as ingredients cook faster and more evenly than in conventional ovens. Too much or too little single cream can alter the texture and taste of dishes like custards, soups, or cakes. <h3>Tips for Measuring Single Cream in Cups</h3>  
<p>Use a liquid measuring cup for accuracy, filling it to the 2.68-cup mark for 635 ml of single cream. Avoid packing or shaking the cream, as this can introduce air bubbles and affect the measurement. For best results, pour slowly and check at eye level.</p>
