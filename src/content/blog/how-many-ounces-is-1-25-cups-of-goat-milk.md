<h2>How to Measure 1.25 Cups of Goat Milk Correctly</h2>  
<p>To measure 1.25 cups of goat milk, use a liquid measuring cup for the most accurate results. Fill it to the 1-cup mark, then add another quarter cup. If you don’t have a measuring cup, you can use a kitchen scale—10 fluid ounces of goat milk weighs roughly 295 grams.</p>
