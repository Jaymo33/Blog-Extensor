<h3>How to measure honey without the mess</h3>  
<p>Measuring honey can be tricky due to its sticky texture. To get an accurate 1.33 cups (16 oz), lightly grease your measuring cup with oil first—this helps the honey slide out cleanly. Alternatively, use a kitchen scale for precision. If you’re working with a smaller quantity, remember that 1 cup of honey equals roughly 12 ounces.
