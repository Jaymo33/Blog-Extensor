<h2>Why accurate measurements matter in air fryer recipes</h2>  
<p>Baking powder is a leavening agent, and using the correct amount is crucial for achieving the desired texture in your baked goods. <h3>How to measure baking powder correctly</h3>  
<p>To measure 1.66 cups of baking powder accurately, use a dry measuring cup and level it off with a straight edge. Scooping directly from the container can lead to compacted powder, resulting in more than needed. For best results, fluff the baking powder with a spoon before measuring. If you’re using a kitchen scale, 13.28 ounces is the target weight for 1.66 cups.</p>
