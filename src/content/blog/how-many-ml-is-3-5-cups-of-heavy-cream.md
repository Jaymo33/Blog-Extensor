<h2>Understanding the Conversion</h2>  
<p>Converting cups to millilitres can be tricky, especially when dealing with ingredients like heavy cream. One US cup is roughly 236.6 ml, so 3.5 cups of heavy cream equals about 828 ml. If you’re working with UK measurements, note that a UK cup is slightly larger at 284 ml, but most recipes default to US standards.
