<h2>How to measure crushed garlic correctly</h2>
<p>Measuring crushed garlic can be tricky due to its sticky texture. To convert 1.25 cups of crushed garlic in ounces, use a kitchen scale for the most accurate results. If you don’t have a scale, lightly pack the garlic into a measuring cup without compressing it too much. This method ensures you’re close to the 10-ounce mark. For air fryer recipes, consistency is crucial, so always measure ingredients like garlic carefully.</p>
