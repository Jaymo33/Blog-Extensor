<h2>Understanding Cup to ml Conversions</h2>
<p>Converting cups to millilitres can be tricky, especially since UK and US cup measurements differ slightly. In the UK, 1 cup is 250 ml, while in the US, it's 240 ml. For 0.5 cups of vinegar, this means 125 ml in the UK and 120 ml in the US. However, most recipes, including those on <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/>airfryerrecipe.co.uk</a>, standardise to 118 ml for simplicity.</p>
