<p>Air fryers rely on precise measurements to achieve the perfect texture and doneness. Too much water can lead to soggy results, while too little may cause dryness. <h2>UK vs US Cup Measurements for 275 Grams of Water</h2>
<p>The slight difference between UK and US cup measurements matters in baking. A UK cup holds 250ml, while a US cup is 240ml. This means 275 grams of water converts to 1.16 UK cups or 1.15 US cups. For international recipes on <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk>airfryerrecipe.co.uk</a>, always note which measurement system the author uses.</p>

<h3>How to Measure 275 Grams of Water Without Scales</h3>
<p>If you don't have kitchen scales, use the displacement method. Fill a liquid measuring cup to 1 cup, then add water until it reaches 1.15 cups (US) or 1.16 cups (UK) mark. Alternatively, 275 grams equals 275ml, as water's density is 1g/ml. This trick works perfectly for air fryer recipes requiring precise water measurements.</p>
