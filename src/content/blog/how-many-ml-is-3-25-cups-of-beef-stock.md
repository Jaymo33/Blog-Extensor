<h2>How to Measure 3.25 Cups of Beef Stock Correctly</h2>  
<p>To measure 3.25 cups of beef stock accurately, use a liquid measuring cup with clear ml markings. Fill it to the 768.91 ml line for precision. If your measuring cup only shows cups, fill it three times to the one-cup mark, then add a quarter cup. For even better accuracy, consider using a digital kitchen scale set to ml mode.</p>
