<h3>How to Measure 310 ml of Soy Sauce Without a Measuring Cup</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a measuring cup, you can still measure 310 ml of soy sauce accurately. A standard UK metric cup holds 250 ml, so 310 ml is just over 1 and 1/4 cups. Alternatively, use a kitchen scale—310 ml of soy sauce weighs roughly 318 grams. This method is handy for baking or making sauces where precision is key.</p>
