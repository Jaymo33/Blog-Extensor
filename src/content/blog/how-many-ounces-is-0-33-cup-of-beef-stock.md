<h3>How to Measure 0.33 Cup of Beef Stock</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a measuring cup, you can use a kitchen scale to weigh 2.64 oz of beef stock. This method is often more accurate, especially for liquids. Alternatively, 0.33 cup is roughly one-third of a standard cup, which can be eyeballed if you’re in a pinch. For precise results, we recommend using proper tools.</p>
