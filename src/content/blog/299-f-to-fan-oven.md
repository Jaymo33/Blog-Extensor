<h3>How Fan Ovens Differ from Conventional Ovens</h3>

The 299 F to fan oven conversion accounts for this efficiency. Unlike static heat, the moving air prevents cold spots and ensures uniform cooking. This is why air fryer recipes often mirror fan oven settings rather than standard oven temperatures.</p>
