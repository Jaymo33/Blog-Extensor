<h3>US vs. UK Cup Measurements</h3>  
<p>In the US, 1 cup equals 240 ml, while in the UK, it's 250 ml. This means 85 ml of lemon juice converts to 0.36 cups (US) or 0.34 cups (UK). If your air fryer recipe originates from another country, always double-check which measurement system it uses. A slight variance might not ruin a marinade, but it can affect delicate desserts.</p>  

<h2>How to Measure 85 ml of Lemon Juice Without a Scale</h2>  
<p>Don’t have a measuring jug? No problem! A standard tablespoon holds about 15 ml, so 85 ml of lemon juice is roughly 5.5 tablespoons. For smaller quantities, a teaspoon holds 5 ml, meaning you’d need 17 teaspoons. If precision is critical, invest in a kitchen scale or measuring spoons—especially for recipes like <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>air fryer lemon drizzle cake</a>.</p>
