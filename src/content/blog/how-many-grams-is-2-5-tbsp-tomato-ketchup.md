<h3>How to measure 2.5 tbsp of tomato ketchup</h3>
<p>For the most accurate 2.5 tbsp tomato ketchup to grams conversion, use a digital scale. Spoon the ketchup into a measuring tablespoon without packing it down, then level off the top. Repeat until you've measured 2.5 tablespoons, which should weigh about 37.5 grams. This method works perfectly for <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/vegetables>vegetable coatings</a> and glazes.</p>
