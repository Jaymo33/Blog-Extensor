<h3>How to measure 15g of water without a scale</h3>  
<p>Not everyone has a kitchen scale handy, but there are easy ways to measure 15g of water. One tablespoon of water weighs roughly 15 grams, making it a handy substitute. Just ensure you’re using a standard measuring spoon for accuracy. Alternatively, a small shot glass or medicine cup can also help you eyeball the right amount.</p>
