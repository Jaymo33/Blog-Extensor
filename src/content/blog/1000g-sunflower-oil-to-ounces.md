<h3>How to Measure 1000g of Sunflower Oil Accurately</h3>  
<p>Using a digital kitchen scale is the easiest way to measure 1000g of sunflower oil. Set your scale to grams, pour the oil slowly, and stop at the 1000g mark. If your scale only displays ounces, aim for 35.27 ounces instead. For smaller quantities, you can use measuring cups, but a scale ensures greater accuracy.
