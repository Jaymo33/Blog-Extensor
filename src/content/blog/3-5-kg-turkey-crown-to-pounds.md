<h2>Tips for air frying a 3.5 kg turkey crown</h2>
<p>Air frying a 3.5 kg turkey crown requires some preparation to ensure even cooking and crisp skin. Pat the turkey crown dry with kitchen paper before seasoning to help the skin become golden and crispy. Rub with oil, butter, or your favourite herbs, and truss it neatly if needed to maintain a uniform shape. Preheat your air fryer to 180°C for a few minutes before adding the turkey crown. Place it breast-side up in the basket, making sure there's enough space around for air circulation. If your air fryer is small, consider using a rack or rotating the crown halfway through.</p>
<p>Monitor the internal temperature with a meat thermometer inserted into the thickest part of the breast, avoiding the bone. Once it reaches 75°C, remove the turkey crown and let it rest covered with foil for at least 20 minutes. This allows the juices to redistribute, resulting in moist and flavourful meat. After cooking, don't forget to clean your air fryer promptly to maintain its performance.
