<h3>How to measure 0.75 cup of greek yogurt</h3>  
<p>Use a liquid measuring cup for the most accurate results. Scoop the yogurt gently to avoid air pockets, and level it off with a straight edge. If you don’t have a measuring cup, 6 ounces is roughly three-quarters of a standard UK yogurt pot.
