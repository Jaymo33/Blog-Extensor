<h3>How to Measure 150g of Water Accurately</h3>
<p>To measure 150g of water, use a digital kitchen scale for the most accurate results. If your scale displays ounces, you'll see it reads close to 5.29 oz for 150g. For those without scales, a measuring jug with both metric and imperial markings can work, though scales are more precise. Always measure water at room temperature for consistency.</p>

<h2>Tips for Measuring Water in Air Fryer Recipes</h2>
<p>Always use fresh, clean water for the best results in your air fryer dishes. When measuring, place your container on the scale first and tare it to zero before adding water. This ensures you're only measuring the water's weight, not the container. For sticky ingredients, lightly oil the measuring cup to help water slide out easily.</p>
