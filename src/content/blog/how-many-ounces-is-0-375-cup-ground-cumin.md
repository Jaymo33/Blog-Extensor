<h2>How to measure 0.375 cup of ground cumin without a scale</h2>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, measuring 0.375 cup of ground cumin can be tricky but not impossible. First, fluff the cumin in its container to avoid compacting it. Then, lightly spoon it into a measuring cup until it’s slightly heaped, and level it off with a straight edge. This method helps ensure you’re close to the 1.5-ounce mark. For recipes like air-fried falafel or cumin-dusted shrimp, this precision ensures even flavour distribution.</p>
