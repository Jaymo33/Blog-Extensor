<h3>How to Measure Rolled Oats Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t own a kitchen scale, measuring cups are your next best option. Fluff the oats lightly with a fork before scooping to avoid compacting them. Fill the cup gently and level it off with a straight edge for accuracy. Remember, 375 grams of rolled oats is roughly 4.5 cups, but slight variations can occur based on how packed the oats are.</p>  

<h2>Converting Grams to Cups for Different Oats</h2>  
<p>Not all oats are created equal—steel-cut, quick, and rolled oats have different densities. Rolled oats, the most common type, weigh about 80 grams per cup. This means 375 grams of rolled oats equals roughly 4.5 cups.
