<h2>How to Convert Cups to Millilitres</h2>  
<p>Converting 3.25 cups of lime juice to ml is straightforward once you know the ratio. One US cup equals 236.59 ml, so multiplying by 3.25 gives you 768 ml. For UK cups, which are slightly larger at 284 ml, the calculation would differ. Always check your recipe’s origin to avoid discrepancies.</p>
