<h3>How to measure peanut butter correctly</h3>  
<p>Measuring sticky ingredients like peanut butter can be tricky. For best results, use a dry measuring cup and lightly grease it with oil to help the peanut butter slide out easily. Alternatively, weigh it on a kitchen scale for pinpoint accuracy. If you’re converting 2 cups of peanut butter to ounces, a scale simplifies the process. This method is especially useful for air fryer baking, where precision matters.</p>
