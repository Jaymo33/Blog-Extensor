<h3>Tips for Measuring Lemon Juice Correctly</h3>  
<p>To measure 610 ml of lemon juice accurately, use a liquid measuring cup with clear metric markings. Avoid using dry measuring cups, as they won't account for the liquid's meniscus. Freshly squeezed lemon juice may contain pulp, so strain it if a smooth consistency is needed. For consistent results, consider using bottled lemon juice, which offers uniform acidity and flavour.</p>
