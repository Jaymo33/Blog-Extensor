<h3>Tips for Cooking Lentils in an Air Fryer</h3>  
<p>Pre-soaking lentils reduces cooking time and improves texture. For 325 grams of lentils, soak them for at least 30 minutes before air frying. <h2>Common Mistakes When Measuring Lentils</h2>  
<p>Packing lentils too tightly into a cup can lead to inaccurate measurements. Instead, spoon them lightly into the measuring cup and level off the top. For 325 grams of lentils, this ensures you get the right amount for your recipe. Overcrowding the air fryer basket is another common issue—cook in batches if needed for even results.</p>
