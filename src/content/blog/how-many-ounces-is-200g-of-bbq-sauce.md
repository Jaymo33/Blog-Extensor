<h2>How to Measure 200g of BBQ Sauce</h2>
<p>If you don’t have a scale, you can still measure 200g of BBQ sauce using volume. Roughly, 200g is about 12 tablespoons or ¾ cup. However, this can vary slightly depending on the sauce’s thickness. For the most accurate results, we recommend using a kitchen scale set to grams or ounces.</p>

<h2>Tips for Working with BBQ Sauce in the Air Fryer</h2>
<p>BBQ sauce can caramelise quickly in the air fryer, so it’s best to apply it during the last 5-10 minutes of cooking. This prevents burning and ensures a glossy, sticky finish. For dishes like wings or drumsticks, toss them in 200g of BBQ sauce after they’re nearly done, then return them to the air fryer for a final crisp.</p>
