<h2>How to measure 4 tbsp of marmalade accurately</h2>
<p>To measure 4 tbsp of marmalade, use a standard UK tablespoon, which holds about 15ml. Scoop the marmalade without packing it down to avoid excess. For greater precision, a kitchen scale is your best friend—simply tare the scale and measure until it reads 80 grams. This method eliminates guesswork and guarantees consistency.</p>
