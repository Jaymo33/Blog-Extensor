<p>325 grams of olive oil equals approximately 1.4 cups. This conversion is essential for air fryer recipes, ensuring accurate measurements for perfect results every time. <h2>Why Accurate Olive Oil Measurements Matter</h2>  
<p>Using the right amount of olive oil in your air fryer recipes ensures even cooking and optimal texture. Too much oil can make food greasy, while too little may lead to dryness. For example, when making crispy potatoes or roasted vegetables in your air fryer, 325 grams of olive oil (about 1.4 cups) provides the ideal balance.</p>  

<p>Olive oil is a staple in Mediterranean and British cooking, often used in recipes like air-fried chicken or roasted vegetables. <h3>How to Measure 325 Grams of Olive Oil Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a digital scale, use a measuring cup to approximate 1.4 cups for 325 grams of olive oil. Pour slowly to avoid spills, and level off the top with a straight edge.
