<h2>How to Measure 0.25 Cups of Butter in Grams</h2>
<p>Measuring 0.25 cups of butter in grams is straightforward if you have a kitchen scale. Simply scoop the butter into a measuring cup, level it off, and then weigh it. If you don’t have a scale, remember that 0.25 cups of butter equals roughly 57 grams. This measurement is particularly useful for <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/cakes>cake recipes</a> where precision is key.</p>
<p>For those who prefer sticks of butter, 0.25 cups is half a standard US stick (113 grams). In the UK, butter is often sold in 250g blocks, so 0.25 cups would be about a quarter of that. Always check your recipe’s origin to ensure you’re using the correct measurement system.</p>
