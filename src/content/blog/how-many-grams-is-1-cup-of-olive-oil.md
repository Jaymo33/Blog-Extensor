<h3>How to Measure Olive Oil Correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate results, use a kitchen scale to weigh your olive oil. If you don’t have one, a liquid measuring cup will do—just ensure it’s levelled off. Remember, 1.0 cups of olive oil in grams is 224, but slight variations can occur based on temperature and oil density.</p>
