<h2>How to Measure 510 ml of Chicken Broth Without a Measuring Cup</h2>  
<p>If you don’t have a measuring cup, you can still measure 510 ml of chicken broth using household items. A standard UK mug holds about 250 ml, so two mugs plus a tablespoon (15 ml) will get you close. Alternatively, a large US cup is 240 ml, meaning 510 ml is roughly 2 cups and 2 tablespoons.
