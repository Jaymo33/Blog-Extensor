<h2>Understanding Half And Half Measurements</h2>  
<p>Half and half is a dairy product made by combining equal parts whole milk and cream. It’s commonly used in baking, coffee, and sauces, making accurate measurements crucial. When converting 685 ml of half and half to cups, it’s important to note that 1 cup equals 240 ml. This means 685 ml translates to roughly 2.9 cups, which is useful for recipes requiring precise liquid measurements.
