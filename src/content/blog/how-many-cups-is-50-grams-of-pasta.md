<h3>How to Measure 50 Grams of Pasta Without Scales</h3>  
<p>No kitchen scales? No problem! You can estimate 50 grams of pasta using a standard measuring cup. For long pasta like spaghetti, a bundle roughly the diameter of a £1 coin equals about 50 grams. For smaller shapes like macaroni, fill the cup just under halfway for 50 grams. Remember, these are approximations, but they’ll get you close enough for most air fryer recipes.</p>
