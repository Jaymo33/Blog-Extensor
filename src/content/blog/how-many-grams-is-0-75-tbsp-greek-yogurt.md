<h3>How to measure 0.75 tbsp of greek yogurt without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a digital scale, use a standard UK tablespoon (15ml) and fill it halfway for 0.75 tbsp. Greek yogurt is thick, so levelling it off with a knife ensures accuracy.
