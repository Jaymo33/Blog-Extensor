<h2>How to Measure 785 ml of Water Without a Measuring Jug</h2>
<p>If you don’t have a measuring jug, you can still measure 785 ml of water accurately. One standard metric cup holds 250 ml, so 3 cups would give you 750 ml, leaving you just 35 ml short. A tablespoon holds about 15 ml, so adding 2 tablespoons and 1 teaspoon will get you close to 785 ml. This method is handy for quick adjustments in recipes.</p>
