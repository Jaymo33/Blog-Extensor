<h3>How to measure 0.75 tbsp of soy sauce</h3>
<p>Measuring odd fractions like 0.75 tbsp can be tricky without the right tools. Use a tablespoon measure and fill it three-quarters full for accuracy. Alternatively, measure 11.25 grams directly on a digital kitchen scale for perfect precision. For sticky sauces like soy, lightly grease your measuring spoon first to ensure all the liquid slides out cleanly into your mixing bowl or marinade.</p>
