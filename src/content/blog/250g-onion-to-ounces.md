<p>When cooking with an air fryer, ingredient measurements can affect texture and flavour. Onions, in particular, release moisture as they cook, so getting the right amount is crucial. <h3>How to measure 250g of onion accurately</h3>  
<p>Using a digital kitchen scale is the most precise way to measure 250g of onion. Simply peel and roughly chop your onion, then place it on the scale until you hit the 250g mark. If you don’t have a scale, one medium-sized onion typically weighs around 150g, so you’d need about 1.5 onions to reach 250g.</p>
