<p>If you don't have digital scales, you can still measure 0.25 tbsp (3.75g) of hot sauce accurately. Use a standard tablespoon measure and fill it to the quarter mark. For even more precision, consider using measuring spoons that include 1/4 tablespoon measurements. This method works well for most <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/chicken>chicken recipes</a> where you want controlled heat distribution.</p>
