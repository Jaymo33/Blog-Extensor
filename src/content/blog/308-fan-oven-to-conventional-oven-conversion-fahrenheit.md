<h2>Why Convert 308 Fan Oven to Fahrenheit?</h2>
<p>Fan ovens circulate hot air, reducing cooking times and temperatures compared to conventional ovens. Increasing the temperature to 338°F compensates for the lack of air circulation. This is especially important for delicate dishes like <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/cakes>cakes</a>, where precise heat matters.</p>
