<h3>How to measure evaporated cream correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate results, use a kitchen scale to weigh your evaporated cream. If you don’t have one, spoon the cream into a measuring cup and level it off with a knife. Avoid packing it down, as this can lead to over-measuring.
