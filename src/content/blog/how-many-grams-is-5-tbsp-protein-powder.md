<h2>How to measure 5 tbsp protein powder accurately</h2>
<p>The most reliable way to convert 5 tbsp of protein powder into grams is by using digital kitchen scales. Scoop the powder lightly into your tablespoon without packing it down, then level off the top. Repeat this five times and weigh the total amount for the most accurate measurement. Different protein powders have varying densities, so whey protein might weigh slightly less than casein per tablespoon.
