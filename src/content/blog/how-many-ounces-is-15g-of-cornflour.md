<h3>How to Measure 15g of Cornflour Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a scale, you can use measuring spoons to approximate 15g of cornflour. One tablespoon of cornflour is roughly 10g, so 1.5 tablespoons will give you close to 15g. However, for best results, especially in baking, a scale is recommended.</p>

<h2>Tips for Storing Cornflour</h2>  
<p>To keep cornflour fresh, store it in an airtight container in a cool, dry place. Moisture can cause clumping, which affects measurement accuracy. If your cornflour has hardened, sift it before use to ensure smooth incorporation into your air fryer recipes.</p>
