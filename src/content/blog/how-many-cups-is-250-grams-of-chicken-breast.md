<h3>How Preparation Affects Cup Measurements</h3>

Whole chicken breasts, however, aren’t practical to measure in cups unless cooked and chopped. For consistent results in your air fryer, stick to one preparation style when converting 250 grams of chicken breast to cups.</p>

<p>Packing chicken tightly into a cup can skew measurements—250 grams should sit loosely in the cup for accuracy. Also, raw versus cooked chicken weighs differently; always measure raw unless specified. For 250 grams of chicken breast in cups, fluff the pieces lightly to avoid overestimating.</p>
