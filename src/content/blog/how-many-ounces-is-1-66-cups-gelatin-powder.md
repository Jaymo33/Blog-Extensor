<h2>Why accurate gelatin measurements matter</h2>  
<p>Using the correct amount of gelatin powder ensures your recipes set properly. Too little, and your dessert won’t firm up; too much, and it becomes rubbery. Air fryer recipes often rely on precise measurements to achieve the perfect texture. Gelatin is particularly finicky, so converting 1.66 cups to ounces (13.28 oz) guarantees consistency. <h3>How to measure gelatin powder correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate results, use a kitchen scale to weigh gelatin powder in ounces. Scooping directly from the bag can lead to uneven measurements. Fluff the powder first, then spoon it into a measuring cup before levelling it off. This method ensures 1.66 cups of gelatin powder translates to 13.28 ounces.
