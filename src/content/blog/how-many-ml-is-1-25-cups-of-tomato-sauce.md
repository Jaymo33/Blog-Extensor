<h3>How to Measure 1.25 Cups of Tomato Sauce Correctly</h3>  
<p>For best results, use a liquid measuring cup to measure 1.25 cups of tomato sauce. Fill it to the 1-cup mark, then add another quarter cup. Alternatively, use a kitchen scale set to millilitres for precision. If you’re scaling a recipe, remember that 1.25 cups of tomato sauce in ml is 295.735. This method works for both UK and US measurements, so you’ll never go wrong.</p>
