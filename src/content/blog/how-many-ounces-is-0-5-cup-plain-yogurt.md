<h3>How to measure 0.5 cup of plain yogurt accurately</h3>  
<p>For the most precise measurement, use a kitchen scale to weigh 4.25 ounces (120 grams) of plain yogurt. If you don’t have a scale, spoon the yogurt into a measuring cup and level it off. Avoid packing it down, as this can lead to excess.
