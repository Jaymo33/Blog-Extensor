<h3>How to measure 40g of minced onion without a scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can estimate 40g of minced onion using measuring spoons or cups. One tablespoon of minced onion weighs around 10g, so four tablespoons will give you roughly 40g. Alternatively, a packed 1/4 cup of minced onion is close to 40g. Remember, these are approximations, and for the best results, a scale is ideal.
