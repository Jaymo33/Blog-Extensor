<h3>How to measure 0.125 cup of olive oil</h3>  
<p>Measuring 0.125 cup of olive oil is simple with the right tools. Use a liquid measuring cup with clear markings for small amounts. Alternatively, a tablespoon measure can help—0.125 cup equals 2 tablespoons (1 oz). For sticky ingredients like olive oil, lightly grease the measuring spoon for easy pouring. Precision matters, especially in air fryer cooking where oil amounts affect texture and browning.</p>

<h3>Converting other olive oil amounts</h3>  
<p>If you need to scale up or down, remember that 1 cup of olive oil equals 8 oz. So, 0.25 cup would be 2 oz, and 0.5 cup would be 4 oz. These conversions are handy for batch cooking or meal prep in your air fryer. Always measure liquids at eye level for accuracy, especially with small quantities like 0.125 cup (1 oz).</p>
