<h2>Why Convert 405 Fan Oven to Conventional Oven?</h2>
<p>Fan ovens circulate hot air, cooking food faster and more evenly than conventional ovens. This means recipes often require lower temperatures or shorter cooking times. If you're using a conventional oven, converting 405 fan oven to Fahrenheit ensures your dish doesn’t overcook or burn. This is especially useful for baking delicate items like cakes or cookies. <h2>How to Adjust Cooking Times</h2>
<p>When converting 405 fan oven to Fahrenheit, remember that conventional ovens may need slightly longer cooking times. Start by reducing the temperature by 20-25°F, as we’ve done here (405°F to 375°F), and monitor your dish closely. For example, a cake that takes 25 minutes in a fan oven might need 30 minutes in a conventional oven. Always use a skewer or thermometer to check for doneness.</p>
