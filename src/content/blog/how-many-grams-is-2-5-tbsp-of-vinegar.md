<h2>How to measure 2.5 tbsp of vinegar accurately</h2>
<p>For the most precise measurement, use a digital kitchen scale to weigh your vinegar. Simply set your measuring spoon over the scale, tare it to zero, then pour the vinegar until you reach 37.5 grams. If you don't have scales, use standard UK tablespoons (15ml each) and fill them level for consistent results. Remember that different vinegar types (like balsamic or apple cider) have slightly different densities, but 2.5 tbsp will generally weigh around 37.5 grams.</p>
