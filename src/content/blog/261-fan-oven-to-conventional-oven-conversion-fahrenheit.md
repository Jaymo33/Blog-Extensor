<p>261°F in a fan oven converts to approximately 240°C in a conventional oven. This conversion is essential when adapting recipes designed for fan-assisted ovens to standard ovens or air fryers. The difference occurs because fan ovens circulate hot air more efficiently, requiring lower temperatures for equivalent cooking results.</p>

<h2>How to Convert 261 Fan to Conventional Oven</h2>
<p>The general rule for converting 261 fan oven to fahrenheit in a conventional oven is to increase the temperature by 20-25°F. This compensates for the reduced air circulation in standard ovens. For baking perfect <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/cakes>cakes</a>, this adjustment ensures even rising and proper browning.</p>

<h3>Special Considerations for Different Foods</h3>
<p>Delicate items like soufflés or custards require precise temperature control when converting 261 fan oven to conventional oven settings. The increased heat in conventional ovens can cause these dishes to set too quickly or develop a skin. For perfect results, consider lowering the rack position and using a water bath.</p>
