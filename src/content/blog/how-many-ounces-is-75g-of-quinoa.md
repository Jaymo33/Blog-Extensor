<h3>How to Measure 75g of Quinoa Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can still measure 75g of quinoa using volume. One tablespoon of quinoa weighs about 10g, so 7.5 tablespoons equal 75g. Alternatively, 75g of quinoa is roughly 1/3 cup. While this method isn’t as precise as weighing, it’s handy in a pinch.
