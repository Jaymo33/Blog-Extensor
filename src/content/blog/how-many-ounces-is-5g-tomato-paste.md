<h3>How to measure 5g of tomato paste without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don't have a digital scale, you can estimate 5g of tomato paste using teaspoons. One level teaspoon holds roughly 5g of tomato paste, which is about 0.18 ounces. This method works well for small quantities in air fryer recipes.</p>
