<h2>How to measure 200g of whipping cream without scales</h2>  
<p>If you don't have digital scales, you can still measure 200g of whipping cream using volume measurements. Approximately 200g of whipping cream equals 200ml, as cream has a similar density to water. Use a liquid measuring jug to get close to the 200ml mark, which will give you roughly 7.05 ounces of cream.</p>  
<p>Remember that whipped cream expands in volume, so these measurements apply to liquid whipping cream before whipping. For best results in your air fryer recipes, always measure the cream before whipping.
