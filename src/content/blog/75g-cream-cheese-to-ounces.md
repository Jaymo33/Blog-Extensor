<h2>How to Measure 75g of Cream Cheese</h2>  
<p>If you don’t have a scale, you can still measure 75g of cream cheese (2.65 oz) using tablespoons. One tablespoon of cream cheese weighs roughly 15g, so five tablespoons will give you 75g. This method is handy for quick recipes or when you’re in a pinch. For air fryer dishes like stuffed chicken or desserts, precision ensures even cooking and perfect results.</p>
