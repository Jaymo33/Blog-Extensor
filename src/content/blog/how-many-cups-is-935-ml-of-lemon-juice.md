<h2>Understanding the Conversion</h2>

<h3>UK vs US Cup Measurements</h3>
<p>It's worth noting that UK and US cup measurements differ slightly. In the UK, 1 cup is 250 ml, while in the US, it's 236.588 ml. For 935 ml of lemon juice, this means 3.74 cups in the UK and 3.95 cups in the US. Always check which standard your recipe uses to avoid discrepancies.</p>
