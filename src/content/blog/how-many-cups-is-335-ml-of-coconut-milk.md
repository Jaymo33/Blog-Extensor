<h3>How to Measure 335 ml of Coconut Milk Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a measuring jug, you can use standard cups to measure 335 ml of coconut milk. A UK metric cup holds 250 ml, so 335 ml is roughly 1 and 1/3 cups plus a tablespoon. For US cups (240 ml), it’s about 1.42 cups. Shake the coconut milk well before measuring, as it separates in the can. Stirring ensures an even consistency for accurate results.</p>
