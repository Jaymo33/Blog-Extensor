<h3>UK vs. US Cup Measurements</h3>  
<p>In the UK, 1 cup equals 284 ml, while in the US, it's 237 ml. This means 835 ml of milk converts to approximately 3.5 cups in the UK and 3.52 cups in the US. The difference might seem small, but for recipes requiring exact proportions, it’s worth noting. If you're following an American recipe, adjust accordingly to avoid discrepancies.</p>
