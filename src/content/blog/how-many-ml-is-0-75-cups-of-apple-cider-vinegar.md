<h2>How to Measure 0.75 Cups of Apple Cider Vinegar</h2>  
<p>For the most accurate results, use a liquid measuring cup with clear ml markings. Fill it to the 0.75 cup line, or measure 177 ml directly. Avoid using dry measuring cups, as they won’t account for the liquid’s meniscus (the slight curve at the top). If you don’t have a measuring cup, 12 tablespoons also equal 0.75 cups (177 ml).</p>

<h2>Converting 0.75 Cups of Apple Cider Vinegar in UK and US Measurements</h2>  
<p>In the UK, 1 cup equals 284 ml, but most modern recipes use the US standard (240 ml per cup). For 0.75 cups, this means 177 ml in US measurements or 213 ml in UK measurements. Always check which standard your recipe follows—our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>recipe collection</a> uses US cups for consistency.</p>  
<p>If you’re adapting a British recipe, adjust the ml accordingly. For instance, if a UK recipe calls for 0.75 cups (213 ml), reduce it to 177 ml for US measurements to avoid an overly acidic result.</p>

<h3>Tips for Storing Apple Cider Vinegar</h3>
