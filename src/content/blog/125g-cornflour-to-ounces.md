<p>Cornflour is a staple in air fryer cooking, often used for crispy coatings or as a thickening agent. Using the wrong amount can lead to soggy textures or overly dense bakes. Converting 125g of cornflour to ounces (4.41 oz) ensures your recipes turn out as intended. <h2>Common uses for 125g of cornflour in air fryers</h2>  
<p>From crispy coatings to gluten-free baking, 125g of cornflour (4.41 oz) is a versatile ingredient. It’s perfect for air-fried chicken, fish, or even onion rings. The light texture ensures a golden crunch without excess oil, making it a healthier alternative to deep frying.</p>  

<p>Cornflour also works wonders in air fryer desserts like sponges or shortbread. Its fine consistency helps create a tender crumb. For inspiration, try our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/cakes>air fryer cake recipes</a> that use this exact measurement.</p>

<p>Keep your cornflour in an airtight container to prevent clumping. Humidity can affect its texture, so store it in a cool, dry place. Proper storage ensures your 125g (4.41 oz) measurements remain accurate for future recipes.</p>
