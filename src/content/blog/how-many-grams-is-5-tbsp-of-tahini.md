<h3>How to measure tahini properly</h3>  
<p>To measure tahini accurately, use a flat-edged utensil to level off the tablespoon. Stirring the tahini first ensures a consistent texture, as it tends to separate. For sticky ingredients like tahini, lightly greasing the spoon can help it slide off easily. This method ensures you get exactly 15 grams per tablespoon, totalling 75 grams for 5 tbsp.</p>
