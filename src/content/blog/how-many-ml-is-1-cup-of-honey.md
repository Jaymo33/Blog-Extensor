<h2>Converting 1.0 Cups of Honey to ml</h2>  
<p>The standard conversion for 1.0 cups of honey is 340ml, but this can vary slightly based on honey type and temperature. Raw honey is thicker than processed honey, so it may measure slightly less in ml for the same cup volume. For air fryer cooking, we recommend using liquid measuring cups or a kitchen scale for the most accurate results. If you’re working with a sticky ingredient like honey, lightly greasing your measuring cup can help.</p>
