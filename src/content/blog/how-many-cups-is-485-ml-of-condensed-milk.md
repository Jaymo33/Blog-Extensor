<h3>How to Measure 485 ml of Condensed Milk</h3>  
<p>To measure 485 ml of condensed milk accurately, use a liquid measuring cup with clear markings. Pour slowly to avoid air bubbles, and level off the top with a spatula for precision. If you don't have a measuring cup, 485 ml is roughly equivalent to 2 standard US cups or 1.7 UK cups. For sticky ingredients like this, lightly greasing the measuring cup can help it slide out more easily.</p>
