<h3>How to Measure 475 Grams of Pasta Without Scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can still measure 475 grams of pasta using cups. A standard UK cup holds about 100 grams of dried pasta, so 4.75 cups will give you roughly 475 grams. For smaller shapes like macaroni, fill the cup lightly to avoid packing too tightly. Larger shapes, like fusilli, may require slightly more cups to reach 475 grams. Always fluff the pasta in the cup for an accurate measurement.</p>
