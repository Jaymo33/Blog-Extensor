<h2>Why Convert 500g of Hot Sauce to Ounces?</h2>  
<p>Many UK recipes, especially those for air fryers, use metric measurements like grams. However, some kitchen scales or international recipes might list ingredients in ounces. <h2>How to Measure 500g of Hot Sauce Accurately</h2>  
<p>Using a digital kitchen scale is the easiest way to measure 500g of hot sauce. Set your scale to grams, tare the container, and pour the sauce until it hits 500g. If your scale only displays ounces, aim for 17.64 oz. For those without scales, a standard UK measuring jug can help, though it's less precise.</p>

<h2>Common Mistakes When Converting Grams to Ounces</h2>  
<p>One frequent error is confusing fluid ounces with weight ounces. Hot sauce is measured by weight (ounces), not volume (fluid ounces). Another pitfall is rounding too early—17.64 oz is more precise than 18 oz, which could throw off your recipe. Always use exact conversions for the best results.</p>
