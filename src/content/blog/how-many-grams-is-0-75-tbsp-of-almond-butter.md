<h3>How to measure 0.75 tbsp almond butter accurately</h3>
<p>The easiest way is to use digital kitchen scales set to grams. Simply scoop your almond butter until the scale reads 11.25g. If you don't have scales, fill a tablespoon three-quarters full - though this method is less precise. For sticky ingredients like almond butter, lightly greasing your measuring spoon can help it slide out cleanly.</p>
