<h2>Understanding Fan Oven Conversions</h2>
<p>Fan ovens circulate hot air for even cooking, requiring lower temperatures than conventional ovens. When converting 465 F to fan oven settings, you're essentially adjusting for this efficient heat distribution. Many air fryers operate similarly to fan ovens, making this conversion particularly useful for <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>air fryer recipes</a>.</p>
<p>The general rule for converting Fahrenheit to Celsius in fan ovens is to subtract 32, multiply by 5/9, then reduce by 20°C for the fan setting. For 465 F, this calculation gives us (465-32)×5/9=240.5°C, which we round to 240°C for practical cooking purposes.</p>
