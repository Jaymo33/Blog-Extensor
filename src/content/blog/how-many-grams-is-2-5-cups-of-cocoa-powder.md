<h3>UK vs. US Cup Measurements</h3>  
<p>While 2.5 cups of cocoa powder in grams is roughly 250 grams in both the UK and US, slight variations exist due to cup sizing. The UK uses a metric cup (250ml), whereas the US uses a customary cup (240ml). For most baking purposes, the difference is negligible, but precision matters in delicate recipes. If you're adapting a US recipe for your air fryer, sticking to weight measurements avoids confusion. <h2>How to Measure Cocoa Powder Without a Scale</h2>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, spoon the cocoa powder into your measuring cup and level it off with a knife. Avoid packing it down, as this can lead to over-measuring. For 2.5 cups of cocoa powder, lightly fill each cup and sweep off the excess. This method gets you close to 250 grams, though a scale is still best. Air fryer recipes often require exact ratios, so investing in a scale pays off.
