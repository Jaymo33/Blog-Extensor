<h3>How to Measure 185 ml of Balsamic Vinegar Without a Measuring Cup</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a measuring cup, you can use a kitchen scale for precision. Since balsamic vinegar has a similar density to water, 185 ml weighs about 185 grams. Alternatively, a tablespoon holds around 15 ml, so 185 ml is roughly 12 tablespoons plus a teaspoon. This method works well for smaller quantities in air fryer recipes.</p>

<h3>Common Mistakes When Measuring Balsamic Vinegar</h3>  
<p>One mistake is assuming all liquids measure the same. Balsamic vinegar’s viscosity means it pours slower than water, so eye-balling can lead to inaccuracies. Always use a clear measuring cup for 185 ml of balsamic vinegar to ensure you hit the 0.78-cup mark. This precision guarantees your air fryer dishes turn out as intended.</p>
