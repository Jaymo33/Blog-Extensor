<h3>How to measure flaxseed correctly</h3>  
<p>Use a dry measuring cup for flaxseed, levelling it off with a knife for accuracy. Scooping directly from the bag can compress the seeds, leading to uneven amounts. If you don’t have a scale, remember that 0.5 cup of flaxseed is roughly 2.5 oz. This tip is especially useful for air fryer recipes where consistency is key.</p>

<h3>Storing flaxseed for freshness</h3>  
<p>Keep flaxseed in an airtight container in the fridge to prevent rancidity. Whole seeds last longer than ground flaxseed, which can lose potency. If your 0.5 cup (2.5 oz) measurement tastes bitter, it’s time to refresh your stash. Proper storage ensures your air fryer recipes always turn out delicious.</p>
