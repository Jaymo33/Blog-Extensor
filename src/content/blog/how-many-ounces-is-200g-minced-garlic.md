<h3>How to Measure Minced Garlic Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>No kitchen scale? No problem. One tablespoon of minced garlic weighs roughly 10g, so 200g would be about 20 tablespoons. However, this method isn’t as precise as weighing, so for best results, invest in a digital scale.
