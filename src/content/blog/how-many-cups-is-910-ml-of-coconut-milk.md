<h2>Converting 910 ml of Coconut Milk to Cups</h2>  
<p>To convert 910 ml of coconut milk to cups, divide the millilitres by 236.59 (the number of ml in a standard US cup). This gives you roughly 3.85 cups. If you're using a UK cup, which holds 284 ml, the conversion would be about 3.2 cups. Always check which cup measurement your recipe uses to avoid mistakes.</p>

<h3>Tips for Measuring Coconut Milk</h3>  
<p>Use a liquid measuring cup for the most accurate results. Pour the coconut milk slowly to avoid spills and check the measurement at eye level. If your recipe calls for a partial cup, like 3.85 cups, you can eyeball it or use tablespoons for precision. One tablespoon is roughly 15 ml, so 910 ml would be about 61 tablespoons.</p>
