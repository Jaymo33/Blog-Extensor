<h2>How we calculated 0.75 tbsp chopped onion in grams</h2>
<p>Our conversion uses standard UK tablespoon measurements where 1 tbsp equals about 7.5 grams of chopped onion. Therefore, 0.75 tbsp chopped onion weight in grams is calculated by multiplying 7.5 by 0.75, giving 5.625 grams (rounded to 5.6g).</p>
<p>This matches the typical density of freshly chopped yellow or white onions. Red onions might be slightly heavier due to their tighter cell structure.
