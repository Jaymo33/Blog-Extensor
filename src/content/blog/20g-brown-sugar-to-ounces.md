<h2>How to Convert 20g of Brown Sugar to Ounces</h2>  
<p>Converting grams to ounces is straightforward: 1 gram equals 0.035 ounces. So, 20g multiplied by 0.035 gives you 0.71 ounces. This conversion is especially useful for UK bakers following American recipes, which often use ounces or cups. Keep our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-conversion-chart>conversion chart</a> bookmarked for quick reference.</p>  

<h3>Using a Digital Scale for Precision</h3>  
<p>A digital scale is the best tool for measuring 20g of brown sugar accurately. Simply place your bowl on the scale, tare it to zero, and add the sugar until it hits 20g. This method eliminates guesswork and ensures consistent results in your air fryer bakes.</p>
