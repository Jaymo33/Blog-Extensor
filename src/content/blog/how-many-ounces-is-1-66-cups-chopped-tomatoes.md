<h3>How to Measure Chopped Tomatoes Correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate conversion, lightly pack the chopped tomatoes into a measuring cup without crushing them. A standard US cup holds about 8 ounces of chopped tomatoes, so 1.66 cups would be roughly 13.28 ounces. If you're in the UK, fluid ounces and weight ounces differ slightly, so double-check your measurements.</p>
