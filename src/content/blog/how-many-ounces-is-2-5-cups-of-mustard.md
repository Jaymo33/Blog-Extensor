<h2>How to measure mustard correctly</h2>
<p>To convert 2.5 cups of mustard to oz, use a liquid measuring cup for runny mustard or a dry measuring cup for thicker types. Level off the top with a straight edge for accuracy. If your recipe calls for 20 oz of mustard, you'll know it's equivalent to 2.5 cups. This method works for all mustard varieties, from Dijon to whole grain.</p>
