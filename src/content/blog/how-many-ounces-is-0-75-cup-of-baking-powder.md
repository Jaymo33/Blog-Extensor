<h3>How to Measure 0.75 Cup of Baking Powder Correctly</h3>  
<p>To measure 0.75 cup of baking powder accurately, use the spoon-and-level method. Scoop the powder into your measuring cup without packing it down, then level it off with a straight edge. This prevents over-measuring, which is common when dipping the cup directly into the container. For best results, weigh it out—6 ounces is your target.</p>
