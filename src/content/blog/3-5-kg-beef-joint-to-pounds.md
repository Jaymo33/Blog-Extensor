<h2>How to convert 3.5 kg beef joint to pounds manually</h2>
<p>The conversion from kilograms to pounds is straightforward once you know the basic formula. One kilogram is approximately equal to 2.20462 pounds, so to find what is 3.5 kg beef joint in pounds, you multiply 3.5 by 2.20462. This calculation gives you 7.71617 pounds, which is typically rounded to 7.72 pounds for practical use. Keeping a calculator or conversion app handy can make this process even easier. <h2>Cooking a 3.5 kg beef joint in your air fryer</h2>
<p>Cooking a 3.5 kg beef joint in an air fryer requires some adjustments compared to traditional oven roasting. First, ensure your air fryer basket is large enough to accommodate the joint comfortably; not all models can handle a cut this size. Preheat your air fryer to 190°C for best results, which helps sear the outside and lock in juices. Calculating the cooking time based on the weight is essential—approximately 20 minutes per pound plus an extra 20 minutes is a good rule of thumb.
