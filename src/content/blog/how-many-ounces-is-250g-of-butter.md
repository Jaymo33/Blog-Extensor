<h3>How to measure 250g of butter without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can still measure 250g of butter using volume. One standard UK block of butter is 250g, which is roughly equivalent to 8.82 ounces. Alternatively, 250g of butter is about 1 cup plus 2 tablespoons. This method works well for air fryer recipes like garlic butter shrimp or buttery roasted potatoes.</p>
