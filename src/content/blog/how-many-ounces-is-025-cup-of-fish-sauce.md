<h3>How to Measure 0.25 Cup of Fish Sauce Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, use a liquid measuring cup for 0.25 cup of fish sauce. Fill it to the quarter-cup mark, ensuring the meniscus (the curve at the liquid’s surface) aligns with the measurement line. Alternatively, 4 tablespoons equal 0.25 cup, so you can use a tablespoon measure for smaller batches. This method is handy when preparing sauces or dressings for air-fried dishes like <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/chicken>air fryer chicken recipes</a>.</p>
