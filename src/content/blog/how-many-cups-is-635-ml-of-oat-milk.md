<h2>Understanding the Conversion from ml to Cups</h2>
<p>Converting millilitres to cups can be tricky, especially when dealing with liquids like oat milk. The standard conversion is 1 cup equals 236.588 ml, which means 635 ml of oat milk is roughly 2.68 cups. This measurement is crucial for recipes where precision matters, such as baking or air frying. <p>Different countries use different cup sizes, which can add to the confusion. In the UK, a metric cup is 250 ml, while in the US, it's 240 ml. For 635 ml of oat milk, this means approximately 2.54 cups in the UK and 2.65 cups in the US. Always double-check your recipe's origin to ensure accuracy.
