<h2>Converting 160 ml of Honey to Cups</h2>  
<p>In the US, 1 cup equals 240 ml, so 160 ml of honey is roughly 0.68 cups. In the UK, 1 cup is 284 ml, making 160 ml about 0.56 cups. This difference might seem small, but it can affect recipes like air fryer desserts or marinades. Always measure honey carefully for consistent results.</p>  
<p>For sticky ingredients like honey, use a liquid measuring cup or weigh it for precision. If you're working with <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/dessert>dessert recipes</a>, even slight measurement errors can alter texture. A digital scale is ideal, but if using cups, spoon the honey in and level it off.</p>
