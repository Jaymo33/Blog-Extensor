<h2>Understanding the Conversion</h2>  
<p>Converting millilitres to cups can be tricky, especially with ingredients like double cream, which has a thick consistency. The standard conversion for liquids is 1 cup = 236.588 ml, but slight variations may occur based on density. For 985 ml of double cream, the calculation gives approximately 4.16 cups. This precision is crucial for recipes like custards or air-fried cheesecakes, where texture matters.</p>
