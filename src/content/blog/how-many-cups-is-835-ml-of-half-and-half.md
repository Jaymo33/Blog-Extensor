<h2>How to Measure 835 ml of Half And Half Without a Scale</h2>  
<p>If you don’t have a measuring jug with millilitre markings, you can still measure 835 ml of half and half using standard cups. A US cup holds about 240 ml, so dividing 835 ml by 240 gives you roughly 3.5 cups. For UK cooks, a metric cup is 250 ml, making the conversion slightly different at around 3.34 cups.</p>
