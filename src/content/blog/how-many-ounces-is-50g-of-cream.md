<h3>How to Measure 50g of Cream Accurately</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate results, use a digital kitchen scale set to grams. Scoop your cream into a measuring cup or spoon until it reaches 50g, then convert to ounces (1.76 oz). If you don’t have a scale, remember that 50g of cream is roughly 3.5 tablespoons, but weighing is always more reliable. This method ensures your air fryer dishes turn out perfectly every time.</p>
