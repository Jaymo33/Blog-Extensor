<p>The conversion from kilograms to pounds is straightforward once you know the basic formula: 1 kilogram equals approximately 2.20462 pounds. For most culinary purposes, rounding this to 27.56 pounds is perfectly acceptable and makes mental maths much easier. If you prefer a quicker method, you can remember that 1 kg is roughly 2.2 pounds, so 12.5 kg would be about 27.5 pounds, which is close enough for ingredient weighing.
