<p>25g of quinoa equals approximately 0.88 ounces. This conversion is handy for air fryer recipes, where precise measurements can make all the difference. <h2>Why Convert 25g of Quinoa to Ounces?</h2>
<p>Many air fryer recipes, especially those from the UK, use grams as the standard measurement. However, some kitchen scales or recipe books might list ingredients in ounces. Converting 25g of quinoa to ounces (0.88 oz) helps you follow recipes accurately. This is particularly useful if you're using an American recipe or a scale that only measures in ounces.</p>

<h2>How to Measure 25g of Quinoa</h2>
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can use measuring spoons or cups to estimate 25g of quinoa. One tablespoon of quinoa weighs roughly 10g, so 2.5 tablespoons will give you close to 25g. For ounces, this translates to just under 0.88 oz. Remember, these are approximations, so a scale is always best for accuracy.</p>
