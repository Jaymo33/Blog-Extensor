<h2>Why accurate measurements matter in air fryer cooking</h2>

That’s why converting 125g of garlic paste to ounces (4.41 oz) ensures consistency in every recipe.</p>

<h3>How to measure 125g of garlic paste without a scale</h3>  
<p>Don’t have a kitchen scale? No problem. You can estimate 125g of garlic paste using tablespoons. One tablespoon of garlic paste weighs roughly 15g, so 125g equals about 8.3 tablespoons. For most recipes, rounding to 8 tablespoons works fine. Just remember, slight variations won’t ruin your dish, but precision ensures the best results.</p>

<h3>Storing garlic paste for future use</h3>  
<p>If you’ve converted 125g of garlic paste to ounces but don’t need it all, proper storage is key. Transfer unused paste to an airtight container and refrigerate for up to two weeks. For longer storage, freeze portions in ice cube trays—each cube holds about 15g (0.53 oz), making it easy to measure for future recipes. This trick saves time and reduces waste.</p>
