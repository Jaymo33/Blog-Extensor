<h2>Converting 0.25 cup semi skimmed milk to ounces</h2>
<p>The standard conversion for 0.25 cup of semi skimmed milk to ounces is straightforward: 1 UK cup equals 8.45 fluid ounces, making 0.25 cup equal to 2.11 fluid ounces. However, for weight measurements in air fryer cooking, we typically use 2 ounces as the practical equivalent. This slight rounding makes measuring easier without sacrificing recipe accuracy.</p>
