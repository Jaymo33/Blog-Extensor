<h3>How to measure 10g of mayonnaise without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, use a measuring spoon to approximate 10g of mayonnaise. A level tablespoon holds about 15g, so two-thirds of a tablespoon will give you roughly 10g (or 0.35 oz). For thicker mayo, compact it slightly for accuracy. This method works well for recipes like <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>air fryer sauces</a>, where small adjustments matter.</p>
