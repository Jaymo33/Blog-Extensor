<h3>UK vs. US Cup Measurements for Almond Milk</h3>
<p>It's worth noting that UK and US cup measurements differ slightly. In the UK, 1 cup equals 284 ml, while in the US, it's 240 ml. This means 910 ml of almond milk is 3.85 cups in the UK and 3.84 cups in the US. For most recipes, this small difference won't matter, but for precision baking, it's good to be aware. If your air fryer recipe originates from another country, double-check which cup measurement it uses to avoid mishaps.</p>
