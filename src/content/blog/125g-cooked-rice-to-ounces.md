<p>To measure 125g of cooked rice, use a digital kitchen scale for accuracy. Scoop the rice into a bowl on the scale until it hits 125g. If you don't have a scale, 125g is roughly half a UK cup of cooked rice. For air fryer cooking, evenly spreading the rice ensures it reheats or crisps properly. <h3>Tips for Cooking Rice in an Air Fryer</h3>  
<p>If you're cooking rice from scratch in the air fryer, rinse it first to remove excess starch. Use a heatproof dish or liner to prevent sticking. For 125g of uncooked rice, add 250ml of water and cook at 180°C for 12-15 minutes. Fluff with a fork before serving. This method works great for meal prep or quick sides.</p>
