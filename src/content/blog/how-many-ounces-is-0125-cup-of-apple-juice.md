<h2>How to measure 0.125 cup of apple juice</h2>  
<p>If you don’t have a 0.125 cup measure, use a tablespoon for accuracy. Since 0.125 cup equals 2 tablespoons, it’s an easy way to measure 1 oz of apple juice. For UK cooks, note that 1 UK fluid ounce is roughly the same as 1 US fluid ounce, so the conversion remains consistent.</p>  
<p>Liquid measuring cups with clear markings are ideal for checking 0.125 cup measurements. Alternatively, a kitchen scale set to ounces can verify the weight of 0.125 cup of apple juice.
