<h3>Converting cups to ounces for mustard</h3>  
<p>Converting 0.375 cup of mustard to ounces is straightforward once you know the density. Mustard weighs roughly 8 ounces per cup, so 0.375 cup equals 3 ounces. This conversion applies to most prepared mustards, including Dijon and yellow varieties. For specialty mustards with added ingredients, slight variations may occur. Always double-check if precision is key for your air fryer dish.</p>
