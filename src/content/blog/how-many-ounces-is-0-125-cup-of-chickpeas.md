<h3>How to measure 0.125 cup of chickpeas</h3>  
<p>Measuring 0.125 cup of chickpeas is simple with the right tools. Use a standard measuring cup and fill it to the 1/8th mark for accuracy. If you don’t have a measuring cup, a kitchen scale can confirm the weight is around 1 ounce. For best results, rinse and drain canned chickpeas before measuring to remove excess liquid. This ensures your air fryer chickpeas turn out crispy and delicious.</p>

<h3>Common mistakes when measuring chickpeas</h3>  
<p>One common mistake is not accounting for the chickpeas' density, which can affect the weight. Packed or loosely filled cups will yield different ounce measurements, so aim for consistency.
