<h3>How to Measure 125g of Chilli Sauce Accurately</h3>  
<p>Using a digital kitchen scale is the easiest way to measure 125g of chilli sauce. Simply place your container on the scale, tare it to zero, and pour until you hit 125g. If you don’t have a scale, 4.41 ounces is roughly ¼ cup plus 1 tablespoon. This method works well for most air fryer sauces and marinades.
