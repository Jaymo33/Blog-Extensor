<h2>How to Measure 60 ml of Balsamic Vinegar Without a Measuring Cup</h2>
<p>If you don’t have a measuring cup handy, you can still measure 60 ml of balsamic vinegar using everyday kitchen tools. A standard tablespoon holds about 15 ml, so four tablespoons equal 60 ml (or 0.25 cups). This method is handy for quick adjustments while cooking.</p>
