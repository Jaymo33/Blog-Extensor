<h3>How to Measure Sunflower Oil for Air Fryer Recipes</h3>  
<p>Measuring sunflower oil by weight (ounces) is often more accurate than using volume (cups). A kitchen scale ensures you get the exact amount needed, especially for recipes where oil plays a critical role. If you don’t have a scale, remember that 1 cup of sunflower oil weighs about 8 ounces, so 1.25 cups equals 10 ounces.</p>
