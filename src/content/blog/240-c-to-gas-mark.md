<h2>Understanding 240 C to Gas Mark Conversion</h2>

This temperature sits at the higher end of the oven scale, ideal for quick roasting or creating that perfect crust on bread. When using an air fryer at 240 C, you'll achieve similar results to a conventional oven at Gas Mark 9, though cooking times may vary slightly due to the air fryer's efficient circulation.</p>
