<h3>How to measure 75g of plain yogurt accurately</h3>  
<p>Using a digital kitchen scale is the easiest way to measure 75g of plain yogurt. Simply place your bowl on the scale, tare it to zero, and spoon in the yogurt until you hit 75g. If you don’t have scales, 75g is roughly 5 tablespoons, but weights can vary slightly depending on yogurt thickness. For best results, stick to weighing for precision.</p>
