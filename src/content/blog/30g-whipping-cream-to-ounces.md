<h2>How to measure 30g of whipping cream without scales</h2>
<p>If you don't have digital scales, you can still measure 30g of whipping cream (approximately 1.06 ounces) using tablespoons. Two level UK tablespoons equal roughly 30g of whipping cream. For even more accuracy, use the displacement method with water in a measuring jug.</p>

<p>After measuring your 30g (1.06 ounces) portion, store remaining cream in an airtight container. It typically lasts 3-5 days in the fridge. For longer storage, freeze measured portions in ice cube trays - each cube holds about 15g (0.53 ounces).</p>
