<h3>Measuring Cream Without a Scale</h3>
<p>Don’t have a kitchen scale? No problem. You can measure 285 ml of cream using a liquid measuring cup. Fill it to the 1-cup mark, then add another 45 ml (about 3 tablespoons) to reach 285 ml. This method works well for most recipes, including air fryer dishes like custards or creamy dips.</p>
