<h2>How to Measure 250g of Baking Soda Accurately</h2>  
<p>The best way to measure 250g of baking soda is with a digital kitchen scale. Scoop the baking soda into a bowl on the scale until it reaches 250g. If you don’t have a scale, 250g is roughly 8.82 ounces, or about 1.25 US cups. However, weighing is always more accurate for baking.</p>
