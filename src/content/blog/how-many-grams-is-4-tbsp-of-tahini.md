<h3>How to Measure Tahini Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a scale, use a standard UK tablespoon to measure tahini. Fill it level for accuracy, scraping off any excess. Remember, tahini’s thick consistency means it can vary slightly, so 4 tbsp tahini in grams might be between 58-62 grams depending on density.</p>
