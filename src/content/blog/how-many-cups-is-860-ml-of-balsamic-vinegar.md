<h2>Converting 860 ml of Balsamic Vinegar to Cups</h2>
<p>The standard conversion for millilitres to cups is straightforward. One cup equals 236.588 ml, so 860 ml divided by 236.588 gives you roughly 3.63 cups. This measurement is consistent whether you're in the UK or the USA, making it a universal guide for recipes.</p>
