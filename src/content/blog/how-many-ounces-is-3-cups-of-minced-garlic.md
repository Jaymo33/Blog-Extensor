<h3>How to measure minced garlic correctly</h3>  
<p>To get an accurate 3 cups minced garlic to ounces conversion, pack the garlic lightly into a measuring cup without pressing too hard. Overpacking can lead to more garlic than intended, altering the flavour profile of your dish. If you're using pre-minced garlic from a jar, drain excess liquid first for the most precise measurement.</p>
