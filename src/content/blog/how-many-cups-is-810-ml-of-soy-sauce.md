<h2>Understanding the Conversion</h2>  
<p>Converting millilitres to cups can be tricky, especially with liquids like soy sauce. The standard conversion is 1 cup = 236.588 ml, so 810 ml works out to roughly 3.42 cups. This measurement is crucial for recipes where balance matters, such as stir-fries, glazes, or air fryer marinades. If you're using an air fryer, precise measurements help avoid overly salty or bland dishes. For more conversions, check out our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-conversion-chart>air fryer conversion chart</a>.</p>  

<p>Different countries use varying cup sizes, but the metric cup (250 ml) is common in the UK. If you're following a US recipe, note that their cup is slightly smaller (236.588 ml). Always double-check your recipe's origin to ensure accuracy. For air fryer cooking, even small discrepancies can affect the outcome, so measuring correctly is key.</p>
