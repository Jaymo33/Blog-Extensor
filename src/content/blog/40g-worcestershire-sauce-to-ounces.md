<h2>How to Measure 40g of Worcestershire Sauce</h2>
<p>If you don’t have a scale, you can use volume measurements. Roughly, 40g of Worcestershire sauce is about 2.5 tablespoons. However, weighing it is more precise, especially for air fryer recipes where balance is key. For more recipe ideas, explore our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>air fryer recipes</a> collection.</p>

<h3>Using a Kitchen Scale</h3>
<p>Place a small bowl on your scale, tare it to zero, then pour the Worcestershire sauce until it reads 40g. This method is foolproof and ensures accuracy. If your scale displays ounces, it should show 1.41 oz for 40g.</p>
