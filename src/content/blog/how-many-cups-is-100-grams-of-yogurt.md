<p>When working with yogurt in recipes - especially for air fryer dishes like marinades, dips, or baked goods - precision matters. For popular air fryer recipes like tandoori chicken or yogurt-marinated kebabs, getting the yogurt quantity right affects both texture and flavour.</p>
