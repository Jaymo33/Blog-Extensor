<h3>How to measure chopped garlic accurately</h3>
<p>Measuring chopped garlic can be tricky due to its irregular shape. For the most accurate results, use a kitchen scale to weigh out 15 grams. If you don’t have a scale, lightly pack the garlic into the tablespoon to ensure consistency.
