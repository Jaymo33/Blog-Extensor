<h2>How to Measure 100g of Soy Milk Accurately</h2>  
<p>For the most precise measurement, use a digital kitchen scale set to grams. Simply pour the soy milk until it reaches 100g, then switch the scale to ounces to see the equivalent. If you don’t have a scale, 100g of soy milk is roughly 100ml, as its density is close to water.
