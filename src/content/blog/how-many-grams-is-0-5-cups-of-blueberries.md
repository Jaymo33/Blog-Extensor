<h3>Tips for Measuring Blueberries</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate results, use a kitchen scale to weigh your blueberries. Scooping them into a cup can lead to inconsistencies due to varying berry sizes. If you don’t have a scale, lightly pack the blueberries into the measuring cup without crushing them. This method ensures you’re as close to 75 grams as possible for 0.5 cups.</p>
