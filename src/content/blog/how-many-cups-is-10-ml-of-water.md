<h3>The Difference Between US and UK Cup Measurements</h3>
<p>It's worth noting that a US cup (240 ml) differs slightly from a UK cup (250 ml). When converting 10 ml of water to cups, this small variation becomes negligible, but for larger quantities, it can make a noticeable difference in your cooking results.</p>

<h2>Common Uses for 10 ml of Water in Air Fryer Recipes</h2>
<p>You might wonder why you'd need such a precise measurement as 10 ml of water in your air fryer cooking. This small quantity is often used in marinades, to create steam for certain dishes, or to adjust the consistency of batters and sauces. Getting the measurement right ensures your food cooks perfectly in the air fryer's unique environment.</p>
<p>For example, when preparing <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/chicken>air fryer chicken recipes</a>, adding just 10 ml of water to your marinade can help distribute flavours evenly without making the chicken soggy.
