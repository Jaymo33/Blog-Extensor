<h2>Converting 0.125 Cup Sugar Syrup to Ounces</h2>  
<p>Converting 0.125 cup of sugar syrup to ounces is straightforward once you know the ratio. In the UK, 1 cup of liquid equals 8 fluid ounces, so 0.125 cup is precisely 1 fluid ounce. However, if you’re working with a kitchen scale, 0.125 cup of sugar syrup weighs about 1.04 ounces (30 grams) due to its density. This slight variance won’t affect most recipes, but for baking, it’s worth noting. <p>If you don’t have a measuring cup, you can use a tablespoon to measure 0.125 cup of sugar syrup. Since 1 cup contains 16 tablespoons, 0.125 cup equals 2 tablespoons.
