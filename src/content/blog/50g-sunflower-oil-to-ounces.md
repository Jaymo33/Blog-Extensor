<h2>How many ounces is 50g of sunflower oil?</h2>  
<p>50 grams of sunflower oil equals approximately 1.76 ounces. This conversion is essential for air fryer recipes where precise measurements ensure perfect results. Whether you’re making crispy chips or a moist cake, knowing how to convert grams to ounces can save time and avoid mishaps. <h2>How to measure 50g of sunflower oil without scales</h2>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can measure 50g of sunflower oil using tablespoons or a measuring cup. One tablespoon of sunflower oil weighs about 14g, so 3.5 tablespoons will give you roughly 50g. Alternatively, 50g is just under ¼ cup (60ml) of oil. For precise air fryer recipes, like those on <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/chicken>our chicken recipe category</a>, accuracy is key.</p>  

<p>Another method is to use a liquid measuring jug with ounce markings. Since 50g equals 1.76 ounces, you can pour the oil until it reaches this mark. This is especially useful for recipes requiring small amounts of oil, such as air-fried vegetables or lean meats. Always level off your measurements for the best results.</p>  

<h3>Does the type of oil affect the conversion?</h3>  
<p>While most oils have similar densities, slight variations exist. Sunflower oil weighs about 0.92 grams per millilitre, so 50g takes up slightly more volume than denser oils like coconut oil. However, the 50g to ounces conversion (1.76 oz) remains consistent for sunflower oil.
