<h3>How to measure 0.5 tbsp of soft cheese without a scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a scale, fill a tablespoon halfway with soft cheese and level it off. Remember, this method is less precise than weighing, but it works in a pinch. For sticky cheeses, lightly grease the spoon first to help release the cheese cleanly.</p>
