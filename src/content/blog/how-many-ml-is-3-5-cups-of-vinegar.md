<h2>Understanding Cup to ml Conversions</h2>  
<p>Converting cups to millilitres is straightforward once you know the standard measurement. In the UK and most metric systems, 1 cup equals 236.588 ml. For 3.5 cups of vinegar, simply multiply this value by 3.5, giving you 828 ml. This conversion is essential for air fryer recipes, where liquid measurements can impact texture and flavour.</p>
