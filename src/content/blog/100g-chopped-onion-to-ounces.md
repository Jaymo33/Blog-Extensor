<h3>How to measure 100g of chopped onion</h3>
<p>The most accurate way to measure 100g of chopped onion is using digital kitchen scales. Simply place your bowl on the scale, tare it to zero, then add onion until you reach 100 grams (3.53 ounces). If you don't have scales, roughly two medium-sized onions, finely chopped, will give you about 100 grams. Remember that onion sizes vary, so scales are always best for precision.</p>

<h3>Tips for cooking with onions in your air fryer</h3>
<p>To get the best results from your 100g of chopped onion (3.53 ounces) in the air fryer, consider these tips. First, toss the onions with a small amount of oil to help them crisp up rather than steam. Second, shake the basket occasionally for even cooking. Third, if your recipe includes other ingredients, add the onions partway through cooking if you want to maintain some texture.</p>
