<h2>Why Fan Oven Temperatures Differ</h2>
<p>Fan ovens circulate hot air constantly, cooking food faster and more evenly than conventional ovens. The moving air transfers heat more efficiently to food surfaces, meaning you can typically reduce temperatures by 15-20 degrees Celsius. This principle applies equally to modern air fryers, which are essentially compact convection ovens.
