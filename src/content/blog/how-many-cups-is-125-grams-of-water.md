<h2>UK vs US Cup Measurements for 125 Grams of Water</h2>
<p>In the UK, 1 cup holds 284 ml of water, making 125 grams roughly 0.53 cups. In the US, where 1 cup is 240 ml, 125 grams equals about 0.5 cups. This small difference matters in air fryer recipes requiring exact hydration levels, like doughs or batters. Always check whether your recipe uses UK or US measurements.</p>
