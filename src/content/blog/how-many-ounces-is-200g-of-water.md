<h3>How to Measure 200g of Water Accurately</h3>
<p>To measure 200g of water, use a digital kitchen scale for the most accurate results. Place a measuring jug on the scale, tare it to zero, then pour water until it reaches 200g. If your scale displays ounces, it should read around 7.05 oz. For those without a scale, 200ml of water is roughly equivalent to 200g, as water's density is 1g per ml. This trick works well for most air fryer recipes.</p>

<h3>Converting Other Amounts of Water</h3>
<p>If you need to convert other amounts, remember that 1 gram of water equals approximately 0.035 ounces. So, 100g would be 3.53 oz, and 300g would be 10.58 oz.
