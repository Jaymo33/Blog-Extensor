<h3>How to Measure 125g of Agave Syrup Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a scale, you can use tablespoons for a rough estimate. One tablespoon of agave syrup weighs about 21g, so 125g would be roughly 6 tablespoons. However, this method isn’t as precise, so we recommend investing in a scale for best results. For sticky ingredients like agave syrup, lightly greasing your measuring spoon can help it slide off easily.</p>
