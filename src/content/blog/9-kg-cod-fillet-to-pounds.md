<h3>Storage and food safety considerations</h3>
<p>When working with a large 9 kg cod fillet converted to pounds (19.84 lbs), proper storage becomes critical for food safety. Fresh cod should be refrigerated immediately after purchase and cooked within 1-2 days. If you need to store it longer, portion the fish and freeze it in airtight containers or freezer bags, where it will keep for up to 3 months. When thawing frozen cod, do so gradually in the refrigerator rather than at room temperature to prevent bacterial growth. Cooked cod can be refrigerated for 3-4 days or frozen for 2-3 months. Always use a food thermometer to ensure your air fried cod reaches the safe internal temperature of 63°C throughout.</p>
