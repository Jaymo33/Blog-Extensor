<h2>How to Measure 1000g of Cornflour in Ounces</h2>  
<p>The conversion from grams to ounces is straightforward: 1 gram equals 0.03527 ounces. To find out how many ounces are in 1000g of cornflour, simply multiply 1000 by 0.03527. The result is 35.27 ounces, which is the exact weight you need for your recipe.</p>
