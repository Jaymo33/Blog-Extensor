<h3>How to measure chilli sauce correctly</h3>  
<p>Use a flat-edged knife to level off your tablespoon for the most accurate measurement. If you don’t have scales, remember that 3 tbsp of chilli sauce is roughly 45g. For sticky sauces, lightly grease the spoon first to ensure it slides off easily. This trick works well for air fryer marinades and glazes.</p>
