<h2>Understanding Double Cream Measurements</h2>  
<p>Double cream is a staple in many recipes, from desserts to savoury dishes. Measuring it accurately is crucial, especially when adapting recipes for your air fryer. In the UK, 1 cup equals 284 ml, while in the US, 1 cup is 240 ml. This slight difference means 610 ml of double cream converts to roughly 2.57 UK cups or 2.58 US cups. Always check which measurement system your recipe uses to avoid errors.</p>
