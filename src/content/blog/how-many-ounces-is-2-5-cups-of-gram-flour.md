<h3>How to measure gram flour correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate conversion of 2.5 cups to ounces, use a kitchen scale. Scooping flour directly from the bag can compact it, leading to uneven measurements. Instead, spoon the flour into the measuring cup and level it off. This method ensures you get the correct 10.6 oz for 2.5 cups.
