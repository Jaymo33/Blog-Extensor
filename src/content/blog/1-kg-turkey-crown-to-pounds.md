<h2>Cooking Your 2.2 Pound Turkey Crown Perfectly</h2>
<p>Cooking a 2.2 pound turkey crown (1 kg) in your air fryer requires specific techniques for optimal results. Cook for approximately 55-65 minutes, checking the internal temperature reaches 75°C at the thickest part using a meat thermometer. Let the turkey crown rest for 10-15 minutes after cooking to allow juices to redistribute throughout the meat.
