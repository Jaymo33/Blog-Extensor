<p>15g of plain yogurt equals approximately 0.53 ounces. This conversion is handy when following air fryer recipes that require precise measurements. Whether you're whipping up a marinade or a healthy dip, knowing the exact weight ensures perfect results every time.</p>

<h2>Why accurate measurements matter in air fryer cooking</h2>  
<p>Air fryers rely on precise ingredient ratios to deliver crispy, evenly cooked dishes. Using the right amount of yogurt, like 15g, can affect texture and moisture levels. Too much or too little can throw off your recipe, especially in baked goods or coatings. For more tips on perfecting your air fryer meals, check out our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>recipes section</a>.</p>  

<p>Many UK recipes list yogurt in grams, but some kitchen scales display ounces. Converting 15g of plain yogurt to ounces (0.53 oz) ensures you never second-guess measurements. This is particularly useful for dishes like marinades or air-fryer cakes, where precision is key.</p>  

<h3>How to measure 15g of plain yogurt without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have digital scales, use a tablespoon to approximate 15g of plain yogurt. One level tablespoon typically holds around 15g, but this can vary slightly with thickness. <h3>Storing leftover plain yogurt</h3>  
<p>After measuring out 15g, seal your yogurt container tightly and refrigerate. Proper storage keeps it fresh for future air fryer recipes. Always check for spoilage before use, especially if it’s been open for a while.</p>  

<h2>Converting other yogurt amounts to ounces</h2>  
<p>While 15g equals 0.53 ounces, you might need other conversions. For example, 30g is roughly 1.06 ounces, and 50g is about 1.76 ounces. Bookmark our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-conversion-chart>conversion chart</a> for quick reference during cooking.</p>
