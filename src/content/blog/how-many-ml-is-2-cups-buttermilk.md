<h3>How to Measure Buttermilk Correctly</h3>  
<p>Pour buttermilk into a liquid measuring cup placed on a flat surface for accuracy. The meniscus (curve at the top) should align with the 473 ml mark for 2.0 cups. If using a scale, 473 ml of buttermilk weighs roughly 484 grams. For sticky ingredients, lightly grease the measuring cup first.
