<h3>How to Measure 0.75 Cup of Chilli Sauce</h3>  
<p>Measuring 0.75 cup of chilli sauce is simple with the right tools. Use a liquid measuring cup for accuracy, filling it to the three-quarter mark. If you don’t have a measuring cup, a kitchen scale set to ounces (6 oz) works just as well. For sticky sauces like chilli, lightly grease the measuring cup for easy pouring. This method ensures you get the exact amount needed for your air fryer recipe.</p>
