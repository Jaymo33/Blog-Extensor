<h3>How to measure 0.5 tbsp of honey without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have digital scales, lightly grease a measuring spoon with oil to help the honey slide off easily. Fill the spoon just halfway for 0.5 tbsp (10.5g), levelling it with a knife.
