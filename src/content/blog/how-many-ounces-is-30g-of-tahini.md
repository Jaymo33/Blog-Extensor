<h3>How to measure tahini without a scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a scale, you can estimate 30g of tahini using tablespoons. One tablespoon of tahini weighs roughly 15g, so two tablespoons will give you close to 30g. However, for accuracy, a scale is always best. This method works well for recipes where slight variations won’t affect the outcome. For precise measurements, consider investing in a kitchen scale.</p>
