<h2>How to Measure 210 ml of Tomato Sauce</h2>
<p>If you don’t have a measuring jug, you can use a standard cup to measure 210 ml of tomato sauce. A US cup holds 240 ml, so 210 ml is just under a full cup. For UK cooks, a metric cup is 250 ml, making 210 ml roughly 0.84 cups. This small difference might seem negligible, but it can impact recipes requiring precision.</p>
