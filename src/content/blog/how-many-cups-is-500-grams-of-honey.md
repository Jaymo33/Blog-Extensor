<h2>Converting 500 Grams of Honey to Cups</h2>
<p>To convert 500 grams of honey to cups, you’ll need a liquid measuring cup. Spoon the honey into the cup gently to avoid air pockets, which can skew the measurement. Level it off with a knife for accuracy. Remember, 500 grams of honey equals about 1.5 cups, whether you’re in the UK or the US. This conversion is particularly useful for air fryer recipes like honey-glazed vegetables or desserts.</p>
