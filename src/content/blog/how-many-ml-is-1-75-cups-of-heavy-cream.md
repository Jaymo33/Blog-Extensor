<h3>How to Measure Heavy Cream Correctly</h3>  
<p>To measure 1.75 cups of heavy cream accurately, use a liquid measuring cup with clear markings. Fill it to the 1.75-cup line without compressing the cream. For millilitres, a metric measuring jug is ideal. If you’re scaling a recipe, remember that 1 cup of heavy cream equals roughly 237 ml, so 1.75 cups is about 414 ml. This method works for both UK and US measurements, though slight variations may occur based on cream density.</p>
