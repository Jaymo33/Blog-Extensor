<h3>How to Measure 25g of Cream Cheese Without Scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have scales, use tablespoons to measure cream cheese. One tablespoon of cream cheese is roughly 15g, so 25g is just under two tablespoons. For softer cream cheese, pack it lightly to avoid air gaps. This method works well for recipes like <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>air fryer stuffed mushrooms</a>.</p>
