<h2>US vs UK Cup Measurements</h2>  
<p>It's crucial to note that US and UK cup measurements differ slightly. A US cup holds 240 ml, while a UK cup holds 284 ml. This means 960 ml of vinegar converts to roughly 4.06 US cups or 3.38 UK cups. Always check which measurement system your recipe uses to avoid mistakes.</p>

<h3>How to Measure 960 ml of Vinegar Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don't have a measuring jug, you can use standard cups to measure 960 ml of vinegar. For US cups, fill four cups and add an extra tablespoon. For UK cups, use three cups and top up with about 40 ml. This method works well for most air fryer recipes, from marinades to sauces.</p>

<p>If you have leftover vinegar after measuring 960 ml, store it in a cool, dark place to maintain its quality. Glass bottles with airtight lids are ideal for preserving acidity and flavour. This ensures your vinegar stays fresh for future air fryer recipes.</p>
