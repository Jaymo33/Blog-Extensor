<h2>How to Measure 485 ml of Evaporated Milk</h2>  
<p>Measuring liquids accurately is key to successful cooking. For 485 ml of evaporated milk, you can use a liquid measuring cup marked in millilitres or cups. If your cup only has metric measurements, fill it to the 485 ml line. Alternatively, use a standard US cup (240 ml) and measure just over two cups to reach 485 ml.</p>  

<p>If you don't have a measuring cup, a kitchen scale can help. Since evaporated milk has a similar density to water, 485 ml weighs roughly 485 grams.
