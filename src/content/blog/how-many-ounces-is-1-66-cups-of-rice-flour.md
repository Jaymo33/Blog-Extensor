<h3>How to Measure Rice Flour Correctly</h3>  
<p>Measuring rice flour by weight (ounces) is more reliable than using cups, as scooping methods can vary. Fluff the flour first, then spoon it into a measuring cup and level it off. For 1.66 cups, this method should yield roughly 7.5 oz. If you’re scaling a recipe, a kitchen scale is your best friend—especially for air fryer baking, where precision is key.</p>
