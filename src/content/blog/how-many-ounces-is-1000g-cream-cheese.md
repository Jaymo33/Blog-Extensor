<h3>How to Measure 1000g of Cream Cheese</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate measurement, use a digital kitchen scale set to grams. If your scale only displays ounces, input 35.27 oz for 1000g of cream cheese. Block cream cheese is easier to measure than tubs, as it can be cut precisely. For air fryer recipes, softened cream cheese blends better, so measure it before softening to avoid discrepancies.</p>
