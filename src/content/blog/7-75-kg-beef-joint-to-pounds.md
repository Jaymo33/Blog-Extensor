<h2>Tips for Cooking a 7.75 kg Beef Joint in Your Air Fryer</h2>
<p>Cooking a 7.75 kg beef joint, which is about 17.09 pounds, in an air fryer requires some preparation and attention to detail. First, ensure your air fryer basket is large enough to accommodate the joint without overcrowding; you might need to use a rack or trivet for even air circulation. Pat the beef dry with kitchen paper and season it well before placing it in the preheated air fryer. Set the temperature according to your recipe, usually between 180°C and 200°C for beef, and calculate the time based on the weight in pounds. Remember that air fryers cook faster than conventional ovens, so keep an eye on it and use a thermometer to avoid overcooking.</p>
<p>Resting the beef joint after cooking is just as important as the cooking process itself.
