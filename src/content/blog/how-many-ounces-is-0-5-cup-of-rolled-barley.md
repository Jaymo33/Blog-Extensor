<h3>How to Measure Rolled Barley Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, use the spoon-and-level method for dry ingredients. Lightly spoon rolled barley into a measuring cup until it’s slightly heaped, then level it off with a straight edge. This method helps avoid compacting the barley, which can lead to inaccurate measurements. Remember, 0.5 cup of rolled barley should weigh roughly 1.5 ounces for best results.
