<h2>Converting 510 ml of Honey to Cups</h2>  
<p>The standard conversion for honey is roughly 1 ml = 0.0042 cups, so 510 ml equals about 2.15 cups. However, honey’s viscosity can cause slight variations depending on temperature. Cold honey is denser, while warm honey flows more freely. For the most accurate results, measure honey at room temperature. This ensures your <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/dessert>dessert recipes</a> turn out just right.</p>
