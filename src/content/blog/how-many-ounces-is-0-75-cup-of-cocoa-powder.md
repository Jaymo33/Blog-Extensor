<p>0.75 cup of cocoa powder equals approximately 2.25 ounces (or 2.25 oz) when measured accurately. This conversion is essential for baking, especially when using an air fryer for cakes, brownies, or other cocoa-based treats. Getting the measurement right ensures your recipes turn out perfectly every time.</p>  

<h2>Why Accurate Measurements Matter</h2>  
<p>When baking, precision is key—especially with cocoa powder. Too much or too little can drastically alter the texture and flavour of your dessert. For air fryer recipes, where cooking times and temperatures are tightly controlled, exact measurements ensure even cooking. Whether you’re making a chocolate cake or fudgy brownies, knowing that 0.75 cup of cocoa powder is 2.25 ounces helps avoid mishaps.</p>

<h3>How to Measure Cocoa Powder Correctly</h3>  
<p>To measure 0.75 cup of cocoa powder accurately, spoon the powder into a measuring cup and level it off with a knife. Avoid packing it down, as this can lead to an overly dense measurement. For best results, use a digital kitchen scale to confirm the weight—2.25 ounces is your target.</p>

<h3>Converting Other Cocoa Powder Measurements</h3>  
<p>Need to adjust your recipe? Here’s a quick reference: 1 cup of cocoa powder equals 3 ounces, so 0.25 cup is 0.75 ounces, and 0.5 cup is 1.5 ounces. This makes scaling recipes up or down simple, whether you’re baking for one or a crowd.</p>
