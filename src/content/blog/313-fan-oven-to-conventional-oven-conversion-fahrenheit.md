<h2>How to Adjust Cooking Times</h2>
<p>When converting 313 fan oven to conventional oven in Fahrenheit, you’ll need to adjust cooking times as well. Conventional ovens typically take longer to cook food evenly, so increase the time by 10-15%. For instance, if a recipe calls for 20 minutes in a fan oven at 313°F, aim for 22-25 minutes in a conventional oven at 325°F. Always check for doneness with a skewer or thermometer to avoid overcooking.</p>

<p>This adjustment is particularly important for delicate dishes like cakes or pastries, where precise temperatures matter.
