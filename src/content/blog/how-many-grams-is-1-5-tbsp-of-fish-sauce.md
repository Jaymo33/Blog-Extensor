<h2>How to measure fish sauce accurately</h2>
<p>To measure 1.5 tbsp of fish sauce (22.5g), use a standard UK tablespoon and fill it to the brim. For even greater precision, a digital kitchen scale is ideal. Simply tare your scale with the container on it, then pour the fish sauce until it reaches 22.5 grams. This method is perfect for recipes like air fryer salmon or Thai-inspired dishes.</p>

<p>Beyond 1.5 tbsp, here are other useful fish sauce conversions: 1 tbsp = 15g, 2 tbsp = 30g, and 3 tbsp = 45g. These measurements are invaluable when adapting recipes for your air fryer. For instance, if a recipe calls for 3 tbsp (45g) of fish sauce but you're cooking for one, halving it to 1.5 tbsp (22.5g) is straightforward.</p>
