<h3>How to Measure 5g of Lemon Juice Accurately</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate conversion of 5g lemon juice to ounces, use a digital kitchen scale set to grams. If your scale displays ounces, it should read around 0.18 oz. Alternatively, you can use measuring spoons—5g is roughly one teaspoon of lemon juice, though this can vary slightly depending on the lemon's ripeness and juiciness.</p>

<p>When heated in an air fryer, lemon juice reduces slightly due to evaporation, but the weight difference for 5g is minimal. If using it in a sauce or glaze, account for this by adding a tiny bit extra if needed. Always taste as you go to balance flavours perfectly.</p>  

<h2>Tips for Storing and Using Lemon Juice</h2>
