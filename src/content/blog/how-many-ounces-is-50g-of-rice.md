<h2>How to measure 50g of rice in ounces</h2>  
<p>Measuring 50g of rice in ounces is straightforward with a kitchen scale. Simply set your scale to grams, weigh out 50g, and note the equivalent in ounces (1.76 oz). If you don’t have a scale, use a measuring cup: 50g of uncooked rice is roughly ¼ cup, which translates to just under 2 ounces. This method works well for most rice types, from basmati to jasmine.</p>

<h3>Tips for cooking 50g of rice in the air fryer</h3>  
<p>To cook 50g of rice in the air fryer, rinse it first to remove excess starch. Add the rice to a heatproof dish with 100ml of water (a 1:2 rice-to-water ratio works best). Cook at 180°C for 15–20 minutes, stirring halfway. This method ensures fluffy rice without the need for a stove.
