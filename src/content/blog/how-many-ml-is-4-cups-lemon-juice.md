<h2>How to Measure 4.0 Cups of Lemon Juice Correctly</h2>
<p>When converting 4.0 cups of lemon juice to ml, use a liquid measuring cup for accuracy. The UK and US cup measurements differ slightly, but for lemon juice, 4.0 cups consistently equals 946 ml. This conversion works for both freshly squeezed and bottled lemon juice.</p>
<p>For air fryer recipes, consider using a kitchen scale alongside your ml measurements. At <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-conversion-chart>our conversion chart</a>, you'll find that 4.0 cups (946 ml) of lemon juice weighs approximately 960 grams. <h3>Tips for Working With Lemon Juice in Your Air Fryer</h3>
<p>When using 4.0 cups of lemon juice (946 ml) in air fryer recipes, remember that citrus can react with some metals. Stick to ceramic or glass containers when marinating. For best results, pat proteins dry before air frying to prevent steaming and ensure crispiness.</p>

<h3>Storing Leftover Lemon Juice</h3>
<p>If you don't use all your 4.0 cups (946 ml) of lemon juice at once, proper storage is key. Freeze portions in ice cube trays for easy future use in air fryer recipes. Thawed lemon juice works nearly as well as fresh for most cooking applications.</p>
