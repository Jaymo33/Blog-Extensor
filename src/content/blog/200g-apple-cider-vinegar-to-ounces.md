<h3>How to Measure 200g of Apple Cider Vinegar Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a scale, 200g of apple cider vinegar is roughly 200ml, as its density is close to water. This translates to just under a cup (since 1 cup is about 240ml). For ounces, use a liquid measuring cup with oz markings—200g will fill to around 7.05 oz. This method works well for most air fryer recipes, from dressings to deglazing.</p>
