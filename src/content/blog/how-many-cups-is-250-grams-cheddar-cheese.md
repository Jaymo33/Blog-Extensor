<h3>How to Measure 250 Grams of Cheddar Cheese Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a scale, use the spoon-and-level method for grated cheese. Lightly spoon the cheese into a measuring cup without packing it down. For 250 grams of cheddar cheese, this should fill about 2.5 cups. Avoid pressing the cheese, as this can lead to over-measuring.
