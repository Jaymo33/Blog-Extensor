<h2>How to measure 100g of chopped garlic</h2>
<p>Measuring 100g of chopped garlic is straightforward with a kitchen scale. Simply place your bowl on the scale, tare it to zero, and add the garlic until it reaches 100g. If you don’t have a scale, 100g of chopped garlic is roughly 3.53 ounces, or about 20-25 cloves, depending on size. This method ensures you’re adding the right amount to your air fryer dishes.</p>
