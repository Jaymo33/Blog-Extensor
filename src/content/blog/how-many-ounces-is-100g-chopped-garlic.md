<h2>How to measure 100g of chopped garlic</h2>
<p>Measuring 100g of chopped garlic is straightforward with a kitchen scale. Simply place your bowl on the scale, tare it to zero, and add the garlic until it reaches 100g. If you don’t have a scale, 100g of chopped garlic is roughly 3.53 ounces, or about 20-25 cloves, depending on size. This method ensures you’re adding the right amount to your air fryer dishes.</p>
<p>For those who prefer visual cues, 100g of chopped garlic fills about a half-cup measure. However, weighing is always more accurate, especially for recipes where precision matters. <h2>Tips for storing chopped garlic</h2>
<p>If you’ve prepped 100g of chopped garlic but don’t need it all, store the rest properly to maintain freshness. Place it in an airtight container and refrigerate for up to a week. You can also freeze chopped garlic in ice cube trays for longer storage. This way, you’ll always have the right amount on hand for your air fryer creations.</p>
