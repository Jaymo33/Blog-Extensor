<h3>UK vs. US Cup Measurements</h3>  
<p>Note that a UK cup (250ml) differs slightly from a US cup (240ml). For 50 grams of plain flour, the difference is minimal, but for larger quantities, it’s worth adjusting. Always check the recipe’s origin—our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk>airfryerrecipe.co.uk</a> uses UK standards. If adapting a US recipe, reduce the cup measurement by about 1 tablespoon per cup to compensate.</p>
