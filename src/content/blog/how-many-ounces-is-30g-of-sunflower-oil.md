<h3>How to Measure Sunflower Oil Without Scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have kitchen scales, you can still measure 30g of sunflower oil using tablespoons. One tablespoon of sunflower oil weighs roughly 14g, so 30g is just over two tablespoons.
