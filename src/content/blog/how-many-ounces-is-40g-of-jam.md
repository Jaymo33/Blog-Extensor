<h2>How to Measure 40g of Jam Accurately</h2>
<p>Using a digital kitchen scale is the easiest way to measure 40g of jam. Simply place your container on the scale, tare it to zero, then spoon in the jam until you hit 40g. If you don't have a scale, two level tablespoons roughly equal 40g.</p>
