<h3>How to measure brown sugar correctly</h3>  
<p>To get an accurate 36 grams from 3 tbsp of brown sugar, lightly pack the sugar into the spoon without pressing too hard. Overpacking can add extra weight, while underpacking may leave your recipe too dry. For consistent results, consider using a <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-conversion-chart>conversion chart</a> to double-check other common measurements.</p>
