Air fryers often display temperatures in Celsius or Fahrenheit, so understanding how 374 F translates to Gas Mark 5 helps when following older recipes. <h3>Why 374 F (Gas Mark 5) Works So Well</h3>
<p>This temperature hits the sweet spot for Maillard reaction - the chemical process that creates delicious browning on foods. It's hot enough to crisp potatoes or chicken skin but not so hot that delicate items like cakes will burn.
