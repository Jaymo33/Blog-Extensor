<h2>How to Measure Protein Powder Correctly</h2>  
<p>Measuring protein powder can be tricky due to its varying density. Scooping directly from the bag often leads to inconsistent amounts. For the most accurate results, use a kitchen scale to weigh your protein powder in ounces. If you don't have a scale, spoon the powder into your measuring cup and level it off with a knife.</p>  

<h3>Using a Kitchen Scale</h3>  
<p>A kitchen scale is the most reliable tool for converting 1.33 cups of protein powder to ounces. Simply place your container on the scale, tare it to zero, and add the powder until you reach 10.64 ounces. This method eliminates guesswork and ensures precision, which is especially useful for <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/meal-prep>meal prep</a> recipes.</p>
