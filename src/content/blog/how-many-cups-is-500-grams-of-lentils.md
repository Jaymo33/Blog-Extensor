<p>Getting the right amount of lentils is crucial for air fryer cooking. Too many lentils can overcrowd the basket, leading to uneven cooking, while too few might dry out. Lentils expand when cooked, so starting with the correct 500 grams to cups ratio ensures perfect texture. This is especially important for recipes like air-fried lentil patties or crispy lentil snacks. <h2>UK vs. US Cup Measurements for Lentils</h2>
<p>In the UK, 500 grams of lentils equals approximately 2.5 cups, while in the US, it’s closer to 2.1 cups. This difference stems from the slight variation in cup sizes between the two regions. <p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, use the spoon-and-level method for accuracy. Gently spoon lentils into a measuring cup and level off the top with a knife.
