<h3>How to Measure Brown Sugar Correctly</h3>  
<p>To measure 1.25 cups of brown sugar accurately, lightly spoon it into the measuring cup and level it off. Avoid packing it down unless the recipe specifies "firmly packed." For gram conversions, a digital scale is your best friend.
