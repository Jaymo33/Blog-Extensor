<h3>How to Measure Shredded Carrot Correctly</h3>  
<p>To measure 0.375 cup of shredded carrot, lightly pack the carrots into a measuring cup without compressing them too tightly. Overpacking can lead to more than 1.5 ounces, altering your recipe’s balance. If you don’t have a measuring cup, a kitchen scale is the most accurate tool. Simply set it to ounces and weigh out 1.5 oz for perfect results every time.</p>
