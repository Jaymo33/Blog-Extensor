<h3>How to measure 0.75 tbsp of sunflower oil without scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can still measure 0.75 tbsp of sunflower oil accurately. A standard UK tablespoon holds 15ml, so three-quarters of that is roughly 11.25ml. Since sunflower oil weighs about 0.92g per ml, this comes to around 10.6 grams. For consistent results, use a measuring spoon set or a graduated liquid measuring cup.</p>
