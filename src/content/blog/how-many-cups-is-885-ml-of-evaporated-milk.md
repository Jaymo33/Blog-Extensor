<p>885 ml of evaporated milk is approximately 3.74 cups in the US measurement system and roughly 3.5 cups in the UK. <h2>US vs UK Cup Measurements</h2>  
<p>In the US, 1 cup equals 240 ml, while in the UK, 1 cup is 284 ml. This means 885 ml of evaporated milk converts to about 3.74 US cups or 3.5 UK cups. If you're using a recipe from <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>airfryerrecipe.co.uk</a>, it's likely based on UK measurements, so double-check before you start cooking.</p>  

<p>For air fryer users, this distinction is crucial because many modern appliances come with preset programs based on standard measurements. Using the wrong cup size could throw off cooking times and temperatures. If you're unsure, measure your cups beforehand or use a kitchen scale for precision.</p>

<p>If you don't have a measuring jug or scale, you can use a standard drinking glass to estimate 885 ml of evaporated milk.
