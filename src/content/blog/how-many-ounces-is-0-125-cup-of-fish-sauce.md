<h2>Why Accurate Fish Sauce Measurements Matter</h2>  
<p>Fish sauce is a potent ingredient, and even small variations can drastically alter your dish’s taste. Using 0.125 cup of fish sauce (1 oz) ensures you don’t overpower your air fryer meals. Too much fish sauce can make food overly salty, while too little might leave it bland.</p>

<h3>How to Measure Fish Sauce Correctly</h3>  
<p>Liquid ingredients like fish sauce should be measured in a clear measuring cup for accuracy. Pour the fish sauce to the 0.125 cup mark, or use a scale for 1 oz. Avoid guessing—especially when preparing <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/shrimp>shrimp dishes</a> in your air fryer, where precise seasoning is key.</p>
