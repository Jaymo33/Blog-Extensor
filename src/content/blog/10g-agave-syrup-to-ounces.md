<h2>How to Measure 10g of Agave Syrup</h2>
<p>Measuring 10g of agave syrup can be tricky due to its sticky consistency. The best method is to use a digital kitchen scale for precision. Place your container on the scale, tare it to zero, then pour the syrup until it reads 10g. If you don’t have a scale, 10g is roughly two teaspoons, but this can vary slightly depending on the syrup’s density.
