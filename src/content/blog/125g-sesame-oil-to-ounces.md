<h3>How to Measure 125g of Sesame Oil Without Scales</h3>  
<p>Don’t have a kitchen scale? No problem! You can still measure 125g of sesame oil (4.41 oz) using standard measuring spoons or cups. One tablespoon of sesame oil weighs roughly 14g, so 125g would be just under 9 tablespoons. Alternatively, 125g is about half a US cup or just over 0.4 UK cups. Keep in mind that these are approximations, so for best results, invest in a digital scale.</p>
