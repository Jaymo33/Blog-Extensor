<h2>Understanding Fan Oven vs Conventional Oven Temperatures</h2>
<p>Fan ovens circulate hot air around the food, cooking it more evenly and quickly than conventional ovens. This means you typically need to reduce the temperature by about 20°C when converting from fan to conventional. For 232 fan oven to conventional oven conversion, this means setting your oven to around 210°C. This principle applies whether you're baking, roasting, or using an air fryer.</p>
