<h3>How to Measure 10g of Soft Cheese Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a scale, use a tablespoon to measure 10g of soft cheese. One level tablespoon of most soft cheeses weighs roughly 15g, so two-thirds of a tablespoon will give you close to 10g (0.35 oz). This method works well for spreads or dollops in recipes like air-fried jalapeño poppers or cheesecake fillings.</p>
