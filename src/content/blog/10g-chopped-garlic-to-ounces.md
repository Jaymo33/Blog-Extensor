<h3>How to Measure 10g of Chopped Garlic Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don't have a kitchen scale, you can estimate 10g of chopped garlic using tablespoons. Roughly, one tablespoon of finely chopped garlic weighs around 10g (0.35 ounces). However, this can vary slightly depending on how finely you chop it. For best results, investing in a digital scale is recommended.</p>

<h3>Storing Chopped Garlic for Future Use</h3>  
<p>If you've prepped extra chopped garlic, store it in an airtight container in the fridge for up to a week. You can also freeze it in ice cube trays with a bit of oil for longer storage. This way, you'll always have 10g portions ready for your next air fryer creation.</p>
