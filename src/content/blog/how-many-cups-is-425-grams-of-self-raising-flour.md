<h3>UK vs US Cup Measurements</h3>
<p>The UK and US use slightly different cup sizes, which matters when converting 425 grams of self-raising flour. A UK cup holds 250ml, while a US cup is 240ml. This means 425g of self-raising flour equals roughly 3.4 UK cups or 3.5 US cups. Always check your recipe's origin for the best results.</p>
