<h3>How to Measure 125g of Tahini Accurately</h3>  
<p>Using a digital kitchen scale is the easiest way to measure 125g of tahini. Simply place your bowl on the scale, tare it to zero, and spoon in the tahini until you hit 125g. If your scale displays ounces, aim for 4.41 oz. No scale? A tablespoon of tahini weighs roughly 15g, so 8 tablespoons plus a teaspoon will get you close to 125g.
