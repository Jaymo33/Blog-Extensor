<h2>How to Measure 0.25 Cups of Water Correctly</h2>
<p>Measuring 0.25 cups of water might seem simple, but there are a few tricks to ensure accuracy. First, use a clear liquid measuring cup placed on a flat surface. Fill it to the 0.25 cup mark at eye level to avoid over- or under-pouring. For even greater precision, a digital kitchen scale set to grams will confirm you’ve got exactly 59 grams.</p>
<p>If you don’t have a measuring cup, you can use tablespoons—0.25 cups equals 4 tablespoons.
