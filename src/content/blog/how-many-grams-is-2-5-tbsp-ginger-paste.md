<h3>How to Measure Ginger Paste Correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate conversion of 2.5 tbsp ginger paste to grams, use a kitchen scale. Scoop the paste lightly without packing it down to avoid excess. If you don’t have a scale, remember that 1 tbsp of ginger paste is roughly 15 grams, making 2.5 tbsp equal to 37.5 grams.</p>
