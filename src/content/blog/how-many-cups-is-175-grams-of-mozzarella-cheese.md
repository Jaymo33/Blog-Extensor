<p>Using the right amount of mozzarella cheese ensures your dishes have the perfect texture and flavour. <p>Different forms of mozzarella (shredded, cubed, or sliced) may slightly alter the cup measurement. Shredded cheese packs more densely, while cubed cheese takes up more space. Always measure after shredding for the most accurate results. For cheesy bakes or melts, sticking to 175 grams ensures consistency every time.</p>  

<h3>Converting Mozzarella Cheese for Air Fryer Recipes</h3>  
<p>Converting 175 grams of mozzarella cheese to cups is simple if you know the density. Shredded mozzarella typically weighs around 113 grams per cup, so 175 grams equals roughly 1.5 cups. This measurement works well for most air fryer dishes, from crispy mozzarella bites to cheesy stuffed peppers. If you're preparing a larger batch, adjust accordingly.</p>

<h3>Tips for Measuring Mozzarella Cheese</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate measurement, use a kitchen scale to weigh 175 grams of mozzarella cheese. If using cups, lightly pack the shredded cheese without pressing too firmly.
