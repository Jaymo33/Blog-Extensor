<h2>Understanding Fan Oven vs Conventional Oven Temperatures</h2>
<p>Fan ovens circulate hot air around the food, cooking it more evenly and quickly than conventional ovens. When a recipe calls for 88 fan oven, it's referring to the temperature with the fan assist function. The general rule is to add 20°C when converting from fan to conventional oven settings. This means 88 fan oven becomes 108-110°C in a traditional oven.</p>
