<h2>How to Measure 75 Grams of Olive Oil Without Scales</h2>
<p>Not everyone has a kitchen scale, but you can still measure 75 grams of olive oil accurately. One tablespoon of olive oil weighs about 13.5 grams, so 75 grams is roughly 5.5 tablespoons. Since 16 tablespoons make a cup, this translates to just over 1/3 cup. For air fryer recipes, this precision ensures even cooking and perfect crispiness.</p>
<p>If you're using a liquid measuring cup, pour the oil slowly to avoid spills and check at eye level. Remember, 75g olive oil to cups is a volume measurement, so avoid compacting or guessing. <h3>UK vs. US Cup Measurements</h3>
<p>In the UK, a standard cup is 250ml, while in the US, it's 240ml. This slight difference means 75 grams of olive oil in cups might vary by a fraction. For most air fryer recipes, this won’t make a noticeable difference, but it’s good to know for precision baking. Always check where your recipe originates if accuracy is critical.</p>
