<p>2 cups of shredded carrot equals approximately 8 ounces in weight. This measurement is essential for air fryer recipes where precision can affect cooking times and results. <h3>How to measure shredded carrot correctly</h3>
<p>To measure 2 cups of shredded carrot accurately, lightly pack the carrot into a measuring cup without compressing it too much. This method gives you the standard 8-ounce weight. For best results, use fresh carrots and shred them uniformly for even cooking in your air fryer.</p>
