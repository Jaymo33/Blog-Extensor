<h2>How to measure minced garlic correctly</h2>  
<p>To get the most accurate 2.5 cups of minced garlic in ounces, use a kitchen scale for precision. Scoop the minced garlic into a measuring cup without pressing down too hard, then level it off with a knife. If you don't have a scale, remember that 1 cup of minced garlic is roughly 8 ounces, making 2.5 cups equal to 20 ounces. This method works well for air fryer recipes like garlic Parmesan chicken or crispy garlic kale chips.</p>
