<h3>How to Measure Chopped Tomatoes Correctly</h3>  
<p>To get an accurate 10-ounce measurement from 1.25 cups of chopped tomatoes, pack them lightly into a measuring cup. Avoid pressing down too hard, as this can compress the tomatoes and throw off your measurement. If you’re using fresh tomatoes, chop them uniformly for consistent results.
