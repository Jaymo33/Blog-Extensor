<h2>Understanding Buttermilk Measurements</h2>  
<p>Buttermilk is a staple in many recipes, from cakes to marinades, and getting the measurement right is crucial. In the US, 1 cup equals 240 ml, so 110 ml is roughly 0.46 cups. In the UK, where 1 cup is 250 ml, 110 ml translates to 0.44 cups. This small difference can impact your recipe, especially in baking.
