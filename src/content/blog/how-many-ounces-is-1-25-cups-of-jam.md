<h3>How to Measure 1.25 Cups of Jam Correctly</h3>
<p>Measuring jam can be tricky due to its thick consistency. For the most accurate results, use a liquid measuring cup and lightly grease it to help the jam slide out easily. Alternatively, weigh it on a kitchen scale—1.25 cups of jam should weigh around 10 ounces. This method is especially useful for sticky ingredients like jam, ensuring you get the exact amount needed for your recipe.</p>
