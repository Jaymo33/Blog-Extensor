<h3>How to Measure 1.25 Cups of Jam Correctly</h3>
<p>Measuring jam can be tricky due to its thick consistency. For the most accurate results, use a liquid measuring cup and lightly grease it to help the jam slide out easily. This method is especially useful for sticky ingredients like jam, ensuring you get the exact amount needed for your recipe.</p>
