<h3>Measuring Heavy Cream Without a Cup</h3>
<p>If you lack measuring cups, use a metric jug: 240ml reaches just below the 250ml mark. For smaller quantities, tablespoons help – 16 tbsp equal 1.0 cups (240ml). For air fryer cooking, we recommend digital scales: 1.0 cups heavy cream weighs ~240 grams.</p>
