<h3>How to Measure 475 Grams of Olive Oil Without Scales</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can still measure 475 grams of olive oil using cups. One cup of olive oil weighs around 225 grams, so 475 grams is just over 2 cups. Use a liquid measuring cup for accuracy, and avoid packing the oil down.
