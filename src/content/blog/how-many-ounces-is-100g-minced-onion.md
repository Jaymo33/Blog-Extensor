<p>If you don’t have kitchen scales, use measuring spoons or cups for minced onion. One tablespoon of minced onion weighs roughly 10g, so 100g would be about 10 tablespoons. For ounces, 3.53 oz is just under half a US cup. Keep in mind that minced onion’s texture can affect volume, so scales are best for precision.
