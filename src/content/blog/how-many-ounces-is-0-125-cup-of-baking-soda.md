<h3>How to measure 0.125 cup of baking soda</h3>  
<p>Measuring 0.125 cup of baking soda can be tricky without the right tools. A standard UK measuring cup holds 250ml, so 0.125 cup is equivalent to 30ml or 2 tablespoons. For the most accurate results, use a digital kitchen scale to weigh out 1 ounce (28 grams) of baking soda. This method eliminates guesswork and ensures your air fryer recipes are spot-on.</p>
