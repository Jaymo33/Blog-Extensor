<h3>UK vs US Cup Measurements</h3>
<p>It's worth noting that UK and US cup measurements differ slightly. A UK cup is 284 ml, while a US cup is 240 ml. This means 335 ml of lemon juice is 1.42 cups in the UK and 1.41 cups in the US. For most home cooking, this small difference won't be noticeable, but for precision baking, it's good to know.</p>
