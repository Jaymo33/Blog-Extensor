<h3>How to Measure Cocoa Powder Correctly</h3>  
<p>To measure 1.75 cups of cocoa powder accurately, fluff the powder first to prevent clumping. Gently spoon it into a measuring cup without packing it down, then level it off with a knife. This method ensures you get the correct 175-gram equivalent. For air fryer recipes, sifting the cocoa powder afterward helps avoid lumps in your batter.
