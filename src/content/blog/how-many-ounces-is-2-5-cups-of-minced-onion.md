<p>Using the correct amount of minced onion ensures your dish isn’t overpowering or lacking in flavour. Air fryers cook food quickly, so uneven measurements can lead to inconsistent results. <h3>How to measure minced onion correctly</h3>  
<p>To get an accurate 10 ounces from 2.5 cups of minced onion, pack it lightly into a measuring cup without pressing too hard. Overpacking can lead to more onion than needed, altering your recipe’s balance. A kitchen scale is the most precise tool, but if you don’t have one, our conversion guide ensures you’re close enough.</p>
