<h3>How to Measure Rice Flour Correctly</h3>  
<p>To measure 2.5 cups of rice flour accurately, spoon the flour into a measuring cup and level it off with a knife. Avoid packing it down, as this can lead to excess flour and throw off your recipe. For best results, weigh it using a kitchen scale—10.6 oz is the precise weight for 2.5 cups.</p>
