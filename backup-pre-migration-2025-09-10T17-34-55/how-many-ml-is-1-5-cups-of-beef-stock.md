<h2>Why Accurate Measurements Matter in Air Fryer Cooking</h2>  
<p>Air fryers rely on rapid air circulation to cook food evenly, and liquid measurements play a key role in achieving the right texture. Too much beef stock can make dishes soggy, while too little may leave them dry. Converting 1.5 cups of beef stock to 355 ml ensures you hit the sweet spot. This precision is especially important for recipes like air-fried beef casseroles or braised dishes, where liquid balance is critical.</p>
