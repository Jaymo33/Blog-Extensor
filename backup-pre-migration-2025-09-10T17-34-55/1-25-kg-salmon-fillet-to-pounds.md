This weight represents a substantial salmon fillet that can easily feed four to six people depending on portion sizes. <h2>Practical tips for cooking 1.25 kg salmon fillet in your air fryer</h2>
<p>Now that you know your 1.25 kg salmon fillet converts to approximately 2.76 pounds, let's discuss how to cook it perfectly. Season generously with salt, pepper, and your favourite herbs – dill, lemon thyme, and parsley work wonderfully with salmon. Preheat your air fryer to 200°C for about 3 minutes to ensure even cooking from the moment the salmon goes in. Place the salmon skin-side down if it has skin, or simply place it evenly in the basket if it's skinless.</p>

<p>Cooking times will vary depending on your specific air fryer model and the thickness of your 1.25 kg salmon fillet.
