<h3>How We Calculated 1.66 Cups to Ounces</h3>
<p>The standard conversion is 1 cup of liquid equals 8 ounces. Therefore, 1.66 cups of lime juice converts to 13.28 ounces (1.66 x 8 = 13.28). This measurement applies to freshly squeezed lime juice or store-bought varieties, though fresh juice often provides more vibrant flavour for your air fryer creations.</p>
