<h3>How to Measure 500g of Soy Milk Accurately</h3>  
<p>To measure 500g of soy milk, use a digital kitchen scale for the most accurate results. Place your measuring jug on the scale, tare it to zero, then pour the soy milk until it reaches 500g. If your scale displays ounces, it should read around 17.64 oz. For those without a scale, 500g of soy milk is roughly 2 metric cups (500ml), but weighing is always more precise. This method is especially useful for air fryer recipes where consistency is key, like in our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/dessert>dessert section</a>.</p>
