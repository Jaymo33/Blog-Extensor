<h3>How to measure 0.375 cup of paprika accurately</h3>
<p>For the most accurate conversion of 0.375 cup paprika to ounces, use a kitchen scale. Scoop the paprika into your measuring cup without packing it down, then level it off with a straight edge. Transfer it to the scale to verify it’s approximately 1.5 ounces. This method works perfectly for all your <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>air fryer recipes</a>, ensuring you never over- or under-season your dishes.</p>
