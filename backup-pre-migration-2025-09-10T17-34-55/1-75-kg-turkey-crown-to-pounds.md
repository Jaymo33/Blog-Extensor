<h3>Using Conversion in Air Fryer Cooking</h3>
<p>Once you know your 1.75 kg turkey crown is 3.86 pounds, you can accurately set your air fryer time and temperature. A good rule of thumb is to cook turkey crown at 180°C for about 20 minutes per pound, plus an extra 20 minutes.
