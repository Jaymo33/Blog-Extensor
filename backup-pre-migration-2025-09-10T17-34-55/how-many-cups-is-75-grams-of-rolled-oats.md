<h3>How to Measure 75 Grams of Rolled Oats Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, use the spoon-and-level method for accuracy. Lightly spoon the rolled oats into a measuring cup until slightly heaped, then level off the top with a straight edge. Avoid packing the oats down, as this can lead to an incorrect measurement. For best results, stick to the ¾ cup mark when converting 75 grams of rolled oats to cups.</p>
