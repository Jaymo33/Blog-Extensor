<h2>How to measure ginger paste for air fryer recipes</h2>
<p>Ginger paste can be tricky to measure because of its thick consistency. For 1.25 cups (10 ounces), use a liquid measuring cup and level it off with a spatula. If you don't have measuring cups, a kitchen scale set to ounces will give you the most accurate result. This method works perfectly for <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/vegetables>air fryer vegetable recipes</a> that call for ginger paste.</p>

<h3>Converting between cups and ounces</h3>
<p>The general rule is that 1 cup of ginger paste equals 8 ounces, so 1.25 cups would be 10 ounces. However, density can vary slightly between brands.
