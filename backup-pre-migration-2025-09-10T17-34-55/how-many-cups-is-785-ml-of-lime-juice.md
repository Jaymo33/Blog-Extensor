<h2>How to Measure 785 ml of Lime Juice</h2>
<p>Measuring lime juice accurately starts with the right tools. A liquid measuring cup with millilitre markings is ideal, but if you only have standard cups, you’ll need to convert 785 ml to cups. As mentioned, 785 ml is roughly 3.3 cups. To measure this, fill your cup to the 3-cup mark, then add another 1/3 cup to reach the correct amount. This ensures your air fryer recipes, like lime-glazed shrimp or citrus-marinated tofu, turn out just right.</p>
<p>For those who prefer weighing ingredients, 785 ml of lime juice weighs about 785 grams, as lime juice has a similar density to water. However, cups are more common in recipes, so sticking to volume measurements is often easier.
