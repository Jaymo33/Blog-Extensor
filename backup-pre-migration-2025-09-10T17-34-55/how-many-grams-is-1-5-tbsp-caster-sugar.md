<h3>The science behind caster sugar measurements</h3>
<p>Caster sugar has finer crystals than granulated sugar but isn't as powdery as icing sugar. This affects how it packs into a tablespoon measure. When converting 1.5 tablespoon caster sugar UK grams, we account for this density difference. The standard conversion is 15g per level tablespoon, making 1.5 tbsp equal to 22.5g. This precision matters in air fryer baking where even small measurement variations can impact results.</p>

<h2>How to measure 1.5 tbsp caster sugar accurately</h2>
<p>For the most accurate 1.5 tbsp caster sugar how many grams conversion, use proper measuring techniques. First, fluff the sugar in its container to prevent packing. Then, spoon it lightly into the tablespoon measure without pressing down. Level off the excess with a straight edge for a precise measurement. For absolute accuracy, especially in air fryer recipes, we recommend using digital scales to measure the 22.5 grams directly.</p>
<p>If you don't have scales, remember that 1.5 tablespoons of caster sugar to g conversion equals roughly three UK teaspoonfuls (since 1 tbsp = 2 tsp). However, for consistent results in your air fryer cakes and desserts from <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/cakes>our cake recipes</a>, investing in a basic kitchen scale is worthwhile.
