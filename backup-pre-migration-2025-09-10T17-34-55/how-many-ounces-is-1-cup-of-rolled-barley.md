<p>1 cup of rolled barley weighs approximately 3.5 ounces (99 grams) in UK measurements. This conversion is essential for air fryer recipes where precise measurements can affect cooking times and texture. Whether you're making barley porridge or adding it to air-fried veggie burgers, knowing this weight ensures consistent results every time.</p>

<h2>Why accurate measurements matter for air fryer cooking</h2>  
<p>Air fryers cook food quickly and evenly, but only if ingredients are measured correctly. Too much or too little barley can throw off moisture levels and cooking times. For example, if you're making a barley-stuffed chicken breast in your air fryer, using the right amount ensures even cooking and perfect texture. <h3>How to measure rolled barley correctly</h3>  
<p>The most accurate way to measure 1 cup of rolled barley is to use digital kitchen scales. Simply scoop the barley into your measuring cup, then weigh it to confirm it's 3.5 ounces. If you don't have scales, fluff the barley with a fork before gently spooning it into the cup and levelling off the top with a straight edge.</p>  

<h2>Converting between cups and ounces for different barley types</h2>  
<p>While 1 cup of rolled barley equals 3.5 ounces, other barley forms have different weights. Pearl barley, for instance, weighs about 7 ounces per cup due to its denser structure. Always check which type your recipe specifies - this is particularly important for air fryer cooking where density affects heat penetration.</p>

<h3>Storing rolled barley for air fryer recipes</h3>  
<p>To maintain freshness and accurate measurements, store rolled barley in an airtight container in a cool, dark place. Proper storage prevents moisture absorption which could alter the weight per cup. If your barley develops an off smell or appearance, it's best to replace it before using in your air fryer.</p>  

<h2>Common uses for 1 cup of rolled barley in air fryer cooking</h2>  
<p>One cup (3.5 ounces) of rolled barley is perfect for making single-serving portions in your air fryer. Try it in barley patties, stuffed peppers, or as a base for grain bowls. The quick cooking time of air fryers makes barley an excellent choice for healthy weeknight meals.</p>

<h3>Adjusting cooking times for rolled barley in air fryers</h3>  
<p>When using 1 cup (3.5 ounces) of rolled barley in air fryer recipes, remember it typically requires about 15-20 minutes at 180°C. Always check individual recipes as cooking times may vary depending on other ingredients and your specific air fryer model.</p>
