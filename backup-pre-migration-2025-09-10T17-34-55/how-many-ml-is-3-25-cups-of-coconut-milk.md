<h3>US vs. UK Cup Measurements</h3>  
<p>It’s worth noting that US and UK cup sizes differ slightly, but for coconut milk, the variance is minimal. A US cup is 240 ml, while a UK cup is 250 ml—meaning 3.25 US cups equal 780 ml, and 3.25 UK cups equal 812.5 ml. For simplicity, we’ve used the US standard (768 ml) as it’s more commonly referenced in recipes. Always check your recipe’s origin to avoid confusion.</p>
