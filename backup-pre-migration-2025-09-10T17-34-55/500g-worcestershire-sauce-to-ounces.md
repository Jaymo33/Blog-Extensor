<h2>How to Measure 500g of Worcestershire Sauce</h2>
<p>Measuring Worcestershire sauce by weight is more accurate than using volume, especially for air fryer recipes. A digital kitchen scale is the best tool for this task. Simply place your container on the scale, tare it to zero, and pour the sauce until it reaches 500g. If you don’t have a scale, 500g of Worcestershire sauce is roughly 17.64 ounces, or about 1.1 cups.</p>
