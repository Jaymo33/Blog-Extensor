<p>0.125 cup of shredded coconut equals approximately 0.5 ounces (14 grams). This measurement is essential for air fryer recipes where precision ensures even cooking and perfect texture. Whether you’re making coconut-crusted shrimp or adding a tropical twist to desserts, knowing the exact weight helps avoid over or under-measuring.</p>  

<h2>Why Accurate Measurements Matter in Air Fryer Cooking</h2>  
<p>Air fryers rely on hot air circulation to cook food evenly, and incorrect ingredient quantities can lead to uneven results. For example, too much shredded coconut in a coating might burn before the food cooks through. On the other hand, too little could leave your dish lacking flavour and crunch. That’s why converting 0.125 cup of shredded coconut to ounces (0.5 oz) is so useful.</p>

<h3>How to Measure Shredded Coconut Correctly</h3>  
<p>When measuring shredded coconut, always fluff it lightly with a fork before scooping. Packing it down too tightly can lead to an inaccurate measurement. For 0.125 cup (which is 2 tablespoons), gently spoon the coconut into the measuring cup and level it off with a straight edge.</p>  

<h2>Converting Other Coconut Measurements</h2>  
<p>If your recipe calls for a different amount of shredded coconut, here’s a quick reference guide: 0.25 cup = 1 oz, 0.5 cup = 2 oz, and 1 cup = 4 oz. These conversions are handy when scaling recipes up or down, especially for batch cooking in your air fryer.</p>

<h2>Common Mistakes When Measuring Shredded Coconut</h2>  
<p>One of the biggest mistakes is using a liquid measuring cup instead of a dry one. Dry measuring cups are designed to be levelled off, giving you the most accurate measurement. Another error is compressing the coconut, which can nearly double its weight per cup.</p>
