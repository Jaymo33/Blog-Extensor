<h2>How to measure soy milk for air fryer recipes</h2>  
<p>Measuring soy milk accurately is easy with the right tools. Use a liquid measuring cup for the most precise results, filling it to the 2-cup mark. If you’re using a scale, 16 oz is your target weight. For recipes requiring smaller amounts, remember that 1 cup of soy milk equals 8 oz. This method works for all air fryer dishes, from savoury sauces to sweet desserts.</p>

<h2>Converting soy milk for UK air fryer recipes</h2>  
<p>In the UK, fluid ounces (fl oz) are the standard for liquid measurements. Since 2 cups of soy milk equal 16 fl oz, this conversion is straightforward. However, if a recipe calls for weight in ounces (oz), ensure you’re using the correct measurement. Most air fryer recipes specify fluid ounces for liquids like soy milk, making it easier to follow along.</p>
