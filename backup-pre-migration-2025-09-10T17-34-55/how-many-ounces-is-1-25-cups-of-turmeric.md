<h2>How to measure turmeric for air fryer recipes</h2>  
<p>Measuring turmeric correctly is simple if you follow a few tips. First, fluff the turmeric powder in its container to avoid compacting it. Then, lightly spoon it into your measuring cup and level it off with a straight edge. This ensures you’re not packing in too much, which could skew your measurement. For 1.25 cups of turmeric, you’ll need to repeat this process until you reach the desired amount.</p>  

<p>If you’re using fresh turmeric, the conversion differs slightly. Grated fresh turmeric is less dense, so 1.25 cups would weigh closer to 4 ounces. <h3>Common mistakes when measuring turmeric</h3>  
<p>One of the biggest mistakes is scooping turmeric directly from the bag with the measuring cup, which compacts the spice and leads to an inaccurate measurement. Another error is eyeballing the amount, especially in small quantities like 1.25 cups. Even a slight deviation can affect the flavour profile of your dish, particularly in air fryer recipes where spices are highlighted.</p>
