<h2>How to Measure Maple Syrup Correctly</h2>
<p>Measuring maple syrup can be tricky due to its thick, sticky consistency. To get an accurate 3.25 cups (768.91 ml), use a liquid measuring cup with clear markings. Pour slowly to avoid air bubbles, and level off the top with a spatula. For smaller quantities, a syringe or kitchen scale can also be helpful.</p>
<p>If you’re working with a recipe that calls for 3.25 cups of maple syrup in ml, remember that UK and US cup measurements differ slightly. In the UK, 1 cup is 284 ml, while in the US, it’s 236.59 ml. Always check which standard your recipe uses to avoid discrepancies.
