<h2>Why accurate quinoa measurements matter</h2>  
<p>Getting the right amount of quinoa ensures your air fryer recipes turn out perfectly. Too much quinoa can lead to uneven cooking, while too little might leave you with a dry or underwhelming dish. For example, if you're making a quinoa salad or a protein-packed side, 3 cups of quinoa (24 ounces) is a great starting point.</p>  
<p>Quinoa expands when cooked, so measuring correctly prevents overflow in your air fryer basket. <h2>Converting 3 cups of quinoa to ounces</h2>  
<p>In the UK, 3 cups of quinoa translates to roughly 24 ounces (680 grams). This measurement is based on standard UK cup sizes, which differ slightly from US measurements. If you're using a kitchen scale, always opt for weight measurements for the most accurate results.</p>  
<p>For air fryer cooking, this conversion ensures even heat distribution. Whether you're roasting quinoa for a crunchy topping or cooking it as a base for <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>air fryer recipes</a>, knowing the exact weight prevents mishaps. If you're unsure, our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-conversion-chart>conversion chart</a> can help double-check your measurements.</p>  

<h3>How to measure quinoa without a scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, use the spoon-and-level method for dry quinoa. Fill your measuring cup without packing it down, then level it off with a straight edge. This ensures you’re not accidentally adding extra quinoa, which could throw off your recipe.</p>  

<h2>Using quinoa in your air fryer</h2>  
<p>Quinoa works brilliantly in the air fryer, whether you're toasting it for extra crunch or cooking it as a grain base. For 3 cups of quinoa (24 ounces), preheat your air fryer to 180°C and spread the quinoa evenly in the basket. Cook for 10-12 minutes, shaking halfway for even results.</p>  
<p>Pair your cooked quinoa with other <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/vegetables>vegetable-based air fryer recipes</a> for a balanced meal. <h3>Common mistakes when measuring quinoa</h3>  
<p>Avoid packing quinoa into the cup, as this can add up to 30% more than intended. Always fluff the quinoa with a fork before measuring to prevent clumping. For best results, weigh your quinoa after measuring to confirm accuracy.</p>
