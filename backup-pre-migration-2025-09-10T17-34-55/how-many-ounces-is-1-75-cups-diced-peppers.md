<h3>How to Measure Diced Peppers Correctly</h3>  
<p>To get an accurate 1.75 cups (14 oz) of diced peppers, pack them lightly into a measuring cup without crushing. The size of the dice matters—smaller pieces may settle more densely, so adjust if needed. For air fryer recipes, uniformity in size ensures even cooking, whether you're roasting or sautéing.</p>
