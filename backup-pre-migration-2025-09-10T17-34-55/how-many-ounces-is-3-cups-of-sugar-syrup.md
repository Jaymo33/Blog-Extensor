<h3>How to Measure Sugar Syrup Correctly</h3>  
<p>When converting 3 cups of sugar syrup to ounces, always use a liquid measuring cup for consistency. Fill it to the brim and level it off for an exact measurement. If you’re using a scale, 1 cup of sugar syrup weighs roughly 8 ounces, so 3 cups would be 24 ounces. For sticky ingredients like syrup, lightly greasing the measuring cup can help with pouring.</p>
