<h3>How to measure 0.25 cup of frozen corn without a scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t own a kitchen scale, use a dry measuring cup for frozen corn. Lightly fill the cup without packing it down, then level it off with a straight edge. This method ensures you get close to the 1.25-ounce mark. Avoid thawing the corn first, as it can alter the weight and texture.</p>

<h3>Common mistakes when measuring frozen corn</h3>  
<p>One mistake is thawing frozen corn before measuring, which increases moisture and weight. Always measure it straight from the freezer for accuracy. Another error is packing the cup too tightly, leading to more than 1.25 ounces. A light, loose fill is best for consistent results.</p>
