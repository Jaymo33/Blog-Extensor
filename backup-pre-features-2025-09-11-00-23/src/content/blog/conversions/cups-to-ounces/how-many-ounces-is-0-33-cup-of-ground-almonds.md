<h3>How to Measure 0.33 Cup of Ground Almonds</h3>  
<p>Use a dry measuring cup for ground almonds, lightly spooning them in without packing. Level off the top with a knife for accuracy. For smaller quantities like 0.33 cup, a kitchen scale ensures precision—1.2 oz is your target. This method works for <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/dessert>desserts</a> like macarons or savoury coatings.</p>
