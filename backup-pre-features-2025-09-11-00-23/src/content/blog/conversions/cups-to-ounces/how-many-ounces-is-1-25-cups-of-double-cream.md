<h3>Common Mistakes When Measuring Double Cream</h3>  
<p>One common mistake is using a dry measuring cup for liquids, which can lead to inaccurate measurements. Another is eyeballing the amount, which often results in too much or too little cream. Always use the right tools to ensure your air fryer recipes turn out as intended.</p>

<p>Double cream adds richness to air fryer dishes, but it's important to use it correctly. For sauces, warm the cream slightly before adding it to prevent curdling.
