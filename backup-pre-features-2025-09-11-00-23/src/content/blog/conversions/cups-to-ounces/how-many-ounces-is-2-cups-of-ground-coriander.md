<h3>How to Measure Ground Coriander Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you’re converting 2 cups of ground coriander to ounces without a scale, use the spoon-and-level method. Gently spoon the coriander into a dry measuring cup until it’s heaped, then level it off with a straight edge. This method prevents compaction and gives you a close approximation of 3.5 ounces. For recipes like air-fried falafel or spiced potatoes, this precision ensures balanced flavours.</p>
