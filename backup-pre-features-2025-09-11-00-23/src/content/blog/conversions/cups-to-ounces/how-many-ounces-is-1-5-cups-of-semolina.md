<h3>How to measure semolina correctly</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate results, use a digital kitchen scale to weigh your semolina. If you don’t have one, fluff the semolina with a spoon before lightly spooning it into your measuring cup. Avoid packing it down, as this can lead to over-measuring. This method ensures you get the right 1.5 cups semolina to ounces ratio every time.</p>
