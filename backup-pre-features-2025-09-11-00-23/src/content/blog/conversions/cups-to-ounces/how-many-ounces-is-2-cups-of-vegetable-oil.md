<h3>How to measure 2 cups of vegetable oil in ounces</h3>  
<p>To measure 2 cups of vegetable oil in ounces, use a kitchen scale for the most accurate results. Place a measuring jug on the scale, tare it to zero, then pour in the oil until it reaches 16 ounces. If you don’t have a scale, remember that 1 cup of vegetable oil weighs about 8 ounces, so 2 cups would be 16 ounces. This method works for all types of vegetable oil, including sunflower, rapeseed, and olive oil blends.</p>
