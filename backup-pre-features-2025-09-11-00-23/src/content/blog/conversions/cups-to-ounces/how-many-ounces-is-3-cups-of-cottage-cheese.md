<p>Measuring cottage cheese isn’t as straightforward as dry ingredients. Its lumpy texture means you’ll need to spoon it into a measuring cup and level it off for accuracy. <h3>Storing leftover cottage cheese</h3>  
<p>If you’ve measured out 3 cups but only used half, proper storage keeps it fresh. Transfer leftovers to an airtight container and refrigerate for up to 5 days. For longer storage, cottage cheese freezes well—just give it a good stir after thawing to restore its texture.</p>
