<h3>How to measure chopped tomatoes</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate results, use a kitchen scale to weigh your chopped tomatoes. If you don’t have one, lightly pack the tomatoes into a measuring cup without crushing them. This method ensures you’re close to the 20-ounce mark for 2.5 cups. Remember, air fryer recipes often require even cooking, so uniform measurements help avoid soggy or unevenly cooked dishes.</p>
