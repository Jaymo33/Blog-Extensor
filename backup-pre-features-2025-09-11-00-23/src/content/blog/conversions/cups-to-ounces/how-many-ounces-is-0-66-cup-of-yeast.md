<p>One of the biggest mistakes is using expired yeast, which won’t rise regardless of how accurately you measure 0.66 cup. Always check the expiry date before baking. Another pitfall is misjudging the density—lightly scooped yeast weighs less than tightly packed, so 0.66 cup can vary. For air fryer recipes, consistency is crucial, so weigh your yeast whenever possible.</p>
