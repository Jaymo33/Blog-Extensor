<h2>Why measuring chia seeds correctly matters</h2>  
<p>Chia seeds are packed with nutrients, but using too much or too little can affect texture and taste. <h3>How to measure 0.5 cup of chia seeds accurately</h3>  
<p>To measure 0.5 cup of chia seeds, lightly spoon them into a measuring cup without packing them down. Level off the top with a straight edge for an exact half-cup. If you don’t have a measuring cup, 2.5 ounces is the equivalent weight. This method ensures you’re not accidentally over-pouring, which can throw off your recipe.</p>
