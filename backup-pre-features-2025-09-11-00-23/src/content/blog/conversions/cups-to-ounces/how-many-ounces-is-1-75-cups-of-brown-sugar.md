<h3>How to Measure Brown Sugar Correctly</h3>  
<p>Brown sugar should be packed firmly into the measuring cup for accuracy. Unlike granulated sugar, which can be scooped loosely, brown sugar’s moisture content means it settles differently. Use the back of a spoon to press it down, ensuring no air gaps. This method guarantees you’re using the right amount—whether it’s 1.75 cups or smaller quantities.</p>
