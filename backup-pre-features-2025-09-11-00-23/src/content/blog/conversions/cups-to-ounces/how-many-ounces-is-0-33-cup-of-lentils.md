<h2>How to measure 0.33 cup of lentils properly</h2>
<p>Measuring dry lentils can be tricky, as they don't settle uniformly in measuring cups. For the most accurate 0.33 cup measurement, use the scoop-and-level method: gently spoon lentils into your measuring cup without packing them down, then level off the top with a straight edge.</p>
<p>For even greater precision, consider using a kitchen scale. Place your measuring cup on the scale, tare it to zero, then add lentils until you reach 2.64 ounces (75 grams). This method eliminates any variation caused by how the lentils settle in the cup.</p>
