<h3>How to Measure 0.33 Cup of Porridge Oats Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a scale, use the spoon-and-level method for dry ingredients. Fluff the oats in the bag, spoon them into a measuring cup, and level off the excess with a knife. For 0.33 cup, fill a 1/3 cup measure and lightly scrape off the top. This method isn’t as precise as weighing, but it’s a handy backup for quick conversions like 0.33 cup porridge oats to ounces.</p>
