<h2>Common Mistakes When Measuring Oats</h2>  
<p>One mistake is scooping oats directly from the bag with a measuring cup, which compacts them and leads to excess weight. Instead, spoon oats lightly into the cup and level off the top. For best results, refer to our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-conversion-chart>air fryer conversion chart</a> to avoid guesswork.</p>  

<p>Another error is assuming all oats weigh the same. Steel-cut oats are denser than rolled oats, so 1 cup may weigh slightly more. Always double-check the type specified in your recipe. If you’re unsure, start with 3.5 ounces and adjust based on texture preferences.</p>
