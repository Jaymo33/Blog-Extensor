<h2>Why Convert 148 C to Fan Oven?</h2>  
<p>Fan ovens and air fryers use a circulating fan to distribute heat, reducing cooking times and ensuring even browning. When a recipe calls for 148 C in a conventional oven, lowering the temperature by 10-20 degrees prevents overcooking in a fan oven. This is especially useful for delicate bakes like cookies or cakes, where precision matters.
