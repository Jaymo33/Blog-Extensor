<h2>Why Convert 146 C to Fan Oven?</h2>
<p>Fan ovens circulate hot air more efficiently than conventional ovens, cooking food faster at lower temperatures. When a recipe states 146 C, it's designed for still air conditions. This principle applies whether you're using a built-in oven or countertop appliances like air fryers.</p>
