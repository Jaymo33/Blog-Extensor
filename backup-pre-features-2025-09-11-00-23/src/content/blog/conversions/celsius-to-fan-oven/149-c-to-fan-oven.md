<h3>Troubleshooting Common Fan Oven Conversion Issues</h3>
<p>If your food cooks too quickly or unevenly at 149 C in fan mode, consider these adjustments. First, verify your oven's calibration - some models run hotter or cooler than their displays indicate. An inexpensive oven thermometer can help identify discrepancies. Second, adjust rack positioning - middle racks typically provide the most even heat distribution in both fan ovens and air fryers. Finally, consider covering dishes with foil if surfaces brown too quickly, removing it for the last few minutes to crisp up.</p>
