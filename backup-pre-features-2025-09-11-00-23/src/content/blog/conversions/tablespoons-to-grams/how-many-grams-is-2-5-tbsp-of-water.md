<h2>How we calculate 2.5 tbsp water to grams</h2>
<p>The standard conversion is that 1 tablespoon of water weighs about 15 grams in the UK. Therefore, 2.5 tbsp would be 37.5 grams. However, for practical cooking purposes, we round this to 37 grams. This slight difference won't affect most air fryer recipes, but it's good to know the exact calculation.</p>
<p>Several factors can affect this measurement slightly. The temperature of the water, the exact size of your tablespoon measure, and how level you fill it all play a role. That's why many professional chefs prefer weighing ingredients like water rather than using volume measures when precision matters.</p>
