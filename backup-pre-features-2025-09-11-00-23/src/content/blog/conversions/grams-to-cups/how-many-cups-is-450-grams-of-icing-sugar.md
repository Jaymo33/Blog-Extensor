<h2>UK vs. US Cup Measurements</h2>
<p>In the UK, 1 cup of icing sugar is roughly 125 grams, so 450 grams equals about 3.6 cups. In the US, 1 cup is approximately 120 grams, making 450 grams closer to 3.75 cups. This difference might seem small, but it can impact recipes, especially in air fryer baking where consistency is vital.</p>
<p>For best results, use a kitchen scale to measure 450 grams of icing sugar directly. If you don’t have one, fluff the sugar with a fork before lightly spooning it into a measuring cup. Avoid packing it down, as this leads to inaccurate measurements.</p>
