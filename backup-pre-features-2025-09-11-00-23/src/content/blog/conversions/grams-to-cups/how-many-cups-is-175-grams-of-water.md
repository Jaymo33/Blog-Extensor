<h2>How to Measure 175 Grams of Water Without Scales</h2>  
<p>Not everyone has a kitchen scale, but you can still measure 175 grams of water accurately. Since 1 gram of water equals 1 millilitre, you can use a liquid measuring cup marked in millilitres. Fill it to the 175ml line, and you’ve got the right amount. Alternatively, if using US cups, fill just under 3/4 cup for 175 grams. This method works perfectly for air fryer recipes like soups or batters.</p>
