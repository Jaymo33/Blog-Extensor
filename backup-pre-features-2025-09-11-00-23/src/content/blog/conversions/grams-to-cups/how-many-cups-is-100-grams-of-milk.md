<p>It's important to note that cup sizes differ between the UK and US. A UK cup holds 284ml, while a US cup is slightly smaller at 240ml. <h3>How to Measure 100 Grams of Milk Without Scales</h3>
<p>If you don't have kitchen scales, you can still measure 100 grams of milk using measuring cups. Since milk has a similar density to water, you can use the water equivalent.
