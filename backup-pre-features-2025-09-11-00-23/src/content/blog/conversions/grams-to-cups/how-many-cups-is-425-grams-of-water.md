<h3>UK vs. US Cup Measurements</h3>  
<p>It’s worth noting that UK and US cup measurements differ slightly. In the UK, 1 cup equals 250ml, while in the US, it’s 240ml. For 425 grams of water, this means roughly 1.8 UK cups or 1.77 US cups—a small but sometimes crucial difference in baking. Always double-check which measurement system your recipe uses.</p>
