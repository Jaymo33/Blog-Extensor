<h2>Practical Tips for Converting 221 Fan Oven to Conventional Oven</h2>
<p>If you're using a conventional oven instead of a fan oven, you might need to increase the temperature slightly to compensate for the lack of air circulation. For example, if a recipe calls for 221°F in a fan oven, you might set your conventional oven to 230°F. This adjustment ensures that the food cooks evenly, similar to how it would in a fan oven.</p>
