<h3>US vs. UK Cup Measurements</h3>  
<p>The difference between US and UK cups affects conversions. A US cup holds 240 ml, while a UK cup is 284 ml. This means 635 ml converts to roughly 2.68 US cups or 2.23 UK cups. For air fryer recipes, always check which measurement system your recipe uses to avoid errors.</p>
