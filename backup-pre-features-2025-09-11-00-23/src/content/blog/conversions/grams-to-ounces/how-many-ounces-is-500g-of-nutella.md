<h2>How to Measure 500g of Nutella</h2>
<p>Measuring 500g of Nutella is straightforward with a kitchen scale. Simply place your jar or bowl on the scale, tare it to zero, and scoop out Nutella until you reach 500g. If you don’t have a scale, 500g is roughly two cups, but a scale is more accurate.</p>
