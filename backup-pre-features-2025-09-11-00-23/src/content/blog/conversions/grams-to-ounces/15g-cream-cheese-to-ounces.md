<h3>How to measure 15g of cream cheese without a scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a kitchen scale, you can still measure 15g of cream cheese (0.53 ounces) using tablespoons. One tablespoon of softened cream cheese weighs roughly 14-15g, making it a close approximation. For firmer cream cheese, slightly heap the tablespoon to hit the 15g mark. This method works well for recipes like <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/dessert>air fryer desserts</a> where precision matters.</p>
