<h2>How to measure 30g of mustard accurately</h2>
<p>If you don’t have a scale, you can use tablespoons to measure 30g of mustard. Roughly two level tablespoons equal 30g, but this can vary slightly depending on the mustard’s thickness. For sticky mustards like Dijon, it’s best to use a scale for precision.</p>
