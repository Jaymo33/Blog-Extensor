<h3>How to Measure 75g of Molasses Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a scale, 75g (2.65 oz) of molasses is roughly 3½ tablespoons. However, molasses’ viscosity means spoon measurements can be imprecise. For best results, lightly grease your spoon or cup to help the molasses slide out cleanly. This trick is handy for air fryer recipes like gingerbread or barbecue sauces where molasses is a star ingredient.</p>
