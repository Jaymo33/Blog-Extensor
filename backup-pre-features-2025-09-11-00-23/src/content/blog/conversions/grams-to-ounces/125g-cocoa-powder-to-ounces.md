<h2>How to Convert 125g Cocoa Powder to Ounces</h2>  
<p>The general rule for converting grams to ounces is dividing the grams by 28.35. For 125g, this calculation gives you roughly 4.41 ounces. This conversion is especially useful if your recipe uses imperial measurements or if your kitchen scale only displays ounces. Always double-check your measurements, especially when working with air fryer recipes, as they often require precision.</p>
