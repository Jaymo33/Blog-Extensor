<h3>How to Measure 500g of Oats Accurately</h3>  
<p>Using a digital kitchen scale is the easiest way to measure 500g of oats. If your scale displays ounces, it should read around 17.64 oz for 500g. For those without scales, a standard UK measuring cup holds roughly 80g of oats, so 500g would be about 6.25 cups. However, weighing is always more precise for air fryer cooking.</p>
