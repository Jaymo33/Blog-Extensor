<h2>How to measure 125g of flour accurately</h2>  
<p>Using a digital scale is the most reliable way to measure 125g of flour. Simply place your bowl on the scale, tare it to zero, and spoon in flour until you hit 125g. If you don’t have a scale, fluff the flour with a fork before lightly spooning it into a measuring cup. Avoid packing it down, as this can lead to over-measuring.</p>
