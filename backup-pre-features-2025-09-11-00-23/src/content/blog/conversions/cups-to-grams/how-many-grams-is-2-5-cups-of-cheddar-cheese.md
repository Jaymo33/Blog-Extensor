<h3>How to Measure Cheddar Cheese for Air Fryer Recipes</h3>
<p>For the most accurate results, weigh your cheddar cheese using a digital scale. If you don’t have one, lightly pack the cheese into a measuring cup without pressing too hard. Avoid compacting the cheese, as this can lead to over-measuring. For recipes like air-fryer nachos or cheesy potatoes, precise measurements ensure even melting and distribution.</p>
