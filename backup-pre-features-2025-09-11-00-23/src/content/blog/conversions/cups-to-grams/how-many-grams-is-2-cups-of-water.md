<h2>How to Convert Cups of Water to Grams</h2>  
<p>Converting cups to grams is straightforward for water, as its density is consistent. One cup of water weighs 236.5 grams, so 2.0 cups equal 473 grams. This conversion applies to both UK and US cup measurements, though it’s always wise to double-check your cup size if using older measuring tools.</p>  
<p>For other liquids like oil or milk, the weight differs due to varying densities. However, water’s 1:1 ml-to-gram ratio makes it simple.
