<h2>Why 0.125 cup of apple cider vinegar matters in air fryer cooking</h2>  
<p>Apple cider vinegar is a staple in many air fryer recipes, from crispy wings to tangy dressings. Measuring 0.125 cup (or 1 oz) accurately ensures balanced flavours. Too much vinegar can overpower a dish, while too little might leave it lacking that signature zing. <p>Many air fryer enthusiasts use apple cider vinegar for its health benefits and versatility. It’s perfect for deglazing pans, tenderising meats, or adding acidity to sauces. When working with small quantities like 0.125 cup, using a liquid measuring cup or kitchen scale guarantees accuracy. <h2>How to measure 0.125 cup of apple cider vinegar without a measuring cup</h2>  
<p>Don’t have a 0.125 cup measure? No problem. One fluid ounce is equivalent to 2 tablespoons or 6 teaspoons. You can also use a standard shot glass, which typically holds 1 oz. For those using scales, 1 fluid ounce of apple cider vinegar weighs approximately 28 grams.</p>  
<p>When preparing air fryer recipes, consistency is key. Whether you’re making <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/chicken>air fryer chicken</a> or vegetable dishes, the right amount of apple cider vinegar makes all the difference. Remember that 0.125 cup equals 1 oz, and you’ll never go wrong with your measurements.</p>  

<h3>Common uses for 0.125 cup of apple cider vinegar in air fryer cooking</h3>  
<p>This small but mighty measurement features in many recipes. Use it for marinades, where 0.125 cup (1 oz) of apple cider vinegar tenderises meats beautifully. It’s also ideal for deglazing your air fryer basket after cooking, lifting stuck-on bits effortlessly. Many <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>air fryer recipes</a> call for this precise amount to balance flavours without overwhelming the dish.</p>  

<h3>Tips for working with apple cider vinegar in your air fryer</h3>  
<p>Always measure 0.125 cup of apple cider vinegar carefully to avoid altering your recipe’s taste. Consider diluting it with water or oil for milder acidity. After use, clean your air fryer promptly with our recommended <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-cleaner>air fryer cleaner</a> to prevent lingering vinegar odours. Store your vinegar in a cool, dark place to maintain its quality between uses.</p>
