<h2>Understanding the Conversion</h2>  
<p>Converting millilitres to cups can be tricky, especially with liquids like single cream. The density and viscosity of single cream mean it doesn’t always fill a cup the same way water would. In the US, a standard cup holds 240 ml, while in the UK, it’s 250 ml. This slight difference affects the conversion, so always check which measurement system your recipe uses.
