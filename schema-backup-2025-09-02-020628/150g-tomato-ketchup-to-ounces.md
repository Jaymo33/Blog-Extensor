<h3>How to Measure 150g of Tomato Ketchup</h3>  
<p>Using a kitchen scale is the easiest way to measure 150g of tomato ketchup. If you don’t have one, a standard tablespoon holds about 15g, meaning 10 tablespoons equal roughly 150g (or 5.29 oz). For sticky sauces like ketchup, lightly greasing the spoon helps with accurate pouring. Need more measurement tips? Visit <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk>airfryerrecipe.co.uk</a>.</p>
