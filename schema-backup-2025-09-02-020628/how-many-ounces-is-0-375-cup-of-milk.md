<h2>How to Measure 0.375 Cup of Milk Without a Measuring Cup</h2>
<p>Don’t have a measuring cup handy? No problem! You can still measure 0.375 cup of milk using tablespoons. Since 1 cup equals 16 tablespoons, 0.375 cup is 6 tablespoons. This method is especially useful for smaller quantities like 3 ounces of milk.</p>

<p>Another trick is to use a kitchen scale. Simply set it to ounces, pour your milk until it hits 3 ounces, and you’re good to go.
