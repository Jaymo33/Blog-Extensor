<h2>Converting 3.0 Cups of Vinegar to ml</h2>  
<p>The conversion from cups to millilitres is straightforward: 1 US cup equals 237 ml, so 3.0 cups of vinegar in ml is 710 ml (rounded to the nearest whole number). This applies to all types of vinegar, whether you’re using it for <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/chicken>chicken recipes</a> or tangy glazes. For UK cooks, note that metric cups (250 ml) differ slightly—3.0 UK cups would be 750 ml.</p>  
<p>If you’re scaling a recipe, remember that 3.0 cups of vinegar converted to ml (710 ml) works for both cooking and cleaning solutions. A mix of vinegar and water is a popular natural cleaner for air fryer baskets—check our guide on <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-cleaner>air fryer maintenance</a> for tips.</p>
