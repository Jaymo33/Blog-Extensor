<h2>How to Convert 0.33 Cup of Onion to Ounces</h2>
<p>Converting cups to ounces isn’t always straightforward, as it depends on the ingredient’s density. For onions, 1 cup of chopped onion typically weighs around 5.3 ounces. Therefore, 0.33 cup of onion translates to roughly 1.76 ounces. This conversion is based on standard UK measurements, so you can trust it for your air fryer recipes.</p>
<p>If you’re unsure, using a digital scale is the most reliable method. Simply place your measuring cup on the scale, tare it, and add the onion until you hit 1.76 ounces.
