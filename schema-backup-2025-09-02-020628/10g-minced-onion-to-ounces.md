<h3>How to measure 10g of minced onion without a scale</h3>  
<p>If you don't have a scale, 10g of minced onion is roughly 2 tablespoons. However, this can vary based on how finely the onion is chopped. For best results, lightly pack the minced onion into the spoon. This method works well for dishes like air-fryer frittatas or savory muffins. Always cross-check with a scale if precision is critical.</p>
