<h2>How to measure 1 cup of lemonade correctly</h2>  
<p>To measure 1 cup of lemonade accurately, use a liquid measuring cup designed for UK fluid ounces. Pour the lemonade to the brim without spilling, and check at eye level for precision. If your recipe calls for ounces instead of cups, remember that 1 cup equals roughly 8.45 UK ounces. This is particularly important for air fryer recipes where liquids reduce quickly due to the intense heat. For more tips on perfect measurements, explore our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/dessert>dessert category</a>, where lemonade often features in sweet treats.</p>  

<h3>Converting cups to ounces for different lemonade types</h3>  
<p>Homemade lemonade might have a slightly different density than store-bought versions, affecting the weight. However, the standard 1 cup to 8.45 ounces conversion works for most recipes. If you're using concentrated lemonade, dilute it first before measuring to ensure accuracy. This is especially useful for air fryer marinades, where consistency impacts how well the flavour sticks to your food.</p>
