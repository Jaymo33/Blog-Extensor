<h2>Tips for cooking 4.25 kg chicken wings in your air fryer</h2>
<p>When working with 4.25 kg chicken wings to pounds (9.37 pounds), batch cooking is your best friend. Most air fryers can handle 1-2 pounds per batch, so you’ll likely need to cook in multiple rounds. Preheat your air fryer to 200°C for crispiness, and arrange the wings in a single layer without overlapping. Cook for 20-25 minutes, flipping halfway through, until golden and crispy. Since you’re dealing with a large quantity, keep cooked wings warm in a low oven while you finish the rest. This method ensures every wing is perfectly cooked and ready to serve together.</p>
