<h2>How to Measure 1.25 Cups of Orange Juice Correctly</h2>
<p>Measuring liquids like orange juice can be tricky if you don’t have the right tools. For 1.25 cups (295.74 ml), use a liquid measuring cup with clear ml markings. Pour the juice to the 295 ml line for accuracy. If your recipe calls for a slight adjustment, round up to 300 ml for simplicity.</p>
<p>For those who prefer weight, 1.25 cups of orange juice weighs roughly 300 grams, but ml is the standard for liquids. If you’re prepping a <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/chicken>chicken dish</a> with an orange juice marinade, precise ml measurements ensure even coating and flavour distribution.</p>
