<h3>How to Measure 0.125 Cup of Milk Without a Measuring Cup</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a measuring cup, you can use a tablespoon to measure 0.125 cup of milk. Since 1 cup equals 16 tablespoons, 0.125 cup is 2 tablespoons, which is also equivalent to 1 fluid ounce. This method ensures accuracy even without specialised tools.</p>  

<h2>Tips for Working with Small Milk Measurements</h2>  
<p>When dealing with small quantities like 0.125 cup of milk, using a small whisk or spoon helps incorporate it evenly into mixtures. For recipes requiring warmed milk, heat it gently to avoid curdling, especially when combining with other ingredients.</p>
