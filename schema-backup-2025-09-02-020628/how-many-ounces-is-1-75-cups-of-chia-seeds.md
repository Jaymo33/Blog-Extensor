<h2>How to measure 1.75 cups of chia seeds</h2>
<p>For the most accurate measurement, use a kitchen scale set to ounces. Scoop chia seeds loosely into a measuring cup until you reach 1.75 cups, then weigh them to confirm 9.5 ounces. If you don’t have a scale, fluff the seeds with a spoon before lightly filling the cup. Avoid tapping or packing them down, as this can lead to over-measuring.
