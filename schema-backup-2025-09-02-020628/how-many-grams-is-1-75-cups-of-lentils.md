Whether you're cooking a hearty lentil stew or baking lentil-based snacks, knowing the exact gram measurement ensures consistent results every time. <h2>Why Accurate Lentil Measurements Matter</h2>
<p>When using your air fryer, precise measurements like 1.75 cups of lentils in grams can affect cooking time and texture. Too many lentils might overcrowd the basket, while too few could dry out during cooking. The 350 gram equivalent of 1.75 cups gives you the perfect portion for most air fryer recipes. This measurement works whether you're making crispy lentil snacks or incorporating lentils into <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/vegetables>vegetable dishes</a>.</p>

<p>Different types of lentils (red, green, or brown) have slightly varying weights, but 350 grams is a reliable average. For best results in your air fryer, we recommend using our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-conversion-chart>conversion chart</a> to adjust other ingredients accordingly. This ensures your lentil-based creations come out perfectly cooked every time.</p>

<h3>UK vs US Cup Measurements</h3>
<p>It's worth noting that UK and US cup measurements differ slightly. The 350 gram conversion applies to the US cup measurement (240ml per cup). If using UK cups (284ml), 1.75 cups would be about 420 grams. Most modern recipes, including those on <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipes>our recipe collection</a>, use the US standard for consistency.</p>

<h2>Converting Lentils for Air Fryer Recipes</h2>
<p>When adapting traditional recipes for your air fryer, converting 1.75 cups of lentils to grams (350g) helps maintain the right ingredient ratios. This is particularly important for dishes like air-fried lentil patties or stuffed vegetables where the lentil quantity affects structure. The air fryer's rapid circulation means precise measurements prevent drying or uneven cooking.</p>

<p>For meal preppers, knowing that 1.75 cups equals 350 grams makes portion control easier. You can batch cook lentil dishes in your air fryer and divide them accurately. <h3>Tips for Measuring Lentils</h3>
<p>For the most accurate 1.75 cups to grams conversion, use kitchen scales rather than volume measurements. If you don't have scales, fluff the lentils before spooning them into the measuring cup. Never pack lentils down as this will give you more than 350 grams. This technique ensures your air fryer recipes turn out perfectly every time.</p>
