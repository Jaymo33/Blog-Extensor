<h3>How to Measure 0.66 Cup of Almonds</h3>  
<p>To measure 0.66 cup of almonds, use a dry measuring cup and fill it just over halfway. For greater precision, a kitchen scale set to ounces will give you the exact 3.17 oz equivalent. This method is especially useful for air fryer recipes where even distribution matters.</p>
