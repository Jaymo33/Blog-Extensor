<h3>How to Measure 125g of Milk Accurately</h3>  
<p>Using a digital kitchen scale is the most accurate way to measure 125g of milk. If your scale displays ounces, you’ll see it’s roughly 4.41 oz. For those without scales, a measuring jug with both metric and imperial markings works well. Just remember that milk’s density means volume and weight aren’t always a 1:1 match.</p>
