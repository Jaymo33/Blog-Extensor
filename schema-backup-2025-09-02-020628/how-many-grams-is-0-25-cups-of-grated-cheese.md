Recipes like air fryer nachos or cheesy stuffed mushrooms rely on precise measurements for the best results. <h3>How to Measure 0.25 Cups of Grated Cheese</h3>  
<p>For the most accurate results, use a kitchen scale to weigh 28 grams of grated cheese. If you don’t have one, lightly pack the cheese into a measuring cup without pressing down too hard. This method prevents over-measuring, which can affect texture and cooking time. For more tips, see our guide on <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-cleaner>keeping your air fryer clean</a> after cheesy recipes.</p>

<h3>Tips for Storing Grated Cheese</h3>  
<p>Keep grated cheese fresh by storing it in an airtight container in the fridge. For longer storage, freeze it in portioned bags—just thaw before use. This ensures you always have the right amount ready for your next air fryer creation.</p>
