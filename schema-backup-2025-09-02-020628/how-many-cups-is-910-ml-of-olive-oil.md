<h2>Understanding Olive Oil Measurements</h2>  
<p>Olive oil is often measured in millilitres (ml) or cups, depending on the recipe. For air fryer cooking, precision is key to avoid overly greasy or dry results. Converting 910 ml of olive oil to cups helps streamline your prep work. In the US, 1 cup equals 240 ml, while in the UK, it's 250 ml. This slight difference means 910 ml translates to 3.85 US cups or 3.6 UK cups.</p>
