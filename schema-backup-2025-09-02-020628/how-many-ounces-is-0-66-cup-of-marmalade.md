<h2>How to measure 0.66 cup of marmalade accurately</h2>
<p>For the most accurate 0.66 cup marmalade to ounces conversion, use a kitchen scale. Simply tare your bowl, then spoon in marmalade until you hit 5.28 ounces. No scale? Lightly grease your measuring cup first—this helps the marmalade slide out cleanly. Fill the cup just below the 2/3 mark (since 0.66 cup is roughly 2/3 cup) for the right amount. Remember, marmalade compresses easily, so avoid packing it down.</p>
