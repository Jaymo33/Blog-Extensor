<h3>UK vs. US Cup Measurements for Yogurt</h3>
<p>In the UK, 1 cup equals 284 ml, while in the US, it’s 240 ml. This means 485 ml of yogurt converts to roughly 2.05 cups in the UK and 2.06 cups in the US. The difference is minimal but worth noting for precision. If your air fryer recipe originates from the US, stick to their cup measurements. For UK-based recipes, use the local standard. Always level off your measuring cups for accuracy. A kitchen scale can also help verify your conversions.</p>
