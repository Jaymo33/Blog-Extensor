<h2>Why Accurate Olive Oil Measurements Matter in Air Fryers</h2>  
<p>Air fryers rely on even heat distribution, and using the right amount of oil is crucial. Too little, and your food may turn out dry; too much, and it could become greasy. For example, 2.25 cups of olive oil (532.32 ml) is often used in marinades or for coating vegetables before air frying. Check out our <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/air-fryer-conversion-chart>air fryer conversion chart</a> for other common measurements.</p>  

<p>Olive oil adds flavour and crispiness, but its viscosity means volume-to-millilitre conversions must be spot-on. Unlike water, olive oil has a different density, so standard cup-to-ml conversions don’t always apply. That’s why we’ve calculated 2.25 cups of olive oil in ml specifically for cooking accuracy.</p>
