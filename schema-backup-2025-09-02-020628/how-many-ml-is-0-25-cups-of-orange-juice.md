<h3>How to Measure 0.25 Cups of Orange Juice Correctly</h3>  
<p>Use a liquid measuring cup for accuracy. Pour the orange juice to the 0.25 cup mark or measure 59 ml in a metric jug. Avoid using dry measuring cups, as they won’t give the same precision. For sticky liquids like orange juice, lightly grease the cup for easy pouring.</p>
