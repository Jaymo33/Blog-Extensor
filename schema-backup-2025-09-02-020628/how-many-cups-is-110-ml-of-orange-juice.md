<h3>How to Measure 110 ml of Orange Juice Without a Scale</h3>  
<p>If you don’t have a measuring jug, you can use a standard cup to measure 110 ml of orange juice. Fill the cup to just under halfway, as 0.46 cups is slightly less than half. For even more accuracy, use tablespoons—110 ml equals about 7.3 tablespoons. This method works well for small-batch recipes, like glazes for air-fried shrimp or tofu.</p>
