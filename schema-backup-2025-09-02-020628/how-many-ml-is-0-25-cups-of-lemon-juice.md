<h3>How to Measure 0.25 Cups of Lemon Juice Correctly</h3>  
<p>To measure 0.25 cups of lemon juice accurately, use a liquid measuring cup with clear markings. Fill it to the 59 ml line, or use a tablespoon (15 ml) for smaller increments. If you’re squeezing fresh lemons, about 1-2 medium lemons will yield the right amount. Always strain the juice to avoid seeds or pulp affecting your measurement.</p>
