<h2>How to Measure 560 ml of Maple Syrup</h2>  
<p>Measuring liquids like maple syrup can be tricky due to their viscosity. For 560 ml, using a liquid measuring cup with clear markings is best. If you don’t have one, a standard metric-to-imperial conversion will do—560 ml equals roughly 2.37 cups.</p>  

<h3>Using a Kitchen Scale</h3>  
<p>For even greater accuracy, weigh your maple syrup. Since 1 ml of maple syrup weighs about 1.33 grams, 560 ml would be approximately 745 grams. This method is ideal for professional bakers or those following precise recipes from <a href=https://www.airfryerrecipe.co.uk/recipe-category/dessert>dessert recipes</a>.</p>
