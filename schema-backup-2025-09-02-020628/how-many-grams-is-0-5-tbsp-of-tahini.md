<h2>How to Measure Tahini Correctly</h2>
<p>Measuring tahini can be tricky due to its thick, sticky consistency. To measure 0.5 tbsp of tahini (7.5 grams), use a levelled tablespoon or a kitchen scale for accuracy. If you don’t have a scale, lightly grease the spoon to help the tahini slide off easily. This method ensures you get the right amount every time.</p>

<h3>Using a Kitchen Scale</h3>
<p>A kitchen scale is the most accurate way to measure 0.5 tbsp of tahini in grams. Simply place your bowl on the scale, tare it to zero, and add tahini until it reaches 7.5 grams. This method eliminates guesswork and ensures consistency in your air fryer recipes.</p>
